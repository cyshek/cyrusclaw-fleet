# Cover answers — Ixllearning, Associate Product Manager, New Grad (ixl-learning-8574203002)

## In a few sentences, tell us why you are interested in the Associate Product Manager role at IXL?

At Microsoft, I've spent the past two years writing feature specs, running user interviews, and shipping products to enterprise customers at scale - work that taught me how much clarity in requirements and user advocacy matter when you're building something millions of people depend on. What draws me to IXL specifically is that the same rigor applies here, but the end users are students and teachers, and the stakes feel even more direct. I've always believed education is one of the highest-leverage places to apply product thinking, and IXL's scale - reaching 1 in 4 students in the US - means that getting the product right has real, compounding impact on how people learn. This role is where I want to deepen that craft.
