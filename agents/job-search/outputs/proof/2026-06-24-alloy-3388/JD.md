## About the team

This Solutions Engineering Associate role sits within Alloy's pre-sales organization. In this role, you will partner closely with Account Executives and Solutions Engineers to support the technical aspects of the sales process to help support and drive deals forward.

Alloy operates in a hybrid-work environment. We look to foster collaboration and community by having our local employees onsite three days a week.

## **What you'll be doing**

Successful Solutions Engineering Associates will develop strong expertise in Alloy’s platform, APIs, and the identity and fraud landscape, enabling them to help present the best technical solutions to our customers.

You will contribute to the technical work that enables prospects and customers to understand how Alloy fits into their tech stack and business processes. This includes, but is not limited to: 

- Delivering product demonstrations for select upsell and cross-sell opportunities

- Building customized demo environments and product demonstration experiences

- Supporting and executing proof-of-concept (POC) and proof-of-value (POV) exercises

- Working cross-functionally with teams like Product, Engineering, and Customer Success to answer technical questions and unblock customer use cases

## **Who we’re looking for**

- 2+ years of experience in a technical or customer-facing role such as Sales Engineering, Solutions Engineering, Solutions Consulting, Technical Support, or a similar role supporting technical products

- A degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience

- Strong presentation and communication skills, with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to both technical and business audiences

- Demonstrated curiosity and a strong desire to learn new technologies quickly in a fast-moving environment

- A thoughtful communicator who listens first and asks targeted follow-up questions to understand customer needs

- Comfortable working in ambiguous environments while maintaining integrity, accountability, and strong collaboration with teammates

- Hands on experience working with APIs, including but not limited to making API calls using cURL, Postman, or related technologies

- Ability to troubleshoot and diagnose technical issues, including debugging API requests and identifying root causes

- Experience reading and working with object-oriented code. Scripting/coding ability is a huge plus!

- Experience working in the Fintech/Banking space is a plus!

We're a lean team, so your impact will be felt immediately, and opportunities will grow as the company scales up. If this all sounds like a good fit for you, why not join us?
