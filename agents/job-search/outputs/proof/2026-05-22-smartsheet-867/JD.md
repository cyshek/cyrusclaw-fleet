We are looking to hire a Product Manager II to join our Product Organization. This role is critical to accelerating our growth by improving our Collaboration Experiences by championing a Product-Led Growth mindset. You will own the strategy for how users collaborate and expand their use of Smartsheet organically, with a focus on maximizing conversion, retention, and viral growth loops through relentless experimentation.

You will report to our Director, Product Management located in our Bellevue, WA office, or you may work remotely from anywhere in the US where Smartsheet is a registered employer.

**You Will:**

- Define a set of features/experiences based on customer needs, market trends, and business outcomes

- Socialize your work and drive alignment with cross-functional leaders; work closely with design and engineering to create compelling, high-quality customer experiences

- Regularly present strategy, product concepts and roadmap to educate, influence and align with senior leadership

- Use internal and external data to guide experimentation, A/B testing, decisions, opportunities and evaluation of success

- Partner with research, product design, data science and analytics teams to run quantitative experiments and analysis to test hypotheses and iteratively develop product capabilities that our customers love

- Support other duties as needed

**You Have:**

- 3+ years of product management experience, with substantial experience in a Product-Led Growth environment or building viral/sharing features

- A data-driven mentality with a deep focus on experimentation, metrics, and analytics

- Experience partnering closely with engineering, design, user research and marketing teams working on agile development and frequent product releases

- High interpersonal skills to build trusted and strategic cross-team partnerships rooted in customer benefit

- Technical degree, or equivalent experience working with engineers to make product decisions on behalf of customers

- Comfortable using AI tools (e.g., Claude, Lovable, Cursor, or similar) to accelerate work, with a curious and experimental mindset toward emerging capabilities. But also able to think critically about where AI adds value vs. where human judgment is essential
