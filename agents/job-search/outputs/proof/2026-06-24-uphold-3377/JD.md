## About Uphold
Uphold is a financial technology company that believes on-chain services are the future of finance. It provides modern infrastructure for on-chain payments, banking, and investments. Offering Consumer Services, Business Services, and Institutional Trading, Uphold makes pioneering financial services easy and trusted for millions of customers in more than 140 countries.

Uphold integrates with more than 30 trading venues, including centralized and decentralized exchanges, to deliver superior liquidity and optimal execution. Uphold never loans out customer assets and is always 100% reserved. The company pioneered radical transparency and uniquely publishes its assets and liabilities every 30 seconds on a public website.

## Job Summary
We are looking for a curious, customer-obsessed Junior Product Manager to own the strategy, execution, and roadmap that makes crypto part of people's daily financial life. This is a role for someone who deeply understands customer pain points, especially around banking services, and is passionate about solving them. You'll work across a fast-moving, cross-functional team and be expected to use AI tools extensively as part of how you work every day. Most importantly, you have felt the pain of using banking, credit, or debit cards and want to solve problems for millions of customers.

## Essential Duties and Responsibilities
- Own and evolve the product roadmap from core bank-like features: new types of accounts, credit and debit cards, and account management
- Deeply understand customer pain points through data, support tickets, user interviews, and direct feedback and translate those insights into product decisions
- Partner with design, engineering, compliance, and customer success to ship features that meaningfully improve the customer experience
- Use AI tools extensively to accelerate research, prioritization, documentation, and analysis — fluency in AI-assisted workflows is expected
- Utilize LLMs to generate prototype applications and experiences in order to accurately communicate your vision
- Write clear, concise product requirements and user stories that keep the customer at the center
- Monitor product performance metrics and synthesize findings into actionable next steps
- Stay close to the competitive neo banking landscape and bring fresh thinking to the team
