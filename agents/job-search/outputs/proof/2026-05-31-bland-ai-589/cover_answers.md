## What’s a habit you have that your friends would make fun of? (Be specific. We value authenticity over polish)

I turn everything into a dashboard. Not just work stuff. I have spreadsheets and little tracking systems for my workouts, my reading, even what I'm cooking through during a given month. My friends give me grief because they'll ask a casual question like 'how's the gym going' and I'll pull up actual numbers and trends instead of just saying 'good.' It started because I was building dashboards at work for platform health and customer impact, and at some point it leaked into my personal life and never left.

The other one is that I cannot leave a small annoying problem alone. If an app or a script I use does something dumb, I'll lose an evening automating around it even when the manual version would have taken thirty seconds. My friends point out the math doesn't work, and they're right, but I get more satisfaction from killing the annoyance permanently than from the time saved. It's the same instinct that made me enjoy cutting operational toil at work, just applied to things that genuinely do not deserve that much effort.
