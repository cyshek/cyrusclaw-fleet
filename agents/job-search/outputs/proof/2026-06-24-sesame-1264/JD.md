**About the Role**

We are seeking a Senior Technical Program Manager to lead Sesame’s data collection and annotation initiatives. In this role, you will gather data requirements from research and product teams, prioritize projects, identify and manage internal or external resources, and ensure the delivery of high-quality data.

**Responsibilities:**

- Lead audio data collection and annotation efforts at Sesame.

- Collaborate with research and product teams to understand and formalize their requirements.

- Identify and manage internal resources and/or external vendors to complete data tasks.

- Ensure the delivery of high-quality work.

- Work closely with tooling engineers to support data-related tasks.

- Oversee data acquisition processes.

**Required Qualifications:**

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related field.

- 5+ years of experience working with machine learning researchers and engineers.

- Proven experience leading data labeling projects.

- Experience managing full-time employees or contractors involved in data labeling.

- Expertise in writing technical specifications for data annotation pipelines.

**Preferred Qualifications:**

- Understanding of ML data pipelines and their applications.

- Experience working with LLMs.

- Familiarity with data labeling for audio technologies, such as speech recognition and speech synthesis.
