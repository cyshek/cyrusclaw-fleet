A small team was burning hours on coordination instead of the high-value work of actually validating recovery, and the toil only grew as we took on more drills.

So I built the fix. I led the 0-to-1 delivery of an internal Resilience Automation Platform that turned the ad hoc planning into self-service scheduling, which cut operational toil by 30% and let us sustain 45+ annual drills. On top of that I shipped an internal AI agent to automate the drill planning itself, restructuring the workflows to cut planning cycle time by 39% and increase drill capacity by 21%.
