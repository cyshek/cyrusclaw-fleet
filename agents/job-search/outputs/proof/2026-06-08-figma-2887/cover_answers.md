# Cover answers — Figma, Program Manager, Voice of the Customer (figma-6008343004)

## Why do you want to join Figma?

At Microsoft I built and ran Azure's company-scale resilience Voice-of-the-Customer loop, pulling signals from Support, partner escalations, and drill telemetry into one narrative that reached Product and Engineering and drove $14M+ in impact across customers like Databricks, Walmart, SAP, and NetApp. I owned the full insight loop end-to-end: surfacing and prioritizing the biggest pain points, aligning Engineering on roadmap changes, then tracking remediation and bringing the wins back to customer-facing teams to prove the program mattered. That is exactly the loop this role describes, and I'm good at the unglamorous parts, defining the reporting rhythm, setting shared data-backed goals with Product partners, and using AI workflows to move faster (I built an internal AI agent that cut cycle time 39%).

Figma appeals to me because it sits at the intersection I care about: a product people genuinely love, and a company that takes the customer's voice seriously enough to staff a real VOC program inside Support rather than treating it as a reporting afterthought. The chance to integrate NPS, Sales, Research, and community into one signal that actually changes the roadmap, at the scale Figma operates, is the kind of build-something-that-matters problem I want to spend my time on. I also like that the role expects a strong point of view and a bias for AI-driven, lightweight processes, because that's how I already work.
