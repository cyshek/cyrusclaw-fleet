# **About the Role**

We're looking for an **Agent Product Manager** to own one of our core product lines and drive it from discovery through deployment.

This is not a generalist PM role. You'll go deep on a specific product area, working directly with engineering, design partners, and customers to understand what needs to be built, why, and how to ship it in a way that scales. You'll be the connective tissue between customer reality and engineering execution, synthesizing feedback from CSMs, TAMs, and customers into clear, actionable product requirements.

The best fit here is someone who obsesses over product longevity, not just individual customer happiness, and who can build rapidly, think in systems, and translate ambiguity into clear direction.

## **What You’ll Do**

**Product Line Strategy**

- Own one or more product lines end-to-end, from discovery through delivery and iteration

- Define product roadmaps grounded in real deployment data and customer feedback, not feature requests

- Prioritize ruthlessly between customer-specific asks and platform-wide improvements

- Establish clear boundaries on what the product can and cannot do, and ensure sales and CS teams understand them

**Cross-Functional Alignment**

- Bridge the gap between sales promises and engineering reality, guide sales on which features to pitch, which customer profiles to target, and what realistic timelines look like

- Work with engineering to shift from one-off custom builds to iterative, productized solutions

- Collaborate with support to build self-service tooling that eliminates manual configuration bottlenecks

- Own the feedback loop: ensure customer insights reach engineering as structured product requirements, not ad hoc requests

## **What You'll Bring**

- 3 to 6 years of product management experience, ideally in B2B SaaS or AI-enabled products

- Strong ability to synthesize qualitative customer feedback into structured, engineering-ready requirements

- Experience working directly with customers or design partners in a product capacity

- Comfort navigating ambiguity and competing priorities across product, engineering, and go-to-market teams

- Familiarity with AI, LLMs, or conversational AI systems, or a strong ability to ramp quickly

- Outcome-driven: you measure success in shipped impact, not process followed

- Low ego, high energy, resilient in a fast-paced environment

- Bonus: experience with enterprise deployments, multi-location operators, or platform infrastructure

- Eagerness to work in person with us in NYC full time
