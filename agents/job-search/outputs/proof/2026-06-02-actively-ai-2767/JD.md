### About Actively AI

[Actively AI](http://www.actively.ai/) is defining a new category: Intelligence-Led Revenue.

Revenue organizations have always been bottlenecked on human capacity. Reps triage which accounts get attention. Context disappears at every handoff. On any given day, the vast majority of accounts have exactly zero people thinking about them.

Actively addresses this at the structural level. Our platform deploys Per-Account AgentsTM across our customers’ TAM, working 24/7 to research, identify opportunities, and advance next steps without being asked. Leading enterprises including Ramp, Ironclad, and Samsara are already making this shift.

Our co-founders are former Stanford AI researchers, and the team comes from Harvard, CMU, Berkeley, Brex, Scale AI, and Google. We've raised $68M from TCV, First Harmonic, Bain Capital Ventures, First Round Capital, and more.

We are hiring an **AI Solutions Architect (GTM)** to partner with our go-to-market team and enterprise prospects at the earliest and most critical stages of the buying journey.

In this role, you will work directly with prospective customers — including CROs, VP-level AI and Data leaders, and RevOps teams — to understand their GTM challenges, evaluate build-vs-buy decisions, and architect AI-powered GTM systems that drive **~30% productivity lifts** across revenue organizations producing **$500M+ annually**.

You will serve as the technical and architectural counterpart to our Enterprise Account Executives, shaping solution narratives, leading technical discovery, and elevating tactical execution problems into **exec-worthy systems of leverage**. This role is not about demos alone — it’s about helping senior leaders see what is now possible with AI-native GTM workflows.

This role exists to give CROs access to **leverage they have never had before**.If you are energized by helping ambitious companies rethink how revenue is built — not incrementally, but structurally — this role will stretch and reward you.

## **What You’ll Do**

### Deal Acceleration & Technical Alignment

- Partner closely with Enterprise AEs, SDRs, and RevOps to drive technical pre-sales from discovery through POC recommendation

- Lead early-stage technical discovery to understand GTM workflows, data environments, integrations, and operational risk

- Explain Actively’s AI-powered architecture, capabilities, and constraints clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders

- Serve as a strategic advisor to CROs and GTM leaders on how AI-powered workflows can augment ICs, managers, and RevOps teams

### Architecture, Leverage & POC Framing

- Architect enterprise-grade GTM operating models that translate tactical AI capabilities into **org-wide productivity leverage**

- Frame build-vs-buy decisions credibly for VP-level AI and Data leaders

- Identify when a deal is ready for a POC and articulate the **value hypothesis, risks, and stakes** that justify it

- Design and deliver demos and working sessions that reflect real customer environments and future-state GTM workflows

### Handoff & Execution

- Execute a clean handoff to the **Agent Product Manager** once a POC is of interest

- Maintain continuity from pre-sales architecture through post-sale execution

## **What We’re Looking For**

- Experience in customer-facing technical roles such as Solutions Architecture or Sales Engineering in enterprise environments

- Strong working knowledge of modern AI systems, including LLMs, orchestration, AI-driven workflows, data pipelines, and evaluation approaches

- Ability to think in **systems and architectures**, not just features or tools

- Comfort operating at executive altitude with CROs, VP-level AI leaders, and GTM operators

- A bias toward clarity, ownership, and real-world customer impact
