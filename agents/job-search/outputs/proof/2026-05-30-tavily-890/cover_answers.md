# Cover answers — Tavily, Forward Deployed Engineer (tavily-1999e01d-f9e3-4f43-a097-812c0800fb0f)

## Why Tavily?

At Microsoft, I shipped an AI agent for drill planning that used LLMs and RAG over internal docs to cut planning cycle time by 39% and lift drill capacity by 21%. Building that taught me firsthand how much retrieval quality determines whether an agent is actually useful, and how much time engineers waste duct-taping search layers that weren't designed for LLMs. Tavily is solving exactly that problem at the infrastructure layer, and I want to be on the side of the stack that makes every downstream agent better.

The Forward Deployed role is the version of my job I like most. At Microsoft I've been embedded with Databricks, Walmart, SAP, and NetApp scoping integrations from pre-sales through post-deployment, codifying recurring patterns into reusable architectures, and feeding partner pain back into the roadmap. Doing that for teams building real agentic systems on Tavily, in NYC, at a stage where my prototypes and customer feedback directly shape the product, is the work I want to be doing next.
