## Why do you want to work at Profound?

At Microsoft I owned the end-to-end discovery, execution, and delivery of Azure's recovery validation platform, turning a black-box manual process into a measurable analytics system that sustained 45+ annual drills and drove $14M+ in business impact. The core of that work was defining how enterprise customers measure and benchmark performance, building the dashboards and self-service tooling that made murky data legible, and staying hands-on writing SQL and fixing data quality issues. That is exactly the muscle the Analytics PM role needs: taking complex signal and turning it into clear, actionable insight that customers trust.

Profound is building the measurement layer for the single biggest shift in marketing in 25 years. As people move from search to answer engines to decide what to buy, brands are flying blind, and giving them visibility into share of voice, content health, and prompt volumes is a genuinely new category, not a reskin of old BI. I want to own products like Answer Engine Insights at a company moving at this pace, in front of customers every day, defining how an entire industry measures itself. That combination of zero-to-one data products, real customer urgency, and a frontier I find genuinely interesting is what pulls me here.
