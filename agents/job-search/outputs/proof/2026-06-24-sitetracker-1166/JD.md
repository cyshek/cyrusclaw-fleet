The Opportunity
This is a critical, career-defining opportunity to bridge the strategic gap between executive business vision and flawless technical execution for our most valued enterprise clients. You will serve as the elite technical authority, defining the architectural blueprint ("the How") that prevents unscalable, fragmented code and ensures every complex integration aligns perfectly with our customers' Value Validation Plans (VVPs). Join a team that is the tip of the spear for a modernized engineering engine, tackling high-stakes architectural challenges alongside a centralized development team.
What You'll Do
You will operate as a continuous "technical co-founder" for our largest enterprise accounts, driving architectural strategy post-go-live. Your expertise will be integral to FDE Agile Pods, where you'll design intricate solutions that efficiently consume delivery time within FDE Tiers or ETS hours, without the administrative burden of SOW budgets or implementation timelines. This role moves beyond project-based configurations, empowering you to solve the most complex, high-stakes architectural challenges and shape the long-term technical health and success of our global partners. You will be instrumental in modernizing our engineering engine, working with an elite centralized development team to build enterprise-grade SaaS solutions that set new industry benchmarks.
