## What is the most relevant technical internal product you have worked on?

The most relevant thing I've built is Azure's Resilience Automation Platform - an internal system I took from 0 to 1 at Microsoft. I defined the product requirements, wrote the self-service scheduling specs, and established the adoption criteria that transitioned execution from a hands-on operational model to a scalable, maintainable one. That combination of data-heavy technical scope, cross-org dependency management, and internal platform ownership is exactly what this role is asking for, which is a big part of why I'm interested.
