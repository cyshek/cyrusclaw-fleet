**What you will do**

- *Own and improve monetization-related user journeys across both B2C and B2B scenarios, including checkout, upgrade, downgrade, renewal, cancellation, refund, and billing communication flows. *

- *Work closely with the monetization strategy lead to translate pricing, packaging, and plan rules into clear and seamless product experiences. *

- *Optimize UI/UX for purchase and subscription management touchpoints across platforms, ensuring users understand their options, status, and next steps. *

- *Help define product requirements for plan display, entitlement presentation, access states, and consumer/business experience coexistence. *

- *Partner with Engineering, Design, Growth, and Operations to deliver monetization features and improve end-to-end user journeys. *

- *Track key funnel metrics, identify friction points, and support experiments and iterations to improve conversion and lifecycle experience.*
