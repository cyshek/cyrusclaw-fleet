Blend is a diverse team of problem solvers who believe that the world’s financial resources should be more accessible. Our cloud banking platform is used by Wells Fargo, U.S. Bank, and over 330 other financial services firms to acquire more customers, increase productivity, and deliver end-to-end digital experiences. Our software enables our customers to process an average of more than $5 billion in loans per day, making it possible for consumers to reach their financial goals faster and lead better lives. Come do work that matters.

 

As a **Sales Engineer- Enterprise**, you’ll be the technical heart of the sales and post-sale expansion process—part product expert, part strategist, part storyteller. You’ll partner closely with Sales, Client Sales and Customer Success Managers who manage our largest clients. The Enterprise Account teams work with financial institutions who are 1-20B AUM to identify opportunities, guide technical evaluations, and deliver compelling product demonstrations tailored to real-world banking needs.

We’re looking for a **dynamic, curious, and highly technical communicator **who’s passionate about digital transformation in financial services. If you thrive at the intersection of technology, strategy, and client-facing collaboration—this is your role. Outstanding Solutions Engineers should have a keen eye for spotting market trends, a willingness to inspire clients, creativity for selling beyond the demo, and the ability to work both independently and within a team. 

**How you'll contribute: **

- Be the go Be the go-to product and technical expert for your solution area, continually expanding your expertise on both product functionality and industry use cases.

- Partner with sales and customer success teams to identify opportunities, qualify technical fit, and lead evaluations from discovery through demo and solution design.

- Deliver exceptional product demonstrations that resonate with stakeholders—from frontline operators to C-level execs—adapting narratives based on their unique goals and challenges.

- Translate complex technical concepts into clear value propositions, including specs, integration needs, architectural diagrams, and implementation paths.

- Collaborate with product, engineering, marketing, product marketing, and business development to feed customer insights back into product strategy and GTM motions.

- Keep a pulse on customer feedback, market trends, and usage patterns—and communicate them internally to shape roadmap and go-to-market strategy.

- Build and maintain documentation, including presentation content, workflow and architecture diagrams, and recorded assets to scale enablement efforts.

- Elevate the quality of internal demo environments, tools, and delivery methods to continuously improve customer engagement
