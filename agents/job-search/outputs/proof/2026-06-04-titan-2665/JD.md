**About Titan**

Titan is an award-winning wealth manager with a mission to increase our generation’s compound growth rate. We plan to do this by putting a human wealth advisor in every pocket. Historically, wealth managers have been accessible to only the ultra-wealthy. With Titan, they’re available to everyone. We harness frontier technologies like artificial intelligence to build a platform that gives advisors superpowers, enabling them to serve clients they historically never could. Backed by a16z, General Catalyst, Sam Altman, and many others, we manage over $1 billion in assets for clients across the country.

**Why Join Titan**

For the right person, we believe Titan will be one of the most rewarding jobs they ever have. 

- **Ambitious Vision: **When we put private wealth management in every pocket, we’ll make a legacy-defining impact in growing our generation’s wealth to new heights.

- **World-Class Investors: **We’re backed by top investors who share our bold vision, including a16z, General Catalyst, Y Combinator, and Sam Altman.

**Role Overview**

You will be the only PM at our company - the central node of how we execute cross-functionally. You’ll be the first, or “founding” PM for Titan’s RIA. So it goes without saying that you should have exceptional PM skills that can thrive in a super fast-paced, complex, multi-stakeholder environment.

We’re looking for people who’ve had similar experiences in their past. An ex-founder background is a plus; so is building complex things zero to one from scratch a part of a high performing team. What matters most is that you bring energy, ownership, attention to detail, strong communication, and a high bar to everything you touch.

**What You'll Do**

In light of the above, this is not a “maintain and optimize” product role; it’s an “all-hands-on-deck,” “zero-to-one” seat. As Titan’s Product Manager, you will:

- Live at the center of our execution motion ensuring that all projects & stakeholders are focused, on track, unblocked, and cohesively organized

- Partner deeply with all teams (Engineering, Design, Wealth Advisory, Client Experience, Investment Management) teams to turn our roadmap into documentation, prototypes, and products that ship

- Ensure we systematically incorporate client feedback & data religiously into our execution motion and product bets

- Help launch new products and features that rapidly validate or invalidate hypotheses

- Drive a culture where speed, courage, and experimentation are balanced with rigor, empathy, and a commitment to excellence

**Qualifications**

- **Founder-like Operator** → Proven ability to take ideas from roadmap to execution in ambiguous environments. Former / Current founders welcome.

- **Scrappy, Organized** → You’re extremely hands-on & resourceful, able to bring order to fast-paced, ambitious start up environments

- **Data-Driven** → You track what matters and use it to make better decisions.

- **Wartime Experience** → You've thrived in a fast-moving, resource-constrained environment. Ideally you've been the first or only PM at a start up high-growth tech company.

- **User Obsession & Insights-driven Mindset** → Deep empathy for clients and passion for translating insights into product decisions.

- **Fintech Experience / Acumen** → Strong understanding of financial products, regulations, and user behavior in the space. Experience in a regulated industry is a plus.
