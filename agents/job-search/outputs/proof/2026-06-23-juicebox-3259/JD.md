**The Role**

We’re looking for a** Product Manager **to build and own the product function at Juicebox from the ground up.

This role will be the connective tissue between customers, engineering, design, sales, and customer success teams. You’ll be the face of the product externally, the voice of the customer internally, and a key partner to our engineering and design teams as we prioritize, ship, and iterate.

This is a highly impactful, high-ownership role with real influence over our product roadmap, workflows, and long-term product strategy.

**You Will**

- Translate customer business needs into clear, comprehensive product requirements (PRDs, specs, tickets)

- Translate Juicebox’s vision into real product features

- Partner closely with engineering and design to lead projects end-to-end

- Act as the primary point of contact for product feedback and requests, both internally and externally

- Spend significant time talking directly with customers to understand pain points, workflows, and unmet needs

- Triage and prioritize bugs vs. features, balancing short-term customer needs with long-term product vision

- Advocate for priorities backed by data, customer insight, and strong judgment

- Work cross-functionally with ​​sales and customer success to ensure tight feedback loops

- Help define and guide quarterly product roadmaps

- Establish lightweight but effective product workflows and processes as the company scales

**You Have**

- 2+ years of Product Management experience (4-5+ years preferred)

- Experience working in early-stage or high growth startup environments

- Strong prioritization, judgment, and communication skills

- Comfort operating in ambiguity and owning outcomes end-to-end

- Proven ability to translate customer needs into clear product requirements
