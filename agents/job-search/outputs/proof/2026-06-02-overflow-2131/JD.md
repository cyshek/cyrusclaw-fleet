### **Who We Are**

Overflow is a Series B Silicon Valley technology company with a bold mission: to inspire the world to give. We are a B2B SaaS philanthropy platform that enables modern giving across cash, stock, and crypto for nonprofits, churches, and mission-driven organizations.

Backed by leading investors including Uncork Capital, Craft Ventures, and Village Global, Overflow builds secure, beautifully designed infrastructure that powers generosity at scale.

### **Our Founding Principles**

Overflow was founded on the belief that generosity transforms people and communities. Inspired by Proverbs 11:24 — “The world of the generous gets larger and larger” — we operate with integrity, stewardship, service, and excellence.

### **Who You Are**

Overflow is seeking a mission-aligned, results-oriented Solutions Engineer to support both the sales and onboarding experience for our partners. In this role, you’ll work cross-functionally with the GTM, Product, Engineering, and Onboarding teams to guide churches and nonprofits through technical discovery, integrations, implementation, and activation. You’ll serve as a trusted technical advisor during the sales process, helping prospects understand Overflow’s platform, integrations, security, and implementation approach while translating technical concepts into clear business value.

Following the sale, you’ll help ensure successful onboarding and implementation across Overflow products — from integrations and data imports to troubleshooting and partner support. The ideal candidate combines technical fluency, strong communication skills, adaptability, and customer empathy with a passion for helping mission-driven organizations amplify generosity and achieve their missions while embodying Overflow’s values in daily work.

### **What You Will Do?**

- **Support the Sales Process:** Partner with Account Executives during discovery calls, demos, technical reviews, and strategic conversations to communicate Overflow’s platform capabilities, integrations, implementation approach, and technical value proposition.

- **Lead Technical Discovery & Solution Design:** Identify customer operational needs, integration requirements, reporting workflows, migration considerations, and ecosystem dependencies in order to recommend scalable solutions tailored to each partner.

- **Drive Successful Implementations:** Support partners through onboarding, activation, integrations, data imports, and troubleshooting to ensure a smooth and successful implementation experience across Overflow products.

- **Collaborate Cross-Functionally:** Work closely with GTM, Product, Engineering, and Onboarding teams to solve technical challenges, improve partner experience, and support customers throughout the full lifecycle.

- **Serve as a Technical Advisor:** Become a subject matter expert on Overflow’s platform, APIs, integrations, and payment infrastructure while translating technical concepts into clear, customer-friendly communication.

- **Improve Processes & Enablement:** Help build scalable Solutions Engineering and Technical Account Management processes, including documentation, implementation guides, training resources, and internal best practices.

- **Problem Solve & Escalate Effectively:** Troubleshoot technical issues, proactively identify implementation risks, and partner with Engineering to ensure bugs and customer feedback are clearly communicated and resolved.

- **Build Customer Confidence:** Act as a trusted technical partner who helps churches and nonprofits feel supported, informed, and confident throughout their partnership with Overflow.
