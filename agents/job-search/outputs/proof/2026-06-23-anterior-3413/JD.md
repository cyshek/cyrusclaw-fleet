## **About the Role:**

The Technical Solutions Architect at Anterior will own how we design and deliver solutions for our enterprise customers — from scoping integrations through to successful deployment. You'll work across engineering, product, clinical, and customer-facing teams to drive programs that span our platform, integrations, and enterprise deployments.

- Designing and driving end-to-end solutions for enterprise customers, from scoping through delivery

- Coordinating across engineering, product, clinical, and customer teams to keep enterprise integrations and deployments on track

- Identifying dependencies, risks, and blockers early — and resolving them before they become problems

- Building lightweight program management processes that fit a fast-moving ~10-person engineering team (not importing big-company overhead)

- Managing the cadence of engineering planning, tracking progress, and ensuring the team has clear priorities

- Working directly with enterprise customers' technical teams to architect integration solutions, scope timelines, and define requirements

- Driving incident response coordination and postmortem follow-through

- Contributing to how we scale our deployment and solutions practice — processes, tooling, and playbooks that work for 5 customers and will still work for 50
