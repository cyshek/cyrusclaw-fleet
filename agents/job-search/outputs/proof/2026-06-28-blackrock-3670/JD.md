**About this role**

Company: BlackRock Investment Management, LLC

Job Title: Associate, Product Manager

Location: 1 University Square Drive, Princeton, NJ 8540

Job Duties: Leverage deep expertise in the Separately Managed Accounts (SMA) business model to deliver end‑to‑end solutions, collaborating closely with Portfolio Managers, Operations, Onboarding, Billing, and Data Engineering teams. Expert knowledge of Aladdin applications to help troubleshoot issues and ensure seamless and timely support for portfolio and account management teams. Partner with cross‑functional stakeholders across Implementation, Onboarding, Marketing and Legal & Compliance in a technical capacity to execute large‑scale client transitions, improving scalability while mitigating operational risk. Support the development of a client account management portal by analyzing data requirements, planning the development pipeline, ensuring accurate and timely delivery of portal data feeds to enable a seamless client experience. Collaborate with engineering and data teams to design data models and semantic layers, manage data pipelines, and implement rigorous data‑quality checks. Define and manage the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for Data Insights capabilities, ensuring alignment with the needs of Business, Portfolio Managers, Advisors, and Operations teams. Strong understanding of SMA client deliverables, such as performance statements, fact sheets, and related reporting, and oversee processes to generate and validate these materials accurately.

Qualifications: Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, or a related field and three (3) years of experience in the job offered or as an Associate, Analyst or a related role. Must have two (2) years of experience with: Separately Managed Accounts (SMA) business model, including client deliverables including performance statements and fact sheets, along with hands-on experience using the BlackRock Aladdin technology platform; and Implementing and managing data quality checks and controls within financial data environments. Must have one (1) year of experience with: Defining data requirements and working with engineering and data teams to design data models, semantic layers and data pipelines; Collaborating with portfolio managers, operations, onboarding and billing teams to support client transitions; Provide management of financial services projects through determining sizing and scope, establishing target timelines, driving execution of component steps, performing testing, preparing project artifacts, and maintaining stakeholder engagement; and Supporting development of client facing portals in financial services, including defining data requirements and ensuring accuracy to delivered data feeds.
