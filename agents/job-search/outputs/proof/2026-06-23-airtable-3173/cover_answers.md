# Cover answers — Airtable, Program Manager, Professional Services - West (airtable-8586865002)

## How are you using AI today in your current role? What specific tools are you using? If applicable, show us your last AI experiment.

At Microsoft, I use AI as an active part of my daily delivery workflow, not as a occasional shortcut. The most concrete example: I built an internal AI agent that handles status reporting, risk synthesis, and planning documentation across our resilience program. Before it existed, pulling together a weekly executive update meant manually aggregating outputs from multiple workstreams, cross-checking RAID logs, and reformatting for different stakeholder audiences. The agent automated that synthesis layer, which cut our planning cycle time by 39% and gave us enough operational headroom to expand drill capacity by 21% without adding headcount.

On the tools side, I work regularly with Copilot across Microsoft 365 for drafting, summarizing stakeholder threads, and accelerating documentation turnaround. I use LLM-powered semantic search tools for dependency tracing across large documentation sets, an approach I first built out during my 2023 internship when I migrated our team's docs to an AI-powered search platform and improved information retrieval speed by 83%. For the agent work specifically, I've used Copilot Studio to wire together automated workflows that connect planning inputs to reporting outputs.

My last experiment was testing whether I could use an LLM to surface early risk signals from a backlog of partner update notes across multiple workstreams, essentially asking it to flag patterns that a human reviewer scanning linearly would miss. The output wasn't production-ready on its own, but it surfaced two sequencing risks I hadn't flagged yet and gave me a starting structure I could validate and act on. That's roughly how I think about where AI creates leverage in delivery operations: not replacing judgment, but compressing the time between raw inputs and a decision-ready synthesis.
