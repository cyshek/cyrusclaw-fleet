**Job summary**

As a member of the Sales Engineering team at DigiCert, you will play a crucial role in ensuring technical presales success. This role involves developing and maintaining a technical understanding of DigiCert solutions, supporting demonstrations and evaluations for prospective customers, and assisting with responses to RFPs/RFIs. You will analyze customer needs to determine the fit for DigiCert solutions, capture feature requests, and provide feedback to Product Management. Collaboration with the sales team and cross-functional teams is essential, as is the ability to travel to customer sites and industry events as needed.

 

**What you will do**

- Partner with other members of the SE team to help ensure technical presales success

- Develop and maintain a technical understanding of DigiCert solutions

- Develop ability to provide or support demonstrations of DigiCert solutions and services to prospects/customers

- Facilitate or support technical evaluations by prospective customers in a Proof of Concept (POC) environment

- Assist other team members with responses to functional and technical elements of RFPs/RFIs

- Develop ability to analyze customer needs to determine potential fit for DigiCert solutions and services to meet customer objectives

- Capture feature request/enhancement input from prospects/customers to provide feedback to Product Management

- Work in conjunction with other members of the sales team and cross-functional teams

- Travel as necessary to customer sites and industry forums/conferences as needed to support the objectives of the department

 

**What you will have**

- 3+ years’ work experience in a cybersecurity or technical role

- Bachelor’s degree or higher (preferably in systems management, computer science or cybersecurity)

- Working knowledge of basic IT environments (Windows, Linux, Mac, web servers, etc.)

- Experience working with Cloud technologies and providers a plus (Azure, AWS, GCP, etc.)

- Familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite

- Basic communication (written & verbal) and interpersonal skills

- Basic diagnostic and problem-solving skills

- Fluent in writing and speaking English

- Must be detailed oriented, understand and process data accurately

- Willingness to accept responsibility and accountability with a customer-first mindset
