**About the Role:****
**

We are hiring a **Platform** **Product Manager** to own and evolve foundational platform capabilities, starting with Identity and Access Management, or IAM. This role sits at the center of how our enterprise AI platform scales: who can access what, how systems integrate, and how customers operate securely and reliably across complex environments.

This is a great role for someone who is technically fluent, highly proactive, and excited to take real ownership of an important product area. You do not need to be an IAM expert on day one, but you should be comfortable digging into technical systems, partnering closely with engineering, asking sharp questions, and making clear product decisions in ambiguous areas.

You’ll work closely with engineering, security, customer-facing teams, and other Product Managers to define, prioritize, and deliver capabilities that support both AI agents and human workflows. Over time, this role may expand into additional shared platform capabilities.

**What You’ll Do:**

- Own and drive foundational platform capabilities, starting with IAM, including authentication, authorization, user management, access control models, and auditability 

- Define the roadmap and backlog for your product area, balancing customer needs, enterprise readiness, and long-term platform direction

- Partner closely with engineering and security to make product decisions, evaluate tradeoffs, and drive execution

- Work with Solution Architects and implementation teams to ensure platform capabilities are practical to deploy, integrate, and operate in real customer environments

- Support integrations with enterprise systems used for access control and provisioning, such as HRIS, CCaaS, ITSM, and identity providers

- Bring structure to ambiguous problems, proactively drive decisions, and keep work moving across multiple parallel workstreams

- Help customer-facing teams understand how platform capabilities work, including configuration, rollout, and operational guidance

- Contribute to the evolution of shared platform capabilities beyond IAM as priorities emerge

**What We’re Looking For:**

- 3+ years of experience in product management or a technical / technical-adjacent role, such as software engineering, solutions architecture, technical program management, implementation, or similar

- Strong technical fluency: you’re comfortable discussing APIs, system behavior, architecture, technical tradeoffs, and implementation details with engineering teams

- Strong product judgment and the ability to make clear decisions in ambiguous, systems-level problem spaces

- High ownership, strong follow-through, and a bias toward action

- Ability to work effectively across engineering, security, customer-facing, and implementation teams

**Bonus Points:**

- Experience with identity, access, or permissions systems, such as IAM, RBAC, authentication flows, SSO, SCIM, or provisioning

- Prior hands-on engineering experience or experience working deeply with technical systems

- Experience with platform, infrastructure, developer-facing, enterprise SaaS, or other technically complex products

- Familiarity with enterprise security or audit concepts, such as least privilege, audit logs, access governance, or compliance workflows
