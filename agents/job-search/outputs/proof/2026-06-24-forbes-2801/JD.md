# Product Manager

**Company:** Forbes
**Location:** United States
**Apply:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/forbes/jobs/5826489004

Forbes is an iconic global media brand that has symbolized success for over a century. Fueled by journalism that informs and inspires, Forbes spotlights the doers and doings shaping industries, achieving success and making an impact on the world. Forbes connects and convenes the most influential communities ranging from billionaires, business leaders and rising entrepreneurs to creators and innovators. The Forbes brand reaches more than 140 million people monthly worldwide through its trusted journalism, signature ForbesLive events and 49 licensed local editions in 81 countries.

We are seeking a strategic and execution-focused Product Manager to lead the continued evolution of the Forbes Intelligence platform, which powers our global rankings and list franchises. This is a remote role.

In this position, you will expand and enhance a core platform that supports Forbes’ most iconic Lists, enabling new verticals, geographies, and revenue opportunities. You will work cross-functionally with Sales, Editorial, Data and Engineering teams to ensure the platform is structured, scalable, and positioned to support growing commercial and audience demand. The ideal candidate is energized by improving and scaling existing systems, bringing structure to complex data workflows, and unlocking monetization opportunities through thoughtful product strategy. You thrive in cross-functional environments, balance long-term platform vision with near-term execution, and are motivated by building products that drive measurable business impact. This role reports to the Director of Product: Acquisition, Conversion & Engagement.

**Responsibilities**

- Own and prioritize the roadmap for expanding the Forbes Intelligence platform to support new rankings, verticals, and geographies

- Identify and deliver platform enhancements that unlock monetization opportunities across subscription, enterprise, licensing, and data products

- Ensure rankings and list data are structured and maintained to support downstream commercial use cases

- Partner with Sales teams to translate platform capabilities into scalable product offerings, pricing models, and packaging strategies

- Improve workflows that move rankings from research to structured data to product, reducing manual effort and increasing speed to market

- Standardize methodologies and data structures to support scalability and repeatability across franchises

- Collaborate closely with Engineering and Data teams to define requirements and deliver scalable platform improvements

- Establish and track KPIs tied to platform adoption, operational efficiency, and revenue contribution

- Monitor platform performance, gather stakeholder feedback, and iterate on capabilities

- Evaluate competitive intelligence products and evolving market demand to inform platform strategy

**The Ideal Candidate**

- 2–5 years of Product Management experience, preferably with digital, data, or platform-based products

- Experience enhancing or scaling existing systems rather than building solely from scratch

- Familiarity with products incorporating AI-driven features, including generative AI, classification, or data automation

- Demonstrated experience supporting revenue-generating features or products

- Strong analytical skills and comfort working with structured data

- Experience partnering closely with Engineering and cross-functional stakeholders

- Interest in media, rankings, research, or intelligence-driven products

- Ability to manage projects independently within clear goals and strategic direction
