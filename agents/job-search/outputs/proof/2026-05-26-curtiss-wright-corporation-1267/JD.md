**Your Challenge:**

- Review customer requests for proposal, specifications, data sheets, and provide technical solutions

- Provide cost and lead time estimates for quoted products and larger scale projects

- Assist with writing technical business proposals

- Maintain historical database records of installed equipment

- Travel to visit customers and support sales efforts in person

**Your Expertise:**

- Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering or other related technical discipline

- 3 – 7 years of related experience with mechanical equipment (application or sales engineering), nuclear experience a plus

- Experience using data management tools (Power BI or other) a plus

- Excellent oral and written communication skills with good knowledge of MS Office

- Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks to meet strict deadlines

- Creative problem solving and analytical skills
