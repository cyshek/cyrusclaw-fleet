TeamDynamix is seeking a strategic, analytical, and customer-focused **Product Manager** to join our remote Product team. In this role, you will drive the delivery and adoption of high-value product capabilities by transforming customer insights, market research, and business objectives into a clear, actionable product roadmap. You will work closely with customers, engineering, and cross-functional partners to define requirements, prioritize enhancements, and guide product releases. This position is ideal for a product professional who thrives at the intersection of strategy, data, and execution.

**About the Role**

- Own and curate product roadmaps that align with customer needs, market opportunities, and business priorities.

- Gather and analyze product feedback and enhancement requests through direct review, documentation, focus groups, and research.

- Partner with the product management team to build, validate, and continuously refine the product roadmap.

- Collaborate with engineering to identify, scope, design, estimate, and deliver product features.

- Document and analyze enhancement requests and trends to inform product direction.

- Deliver clear and effective functional product documentation.

- Plan, coordinate, and manage product version releases.

- Communicate product plans, updates, and value effectively to customers and internal stakeholders.

- Assist marketing in communicating product benefits and supporting launch activities.

- Participate in root cause analysis to recommend product improvements and enhance product fitness.

- Contribute to business process management initiatives by collecting metrics and improving processes.

- Conduct research, planning, and integration activities related to mergers and acquisitions.

- Act as a project manager on select initiatives as needed.

- Assist in developing product pricing strategies as appropriate.

- Communicate with colleagues, management, clients, and partners in a professional and courteous manner.

- Comply with all company regulations, policies, and procedures.

**About You**

- Strategic and analytical: You enjoy evaluating customer feedback, usage data, and market trends to inform product decisions.

- Customer-centered: You are passionate about delivering meaningful value and driving product adoption.

- Collaborative and influential: You build alignment across teams and effectively navigate trade-offs.

- Technically fluent: You understand software development concepts, integrations, and APIs.

- Organized and execution-focused: You balance long-term strategy with day-to-day delivery.

- Clear communicator: You translate complex ideas into concise, actionable documentation and discussions.

**Your Qualifications**

- Bachelor’s degree required.

- 3–5+ years of experience in product management, business analysis, product marketing, professional services, systems development, or IT operations.

- Product management and data-driven or AI-driven feature analysis

- Building integrations with external software systems

- Integrating multiple products into a unified platform

- Adding internationalization or translation capabilities

- Working with user management, authentication, and authorization

- Competitive research and analysis

**Additional preferred, but not required, experience:**

- Experience in IT Operations domains such as ITSM, PPM, Asset Management, or similar

- Administering or implementing the TeamDynamix Work Management platform
