## About the role

You’ll own the full customer arc, from running the technical sale to leading implementation to driving long-term adoption. Pre-sales, you’re in the room: demoing the platform, building POCs, and turning a prospect’s messy ticket queue into a clear automation roadmap. Post-sales, you’re on the ground: shipping integrations, driving adoption, and making sure customers hit real ROI fast. You are the reason customers buy, go live, and stay.

You'll have room to be creative, work autonomously with customers, and shape both the platform and the product vision.

## What You'll Do

- Map a prospect's messiest workflows into a compelling automation roadmap and deliver demos that move deals forward

- Construct working POCs on the fly, fast enough to change the trajectory of a deal in a single meeting

- Speak fluently across IT, HR ops, and finance stakeholders, earning trust from both customers and executive buyers

- Architect AI-powered workflows and configure integrations across identity systems, HR platforms, and SaaS tools, owning the implementation through to a fast and scalable go-live

- Expand automation coverage post-launch, track ROI, and become the technical advisor customer leadership calls first for strategy, not just support

- Bring field learnings back to the product team: edge cases, deployment patterns, and unmet needs that make the platform better for every account
