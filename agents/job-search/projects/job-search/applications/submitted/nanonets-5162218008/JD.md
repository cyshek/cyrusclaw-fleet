**The Role**

We're looking for a technically strong, customer-focused Forward Deployed Engineer to help enterprise customers build and scale Agentic AI — from first conversation to production.

**What You'll Do**

- Be the technical partner for customers, driving deployments from ideation to production.

- Translate customer problems into clear requirements (PRDs, workflows, success criteria) and build them.

- Collaborate with Product, Engineering, and Solutions teams to scope, prioritize, and deliver on time.

- Maintain structured project documentation — plans, trackers, and status updates.

- Own end-to-end testing and validation before every customer release.

**What We're Looking For**

- 1+ years as a Forward Deployed Engineer, software engineer, ML engineer or equivalent — startup experience preferred; founder or founding engineer background is a plus.

- Proficiency in at least one language: Python, Go, JavaScript, or similar.

- Ability to proactively surface customer challenges during onboarding.

- End-to-end ownership mindset — you figure out what you don't know and get it done.

- A humble, collaborative attitude with a high-output, multi-project operating style.
