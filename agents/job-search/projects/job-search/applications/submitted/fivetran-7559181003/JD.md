We’re proud that more organizations continue to leverage our technology every day to become truly data-driven.

**About the Role**

The Product Manager, Technical owns the ongoing health and management of a portfolio of SaaS data connectors in partnership with Fivetran’s product and engineering teams. You will partner with customers & other product managers to understand customer requirements, set priorities, ensure that requirements are translated into workable epics and stories. You are also the go-to person for engineering and other stakeholders when they have questions on requirements, deliverables, and incidents.

This is a full-time position based out of our Oakland office. Our hybrid work model offers a blend of remote flexibility and in-person collaboration, including two days in the office each week to connect and build as a team. The successful candidate must be able to work with overlap with US EST and PST time zones.

**What You’ll Do**

- Monitor the health and performance of the connector portfolio

- Work closely with the larger product team to plan and prioritize feature development

- Manage product backlog, develop feature requirements and set acceptance criteria

- Partner with different stakeholders on internal management communications and business reviews

- Work closely with engineering to execute feature improvements

- Participate in scrum ceremonies

- Partner with engineering to solve incidents

- Work with customers for research, connector onboarding and triaging of incidents

- Drives for clarity and solutions when faced with ambiguity or challenges

- Understand both the technical details and desired business outcomes to drive for results

You will play a key role in the growing and adoption of Fivetran's SaaS Connector Portfolio

**Skills We’re Looking For**

- Strong SQL skills

- Previous experience working as a product manager, product owner, technical program manager, or data analyst role

- Experience working with engineering or technical delivery teams

- Experience working in an Agile environment

- Strong written and verbal communication skills

- Organized and great attention to detail

- Adaptable, multitasker and problem solver

- Great interpersonal skills and ability to collaborate and build relationships

- Embody team values of Curiosity, Ownership, Collaboration, Kindness, and Integrity

- A deep sense of empathy for the customer, ensuring that all solutions are customer-focused and deliver significant value.
