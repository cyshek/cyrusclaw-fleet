### About the Role

A fast-growing, seed-funded B2B SaaS company in the AI-powered sales productivity space is looking for a **Founding Product Manager** to help shape the future of the product. You will partner directly with the CTPO to co-own product vision and strategy across Web, Desktop, and Mobile — blending strategic thinking with hands-on execution to build a scalable, high-quality product organization. This is a rare opportunity to define how AI-first and agentic experiences are built from the ground up.

### What You'll Do

- Co-own the product vision and strategy together with the CTPO.

- Lead end-to-end product discovery and delivery, from ideation through iteration.

- Design and build AI-first and agentic product experiences.

- Drive cross-platform product coherence across Web, Desktop, and Mobile.

- Establish scalable product processes and uplift the broader product organization.

- Align cross-functional teams — engineering, design, and leadership — through strong stakeholder management.

### What We're Looking For

- **3+ years** of experience as a Product Manager, with a focus on engineering-heavy, technically complex products.

- Proven track record delivering AI-powered features and managing the AI product lifecycle.

- Experience as a Founding PM or Lead PM — you know how to build from early-stage.

- End-to-end ownership mindset: from discovery through delivery and continuous iteration.

- Strong data-driven decision making, with the ability to define and track meaningful KPIs and success metrics.

- Strong technical fundamentals — either a technical education background or prior experience as an engineer.

- Ability to align cross-functional stakeholders including engineers, designers, and executives.

- **Fluency in German is required.**

### Nice to Have

- Experience with CRM integrations, sales productivity tools, or enterprise SaaS platforms.

- Background working in a fast-paced, early-stage startup environment.

- Familiarity with GDPR compliance considerations in product design.

### Location & Work Arrangement

This role is based **on-site in Munich, Germany**. Visa sponsorship is available for qualified candidates.
