## Why do you want to work at Profound?

At Microsoft I owned an agent platform end to end that proactively monitors system health, surfaces critical defects, and turns insights into prioritized actions without engineers having to dig through data manually. I architected the UX and orchestration for those background agents and shipped Azure's first proactive resilience capability in 4 months at a 94% recovery rate, cutting planning cycle time 39% and reducing operational toil 30% with LLM-powered tooling, prompt engineering, and RAG. That is almost exactly the problem Profound is solving, just pointed at marketing instead of infrastructure: background agents that watch performance, surface opportunities, and deliver insights automatically.

Profound is sitting at the most interesting shift in marketing in 25 years, and you are not just measuring it, you are building the agents that act on it. The combination of frontier agent systems with real workflows that customers like Ramp, Figma, and Chime actually rely on is the kind of zero-to-one product surface I want to own. I like that PMs here are true product owners doing discovery, execution, and selling, and that the bar is velocity plus quality. I have spent the last two years turning a manual, toil-heavy process into a platform people love, and I want to do that again at the frontier of AI and marketing.
