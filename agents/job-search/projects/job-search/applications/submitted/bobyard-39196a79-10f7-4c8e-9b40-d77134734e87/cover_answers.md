Bobyard's Forward Deployed Engineer role is that same loop, applied to construction, and that's a direct fit.

What draws me to Bobyard specifically is the bet you're making on general contractors. Construction is a massive, underserved market where most software is either too generic or too clunky to stick, and the companies that win will be the ones that actually embed with crews and build around real workflows rather than retrofitting enterprise tooling. The fact that field work at Bobyard directly informs the product roadmap, and that solutions I ship can become platform features, is exactly the kind of leverage I want. I'd rather own outcomes in a market that's still being defined than optimize at the margins of one that's already crowded.
