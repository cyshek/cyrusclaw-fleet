Apple's Health team is seeking a high-energy Site Reliability Engineer. As a member of our dynamic group, you’ll have the rare and rewarding opportunity to craft upcoming products that will delight and inspire millions of Apple’s customers every day.

## Responsibilities

Comfortable with weekly on-call rotation
Design, build, and maintain scalable cloud infrastructure on AWS and/or GCP to support Apple Health software services
Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines to enable fast, reliable, and repeatable software delivery
Write automation tools and scripts in Python and Bash to reduce manual toil and improve operational efficiency
Monitor system health and performance using observability tools such as Grafana, Prometheus, Splunk, and Loki, and drive resolution of incidents
Manage and optimize containerized workloads using Docker and Kubernetes in large-scale production environments
Administer and support distributed data systems including NoSQL databases, Zookeeper, Spark, and Mesos
Collaborate cross-functionally with software engineers, security teams, and product teams to define and implement infrastructure best practices
Own and improve build and release systems, ensuring high availability, reliability, and performance at scale
Conduct root cause analysis on production incidents and implement preventative measures to reduce recurrence
Participate in capacity planning, performance tuning, and cost optimization across cloud environments
Maintain source control workflows and repository hygiene using Git or SVN
Document systems, runbooks, and operational procedures to support team knowledge sharing and continuity
