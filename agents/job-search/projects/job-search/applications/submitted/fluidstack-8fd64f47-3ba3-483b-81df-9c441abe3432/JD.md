Powerful AI will be the biggest lever for human choice we've ever built - but only if models are aligned with what humanity actually wants. There are groups building AI who don't share these goals. # **Role Scope**

- Own the product roadmap and day-to-day management of Fluidstack's logistics systems: WMS, IMS, material request ticketing, and dock management tooling.

- Translate operational needs, edge cases, and process changes into clear requirements and user stories for the development team.

- Ensure all logistics systems are integrated and communicating within the Fluidstack framework, and stay ahead of operational changes to anticipate system updates before they become blockers.

- Define and track system performance metrics, identify gaps, and drive continuous improvement across the logistics tech stack.

- Serve as the primary liaison between logistics operations and the Systems PM and engineering team.
