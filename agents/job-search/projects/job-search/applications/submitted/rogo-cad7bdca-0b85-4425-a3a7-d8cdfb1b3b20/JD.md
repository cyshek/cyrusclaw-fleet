## **The Role**

Security and trust are foundational to Rogo. We already serve the most security-conscious institutions in the world, and we hold ourselves to their bar every day. We're looking for an exceptional product manager to own and advance the product that makes Rogo the most trusted way for these institutions to put AI to work. You'll work across every function at Rogo, and directly with our customers' security teams, to keep us at the leading edge of security, governance, and trust as AI adoption deepens across the industry.

## 
**Key Responsibilities **

- Own and advance Rogo's security, governance, and trust product as a first-class part of the platform

- Give administrators and security leaders clear visibility and control over how AI is used across their organization

- Partner with our customers' security teams, our internal security and engineering teams, and third-party auditors, and turn their requirements into product

- Make it simple for the most demanding institutions in the world to adopt AI with confidence and meet their governance and compliance obligations

- Prototype and build to figure out what is worth shipping. We prototype in design or code (everyone here builds with AI) far more than we write long specs, then partner closely with engineering and design to bring it to life and unblock the team continuously

- Think adversarially: anticipate edge cases, failure modes, and the standards a sophisticated security organization will hold us to

- Delight the user, even when that user isn't your core persona. Go beyond shipping security features. Make the hard stuff legible, fast, and satisfying to use. Turn security and governance into something users want to open, not just a compliance checkbox.
