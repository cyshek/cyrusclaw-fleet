# Cover answers — Gatherai, Solutions Engineer (gather-4610220007)

## What excites you about working with Gather AI? How do your skillset and interests align with our mission?

My work at Microsoft and Amazon Robotics has put me squarely at the intersection of automation, enterprise sales support, and operational scale. At Amazon Robotics I led a 2,000+ unit warehouse robotics pilot, mapping dependencies across 1,200+ stations to achieve zero operational downtime during a complex OS migration. At Microsoft I built ROI-based business cases and technical objection-handling frameworks for enterprise clients like Walmart, Databricks, and SAP, translating complex automation platform capabilities into language that resonated with both technical and operational decision-makers. That combination, understanding the warehouse and logistics environment hands-on while also knowing how to move enterprise deals forward, is exactly what a Solutions Engineer role at Gather AI demands.

What draws me to Gather AI specifically is the software-only, vision-powered approach to warehouse intelligence. Most automation plays require massive capital investment in hardware infrastructure, which creates long sales cycles and adoption friction. Gather AI's model sidesteps that by working with existing equipment, and that makes the ROI conversation with customers much cleaner and more compelling. I find that genuinely interesting from a pre-sales strategy standpoint. The supply chain and logistics space is also at an inflection point where real-time inventory visibility is moving from a nice-to-have to a competitive necessity, and being part of a team that is defining what that looks like at scale is the kind of work I want to be doing.
