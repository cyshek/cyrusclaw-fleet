About The Team  
This role is within our Security organization, which supports Cloud Security, Product Security, Enterprise Security, and Detection & Response. This organization partners with Product, Engineering, Privacy, GRC, IT, and Legal teams to ensure security best practices are applied to protect the company and Outreach customers.
 
The Role  
Are you passionate about contributing to the success of a cybersecurity program through program management? We are seeking an experienced Technical Program Manager to join our Security  
Team. In this role you will drive security programs and execute security projects that will assist executing a strategy to improve security posture. You will provide operational support for cross-functional teams, such as engineering, product, privacy, and legal. You will translate complex technical, regulatory, and security requirements into structured programs, clear technical requirements, and measurable outcomes. 
Location: We are open to remote.  
  
Your Daily Adventures Will Include:   
Drive security vulnerability remediations with Engineering for CSPM, OS Runtime, SAST, SCA, DAST
Apply your technical understanding of Cybersecurity in a hand-on role, driving security programs and providing TPM support
