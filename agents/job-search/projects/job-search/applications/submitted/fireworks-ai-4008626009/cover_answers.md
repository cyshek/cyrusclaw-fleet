# Cover answers — Fireworksai, Forward Deployed Product Manager (fireworks-ai-4008626009)

## Why are you interested in Fireworks AI?

My work at Microsoft has been centered on exactly the problems Fireworks is solving. I built an LLM-powered planning tool that cut planning cycle time by 39%, shaped GenAI product roadmaps from structured customer interviews, and helped enterprise customers like Databricks and Walmart align their infrastructure requirements to Azure platform capabilities. The forward-deployed model, where you sit close to customers, translate their technical needs into product, and then drive that back into the roadmap, is how I've operated day to day. That combination of customer advisory work and internal product definition is where I do my best work.

What draws me to Fireworks specifically is the technical bet you're making. Inference speed and scalability are the unsexy but genuinely hard problems that determine whether enterprise AI deployments actually work in production, and Fireworks has been independently benchmarked as the leader there. I want to work on infrastructure that developers and companies are building on top of, not around. The founding team's background in PyTorch and Vertex AI, and the focus on function calling and multimodal models, tells me this is a team that earns technical credibility rather than just claiming it. That's the kind of environment I want to grow in.

I can start two weeks from the date of a formal offer. That gives me enough time to wrap up any active commitments at Microsoft cleanly and transition without leaving things in a bad spot for my team.
