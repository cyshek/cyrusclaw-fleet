## Your Impact

We are looking for a deep technical authority to join us as a Cisco Splunk Solutions Engineer(SE). This isn't just about knowing a product but being the master of a domain. It's about being the definitive authority who our customers and internal teams turn to for solving the most sophisticated challenges.

As an SE at Cisco, you'll be the authority for your architecture. Act as the driving force behind our most technical sales, demonstrating undeniable expertise and winning the trust of our customers through your deep knowledge and outstanding skills. Your mastery will be the key that unlocks our customers' success.

- Be the Technical Authority: Own and win the technical decision. Lead breakdown sessions, competitive bake-offs, and sophisticated proofs-of-value that prove Cisco's superiority.

- Master and Promote: Serve as the go-to authority; articulating its vision, strategy, and technical details to audiences from engineers to executives.

- Design for Complexity: Architect and validate trailblazing, single-architecture solutions for our customers' most high-reaching requirements, ensuring technical excellence and adaptability.

- Win as a Team: Partner with Cisco ecosystem and Account Managers to provide the critical domain expertise needed to win strategic opportunities. Be the authoritative voice on the account team.

- Mentor and Scale Expertise: Act as a force multiplier, mentoring and enabling the broader SE community on your technology. Develop and share protocols that elevate the entire organization.

- Influence Product Direction: Serve as a critical feedback loop to Cisco's engineering and business units. Your real-world insights will directly influence the roadmap and future of our products.

## Minimum Qualifications

5+ years of demonstrated ability in a pre-sales engineering or a similar deep technical role (e.g., professional services, security analyst, SRE)

5+ years of broad and deep technical knowledge across one of the following areas: Security, observability, event, or network management

## Preferred Qualifications

- Demonstrable experience building and maintaining positive relationships with customers and partners.

- 5+ years of communication and presentation skills with internal partners or external customers

- Observability or security pre-sales experience

- Extensive experience with completing RFXs, competitive analysis, positioning, and first-hand competitive bake-offs.

- Relevant industry certifications relevant to your specialty (e.g., CISSP)

- Strong understanding of automation, APIs, and programmability within your domain of expertise.

- Experience in a thought leadership role (e.g., public speaking, writing technical blogs or whitepapers)
