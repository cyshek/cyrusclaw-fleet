Anrok is the leading AI-driven tax automation platform enabling businesses to expand globally without compliance complexity.

As the digital economy has grown 6x over the last decade, software businesses have gone from not worrying about sales tax to needing to monitor exposure, calculate rates, and file returns across 20+ US states and many countries worldwide. This creates a critical bottleneck for companies that should be able to transact with customers everywhere.

Anrok eliminates this complexity by connecting with billing and payment systems to automate tax monitoring, calculations, and filing end-to-end. Our unified platform handles the ever-changing maze of tax laws at municipal, state, and federal levels—so companies can focus on growth, not compliance.

Our customers include:

- 40% of Forbes Top 50 AI companies

- 20% of Forbes Top 100 Cloud companies

- Top companies like Notion, Anthropic, and Cursor

We're making compliant digital commerce a reality for companies big and small, backed by over $50M from leading investors including Sequoia, Index, and Khosla Ventures.

We're seeking a **Post-Sales Solutions Engineer** to help drive expansion within our existing customer base. You'll transform technical expertise into measurable revenue outcomes, working at the intersection of sales, product, and engineering to unlock new opportunities and ensure long-term partnerships.

You'll become the trusted technical advisor customers rely on, mastering our platform capabilities and developing deep expertise in sales tax compliance and enterprise integrations. By bridging customer business objectives with technical solutions, you'll establish Anrok as the strategic partner customers rely on as they scale their business.

## 
**In this role, you will**

- **Own technical discovery for expansion** — conduct ongoing discovery within existing accounts to uncover new use cases, geographic expansion, and integration opportunities. Create technical roadmaps that map customer objectives to Anrok capabilities.

- **Partner on account strategy** — work closely with Account Managers on expansion prioritization, account planning, and QBRs. Provide technical qualification and strategic insights that inform forecasting.

- **Lead complex implementations** — drive expansion implementations including multi-entity configurations and advanced feature deployments. Create parallel workstreams to accelerate time-to-value and monitor implementation health proactively.

- **Run live technical consultations** — lead troubleshooting calls and configuration sessions with confidence. Demonstrate composure during high-pressure escalations and adapt technical depth based on your audience in real-time.

- **Be the Voice of the Customer** — channel technical feedback, integration requirements, and product gaps to Product and Engineering to influence roadmap priorities.

- **Manage technical escalations** — own the Technical Solutions queue for complex configurations and troubleshooting. Maintain SLAs for response and resolution, document issues comprehensively, and escalate to Engineering with complete context.

- **Build the playbook** — create implementation guides, expansion frameworks, and reusable assets. Mentor new team members and deliver internal training as the SE function scales.
