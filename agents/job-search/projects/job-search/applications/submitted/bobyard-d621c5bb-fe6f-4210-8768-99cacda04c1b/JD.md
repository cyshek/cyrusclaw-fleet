**Company Overview**

Construction is a $13T industry that powers civilization. It’s also running on PDFs, rulers, and 1990s software.

The biggest choke point? Cost estimates. Takeoffs are manual, brutal, and slow — estimators spend days counting materials from drawings by hand. One mistake blows millions.

**Bobyard is automating construction takeoff with AI.**

We use CV + NLP to read drawings like an expert estimator, but 10x faster with less mistakes. We’re turning the most painful part of pre-construction into software.

We’re a lean, elite team backed by the best VCs on earth: **8VC, Primary, and Pear**. We’re building next-gen software to drag the built world into the future.

**Position Overview**

Great products win markets. As we scale our core and launch new 0 → 1 bets, we need a product leader who operates like a founder. You’ll own the product roadmap end-to-end. You will:

- **Live in customer workflows**: Get in the field with estimators, PMs, and GCs. Understand the job better than they can articulate it.

- **Shape vision & roadmap**: Decide what we build, what we kill, and how we win. Turn market chaos into a product that feels inevitable.

- **Create, iterate, and validate new products**: Own 0 → 1. From napkin to revenue. Test, ship, learn, repeat.

- **Execute with engineering and UI/UX**: No handoffs. You’re in the details — specs, Figma, code reviews, user calls.

- **Communicate vision with marketing & sales**: Make the market believe. Arm GTM to sell what we build and shape the category.

**Desired Attributes**

- **Founder-level drive**: You roll up your sleeves. Ambiguity and chaos are where you thrive. You ship.

- **Learning speed & resourcefulness**: No construction background required. You’ll be dangerous in 30 days. You hunt down answers.

- **Passion and work ethic**: Startups are war. This isn’t a cushy PM job at Google. You want to build something legendary.

- **Organized operator**: You’ll get 1000 requests from 100s of customers. You turn noise into signal and signal into product.
