**Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to enrich life for all. **

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn, communicate and advance faster than ever.

As a Product Manager at Micron Technology, Inc. in the Cloud Memory Business Unit (CMBU), you will be charged with defining and executing the strategy for a High Performance Memory product portfolio that will further fortify Micron’s leadership position in the Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML) and High Performance Computing (HPC) business segments. You will be working on leading edge technology driving highest performance memory product serving and enabling customer focused innovation.

You will be responsible for expanding Micron’s presence in the HBM market by actively pursuing and managing customer engagements, ensuring industry inputs are included in defining the strategy and vision of the product portfolio. 

Successful candidates for this role are ambitious and influential leaders who possess an entrepreneurial spirit and the creativity to resolve a wide array of complex challenges. Ideal prospects will have engineering background and analytical skills with a strong sense of ownership on execution and possess good communication skills to drive customer engagements and cross-functional teams towards business goals. Products managers in this role will be self-starters with a can-do attitude who can apply their critical thinking ability to drive the best possible outcome.

**Your Responsibilities include, but not limited to:**

- Maintain an excellent understanding of market requirements and technology trends to enable the development of the product portfolio and competitive analysis reports
- You will represent the Product Management team in all Product Development Team meetings ensuring that customer needs are appropriately represented, and product spec trade-off discussions are translated to business impact
- You will drive Product strategy and roadmap alignment meetings that report the status, highlight key risks to achieving the business objectives
- You will help with developing Stage Gate material for the products and present them as needed for the high-performance memory products
- Development and continued optimization of the business case for each product in your portfolio, including unit cost, capex, revenue, R&D, opportunity cost and return on investment analysis
- Help define and support operational excellence initiatives within the team.
- Support the Portfolio Manager as needed embracing and handling tasks or responsibilities thrown their way while furthering their knowledge and facilitating key aspects of product management
- Participate in the product management community by contributing to corporate process improvement and act as secondary/back-up product manager for assigned products
** Minimum requirements:**

- Bachelors, MBA or Master’s degree in engineering (Preferred)
- Highly motivated person with strong communication and presentation skills with the ability to work with customers as a Micron representative
- Proven experience in being highly structured and organized.
- Demonstrated ability to complete complex technical projects.
- Ability to travel both domestically and internationally
