The achievement I'm most proud of is scaling Azure's recovery validation program from a two-person operation into a fully platformized system. I also served as bridge lead for a sovereign-cloud network isolation test tied to a $1.5B+ enterprise contract, which required managing risk and stakeholder alignment end to end under significant executive visibility. That combination of building the right structures, driving decisions to closure, and keeping nothing from falling through the cracks is exactly what this TPM role at Baseten is describing, and it is the work I find most satisfying.
