## **About PointOne**

PointOne is reinventing how law firms operate. Our AI‑powered timekeeper helps attorneys capture billable time automatically and provides rich insights that transform how legal work is managed. We’re a venture‑backed startup (Y Combinator, Bessemer, 8VC, General Catalyst) made up of software engineers (Jane Street, Google, Stanford, Princeton) and ex-attorneys. Following an excellent seed round and rapid customer adoption, we are continuing to quickly scale our engineering team before we raise our Series A.

## **Who you are**

You have:

- 2-8 years of experience at hyper-growth startups

- A CS degree from a top university

- Strong product sense

- High degree of ownership and the ability to operate autonomously

You're excited to work in-person, collaborate directly with customers, and build products that professionals rely on every day.

## **Why you should join**

**Growth:** As a early member of the team, you will shape your own role and directly inform the fundamental infrastructure behind our product.

**Impact:** A lot of AI tools have trouble showing real impact. Not us—we deliver hard ROI to our customers (which is one reason why we're one of the stickiest tools in a law firm). We’re also a multi-product company that expects our engineers to drive technical and product decisions.

**Equity:** We want to bet on ourselves and our employees—we offer a highly competitive equity component with any compensation package. We are looking for true builders and innovators.

## **What you’ll do**

- Drive the success of strategic customer accounts, directly partnering with and executing alongside law firm leadership.

- Develop novel AI products, providing customers with entirely new workflows.

- Regularly connect with and visit customers to understand needs, prioritize requests, and translate them into technical solutions.

- Prototype and integrate AI pipelines (e.g., retrieval‑augmented generation) to prove out new workflows.

- Lead by example through mentoring future hires, pairing, and code reviews. You'll shape our engineering culture.

We have a fully serverless backend built on top of AWS, consisting of a collection of Go microservices. We use React/Typescript to build client applications across web, desktop, and mobile. We create our own RAG pipelines.
