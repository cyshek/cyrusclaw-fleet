## Responsibilities

Work in a cross-functional team with engineering, operations, finance, and marketing communications through the complete product development cycle from product concept to end of life
Be the authority on the competitive landscape
Evaluate new technologies and make recommendations on which ones to pursue in future products
Define product positioning and key product messages
Evaluate user feedback and integrate key insights back into future product development

Launch products worldwide in conjunction with marketing communications, public relations and events teams

## Minimum Qualifications

7+ years experience in product marketing and/or product management roles, including at least one entire product development cycle from concept to launch.
Hands-on experience formulating product messaging and creating outbound marketing for both technical and consumer audiences, and partnering with engineering during early research and development to define product strategy and roadmap, and with marketing and public relations to craft positioning, and define marketing activities.
Recent experience within the last 3 years in one or more of the following product segments: consumer electronics, tablet/phone industry, consumer hardware experience, experience working with application developers to design custom solutions (e.g. productivity, creativity, communication, gaming, or lifestyle apps), or adjacent.
Ability to drive communication with key stakeholders, the media, and public, as demonstrated by exceptional presentation skills: developing keynote addresses in an area of deep subject matter expertise, synthesizing information, applying critical thinking and maintaining composure under pressure.
