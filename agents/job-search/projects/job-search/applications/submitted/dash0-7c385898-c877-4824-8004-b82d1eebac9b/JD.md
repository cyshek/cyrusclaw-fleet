### **About Dash0**

Join Dash0 and help us define the future of observability. We are OpenTelemetry-native, building a delightful, simple, and AI-centric platform that eliminates vendor lock-in and meaningless toil. Shape a product that developers love—all with transparent pricing and cost-control built in.

**The Opportunity**

Dash0 is looking for a Commercial Solutions Architect to act as a trusted technical advisor to our customers and prospects. You'll bridge the gap between product and customer outcomes — designing, demonstrating, and validating Dash0's technical capabilities in real-world environments.

You'll partner with Sales and Product teams to deliver high-impact Proofs of Concept, guide observability architecture discussions, and ensure customers realize the full technical value of Dash0. This is a role where deep technical expertise meets direct customer impact — you'll be the person prospects remember when they decide to choose Dash0 over Datadog or New Relic.

**What You'll Do**

- Design and architect observability solutions leveraging OpenTelemetry, Kubernetes, and cloud-native technologies

- Translate complex customer requirements into scalable, performant Dash0 implementations

- Develop and execute Proofs of Concept that highlight Dash0's differentiated technical capabilities

- Deliver engaging technical demos and presentations tailored to engineering and executive audiences

- Partner with Account Executives throughout the sales cycle to position Dash0 as the technical solution of choice

- Provide architecture guidance on observability setup, telemetry data modeling, and OpenTelemetry collector design

- Create and maintain reusable demo environments, reference architectures, and POC templates

- Contribute to technical content — blog posts, conference talks, and documentation that drive community engagement

**What You Bring**

- Deep technical knowledge in Kubernetes, OpenTelemetry, observability pipelines, and distributed tracing

- Hands-on experience with instrumentation, telemetry data ingestion, and performance optimization

- Strong understanding of modern DevOps, SRE, and platform engineering practices

- Proven ability to create and execute technical demos, architectures, and POCs that prove business value

- Strong technical communication skills — you can explain complex observability concepts clearly to engineers and decision-makers alike

- Collaborative mindset — you work seamlessly across Sales, Product, and Engineering teams to drive results

- Problem-solving instinct — you thrive on diagnosing and resolving technical challenges with creativity and precision

**Nice to Have**

- Experience working in a pre-sales or Sales Engineering capacity at a B2B SaaS company

- Active contributions to OpenTelemetry or the broader observability ecosystem

- Familiarity with CRM tools like Salesforce or HubSpot

- Experience in a high-growth, venture-backed startup environment
