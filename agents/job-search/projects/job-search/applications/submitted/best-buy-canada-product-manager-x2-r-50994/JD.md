**Product Manager (2 Openings: Reverse Logistics OR Mobile Activation)**

Are you excited by the challenge of modernizing reverse logistics OR the challenge of evolving how we sell Mobile Phones across Canada?

Best Buy Canada has two roles open, one **focused on Reverse Logistics and the other in wireless tech**.

**In the reverse logistics role**, you will lead cross-functional product teams to support the transformation of our reverse logistics, enabling operational efficiency and improved customer experiences across our ecosystem.

**In wireless tech role**, you will work closely with engineering, operations, and business stakeholders to prioritize and deliver experiences that improve the mobile phone activation experience for customers and front-line employees.

We practice a remote-first working model, with opportunities to collaborate at our head office in beautiful Vancouver, BC for strategic, collaborative, and social purposes.

**Join us in supporting the future forward transformation of our organization as a product manager!**

**Role A: reverse logistics - what you'll do:**

- Deliver on the product vision and roadmap for Reverse Logistics.
- Lead an agile, cross-functional delivery team to build scalable and reliable platforms that support reverse logistics and returns operations end-to-end.
- Serve as the functional domain expert in reverse logistics, connecting technical implementation with business context and operational goals.
- Collaborate with internal partners and external vendors to engage, influence, and build strong working relationships.
- Contribute to the broader reverse-logistics modernization journey, unlocking value from new platforms and capabilities and supporting new initiatives.

**What we’re looking for:**

- 4+ years of experience as a product manager delivering software products in complex operational domains (retail operations, logistics, supply chain, or post-purchase experiences).
- Hands-on familiarity with reverse logistics concepts (returns, repairs, return-to-vendor, write-offs/disposition, shipping) and how these connect across systems and operations.
- Experience leading cross-functional agile teams and delivering iterative value through well-shaped roadmaps, backlogs, and clear outcome measures.
- Strong ability to partner with technology, business, and operations teams, translate needs into product requirements, and make effective trade-offs across scope, timeline, and value.

**Role B: wireless tech**

**what you'll do:**

- Deliver on the product roadmap for the Mobile Phone Activations product.
- Partner with engineering and delivery teams in an agile environment to deliver scalable and reliable experiences that support Mobile Phone Activations end-to-end.
- Partner closely with operational stakeholders to understand pain points and translate business needs into well-defined product backlog items and actionable outcomes for delivery teams.
- Serve as the functional domain expert in Mobile Phone Activations, connecting technical implementation with business context and operational goals.
- Contribute to the end-to-end mobile activation evolution, unlocking value from integrated carrier, financing, and fulfillment capabilities while enabling new customer and associate-led activation experiences.

**What we’re looking for: **

- 4+ years of experience as a product manager delivering software products in complex, customer-facing ecosystems, ideally within telecom, retail, or transactional platforms.
- Experience with telecom activation workflows, including plan selection, provisioning, and real-time transaction processing across multiple carrier partners.
- Hands-on experience designing customer and front-line associate experiences, balancing self-serve capabilities with assisted selling in high-volume environments.
- Experience working with systems that handle sensitive customer data (PII), with a strong understanding of privacy, security, and regulatory compliance requirements.
- Strong operational mindset, with familiarity in piloting & iterations for front-line associates.
