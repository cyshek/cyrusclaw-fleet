# Who We Are

Notion is the collaborative AI workspace where teams and agents [think together](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vkpYpWfEK5s). We're building one place where your knowledge, projects, meetings, and AI tools live side by side, so work is faster, clearer, and less fragmented. Millions of individuals, small teams, and large companies run their work on Notion.

Notinos (our employees) are customer zero in bringing this future of work to life. We care about craft, building things that last, and the belief that great work is still fundamentally human. Our goal isn’t to ship the next feature. Each and every team of Notinos is working to set the standard for how humans work together in the AI era. From building a business’s system of record to making and managing AI agents to automating away the busy work, we care deeply about giving our customers more time for their life’s work.

# About the Role

Notion’s sales motion is evolving from product‑led to sales‑led. Many customers start self‑serve, then partner with our team as they scale. As a Solutions Engineer focused on our Enterprise segment, you’ll work with our largest prospects and customers to drive complex evaluations, design scalable architectures, and guide executive stakeholders through the journey from pilot to global rollout.

You’ll partner closely with Account Executives and cross-functional teams to run deep discovery, deliver tailored demos and POCs, and advise on security, governance, and integrations that meet enterprise standards. You’ll also help shape our internal tooling, content, and processes while staying close to customers.

# What You'll Achieve

- Partner with AEs on strategic opportunities in the Enterprise segment to understand goals, risks, and success criteria across business and technical stakeholders

- Lead discovery and design solution approaches in Notion that span knowledge management, projects, and databases, including data modeling and information architecture at scale

- Deliver persuasive executive‑level presentations & demos, and facilitate hands‑on pilots/POCs that prove value, adoption, and measurable ROI

- Guide enterprise security, privacy, and procurement processes including RFPs, DPA reviews, and risk assessments in partnership with Security and Legal

- Build proof‑of‑concepts and example workspaces that showcase how Notion consolidates point tools and enables cross‑functional workflows

- Capture and communicate structured product feedback and enterprise requirements to Product and Engineering; influence roadmap with clear use cases and impact

- Contribute enablement content for sales and SE teams, including demo assets, talk tracks, competitive insights, and repeatable playbooks for enterprise use cases

- Document patterns, field learnings, and best practices to strengthen our internal knowledge base and accelerate future deals

# Skills You'll Need to Bring

- **6+ years** in a customer‑facing technical role such as Solutions/Sales Engineer, Solution Consultant, or similar, ideally at a high‑growth SaaS company

- Track record supporting complex enterprise deals with multiple stakeholders, security reviews, and procurement cycles

- Exceptional communication and storytelling skills, from white-boarding architectures to executive‑level value framing

- Strong project leadership with the ability to run multiple evaluations in parallel, coordinate cross‑functional teams, and deliver crisp next steps

- Adaptable, resourceful, and comfortable operating in a fast‑changing environment

- You don’t need to be an AI expert, but you’re curious and willing to adopt AI tools to work smarter and deliver better results

# Nice to Haves

- Hands‑on experience with Notion as a power user or workspace admin

- Experience working in early stage companies & building processes

- Exposure to AI/automation use cases and platform extensibility, including AI Agents and integrations with AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Salesforce

- Experience leading RFPs, security questionnaires, and enterprise procurement processes

- Background in management consulting, solution architecture, or change management for large deployments
