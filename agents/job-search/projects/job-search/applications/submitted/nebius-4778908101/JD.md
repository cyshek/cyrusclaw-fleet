**The role**

We're hiring a **Senior Product Manager for Physical AI** to own the PM craft end-to-end — customer discovery, requirements, roadmap, prioritization, launch, cross-functional execution — for the first generation of Physical AI cloud products on Nebius. You'll partner closely with our **Field CTO**, who leads the vertical strategic direction and external narrative, while you ship the product that puts that strategy into the world.
You'll spend your time deep in the Physical AI stack (simulation, synthetic data, robot learning, sim-to-real) and in front of robotics, AV, and industrial automation customers.

**What success looks like in 12 months:**

- You've talked to **20+ Physical AI companies and research labs** and built the customer-evidence base that you and the Field CTO use to refine the Physical AI strategy.

- You've shipped the **first Physical AI-specific solution packages** alongside engineering — running real customer workloads, with measurable usage.

- You've stood up the Physical AI product cadence — planning, prioritization, customer review — and it's running.

- You've translated complex Physical AI workflows (simulation → training → eval → deploy) into a **prioritized backlog and concrete requirements** the platform and infrastructure teams are executing against.

- You're the **senior product partner the Field CTO works with** on strategy refinement, technical collateral, reference architectures, and demo material — co-authoring the docs, jointly carrying the customer narrative.

- 2–3 strategic partnership / co-development opportunities have been **identified, qualified, and handed off** to the Field CTO and BD for closing.

- **Senior PM craft.** You can run a product area as a senior IC: write a crisp PRD, build and defend a roadmap, run a clean prioritization meeting, ship to a deadline, and unblock a cross-functional team without escalating. You've done this before, ideally in cloud / AI infra / robotics.

**About Nebius**

Nebius AI is an AI cloud platform with one of the largest GPU capacities in Europe. Launched in November 2023, the Nebius AI platform provides high-end, training-optimized infrastructure for AI practitioners. As an NVIDIA preferred cloud service provider, Nebius AI offers a variety of NVIDIA GPUs for training and inference, as well as a set of tools for efficient multi-node training. 

Nebius AI owns a data center in Finland, built from the ground up by the company’s R&D team and showcasing our commitment to sustainability. The data center is home to ISEG, the most powerful commercially available supercomputer in Europe and the 16th most powerful globally (Top 500 list, November 2023).  

Nebius’s headquarters are in Amsterdam, Netherlands, with teams working out of R&D hubs across Europe and the Middle East. 

Nebius AI is built with the talent of more than 500 highly skilled engineers with a proven track record in developing sophisticated cloud and ML solutions and designing cutting-edge hardware. This allows all the layers of the Nebius AI cloud – from hardware to UI – to be built in-house, distictly differentiating Nebius AI from the majority of specialized clouds: Nebius customers get a true hyperscaler-cloud experience tailored for AI practitioners. We’re growing and expanding our products every day. 

If you’re up to the challenge and are excited about AI and ML as much as we are, join us!
