## Description

Apple is seeking a high-energy, results-driven engineering professional with exceptional leadership ability to join our Silicon Engineering team. In this role, you will be responsible for ensuring that Analog and Mixed-Signal (AMS) IPs for Apple's custom SoCs meet the highest standards of technical excellence, quality, and schedule execution. You will collaborate with world-class engineers across disciplines — from concept to mass production — on the most advanced IP in cutting-edge technology nodes, spanning Apple's consumer devices and large-scale data center infrastructure. This is a rare opportunity to shape the silicon that powers Apple's most iconic products and the data centers that support them at global scale.

## Responsibilities

Drive the development of state-of-the-art Analog/Mixed-Signal IPs from early concept to mass production.
Partner closely with AMS IP design and SoC Integration teams to drive high-impact development milestones — owning issue resolution, bug tracking, and IP consumer syncs to proactively address technical challenges across consumer silicon and data center platforms.
Drive resolution of complex technical dependencies across multidisciplinary teams — including Technology/Library, SoC Design, SoC Physical Design, Design Verification and CAD
Identify program risks early, lead rapid root cause analysis and resolution of silicon issues at the IP level, and develop mitigation strategies — delivering executive-ready presentations in close collaboration with cross-functional partners
Collaborate with hardware and software teams to support silicon validation of AMS IPs across consumer and data center environments
Synthesize large volumes of technical data into compelling narratives and deliver clear, concise project status presentations tailored to audiences ranging from engineers to executive stakeholders

## Minimum Qualifications

BS in Electrical Engineering or Electrical and Computer Engineering and 3+ years relevant experience.
Experience includes a track record of experience in any one of the following: Analog-mixed-signal (AMS) IP design OR Product development/program management/project management in AMS space OR Design engineering management in AMS space.

## Preferred Qualifications

Hands-on design experience and deep technical expertise in AMS IPs for SerDes, LPDDR, HBM, PLLs and/or LDOs.
Experience developing or validating high-reliability IPs for data center infrastructure.
Proven real-time crisis leadership in high-pressure, deadline-driven environments.
Ability to lead with influence — driving alignment and accountability across technical teams, multi-functional organizations, and external vendors.
Exceptional communication and presentation skills, with a demonstrated ability to engage and inform executive audiences effectively.
Advanced knowledge of IP development methodologies, EDA tools and flows, and SoC integration lifecycle spanning both device and data center deployments.
