# Product Manager II

## About the Role

Abnormal Security is seeking an ambitious, growth-minded **Product Manager II** to drive innovation across our AI-native security platform. At Abnormal, we help customers—from Global 2000 enterprises to small and medium-sized businesses—stay safe from sophisticated cyberattacks that can cause financial loss and disrupt business operations. Our AI-driven approach to email security, account takeover detection, and adjacent security use cases has made us one of the fastest-growing cybersecurity companies in the world.

As a Product Manager II, you will be relied upon to **own product definition, customer feedback intake, and multi-quarter roadmap planning** for one complex or multiple moderately complex product areas. You’ll collaborate closely with Engineering, Design, Product leadership, and GTM teams to ship high-quality features, shape product strategy, and deliver measurable customer and business impact.

This is a high-ownership, high-autonomy role for a PM who can move seamlessly between **customer problems, technical tradeoffs, and business outcomes**.

## What You Will Do

- **Own product definition and roadmap for your areas of responsibility**
Define clear problem statements, success metrics, and product requirements for one complex or multiple moderately complex product surfaces, and maintain a 2–3 quarter roadmap that aligns with division and company strategy.

- **Drive discovery informed by customer and market insight**
Partner with customers, field teams, and internal stakeholders to deeply understand user needs, security pain points, and market trends, turning them into clear product hypotheses and prioritized opportunities.

- **Lead cross-functional execution from concept to launch**
Partner with Engineering, Design, Data, and GTM teams to scope solutions, make tradeoffs, manage timelines, and ensure features launch with high quality and on time.

- **Collaborate on product strategy and positioning**
Contribute to product strategy for your domains, influencing how we sequence investments, differentiate in the market, and tell a compelling value story to customers and the field.

- **Champion metrics, experimentation, and continuous improvement**
Define and track product KPIs (adoption, engagement, retention, incident reduction, etc.), analyze outcomes, and drive data-informed iterations to improve customer value and business impact.

- **Partner closely with GTM and customer-facing teams**
Work with Product Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, and Support to ensure successful launches, clear enablement, and tight feedback loops from the field back into the roadmap.

- **Leverage AI and modern tooling for speed and clarity**
Use AI-native tools (e.g., ChatGPT/Claude, v0, analytics platforms) to accelerate research, documentation, prototyping, and decision-making, setting a strong execution and craft bar for the team.

## Must-Have Qualifications

- **3+ years of product management experience** in software, preferably in B2B SaaS or other technical domains.

- Proven experience working on **highly technical products**, collaborating closely with engineering teams, and reasoning about technical tradeoffs and constraints.

- Demonstrated ability to **own a product area end-to-end**: synthesizing customer feedback, defining requirements, prioritizing a roadmap, and driving execution across at least one full product or major feature lifecycle.

- **Strong analytical and problem-solving skills**, with the ability to structure ambiguous problems, define success metrics, and make well-justified decisions using both qualitative and quantitative inputs.

- Excellent **communication and collaboration skills**, with a track record of working effectively with Engineering, Design, and GTM partners, and presenting clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

- High **ownership, attention to detail, and operational excellence** across planning, documentation, and execution.

- A **growth mindset** and comfort operating in a fast-paced, evolving environment, with a bias toward learning, iteration, and continuous improvement.

## Nice-to-Have Qualifications

- Experience in **enterprise SaaS / B2B** environments, especially with security, infrastructure, or platform products.

- Prior experience in **cybersecurity**, security operations, or adjacent domains (e.g., identity, posture management, email security).

- Background as a **software engineer or other deeply technical role**, or equivalent hands-on experience with complex technical systems.

- Experience building **platform capabilities** (e.g., RBAC, notifications, shared services, extensible APIs) that are consumed by multiple product teams or customer personas.

- Familiarity with tools such as **Jira, Confluence, analytics platforms, Gong, Salesforce, and modern AI/UX prototyping tools**.

## Why You’ll Thrive at Abnormal

You’ll thrive in this role if you:

- Enjoy owning meaningful product outcomes and becoming the **most knowledgeable person in the company for your areas of ownership**.

- Love working at the intersection of **AI, security, and user experience**, and want to help customers stay ahead of sophisticated threats.

- Value **high trust, high autonomy**, and want to help shape both product strategy and the craft of product management as Abnormal continues to scale.

If you’re excited to build category-defining security products in an AI-native company while growing your impact and scope as a Product Manager, we’d love to talk.
