## Summary

Why Apple? Advanced audio technology is at the heart of Apple’s products. The Audio and Media Technologies (AMT) team is looking for an Engineering Project Manager to help lead software work in this area. You’ll be responsible for defining and running project plans that move several teams from idea through shipping amazing new features and products. AMT provides the software foundation for Audio across all of Apple's innovative products, from iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch, to Apple TV, AirPods, Beats, and Vision Pro. As a key link between developers and our hardware, AMT drives Apple’s audio playback, processing and recording experiences through several APIs, including CoreAudio.

## Description

You'll build and maintain implementation plans for all audio team commitments across multiple operating systems and hardware products. This will involve maintaining a multi-year roadmap of commitments from and requests of the audio teams. Additional responsibilities include:  
- Define communication between the audio and engineering partners 
- Drive cross-functional software implementation for audio accessories 
- Communicate project progress to all cross-functional stakeholders
