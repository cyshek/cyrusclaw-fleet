# **Our mission is to make exceptional healthcare accessible anytime, anywhere, for anyone.**

At Assort Health, we believe healthcare should feel effortless and connected — quick answers, clear communication, and seamless access to care. That’s why we’re building a new foundation for how patients and providers connect, driven by AI, built to embrace the complexities of healthcare, and tailored to each provider’s unique needs.

Assort is the most comprehensive patient experience platform powered by specialty-specific agentic AI. Assort’s omnichannel AI agents seamlessly integrate with EHR/PMS and complicated provider preferences to eliminate lengthy hold times and inefficiencies that stand in the way of patients getting the care they need.

Since launching in 2023, Assort has managed **over 150M+ patient interactions**, slashing average hold times from **11 minutes to 1 minute**. ## **About the Role:**

We’re looking for a Technical Program Manager to help bring structure, clarity, and coordination to how engineering work gets delivered. In this role, you’ll partner closely with engineering, product and operations to organize AI agent deployments, improve internal processes, and ensure teams are aligned as we scale. You’ll play a key role in turning ad hoc workflows into repeatable systems while helping the engineering organization operate more efficiently. This is a great opportunity for someone early in their career who enjoys solving operational problems, working cross-functionally, and building processes that help technical teams move faster.

## **What you’ll do:**

- Coordinate implementation projects across engineering teams and key stakeholders

- Create clear processes for planning, tracking, and executing implementation work

- Partner with operations to ensure smooth delivery of agent implementation initiatives

- Track progress across workstreams and help keep teams aligned on priorities

- Improve visibility and organization across engineering initiatives

- Support internal engineering operations such as recurring meetings, handoffs, and planning processes

- Help identify operational gaps and build systems that improve how teams collaborate and deliver work

## **What we’re looking for:**

- 2+ years of experience in technical program management, consulting, product operations, or a similar operational role

- Strong communication and organizational skills

- Experience coordinating projects or initiatives across multiple teams

- Ability to bring structure to ambiguous or fast-moving environments

- Experience improving processes or building operational systems

- Technical fluency and comfort working closely with engineering teams

## **Nice to have:**

- Background in computer science, engineering, or a technical field

- Experience as a Technical Program Manager or Technical Project Manager

- Exposure to startup or fast-growing product environments

- Familiarity with healthcare or agent-based technology environments

- Experience using project management tools such as Linear or similar systems
