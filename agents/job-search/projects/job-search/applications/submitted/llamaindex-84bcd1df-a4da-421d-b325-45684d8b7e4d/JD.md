**About the Role**

LlamaIndex is looking for an Applied AI Solutions Architect to sit at the intersection of enterprise sales and deep technical execution. This role is embedded directly in our revenue motion — you're the person who turns a skeptical engineering team into a champion and a promising evaluation into a signed deal.

You'll own the technical relationship from first discovery through proof of concept, working alongside Account Executives on live opportunities with the world's most ambitious AI teams. You're not just demoing a product — you're diagnosing real infrastructure problems, designing solutions, and earning trust with the people who have to live with the architecture long after the contract is signed.

**What You'll Do**

- Lead technical evaluations, proofs of concept, and solution design sessions for enterprise prospects across a range of industries and use cases

- Partner closely with AEs to de-risk deals, surface technical blockers early, and build credible paths to "yes"

- Communicate complex AI and data infrastructure concepts clearly to both engineers and C-level stakeholders in the same conversation

- Design and prototype solutions using LlamaIndex's platform that directly address customer requirements around accuracy, latency, cost, and scale

- Represent the voice of the customer internally — feeding insights to Product and Engineering that sharpen the roadmap

**What We're Looking For**

- Strong engineering foundation with experience in AI/ML systems, data pipelines, or enterprise software architecture

- Demonstrated ability to work in customer-facing technical roles — pre-sales, solutions engineering, or technical consulting

- Comfort operating under ambiguity: you can scope a problem, make reasonable assumptions, and move forward without waiting for perfect information

- GTM instincts — you understand what it takes to build technical consensus across a buying committee

- Clear, confident communicator who can translate system design trade-offs into business risk language for executives

- Prior experience with LlamaIndex or LlamaCloud is not required — we care about how you think and how you ramp
