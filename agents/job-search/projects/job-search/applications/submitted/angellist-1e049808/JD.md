About this Role
 Running a venture fund today requires a team of accountants, analysts, lawyers, and operators. The future is a headless, agentic experience where software runs the back office and the General Partner (GP) focuses on raising capital and picking winners.
 
We’re hiring a product manager to build this future.
 
You'll own the strategy, roadmap, and outcomes for Full Service, our flagship product for emerging managers, and Scout Funds, the product that puts you in the room with some of the largest names in venture, with the ultimate goal of increasing our market share.


## Specifically, you will:

- **Own the agentic strategy for both products**. Pick the workflows to automate next and shape the GP experience as more of the firm is handed off to agents.

- **Talk to GPs constantly.** From a solo manager raising their first fund to the senior partners running our largest firms, turn what you hear into a sharp product point of view.

- **Grow fund formations, AUM, and retention** by building a frictionless product that makes fund managers choose and stay with AngelList.

- **Write product requirements** that turn messy customer and operational realities into work engineering and design can ship.

- **Partner with sales, solutions, and operations** to support existing managers and win new ones

In this role, success is measured by fund count, AUM, retention, and service quality.

## You might be a good fit if you can demonstrate:

- **Domain passion:** deep engagement with the VC ecosystem and a real POV on where it's going; either fluent in fund mechanics (capital calls, distributions, NAV, K-1s) or ready to get there fast

- **AI fluency:** actively builds with AI tools and sees automating fund operations as one of the most compelling product problems in the market

- **Ownership:** closes the gap between formal PM scope and whatever needs to happen for the product to win

- **Customer-centric execution:** growth-oriented, ships and iterates quickly, treats customer feedback as a primary input

- **Sharp communication and range:** precise writer who turns edge cases into shippable requirements; can hold their own with senior leaders at established firms *and* do hands-on execution

- **Relevant experience:** 4+ years PM, ideally fintech, B2B SaaS, or another operationally complex domain
