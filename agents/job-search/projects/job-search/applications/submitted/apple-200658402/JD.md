## Description

You help lead a team of cross-functional partners and external vendors to meet the overall program objectives focusing on mechanical engineering design, development planning and validation cycles of new iPhones. Your ability to deep dive into material fundamentals, creatively solve challenging logistics and technical problems, and collaborate and work well with inter-disciplinary teams are key to delivering the best material solutions and products to our customers.

## Responsibilities

Drive the strategic execution iPhone Product Design, ensuring alignment with overall project goals and objectives.
Champion the project's design goals, ensuring they are integrated into all aspects of the program and holding teams accountable for achieving them.
Lead the creation and adherence of project schedules, ensuring key decision milestones are met across all phases and holding teams accountable.
Manage budgets, track spending, and ensure financial prudence; proactively identifying appropriate scope drivers and their cost implications.
Facilitate clear and consistent communication and alignment between internal teams (Product Design, System EE & RF, Manufacturing) and external partners, ensuring everyone is working towards the same goals.
Provide strong leadership and support during build phases, ensuring timely delivery of components, proactive problem-solving, and definition of validation plans.
Oversee the release process for parts and enclosures, ensuring timely and accurate releases and holding teams accountable for meeting release deadlines.
