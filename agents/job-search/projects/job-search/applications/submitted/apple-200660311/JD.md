Apple Financial Services (AFS) is a global financing organization supporting Apple customers across consumer, business, and education segments worldwide. Our mission is to enable Apple sales today and tomorrow through a platform of global funding partners and world-class innovative financial products.

We are seeking a highly motivated, strategic leader to manage the End of Term Program for Apple Financial Services (AFS) customers globally. In this role, you will drive technology refresh and upgrade programs and operations for Consumer, Business, Education, and Channel customers — maximizing asset value recovery through a robust remarketing ecosystem. This is a high-impact commercial and operations role requiring strong business acumen, drive for results, and the ability to lead through influence across a worldwide organization.

## Description

In this role, you will:

- Lead and execute end-of-term program strategy globally, leading program operations and driving on-time technology refresh rates and maximizing leased asset recovery
- Manage end-of-term operations across both consumer financing programs (e.g., installment plans, trade-in integration) and B2B lease/financing portfolios, ensuring a consistent and seamless customer experience across all segments
- Select, onboard, and manage remarketing partners and device processing vendors to build a scalable global remarketing platform
- Proactively collaborate with Apple's sales teams, AFS funding partners, channel partners, and carriers to integrate AFS seamlessly into enterprise and education strategies
- Identify operational challenges, streamline end-of-lease processes, and develop business models that scale across regions
- Define, track, and report on key program metrics including refresh rates, asset recovery values, return rates, and partner performance across all segments
- Serve as the program ambassador for AFS refresh and upgrade initiatives, ensuring an excellent customer experience at every stage
