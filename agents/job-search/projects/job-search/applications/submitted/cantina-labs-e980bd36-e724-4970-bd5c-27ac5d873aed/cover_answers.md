## What's your favorite feature on Cantina?

The real-time character expression system stands out to me the most. The way Cantina animates personality and emotional nuance into AI characters - rather than just generating static or flatly responsive avatars - feels like a genuinely different bet on what social AI can be. Most platforms in this space treat AI characters as a utility layer. Cantina seems to be treating expressiveness and personality as the core product, which is a much more interesting and harder problem to solve.

From a product perspective, that's also the feature with the most room to grow. The interaction between a user's creative intent and how a character actually responds, moves, and emotes is a workflow problem as much as a technical one - and that's exactly the kind of surface area I find most compelling to work on.
