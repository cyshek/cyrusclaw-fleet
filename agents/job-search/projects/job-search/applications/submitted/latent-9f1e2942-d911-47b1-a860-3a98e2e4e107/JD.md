## About Latent

Latent is building the intelligence infrastructure for healthcare. Our clinical AI platform helps health systems streamline medication access by automating complex workflows — prior authorizations, appeals, and 340B compliance. By surfacing relevant clinical evidence from EHR data, we reduce administrative burden and accelerate time-to-therapy for patients.

We recently raised a $60M Series A at a $600M valuation, backed by Conviction, Spark Capital and other leading investors. We 10x'd revenue last year and now work with 40+ of the nation's leading health systems — including Mount Sinai, UCSF, Yale, Ochsner, and Mayo Clinic.

We move fast, operate with high ownership, and build products that directly improve patient care. We're looking for a Product Manager to define and build the next generation of clinical AI products powering healthcare.

## 
The Role

As a Product Manager at Latent, you will own critical product initiatives from strategy through execution. Working closely with engineering, design, GTM, and customers, you'll identify the highest-impact problems in healthcare operations and deliver scalable solutions for large health systems.

## 
What You'll Do

**Product Strategy & Ownership**

- Define product strategy and roadmap aligned with company goals and customer needs

- Translate ambiguous problems into clear product plans, requirements, and deliverables

- Own product outcomes and metrics for the areas of the platform you lead

**Customer Insight & Discovery**

- Work directly with healthcare customers to understand clinical workflows and operational challenges

- Conduct user interviews and synthesize feedback into actionable product insights

- Balance customer input with long-term vision and strategic priorities

**Execution & Cross-Functional Leadership**

- Partner with engineering, design, and commercial teams to build and ship products quickly

- Write clear product specs and make trade-offs to unblock development

- Drive projects from concept through launch and iteration

**Metrics & Continuous Improvement**

- Define success metrics and run experiments to measure product performance

- Use data and customer feedback to continuously improve features and workflows

## What We're Looking For

- **Product Experience:** 5+ years in product management (or equivalent in engineering, product strategy, or a related field) building enterprise SaaS, AI/automation, or complex workflow products

- **End-to-End Ownership:** Track record of taking a product area from strategy and discovery through launch and measurable outcomes. Experience 0→1 opportunities especially

- **Customer Obsession:** Skilled at embedding with users, running discovery, and translating insights into practical solutions

- **Collaboration:** Thrives in cross-functional environments; builds strong working relationships across engineering, design, GTM, and leadership

- **Communication:** Communicates complex problems and trade-offs clearly to both technical and business audiences

- **Technical Fluency:** Comfortable digging in with engineering on APIs, integrations, system design, and AI-powered workflows

- **Startup Mindset:** Comfortable in fast-paced, ambiguous environments — adaptable, high-ownership, and able to maintain momentum through change

## Nice to Have
