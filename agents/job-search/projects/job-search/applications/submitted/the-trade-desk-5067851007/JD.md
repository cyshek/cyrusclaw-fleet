The Trade Desk is a global technology company and the world’s leading independent platform for digital advertising, with nearly 4,000 employees across more than 30 offices. Our technology helps advertisers reach the right audiences across the open internet — from streaming TV and podcasts to mobile apps, news, and more.  

Advertising powers the content people love. By making it more transparent, effective, and responsible, we help support trusted journalism, quality entertainment, and creators worldwide. The world’s brands and agencies rely on us to reach their customers and grow their businesses responsibly. 

The scale of our platform brings unique technical challenges — from processing massive datasets in real time to building systems that operate reliably on a global scale. When you work here, your impact is worldwide. We welcome diverse perspectives, encourage curiosity, and build teams that learn from one another. If you’re driven to solve meaningful challenges, we’d love to meet you.

 

**WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR**

-We're looking for a seasoned pharma media and advertising expert to serve as the product owner for The Trade Desk's Pharma vertical. You bring deep, firsthand knowledge of how pharmaceutical brands plan, buy, and measure media and you're ready to translate that expertise into platform capabilities that redefine what the Trade Desk can do for healthcare advertisers.  This is not a generalist PM role. You know the pharma media ecosystem from the inside-out: HCP and DTC campaign workflows, health-native DSPs, specialized data partnerships, and the highly complex regulatory frameworks that underpin the industry. You've worked alongside brand teams, agency holding groups, and health data partners, and you understand the nuance of reaching both patients and providers at scale within a tightly regulated environment.  If you thrive at the intersection of deeply regulated industries and cutting-edge ad technology, this role was built for you.

**WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING**
-Work with senior leaders and contribute to the product vision and roadmap for pharma and health media capabilities across The Trade Desk platform, including HCP targeting audience activation workflows, and integrations with health-native DSPs and data partners.
- Meet with customers, conduct user feedback sessions to seek to understand business problems and translate them into scalable product requirements.
- Collaborate with engineering and data science teams to support back-end systems, APIs, and data architectures that support health-specific data ingestion, identity resolution, and measurement pipelines while embedding privacy-by-design principles.

**WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE**
- 1-3 years of experience in pharma media and advertising, with deep hands-on expertise across HCP and DTC campaign strategy, planning, buying, or product, at a pharmaceutical brand, health-specialist agency, health data company, or ad tech platform serving the pharma vertical.
- Comprehensive knowledge of the health media ecosystem and the data partnerships, targeting workflows, and measurement approaches specific to pharmaceutical advertisers.
- Proven experience managing complex product integrations with health-native DSPs and specialized health data platforms.
- Strong working knowledge of the regulatory and compliance landscape governing pharmaceutical advertising: HIPAA, NAI health data guidelines, FDA promotional requirements, and emerging state health privacy frameworks.
- Technical fluency sufficient to partner credibly with engineering teams onAPI design, data pipeline architecture, identity resolution, and access control models.
- Demonstrated ability to drive cross-functional alignment across product, legal, commercial, and engineering stakeholders in ambiguous, fast-moving environments.
- Exceptional communication skills! Able to translate pharma industry requirements into engineering specifications, and complex technical constraints into business-friendly narratives for brand and agency clients.
