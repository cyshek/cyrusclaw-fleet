# 🌿 Mission

The US healthcare system is complex, error-prone, and financially draining. Medical bills and insurance coverage shouldn’t be this hard to navigate. At Granted, we’re building the one solution every American can turn to for help.

Thanks to AI and new regulations, Granted can fight claim denials, correct billing errors, negotiate bills, and make coverage easier to understand—saving people time, money, and stress. Our goal is simple: to be the #1 platform that empowers all Americans to take charge of their healthcare

# 🧭 About the Role

The Automations pod’s mission is to design, build, and operate agentic, end-to-end workflows that turn complex healthcare advocacy case work into reliable, scalable automations. This team owns the case pipeline from activation through resolution — increasing automation coverage, reducing cost to serve, and keeping experiences transparent by escalating to humans when needed.

As the PM for Automations, you’ll sit at the center of how we scale advocacy with speed, precision, and trust. You’ll work across engineering, SMEs, design and ops to transform messy, high-complexity healthcare workflows into structured, intelligent flows that feel effortless to users. Your work will directly shape how we deliver care and resolution at scale — making advocacy more automated, human when it matters, and dramatically more efficient.

# 🛠 What You’ll Do

- Own the product strategy, KPIs and roadmap for the Automations pod.

- Translate messy, multi-step operational workflows into agentic, intuitive product experiences.

- Partner closely with Engineering, Design, SMEs and Ops to drive alignment on and deliver high-leverage automation opportunities.

- Run your own processes to derive actionable user insights from both quantitative and qualitative data.

- Define and run cross-functional processes to test, measure, learn and refine how agentic systems anticipate user needs.

- Ship fast, reliable features that balance automation efficiency with user trust and transparency.

- Establish success metrics that blend operational scale with human-centered experience.
