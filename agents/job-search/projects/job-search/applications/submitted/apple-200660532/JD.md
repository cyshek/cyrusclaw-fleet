## Description

Technical Program Managers at Apple define, lead, and execute data-driven solutions that have meaningful business impact. In this role, you will:

- Own a diverse portfolio of agentic features and machine learning decision points, ensuring they’re aligned with broader program objectives and delivering measurable value
- Drive strategy, prioritization, and delivery of ML - and analytics-powered solutions across multiple business areas
- Guide programs from inception through implementation by partnering with data scientists, analysts, engineers, and business stakeholders
- Serve as a program leader who inspires accountability, high performance, and continuous improvement

## Responsibilities

Identify risks, resolve roadblocks, and keep teams focused and aligned on goals
Translate business needs into clear requirements, project plans, and success metrics
Foster a culture of trust, collaboration, and inclusion across the program team
Communicate technical and analytical results clearly to both technical audiences and senior business stakeholders
Bring structure and visibility to complex programs with many parallel workstreams and moving parts
Establish and track business-critical metrics and ensure data-driven decisions at every stage
