### **THE ROLE**

As a Product Manager for Systems, you will help shape the next generation of Everpure’s industry-leading FlashArray and FlashBlade platforms. This is a high-growth role where you will tackle complex architectural challenges—from performance and scale to sustainability—while collaborating with world-class engineering leaders. You will turn raw data into concrete product improvements, acting as a critical bridge between customer needs and the technical innovations that power thousands of global deployments.

### **WHAT YOU’LL DO**

- 
**Drive Data-Informed Product Evolution:** Analyze telemetry, support trends, and sales patterns to identify system bottlenecks and product gaps, turning these insights into quantitative recommendations that shape our portfolio strategy.

- 
**Define Actionable Product Requirements:** Partner with Solutions Engineers and customers to translate day-to-day challenges into crisp user stories, acceptance criteria, and PRDs for specific hardware and software improvements.

- 
**Execute the Systems Roadmap:** Lead the end-to-end delivery of defined functional areas—from design reviews to launch readiness—ensuring our FlashArray and FlashBlade platforms meet rigorous standards for operability and performance.

- 
**Streamline Global Operations:** Collaborate with Hardware and Operations teams to ensure accurate configuration rules and SKU definitions, making it easier for our field teams and customers to size and deploy Everpure solutions.

- 
**Champion Cross-Functional Alignment:** Act as a central point of contact between Engineering, Purity software, and QA teams to clarify requirements and track the delivery of key features against our organizational goals.

### **WHAT YOU BRING**

- 
**Analytical Problem Solving:** A proven ability to break down complex technical problems into structured, shippable increments using data-driven logic rather than anecdotes.

- 
**Technical Curiosity:** A genuine interest in how high-performance storage systems (like FlashArray and FlashBlade) work, with the hunger to dive deep into hardware, software, and operational details.

- 
**Collaborative Execution:** Experience in a technical product, program management, or engineering-adjacent role where you have successfully managed projects from concept to completion.

- 
**Communication and Influence:** The ability to present clear analysis and propose decisions with confidence while remaining open to feedback and focused on the collective success of the team.

-
