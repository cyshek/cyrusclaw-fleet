# Cover answers — Figma, Technical Program Manager, Security (figma-5982942004)

## Why do you want to join Figma?

At Microsoft, I spent the last year building programs that sit at the intersection of security operations, infrastructure resilience, and cross-functional execution. I led recovery drills across enterprise customers, managed dependencies on a sovereign-cloud isolation test tied to a $1.5B+ contract, and built automation to scale what started as a 2-person operation. The work Figma is describing for this role, owning end-to-end security programs, driving alignment across engineering and business teams, and finding repeatable ways to manage risk at scale, maps closely to what I've been doing and what I want to keep building depth in.

Figma sits in an interesting position: a collaborative design platform that millions of teams depend on daily, now moving deeper into AI-assisted workflows and real-time multiplayer infrastructure. That surface area creates genuinely complex security and compliance challenges, and I find that more compelling than working on security in a more narrowly scoped environment. The mission of making design accessible at scale also means the work has real stakes. I'd rather help secure something people actually use and rely on than work in a security program that's disconnected from product impact.

I'm currently based in Kirkland, WA. I'm open to remote, hybrid, or onsite arrangements and am willing to relocate if needed. Either way, I'm flexible on work location and happy to discuss whatever setup works best for the team.
