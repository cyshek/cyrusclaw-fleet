### About AI Security at Cato Networks

Cato’s AI Security capabilities extend the Cato SASE platform into the AI and SaaS layers, enabling enterprises to securely adopt and govern AI-powered applications and services. AI Security provides real-time visibility, control, and protection across AI-enabled SaaS platforms by addressing modern threats such as identity abuse, data leakage, misconfigurations, insider risk, and unauthorized AI usage. 

By integrating AI Security natively into the Cato SASE Cloud, Cato delivers a unified platform that protects users, networks, SaaS applications, and AI services from a single, globally distributed security architecture.

### Role Overview

We are looking for a Channel Sales Engineer to serve as a technical resource for Cato Networks’ Channel organization, acting as an overlay specialist focused on AI Security.

In this role, you will manage the technical evaluation process with channel partners, working with the Cato Channel team as the primary technical contact and product advocate for Cato’s AI Security solutions.

The evaluation process includes technical discussions, product demonstrations, Proof of Concept execution, and partner education. You will engage with technical and business stakeholders and play a key role across the partner lifecycle.
