## Description

PD EPM is a hands-on programmatic role where you and your team will be responsible for delivering the mechanical assembly through the entire program life-cycle—from architecture definition and early design to mass production. It includes frequent multi-disciplinary collaboration with teams like Product Design, Industrial Design, Materials, Manufacturing, SoC, Display, Camera, Antenna, Battery, and more. You will be responsible for driving the program through the entire development process, creating and maintaining streamlined schedules, driving PD and cross-functional progress, managing manufacturing/assembly upstream and at the CM through China trips, and presenting multiple levels of status updates to org leaders and executives.

## Minimum Qualifications

4+ years of combined experience in program management and mechanical/electrical engineering delivering products comprised of multiple materials
Experience leading full life-cycle product development in highly dynamic and ambiguous work environments, supporting the mechanical assembly and integration of multiple technologies
Excellent communication, teamwork, cross-functional engagement, and strong presentation skills
Ability to anticipate functional direction and roadblocks, prioritize parallel closure paths, effectively distill and communicate relevant data to the appropriate functional and leadership teams
Self-motivated and detail-oriented with demonstrated creative and critical thinking capabilities
Experience engaging with manufacturers on DFM, FAI/CPK, QC, and production timelines
BSME/SE/EE degree or equivalent; MS degree preferred

## Preferred Qualifications

Experience working with Product Design, Industrial Design, Operations, and System Architecture

Experience as primary point of contact with vendors and CM partners to establish a daily build plan, provide clear direction and guidance to meet the program and build objectives
