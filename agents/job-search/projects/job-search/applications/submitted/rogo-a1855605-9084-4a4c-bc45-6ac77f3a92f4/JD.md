## **The Role**

We're looking for an exceptional, high-agency product manager to own Felix, the agent at the core of Rogo. This is one of the most ambitious problems in applied AI: building an agent that does the work of a world-class analyst, reliably, for the most demanding users in finance. You'll work across every function at Rogo to shape what the agent knows and does, and to define how the world's most sophisticated investors work with AI.
