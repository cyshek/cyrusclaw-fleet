## Description

As an Engineering Program Manager for the App Store team, you will influence and lead the delivery of business-critical features built at global scale. Our Program Managers are responsible for shipping new features for the App Store Analytics platform, partnering with cross-geo engineers, product, marketing, design, data science, legal, security, and privacy teams to launch experiences that surprise and delight our users. This is a hands-on role where you will be actively involved in requirements analysis, functional and technical design discussions, testing, proof-of-concept, privacy reviews, and more. This is not a task-based position; you will own the successful end-to-end delivery of complex, customer-facing projects under demanding timelines.

The ideal candidate takes ownership, inspires the team to do their best work, and brings exceptional interpersonal skills, adapting their approach and communication style to keep projects on track and teams aligned as a single unit.
The best Program Managers are inherently curious. They dig deep to understand customer needs, how the underlying technology works, and the critical factors that inform important decisions. That inquisitiveness is a core strength on the App Store team, where we are constantly tackling challenges that have never been solved before, and that is what makes this an extraordinary place to work.

Our App Store Analytics team is nimble, cross-functional, and deeply collaborative, offering broad exposure to a wide range of opportunities within a growing organization. We are a diverse group of problem solvers and builders, continuously reimagining our products, systems, and practices to help people do what they love. A successful candidate will drive initiatives from ideation through launch, translating vision into sustainable, long-term programs and products.
Strong program management fundamentals are essential, as is a genuine passion for solving complex, dynamic problems. If you bring a growth mindset, thrive in a fast-paced environment, and are motivated to build products that matter, this role is for you.
