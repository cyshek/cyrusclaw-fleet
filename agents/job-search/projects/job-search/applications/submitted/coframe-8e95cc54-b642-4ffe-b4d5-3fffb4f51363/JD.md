We’re looking for Forward Deployed Engineers to build custom integrations, workflows, and spearhead new solutions for strategic enterprise customers.

This is a highly technical, high-trust role that sits between engineering, product, and customers. You’ll help solve complex enterprise problems directly while shaping how Coframe expands into larger accounts.

### **You’re a great fit if you have:**

- Experience with enterprise martech stacks and customer systems

- Strong software engineering skills and ability to ship viable solutions

- Strong communication with executives, operators, and technical teams

- Experience launching new products or capabilities in enterprise environments

- High ownership and comfort working with ambiguity

- Strong product and business sense for identifying the highest-value opportunities

- Experience building or scaling an enterprise company in this space is a major plus

- Ability to balance speed, trust, and long-term scalability

### **What you'll do:**

- Build custom integrations and solutions for enterprise customers

- Work directly with customers to understand workflows and technical constraints

- Travel on-site to customer

- Partner with internal engineering teams on implementation strategy

- Resolve complex technical blockers during deployments and growth phases

- Represent engineering in high-value customer relationships

- Help shape expansion opportunities inside strategic accounts

- Help guide product direction based on real customer demand
