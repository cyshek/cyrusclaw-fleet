# Cover answers — Figma, Voice of the Customer Program Manager (figma-6008343004)

## Why do you want to join Figma?

At Microsoft I built and ran Azure's company-scale resilience VOC loop from scratch, integrating signals from Support, partner escalations, and telemetry into a unified narrative that reached Product and Engineering and drove $14M+ in impact. I owned the full insight loop end-to-end, defined the reporting rhythm, set shared data-backed goals with engineering partners, and built AI workflows that cut cycle time by 39%. That is essentially what this role is asking for, and it is the work I find most energizing.

Figma specifically appeals to me because the product sits at the intersection of design, collaboration, and developer workflow, an area where customer feedback is genuinely complex and fast-moving. The VOC program here would pull signals from Support, Sales, NPS, community, and launch data all at once, which is a harder and more interesting synthesis problem than most companies face. I also care about the mission: making design accessible and keeping product teams moving faster together. Building the insight infrastructure that helps Figma do that better is a role I want to own.

## From where do you intend to work?

I am currently based in Kirkland, WA, and I am open to remote, hybrid, or relocating to one of Figma's hub offices in San Francisco or New York. I am flexible and happy to discuss whatever setup works best for the team.
