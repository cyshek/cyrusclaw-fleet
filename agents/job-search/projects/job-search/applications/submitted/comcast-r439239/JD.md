**Job Description**

The Solutions Architect within FreeWheel’s Global Client Solutions organization is responsible for designing and guiding scalable, integrated solutions that address complex client and business requirements across FreeWheel’s platform. This role partners closely with Product, Engineering, and Client Solutions teams to shape solution strategy, influence technical design, and ensure solutions meet performance, scalability, and operational standards.

 

The role leads complex and non-standard client engagements, serving as the primary technical liaison across internal and external stakeholders while owning solution design from concept through deployment. It provides direct technical guidance to clients, drives alignment across teams, and ensures successful implementation of high-impact solutions. In addition, the role contributes to the development of solution frameworks, best practices, and internal tools to drive consistency, efficiency, and technical excellence across the organization.
