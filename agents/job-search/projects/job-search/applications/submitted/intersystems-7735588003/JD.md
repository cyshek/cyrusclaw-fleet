# Sales Engineer - Financial Services

**Company:** InterSystems
**Location:** Boston, MA
**Apply:** https://www.intersystems.com/careers/careers-search/?gh_jid=7735588003
**Greenhouse ID:** 7735588003

---

InterSystems Corporation, a leading developer of database and integration technologies, is seeking candidates for its North American Financial Services Sales Engineering Group. The role is client-facing, fast-paced, wide-ranging and challenging. It requires a mixture of application development and deployment skills, hands-on coding and problem-solving, presentation skills, sales awareness, and business acumen. Occasional regional travel is a requirement for the role.

**Responsibilities:**

- Coordinating with InterSystems' Account Representatives and all other departments to ensure a close, long-term relationship with our clients

- Working closely with System Integrators and Project Managers, especially during the early stages of adoption and technically challenging projects, to ensure successful implementations

- Recommending technical architectures, model design, development patterns, migration strategies, upgrade procedures, and operational best practices

- Designing, building, and presenting Proofs of Concept (PoCs) to prospects and partners

- Delivering standard and customized on-site training in a classroom environment

- Supporting our clients during beta test programs or pre-launch activities

- Supporting marketing activities at trade shows, conferences, seminars etc.

- Keeping up-to-date with InterSystems products and contemporary IT industry developments (including competitive products) and undertaking any relevant self-development or training

- Creating prototypes and coordinating with product management to enhance product offerings based on client desires/needs

- Writing competitive proposals, solution overviews, sales collateral, etc. to support pre-sale efforts

**Required Skills**

**Technical Expertise**:

- 5+ years of experience as a Sales Engineer, Software Developer or related role(s) in the Financial Services domain

- Ability to handle technical questions and provide solutions during the sales cycle.

- Proficiency in architecture design and technical documentation.

- Demonstrated experience with most/all of the following

- Object-Oriented Programming and/or Enterprise Application Development, vibe coding, etc.

- Relational or non-relational databases using SQL or NoSQL

- Data Engineering skills using Python, GenAI, RAG, MCP, etc.

- Modern Analytics architectures - Data Lakes /Data fabrics/Data mesh

- Machine Learning and Generative AI use cases

- HTML, JavaScript, Angular

- XML, XSLT, JSON, SOAP, REST

- Cloud hardware services, containers, kubernetes, etc.

- Windows, Unix/Linux, MAC OS X

- API Management

**Communication Skills**:

- Excellent verbal and written communication skills to interact with a wide range of stakeholders, including clinicians, C-level executives, and software engineers.

- Ability to deliver compelling presentations and product demonstrations.

**Problem-Solving Skills**:

- Strong analytical skills to assess technical feasibility and recommend strategies.

- Ability to lead and manage Proof of Concept (POC), Prototype, and Paid Pilot projects.

**Project Oversight**:

- Experience in leading technical qualification calls and managing RFx processes.

- Ability to oversee implementation teams and ensure successful project delivery.

**Customer Focus**:

- Commitment to ensuring customer satisfaction and reference-ability.

- Ability to provide technical mentoring and transfer of knowledge to customers.

**Collaboration**:

- Ability to work closely with Account Managers, Professional Services, Product Management, Support, and other internal teams.

- Experience in logging and communicating customer needs to Product Management and Development.

**Educational Background**

A degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field.

**Experience:**

- Proven experience in a Sales Engineering or similar technical role.

- Experience with Financial Services-related organizations is a plus.

**Technical Certifications**:

- Relevant technical certifications related to InterSystems products or similar technologies are advantageous.

**Industry Knowledge**:

- Familiarity with the Financial Services industry and its technical requirements.

These skills and qualifications will help ensure success in the Sales Engineer role by enabling effective communication, technical problem-solving, and customer satisfaction.
