# Senior Product Manager, Shopping Experiences

**Company:** Affirm
**Location:** Remote US
**Apply:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/affirm/jobs/7622734003
**Greenhouse ID:** 7622734003

---

Affirm is reinventing credit to make it more honest and friendly, giving consumers the flexibility to buy now and pay later without any hidden fees or compounding interest.

We’re hiring a Senior Product Manager, Shopping Experiences (Direct to Consumer) to launch and scale Affirm's DTC shopping and offers experience. In this role, you’ll identify high-impact growth opportunities, validate them, through rapid experimentation, and bring them to market at scale. You’ll play a critical role in shaping high-impact consumer journeys, App and Web checkout experiences, consumer shopping preferences and Offers to customers. These directly influence conversion, engagement, and revenue.

**What you'll do**

- Define and own the Shopping Experiences roadmap for new growth channels, setting clear hypotheses, success metrics, and sequencing

- Launch and scale checkout flows, offers in app and web, consumer shopping preferences across app and web, partnering closely with Engineering and Design to deliver seamless, high-conversion onboarding experiences.

- Design and execute acquisition programs (e.g., referrals, exploratory partner pilots) with measurable CAC→LTV impact.

- Own funnel performance end-to-end: track weekly acquisitions, 30-day activation, drop-offs by step, and implement robust incrementality testing.

- Collaborate cross-functionally with Risk, Compliance, Marketing, and the Card Foundations team to align dependencies and ensure safe, scalable growth.

- Continuously experiment and iterate to unlock new conversion levers, maximize engagement, and expand card and consumer usage

**What we look for**

- 4+ years in Product Management; Ideally with previous background owning growth/distribution channels

- Track record shipping consumer financial or regulated products; comfort partnering with Risk/Credit, Legal, and Compliance.

- Deep fluency in experiment design, incrementality/attribution, and CAC→LTV optimization.

- Strong UX instincts for moment‑of‑intent surfaces; ability to work across web, app, and partner integrations.

- AI tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and others to research, analyze, visualize experiences and help develop better product vision

- Data chops (SQL/Amplitude‑level self‑serve) and a bias for shipping, learning, and iterating quickly.

- Excellent cross‑functional leadership; experience leading embedded eng/design pods.

- Bachelor's degree in a related field or equivalent practical experience
