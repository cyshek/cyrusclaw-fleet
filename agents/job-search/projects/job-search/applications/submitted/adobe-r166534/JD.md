## The Opportunity

Step into the position of Adobe Experience Platform Data Solutions Engineer in San Jose, CA. Join a dynamic team enthusiastic about innovation and committed to building new solutions on powerful technology! Collaborate with engaged colleagues in an inclusive work environment. Your impact will extend to millions worldwide and help Adobe fulfill its vision of empowering creativity and transforming digital environments.

In this role, you will collaborate within the Adobe Experience Platform suite, leveraging scalable data infrastructure, machine learning models, and a personalization platform. You will independently address data onboarding requests and aid teams in onboarding data to activate campaigns and personalized experiences! The ideal candidate enjoys working together with data science and product SDK teams to develop resilient solutions.

**ATTENTION: **This position requires hands-on expertise with Adobe Experience Platform, including direct involvement in data workflows, identity, and governance procedures within the platform. Candidates with substantial, practical AEP experience will be strongly preferred.

## 

## 

## What you will do

- Build, develop, and maintain scalable data pipelines and ETL processes to support data-driven decision-making.
- Collaborate with multi-functional teams including business partners and engineers to determine data requirements and successfully implement data solutions.
- Have an Enterprise outlook when facing challenging business and data problems. This ensures the solution meets the needs of the entire Enterprise, not just one business unit.
- Possess the knowledge and experience to advocate for the “right” data solution vs. compromises that will not withstand Enterprise rigor.
- Ensure data accuracy and code integrity through strict quality control measures.
- Develop and maintain comprehensive documentation for data processes and solutions.
- Represent internal business teams feedback on platform capabilities that will inform the broader product and engineering roadmap for internal Adobe tools.
 

## 

## What you will need

- Minimum of 1-3 years of hands-on experience with Adobe Experience Platform offerings such as Real Time CDP, Adobe Journey Optimizer, Customer Journey Analytics.
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Demonstrated expertise in schema building, data pipeline configuration, profile management, and ETL procedures.
- Strong proficiency in SQL, Python, API, and data visualization tools.
- Outstanding problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Ability to work collaboratively in an ambitious, fast-paced environment.
- Technical certification in AEP RTCDP strongly preferred.
