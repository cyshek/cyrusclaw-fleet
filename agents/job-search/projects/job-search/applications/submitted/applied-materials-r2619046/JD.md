**Key Responsibilities**

- Execute and coordinate **Service Orders, RTS, TES, and parts disposition** in alignment with CE and Supply Chain processes.
- Maintain **materials, tooling, and spares readiness**; lead weekly parts reviews with Spares Planning and FSO.
- Own **operational KPI tracking and reporting** (SO cycle time, IWN quality, materials aging, training compliance, productivity).
- Analyze trends, identify risks, and drive **standard countermeasures**; track corrective actions to closure.
- Support **workforce productivity** through scheduling coordination, timecard accuracy, and coverage planning.
- Maintain **SOPs, BKMs, and CE documentation**; support onboarding, training compliance, and competency tracking.
- Conduct **quality checks** on IWN documentation, SO compliance, parts handling, and ARK verifications.
- Prepare materials and summaries for **customer visits, audits, and operational reviews**.
- Own operational administration: **IWN updates, iCapability Plus approvals, SO administration, TES workflows, parts returns, WIP/WDRs/OnDemand billing**, and install base accuracy.

### **Scope & Impact**

- Direct impact on **service efficiency, quality, cost, and customer satisfaction**.
- Interfaces with **CE, Supply Chain, Quality, Finance, Training, and Customers**.
- No direct people management; may lead **process improvements** and cross‑functional actions.

### **Minimum Qualifications**

- Experience in **field service operations, CE support, logistics, or semiconductor equipment environments**.
- Strong organizational skills; ability to manage multiple priorities.
- Effective written and verbal communication skills.
- Proficiency with service management or workflow systems.

### **Preferred Qualifications**

- Experience supporting **Customer Engineers / Field Service Engineers**.
- Knowledge of **materials logistics, RMAs, TES, and Service Order workflows**.
- Familiarity with **Applied Materials CE processes and BKMs**.
- Experience with **KPI tracking and continuous improvement**.

### **Core Competencies**

- Operational Execution
- Business Acumen
- Data‑Driven Problem Solving
- Cross‑Functional Collaboration
- Compliance & Quality Discipline

### **Desired skills **

- Expert ability to build **executive presentation materials** that are clear, structured, and persuasive.
- **Advanced Microsoft Excel** capability: strong working knowledge of advanced formulas/functions, data validation, pivot tables/charts, and advanced graphing/charting.
- Excellent communication skills—able to simplify complex topics and deliver **clear, decision-oriented messaging** for senior leaders.
