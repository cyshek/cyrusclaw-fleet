We are seeking a Technical Project/Program Manager to join the Automation Technology Group. In this role, you will lead cross-functional projects that enable automation, process improvement, and operational excellence in our manufacturing operations. You’ll be responsible for managing the end-to-end lifecycle of automation projects — from requirements gathering and vendor coordination to implementation, testing, and handoff to production.

This role requires strong technical acumen, excellent communication skills, and the ability to manage multiple complex projects involving both internal teams and external partners.

**Key Responsibilities**

- Lead the planning, execution, and delivery of automation projects across multiple manufacturing sites.
- Define project scope, goals, and deliverables in collaboration with engineering, operations, IT, and other stakeholders.
- Coordinate cross-functional teams including controls engineers, mechanical engineers, software developers, and operations.
- Track and manage timelines, budgets, resources, risks, and dependencies across all phases of the project lifecycle.
- Work closely with automation vendors and system integrators to ensure on-time, high-quality deliverables.
- Facilitate design reviews, FAT/SAT testing, and commissioning of automation equipment and systems.
- Develop and maintain project documentation including project charters, schedules, risk registers, and status reports.
- Champion continuous improvement by applying lean, Six Sigma, or Agile principles to project execution.
- Support validation, change management, and documentation processes in compliance with industry and company standards.
- Communicate project progress and risks to stakeholders and senior leadership.

**Functional Knowledge**

- Strong understanding of manufacturing automation systems, including robotics, PLCs, SCADA, MES, and/or IoT technologies
- Proven ability to lead cross-functional teams and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
- Works on issues where analysis of situation or data requires review of relevant factors
- Demonstrates in-depth and/or breadth of expertise in own discipline and broad knowledge of other disciplines within the function
- Familiarity with project management methodologies (e.g., PMP, Agile, Scrum, or Waterfall).

**   Business Expertise**

- Experience in analyzing business processes to identify automation and control opportunities.
- Understanding of industry-specific regulations and compliance requirements.

**   Leadership**

- Provide technical guidance and support to team members.

- Reduce manual intervention and human error in critical processes.
- Contribute to the overall success of the organization by driving automation, control, and data connectivity initiatives.

**   Interpersonal Skills**
