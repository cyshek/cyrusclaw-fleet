**Why join us**

Brex is the intelligent finance platform that enables companies to spend smarter and move faster in more than 200 markets. By combining global corporate cards and banking with intuitive spend management, bill pay, and travel software, Brex enables founders and finance teams to accelerate operations, gain real-time visibility, and control spend effortlessly. Brex’s AI-native automation and world-class service eliminate manual expense and accounting tasks for customers so they can focus on what matters most. Tens of thousands of the world's best companies run on Brex, including DoorDash, Coinbase, Robinhood, Zoom, Plaid, Reddit, and SeatGeek.

Working at Brex allows you to push your limits, challenge the status quo, and collaborate with some of the brightest minds in the industry. We’re committed to building a diverse team and inclusive culture and believe your potential should only be limited by how big you can dream. We make this a reality by empowering you with the tools, resources, and support you need to grow your career.

**AI at Brex**

AI Engineering at Brex is redefining how businesses run their finances by building intelligent, autonomous systems directly into the Brex platform. Our teams develop AI agents that don’t just surface insights—they take action, optimizing spend, managing workflows, and making real-time decisions on behalf of our customers. By deeply integrating proprietary financial data with product and platform infrastructure, we’re turning complex financial operations into simple, automated experiences and setting a new standard for how modern finance works.

**What you’ll do**

Brex is building AI-powered products that require tight coordination across engineering, product, infrastructure, security, legal, and finance partners. As an Engineering Program Manager,  you will own cross-functional execution for our AI initiatives and ensure complex, multi-team work actually ships.

This role exists to make ambiguity explicit, sequence work across multiple teams, and close every loop. You will turn strategy into structured plans with clear objectives, deadlines, and decision logs. You will unblock stalled initiatives, escalate when necessary, and hold teams accountable to commitments — without senior engineers having to context-switch into program management.

If you are energized by driving clarity in chaos, enforcing operational rigor, and pushing work across the finish line, this role is for you.

**Where you’ll work**

This role will be based in our Seattle office. We are a hybrid environment that combines the energy and connections of being in the office with the benefits and flexibility of working from home. We require three coordinated days in the office per week — Monday, Wednesday, and Thursday. As a perk, we also have up to four weeks per year of fully remote work!

**Responsibilities**

- Drive end-to-end execution of AI programs across product, engineering, infrastructure, security, legal, and partner teams from planning through launch.

- Translate complex, multi-team initiatives into structured execution plans, with clear milestones, dependencies, timelines, and measurable outcomes.

- Establish and maintain a single source of truth for all workstreams, ensuring visibility into status, risks, decisions, and open actions.

- Lead cross-functional forums and decision reviews, ensuring the right stakeholders are engaged, decisions are documented, and follow-ups are actioned.

- Proactively identify and resolve risks, misalignment, and blockers, escalating when necessary and driving stalled work to completion.

**Requirements**

- 5+ years of experience in program management

- Demonstrated track record shipping complex, multi-team products

- Experience working in AI, agentic or consumer facing technologies

**Bonus points**

- Background in engineering operations, event planning, or other high-intensity operational environments

- Experience shipping AI or ML-powered products

- Experience working on infrastructure-heavy or technically complex initiatives
