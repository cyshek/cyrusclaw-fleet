## Description

We are looking for an exceptional Program Manager that is skilled at ideating, creating, building, leading, and implementing tools, programs, and resources that enable and equip teams and leaders to best care for customers.  This role will develop product strategies, identify opportunities, and design solutions that set the standard for a personalized retail experience.

## Responsibilities

Drive scalable program solutions that remove friction in the customer experience and equip Retail leaders to effectively support their teams.
Partner with key retail stakeholders across AMR and WW to identify ambiguous business challenges, transforming them into structured, high-impact programs or iterating on existing ones.
Independently lead the end-to-end execution of complex initiatives, applying program management best practices and critical analysis to ensure seamless delivery.
Synthesize and communicate high-impact information to senior leaders and cross-functional teams, shaping strategic decisions and ensuring global alignment.
Foster deep, productive partnerships with field leadership and Global Retail Support teams to ensure programs resonate with local market realities.
Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs) that measure program success, specifically focusing on leadership effectiveness and tangible team outcomes.
Serve as a key contributor to the broader program management team by sharing best practices, elevating team operations, and mentoring.

## Minimum Qualifications

7+ years of Project/Program Management experience or equivalent in Retail operations, leadership development, organizational design, or a related field.
Proven track record of navigating complex, ambiguous landscapes and driving scalable solutions across multiple teams.
Demonstrated ability to independently lead large-scale programs from inception to launch with minimal oversight.
Experience gathering insights from diverse global groups and balancing competing priorities for key business partners.
Outstanding organizational, documentation, and communication skills, with extensive experience presenting to and influencing senior executives.

## Preferred Qualifications

Advanced degree or relevant industry certifications.
Deep understanding of Apple Retail store operations, field leadership structures, and the global customer experience.
Experience working in a global organization, specifically interfacing with AMR and WW support teams.
Demonstrated passion for coaching others, fostering inclusive environments, and elevating the capabilities of the broader team.
