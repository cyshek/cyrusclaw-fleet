**What You’ll Do:**

The **Customer Experience (CX) Organization** at CoreWeave is dedicated to ensuring every client running AI workloads at scale has a seamless, reliable, and high-performance experience. This team supports the infrastructure that powers the AI revolution—working across data centers, hardware systems, and customer workloads to maintain the integrity of our cloud platform. **About the role:**

As the** Infrastructure Operations Program Manager**, you will be responsible for operationalizing and scaling CoreWeave’s bare-metal support program globally, initially focusing on the team’s data program. You will lead a pivotal function, serving as the liaison between clients, internal teams, and external vendors to ensure effective data analysis and reporting. You will drive data-first management of client relationships and enable cross-functional partnerships to improve new and existing operational processes and tools.

**In this role, you will:**

- **Own and evolve data processes and tooling** across all support workflows, including break/fix, incident response, maintenance, RMA, and customer escalations.

- **Drive operational insights through data**, improving how we collect, structure, and analyze information to surface trends, enforce accountability, reduce repeat issues, and proactively report on program performance.

- **Partner cross-functionally** with engineering, operations, vendors, and customers to align on data definitions, reporting standards, and workflow instrumentation.

- **Partner with data teams to build and maintain scalable data models and reporting layers**, working with both structured and unstructured data to create reliable tables, views, and dashboards.

- **Transform ad hoc processes into standardized workflows**, enabling consistent tracking and measurement across regions, vendors, and customers.

- **Develop and operationalize key metrics and dashboards**, including backlog, SLA adherence, resolution time, vendor performance, and overall program health.

- **Enable a metrics-driven operating model**, helping the organization transition toward data-informed decision-making, faster time-to-resolution, and improved customer experience.

- **Continuously identify gaps and opportunities** in tooling, reporting, and workflows, and lead initiatives to improve operational efficiency and visibility.

- **Fill additional scope of operational program management** as the team’s needs shift and evolve.

**Who You Are**

- **3+ years of program or operations management experience** in cloud infrastructure, datacenter operations, or high-performance computing environments.

- Strong ability to **bridge operations and data**, with hands-on experience in support environments, data analysis, reporting, and visualization across multiple systems and sources.

- Experience working with **support or service delivery environments** (e.g., ticketing systems, incident management, escalation workflows).

- Proficiency in working with **structured and unstructured data**, including building datasets, defining metrics, and ensuring data quality and consistency.

- Demonstrated ability to **design and implement scalable processes and workflows**, turning ambiguity into repeatable, measurable systems.

- Familiarity with **cloud computing concepts, containerization, and/or infrastructure hardware environments**.

- Working knowledge of **hardware troubleshooting and datacenter operations** is a strong plus.

- Excellent **communication and stakeholder management skills**, with the ability to work effectively across technical and non-technical teams, including external partners.

- Proven **problem-solving mindset**, with the ability to operate effectively in fast-paced, evolving environments.

- Experience creating **clear, customer-facing reports and documentation** that translate operational data into actionable insights.
