The Home Hardware EPM team drives the development of hardware sub-systems for new Home products, typically from architecture definition to mass production. This position offers a unique opportunity to combine technical depth with program management skills and executive exposure.

## Description

Home HW EPM is responsible for leading a broad cross-functional team in developing Home hardware products, leveraging a solid engineering background to recognize, assess, and communicate cross-functional issues and risks. Maintain good communication and provide multiple levels of status to individual contributors and executive management.

## Responsibilities

Drive planning and execution of product prototype builds, including materials planning and factory readiness
Own system-level flexes and boards: manage the internal design review process and release to vendors, review design changes with cross-functional teams, manage vendors and materials, and drive overall schedule for on-time delivery to final assembly
Establish daily build schedules and distribution plans to support the overall project plan for in-region and local builds
Partner closely with cross-functional teams to understand key design changes requiring validation and accurately integrate engineering validation needs into the system build matrix
Identify risks, develop mitigation strategies, and drive resolution of technical build blockers with cross-functional stakeholders
Provide timely, clear executive-level reporting on build progress and critical path status
Partner with Contract Manufacturer (CM) partners for the program, managing factory build events and vendor relationships

## Minimum Qualifications

BS degree in Engineering required; Electrical or Mechanical Engineering preferred
3+ years of experience in hardware engineering or operations program management
Proven ability to effectively manage factory build events and vendor relationships

## Preferred Qualifications

Strong technical background in EE or ME
Excellent communication, presentation, organizational, and leadership skills
Ability to manage complex build matrices and detailed build planning schedules
Superior attention to detail with a strong commitment to rigor and excellence
Self-motivated and proactive with demonstrated creative and critical thinking capabilities
Understands and deals well with rapid development cycles and constant change; remains flexible and calm in the face of uncertainty
Experience interfacing with cross-functional teams including Systems Engineering, Mechanical design, Software/Firmware, Reliability, and Operations.
Ability to travel internationally to support build events
