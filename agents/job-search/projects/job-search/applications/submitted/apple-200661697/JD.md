## Description

As a System EPM, you will work closely with mechanical, electrical, algorithm, and software teams in VPG and across the company to define and develop highly optimized hardware products. You will be responsible for: Driving the development of system requirements based on experience targets and technology constraints. Identifying the most critical questions and organizing teams to find efficient ways to get answers. Understanding the link between technical decisions for the system and customer experience and communicating impacts clearly and concisely.

## Minimum Qualifications

Strong technical background in Electrical, Mechanical, or Software Engineering

Proactive and highly self-motivated; good critical thinking capabilities and strong attention to detail
