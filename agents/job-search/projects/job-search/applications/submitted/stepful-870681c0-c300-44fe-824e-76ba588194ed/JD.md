**The opportunity**:

Stepful is one of the fastest-growing healthcare education platforms in the country, training allied health workers at scale across 12+ certificate programs — CMA, Phlebotomy, PCT, PHT, and more. Our programs run on a tight, high-stakes rhythm: the right instructor, in the right class, at the right time, every single cohort.

We're looking for a Student Success Program Manager to join our operations team in a hybrid role that evolves as you grow with us. You'll start in a coverage capacity while a team member is on a planned leave, owning the instructor-facing logistics that keep our certificate programs running — scheduling, sub coverage, invoice operations, and cohort setup across 90+ instructors. Once that coverage period wraps, you'll transition into a program management role, coordinating and leading a network of coaches and instructors who support post-program career searches for students.

This is a role for someone who thrives in operational complexity, moves quickly through ambiguity, and genuinely cares about the students on the other side of the work.

This full-time, remote role reports to the Manager of Instructional Quality & Operations.

**What you’ll do**:

*Phase 1: Cohort Logistics (first 6–8 weeks)*

You'll begin in a coverage capacity while a team member is on a planned leave, owning the instructor-facing logistics that keep our certificate programs running. This is real operational ownership — not a shadow role — and sets you up with deep program context before you transition into the coach-facing work.

- Own instructor scheduling across 90+ instructors and active certificate programs, ensuring every cohort has the right instructor at the right time

- Coordinate sub coverage when instructors are unavailable, maintaining continuity for students

- Handle invoice operations — reviewing, processing, and tracking instructor invoices accurately and on time

- Own cohort setup operations, ensuring every program is configured and ready to launch without friction

- Serve as the operational point of contact for instructors, fielding questions and resolving scheduling issues as they arise

*Phase 2: Student Coach Program Management*

- Coordinate and support a network of 1099 coaches who provide first-line support to Stepful students

- Lead onboarding of new coaches and drive continuous improvement and best practice sharing to ensure communication reflects Stepful's supportive tone

- Meet weekly 1:1 with coaches to review student load, provide actionable feedback, and support their development

- Assess coach quality and provide constructive guidance on how they can best support students

- Track student, cohort, and partner-level outcomes — including retention, graduation, certification, externship placement, and job placement rates

- Run weekly or biweekly cohort-specific task force meetings to assess progress and hit success metrics

- Proactively maintain data and reports to support the client success team

- Communicate with enterprise partners on student progress
