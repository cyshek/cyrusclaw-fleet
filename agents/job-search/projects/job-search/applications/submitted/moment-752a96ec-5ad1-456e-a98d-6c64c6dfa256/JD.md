## 
**The Role**

We're hiring **Deployment Strategists** to function as the **GM for a Moment engagement,** owning the full lifecycle from first demo through implementation through massive expansion. You're the primary point of accountability for everything the customer needs to succeed and the visionary helping them transform their business.

The role sits at the intersection of engineering, product, and GTM. Every customer should feel like they have a cofounder embedded with them.

## 
**What You'll Do**

- **Pre-Sale: Solutioning & Vision.** Run incredible demos, go onsite to learn how customers actually work, and translate what you learn into a clear architecture, POC, for the goal of a signed contract. Catch bad trajectories early and always think about how to best frame Moment as the AI Operating System for our customer's entire business.

- **Implementation & Launch.** Own getting the customer live, happy, and excited to expand. Drive the implementation plan and manage deliverables across product, engineering, and sales. Roll the platform out in person with key users.

- **Growth & Expansion.** The majority of contract value at Moment lands *after* the initial rollout — your job is taking accounts from zero to one, and then one to one hundred. Run QBRs with the BD owner, monitor adoption, and surface expansion paths.

- **X-Factor.** Bring sharp customer feedback to product and engineering, or action it yourself for faster iteration loops. Champion new product areas, train internal teams to be product experts, and build bespoke demos and MVPs when a customer needs something Moment doesn't yet have.
