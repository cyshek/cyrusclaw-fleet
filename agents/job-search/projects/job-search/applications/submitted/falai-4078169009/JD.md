## About the role: 

We're looking for an experienced Enterprise Product Manager to lead our enterprise product efforts. You'll work closely with large customers, shape pricing and packaging, and help define features that support organization-level use cases.

### **Responsibilities**

- Partner directly with enterprise customers to understand needs and gather feedback

- Drive product decisions around pricing, packaging, and enterprise features

- Define and prioritize org-level capabilities (security, access control, usage, billing, integrations)

- Collaborate with engineering, sales, and leadership to deliver enterprise-ready solutions

- Support enterprise sales cycles with product expertise

### **Requirements**

- 5+ years of product management experience, including enterprise products

- Strong customer-facing experience with large organizations

- Experience with enterprise pricing models and buying processes

- Ability to turn customer input into clear, product direction
