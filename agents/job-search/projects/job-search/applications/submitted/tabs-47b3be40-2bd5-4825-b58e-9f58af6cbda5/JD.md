**Tabs **is the leading AI-native revenue platform for modern finance and accounting teams. Tabs agents automate the entire contract-to-cash lifecycle, including billing, collections, revenue recognition, and reporting, to help teams eliminate manual work and accelerate cash flow.

High-growth companies like **Cursor** and **Statsig** rely on Tabs to generate invoices directly from contracts, reconcile payments in real time, and automate ASC 606 compliance.

Founded in 2023, Tabs has raised over $91 million from [**Lightspeed Venture Partners**](https://lsvp.com), [**General Catalyst**](https://www.generalcatalyst.com), and [**Primary**](https://www.primary.vc). The team is headquartered in New York and brings deep expertise in finance and AI.

**About the Role**

We're growing our product team and hiring Product Managers across multiple domains and levels. Whether you're an experienced PM looking to own a critical surface area or a strong mid-level PM ready for more ownership, we want to talk to you.

Tabs is at an exciting inflection point — we're scaling the platform, expanding into new product areas, and building out the team that will take us there. The right candidates will have the opportunity to shape their domain from the ground up, working closely with engineering, design, and our customers.

In your conversations with us, we'll work together to find the best fit based on your background, experience, and interests. Current areas of investment include **contract processing** (turning unstructured contract data into structured billing and revenue workflows) and **merchant onboarding and configuration** (making it faster and more self-serviceable for merchants to go live on Tabs).

**How We Work**

- We work hard. Short sprints, weekly ship goals, and rapid feedback cycles built around intra-month product releases.

- We work smart. Get in front of the customer, use both data and customer feedback to drive decisions, while keeping the bigger picture in mind.

- We work together. In-office culture with people you want to work with. Lots of whiteboard space. Expensed coffee, lunch, and dinner when in the office.

**What You'll Do**

- Own the strategy, roadmap, and delivery for your domain — from problem discovery through shipped product

- Partner closely with engineering and design to ship high-quality features on fast timelines

- Develop a deep understanding of your customers — finance leaders, accounting professionals, and the internal teams that support them — and use that to drive prioritization

- Bring structure to ambiguous problem spaces and make crisp decisions in the face of incomplete information

- Define success metrics, track outcomes, and iterate based on data and customer feedback

- Collaborate across the product org to ensure your domain fits into the broader Tabs platform coherently

- Depending on your domain: translate unstructured contract and billing data into structured workflows that downstream systems can act on; or build internal tooling and self-serve experiences that reduce implementation burden and accelerate merchant onboarding

**About You**

- 3–7+ years of product management experience in B2B SaaS, fintech, or enterprise software (we're hiring across levels)

- Track record of owning complex problem spaces and shipping meaningful product

- Strong systems thinker — you understand how data flows, how services interact, and how product decisions create downstream consequences

- Comfortable going deep with engineering while staying sharp on customer needs and business impact

- Excellent communicator who can align technical and non-technical stakeholders

- Thrives in fast-moving, high-growth environments where the org is still being shaped

- Experience with financial systems, billing, contracts, or accounting workflows is a plus — but what matters most is intellectual curiosity, strong product instincts, and the ability to get things done
