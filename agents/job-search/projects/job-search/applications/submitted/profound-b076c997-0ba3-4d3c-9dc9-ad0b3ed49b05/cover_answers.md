That loop, writing real code and owning the client relationship end to end, is exactly what the Forward Deployed Engineer role at Profound describes. What draws me specifically to Profound is the category timing. AI search is shifting buying behavior right now, and the window to define how brands measure and act on that shift is narrow. Profound is already at 13% of the Fortune 500 and backed by Lightspeed, Sequoia, and Kleiner Perkins, which tells me the market thesis is landing, but the real technical and go-to-market infrastructure is still being built. That is the stage I want to be at. I want to be the person sitting across from a client's engineering team in the morning and shipping the integration by end of week, and doing it at a company that is defining a new category rather than optimizing an old one.
