## **About the Role**

As a Staff Forward Deployed Engineer, you are the technical authority of the Forward Deployed AI Engineering team. You set the standard for solution quality, own and lead the AI & Data Science function, and ensure that what the team builds in customer accounts flows back into intelligence and tooling that TENEX operates at scale. You think in systems, build for reuse, and make everyone around you better.

## **Job Responsibilities**

- All responsibilities of a Senior Forward Deployed Engineer, operating at the highest level of technical complexity and strategic account engagement

- Serve as the technical lead and quality standard for the Forward Deployed AI Engineering team — reviewing solutions, setting architectural patterns, and ensuring consistency and excellence across all engagements

- Own and lead the AI & Data Science function — building cross-customer intelligence systems that may include vulnerability prioritization models, configuration baselines, threat intelligence pipelines, detection optimization tools, and other areas identified through active FDE customer engagements

- Ensure the feedback loop between FDE customer work and the AI & DS function is tight and continuous — no tickets, no handoffs, direct collaboration

- Work with SE&A to operationalize AI/DS-built tools — ensuring what the team builds is something SE&A engineers can run and customers can benefit from

- Define the technical direction for how FDE-built solutions are documented, packaged, and made available across the broader platform

- Mentor Senior and Forward Deployed Engineers — elevating the team's technical bar across AI, data, and security domains

- Partner with the Director on team strategy, hiring bar, and long-term capability development

- Represent the FDE team's technical perspective in cross-functional conversations with Product, SE&A, and Rapid Deployment
