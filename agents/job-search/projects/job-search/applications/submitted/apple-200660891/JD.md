## Description

You will bring structure and clarity to complex programs, lead change management efforts, and help ensure our matter management and eBilling systems are optimized to meet the evolving needs of the organization. This is a high-impact role where your analytical rigor, communication skills, and ability to manage ambiguity will make a meaningful difference.
