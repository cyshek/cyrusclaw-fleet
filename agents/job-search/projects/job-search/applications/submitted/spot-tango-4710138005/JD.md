**Who We Are****
**
Spot & Tango is an innovative pet health & wellness brand that delivers personalized meal plans on a subscription basis. Our recipes are developed by leading animal nutritionists and are made with only real, human-grade ingredients... and nothing artificial! We have lofty goals (to make every dog in the country healthier, and every pet parent happier!) and we are looking for the right people to help us get there. This is an exciting opportunity to join a VC-backed, high-growth e-commerce startup and have an outsized impact in an extremely fast-paced environment. Plus, you get to think about dogs all day!

**Who You Are**

You are curious, detail-oriented, and excel at using product management skills to build great digital experiences for customers. You love digging into problems and creating thoughtful, user-friendly experiences to address them. You thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment and understand the importance of connecting the dots between customer needs, business goals, and technical constraints in the product and software development lifecycle.

As a Product Manager on Spot & Tango’s Digital Product team, you will partner with product managers to work cross-functionally with software engineers, designers, marketers, and other stakeholders to design and build intuitive, delightful experiences for our customers. This is an exciting opportunity to contribute to the success of a growing ecommerce platform and drive immediate impact at a leading pet health & wellness brand.

**Responsibilities**

- Translate business needs into clear PRDs and tickets in partnership with engineers and stakeholders

- Partner with engineering, design, and business teams to assist in building and QAing high-quality digital products, ensuring alignment across cross-functional teams

- Participate in product launches by reviewing new functionality, logging issues, and helping triage feedback so bugs and enhancements are surfaced and prioritized appropriately

- Work with engineers and QA analysts to identify and prioritize production bugs

- Support development workflows, including sprint planning, ticket refinement, tracking timelines, and flagging risks or blockers

- Collaborate with data engineers and analysts to track feature performance and A/B tests by identifying key metrics, analyzing data, and preparing summaries or insights for stakeholders

**Requirements**

- Bachelor’s degree

- 3-5 years of experience in a product management, software development, program management, strategy, or similar role

- Strong attention to detail

- Excellent communication and collaboration skills with both technical and non-technical teammates

- Strong desire to continue to learn and grow your skills in product management, technology, and software development

- Comfortable working in a fast-paced startup environment and willing to take ownership to help push projects over the finish line

- Passion for digital products and delivering great user experiences

- Dog lover!

*This is a hybrid position based in our Midtown Manhattan office. *
