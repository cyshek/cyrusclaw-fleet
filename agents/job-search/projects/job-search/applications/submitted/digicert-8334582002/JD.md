**Job summary**

We’re looking for a highly technical, execution-driven Product Manager to help advance our Trust Lifecycle Manager platform across cloud, Kubernetes, and emerging identity-security ecosystems. This role is ideal for a product professional who enjoys rolling up their sleeves—diving into cloud services, validating technical workflows hands-on, partnering closely with engineers, and driving early-stage concepts into impactful customer-facing capabilities.

You’ll operate at the intersection of PKI, cloud-native architectures, digital identity, and certificate automation—translating complex technical needs into scalable, intuitive solutions that accelerate customer trust workloads. 

 

**What you will do**

Cloud, Kubernetes & Platform Enablement

- Implement and iterate on integrations with AWS, Azure, and GCP services—including KMS, Key Vault, CloudHSM, ACM, and CAS.

- Partner with engineering to build, test, and validate Kubernetes certificate automation flows (cert-manager, SPIFFE/SPIRE, service meshes, mTLS workflows).

- Drive hands-on prototyping within cloud-native environments, microservices, and containerized deployment architectures.

- Support design and documentation for CLM integrations across hybrid and multi-cloud environments.

Technical Execution & Roadmap Delivery

- Translate customer requirements into technical specifications, user stories, and acceptance criteria.

- Manage and prioritize backlogs for engineering squads in an Agile development structure.

- Build quick proofs-of-concept to evaluate feasibility, integration complexity, and customer value.

- Assist with release planning, validation, and rollout of new features.

Customer Discovery & Validation

- Conduct structured interviews with practitioners (DevOps, CloudOps, Security, Platform Engineering).

- Present concepts, prototypes, and early builds for targeted feedback loops.

- Synthesize insights into actionable product improvements and roadmap proposals.

- Support technical enablement for customers evaluating or adopting new integrations.

Cross-functional Technical Collaboration

- Work with Architecture and Engineering to align on design patterns, scalability needs, and cloud-native security controls.

- Engage with Solutions Architects and Sales Engineers to analyze customer environments and identify use cases.

- Collaborate with Marketing to support technical messaging, diagrams, and solution narratives.

 

**What you will have**

- 3+ years of hands-on experience in product management, technical program management, DevOps, cloud engineering, or a related role.

- Practical experience with at least one major public cloud ecosystem (AWS, Azure, or GCP), especially in security or identity workloads.

- Working knowledge of Kubernetes fundamentals, certificate issuance flows, mTLS, and cloud-native security practices.

- Familiarity with CI/CD tools, container orchestration, and automation frameworks.

- Exposure to PKI, certificate lifecycle management, cryptographic protocols, or related security domains.

- Ability to collaborate effectively with engineers and communicate technical concepts clearly to stakeholders.

- Experience building prototypes or working closely with engineering on proof-of-concepts.

- Strong analytical skills, with comfort using data to guide prioritization and validate decisions.

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field (or equivalent practical experience).

- Understanding of cryptography and security protocols.

- Familiarity with API design and integration patterns.

- Experience with prototyping tools and methodologies.

- Knowledge of agile development practices.

- Comfort with data analysis and experimentation frameworks.

 

**Nice to have**

- Experience with Terraform, Helm, or other IaC tooling.

- Understanding of Zero Trust, workload identity, service mesh technology, and secure connectivity patterns.

- Background in software development, SRE/DevOps, or cloud solution architecture.

- Exposure to certificate automation ecosystems (cert-manager, SPIRE/SPIFFE, Vault PKI).

- Experience supporting marketplace readiness or product listings on AWS, Azure, or GCP.

- Familiarity with design thinking and rapid experimentation methodologies.
