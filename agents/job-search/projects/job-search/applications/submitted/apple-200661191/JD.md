## Description

The Worldwide Business Process Re-engineering (BPR) team is seeking an experienced Program Manager to lead strategic initiatives that directly impact Apple's Supply Chain and Retail Fulfillment Operations. In this role, you'll influence, support, and implement forward-looking capabilities to help Apple stay ahead of the competition.

The Retail Fulfillment Operations portfolio spans a wide range of programs, including the expansion of both online and physical retail stores, new and enhanced fulfillment methods, innovative gifting, payment and affordability options, trade-in and repair strategies, retail replenishment and inventory management, and enabling Apple's 2030 sustainability goals. Success in this role requires deep expertise in supply chain fulfillment operations and the ability to manage complex global projects.

This role leads global cross-functional teams to deliver technology and process solutions that improve customer experience, drive operational efficiency, and support business growth across Retail Fulfillment Operations. You'll also partner with senior leadership to define business process and capability roadmaps aligned to Apple's evolving retail strategy.
The ideal candidate is strategic, detail-oriented, comfortable driving clarity and decisions in a fast-paced, global environment.
