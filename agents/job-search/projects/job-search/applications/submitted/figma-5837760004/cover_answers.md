That combination, performance programs plus AI-native tooling, maps almost directly onto what this role is asking for, and I want to work somewhere where both of those things are first-class concerns rather than side projects.

Keeping model inference latency, FPS, and memory in check while shipping AI features at scale is exactly the kind of cross-cutting, high-visibility challenge I want to own. The platform scope here, spanning desktop, browser, mobile, and WASM, is serious engineering, and Figma's position as the standard toolchain for product teams means the performance work actually reaches millions of users. That matters to me.
