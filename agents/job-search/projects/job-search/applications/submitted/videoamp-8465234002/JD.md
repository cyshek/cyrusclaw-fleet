## About VideoAmp

 

VideoAmp is the tech-first measurement company transforming how advertising is valued, bought, and sold. Powered by currency-grade big data and a best-in-class technology stack, our platform gives advertisers, publishers, and agencies the ability to plan, optimize, and measure media investments across every screen — from linear TV and OTT to CTV and digital video.

VideoAmp is accelerating investment in agentic AI and intelligent optimization technologies — helping clients drive measurable, real-world outcomes in an increasingly complex media landscape. With 880% year-over-year measurement growth, 98% coverage of the TV publisher ecosystem, and partnerships with 11 agency groups and 1,000+ advertisers, we're not just keeping pace with the industry — **we're defining what comes next.**

We believe great work requires great people — people who say "I'll find a way" instead of "it can't be done."

 

## The Role

 

VideoAmp is searching for a seasoned, data-savvy **Product Manager, Methodology** to join the expanding Product Methodology team within our Tech organization. You'll serve as an in-house methodology and analytical expert across our cutting-edge product offerings — from Cross-Screen Content Viewership to Ad Measurement, Outcomes, and Lift.

We're looking for a creative problem-solver with a strong passion for data and technology, and ideally experience or genuine interest in television content, viewership, and measurement. If that sounds like you, we'd love to connect.

 

## What You'll Do

 

- Serve as an in-house methodology and analytical expert for product offerings including Cross-Screen Content Viewership, Ad Measurement, Outcomes and Lift, and more.

- Collaborate with Machine Learning, Data Science, Client Success, and Product teams to ensure projects are delivered accurately and on time.

- Define clear product requirements and validation standards to ensure data accuracy and integrity across product offerings.

- Develop analyses and present Summary of Findings reports to support client inquiries, internal research projects, and product development.

- Participate in client meetings and industry events, including the Media Ratings Council, Client Advisory Committee, and Coalition for Innovative Media Measurement.

- Continuously improve both product and process, with an eye toward repeatability, scalability, and transparency.

 

## What You'll Bring

 

- 3+ years of experience working with large-scale event-level datasets in a Product, Analytics, or Data Science role.

- Experience working with **SQL and/or Python**.

- Familiarity with audience measurement methodologies.

- Experience presenting insights and recommendations to various stakeholders.

- Ability to communicate effectively with teams of varying levels of technical expertise.
