### About the Role

A fast-growing, pre-seed-funded healthtech SaaS startup is hiring a **Forward Deployed Engineer** to build customer-facing solutions and translate clinical and operational needs into shipped software. You'll join a small, high-velocity team in Miami where strong building ability, emotional maturity, and direct customer collaboration are just as important as technical skills. This is an excellent opportunity for early-career engineers who want real ownership, frequent travel, and meaningful impact in healthcare technology.

### What You'll Do

- Build front-end and back-end features using TypeScript, React, Deno, Node, and SQL.

- Collaborate directly with customers — including urgent care centers and emergency medicine groups — to gather requirements and iterate on solutions.

- Deploy and operate services on AWS; troubleshoot and resolve production issues.

- Travel frequently for on-site customer engagements and deployments.

- Contribute to product design and rapid prototyping, moving ideas to shipped code quickly.

### What We're Looking For

- **0–1 years** of professional software development experience (new grads welcome).

- Proficiency in full-stack TypeScript development (frontend and backend).

- Experience with or strong familiarity with: React, SQL, Deno, Node.js, and AWS.

- Strong written and verbal communication skills for customer-facing engineering work.

- Comfortable working directly with clinical or operational stakeholders.

- Willingness to be on-site in Miami and travel regularly for customer engagements.

- Ability to ship reliable software quickly in a fast-paced, early-stage environment.

- Must be eligible to work in the US without employer-sponsored visa.

- Open to candidates relocating from anywhere in the US.

### Compensation & Benefits

- **Base salary:** $80,000 – $130,000 USD annually (slightly negotiable)

- Total compensation package includes food, housing, and transportation coverage during travel, equity, and travel expense reimbursement.

### Location

On-site in **Miami, Florida**. Relocation from anywhere in the US is welcome. This is not a remote role. Frequent travel to customer sites is expected.
