**About the Role**

Abnormal AI is seeking an Outbound Solutions Architect to serve as a technical expert and trusted advisor to existing customers. The primary focus of this role is assisting organizations in transitioning from legacy third-party Secure Email Gateways (SEGs) to platform-native email security (M365/GWS), tightly integrated with Abnormal AI’s platform. In partnership with Customer Success Managers (CSMs) and Professional Services Consultants (PSCs), you will own SEG displacement opportunities end-to-end — leading architecture-level technical discussions, running deep discovery, articulating the technical and business value of Abnormal AI’s platform, and orchestrating successful migrations. The ideal candidate will have deep expertise in email security, a hunter mindset, a proven ability to carry a quota in an outcome-based environment, and the instincts to earn trusted advisor status with large enterprise customers.

**What you will do**

- Drive strategy and prioritization of accounts with legacy Secure Email Gateways (SEGs)

- Generate interest in SEG displacement and drive discovery of priority opportunities, ensuring a strong displacement pipeline of well-qualified accounts

- Lead architecture-level conversations and confidently address common SEG displacement objections with technical depth and credibility

- Translate the SEG capabilities customers rely on — DLP, URL isolation, DMARC services, SMTP relay, sandboxing — into Abnormal-equivalent workflows or partner solutions

- Build customer-specific business cases that quantify risk reduction, cost savings, and operational consolidation

- Support advanced testing and proof of concept requirements via “passthrough testing” and similar workflows to overcome SEG displacement objections

- Collaborate on product direction and strategy efforts with Product Management and Engineering

**Must Haves**

- 5+ years of Sales Engineering, Solutions Architecture, or technical experience with significant depth in email or messaging security

- Deep understanding of email systems: Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and Exchange on-premises

- Strong knowledge of Secure Email Gateways (Barracuda, Mimecast, Proofpoint) and Integrated Cloud Email Security (ICES) solutions and their various deployment methods

- Expertise in email and email security protocols such as SMTP, TLS, DMARC, Encryption, Spam Scanning, Sandboxing, and Payload Analysis

- Demonstrated experience owning SEG replacement projects or equivalent migrations to Integrated Cloud Email Security (ICES) solutions

- Knowledge of regulatory and compliance requirements (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA) relevant to email security

- Hunter mindset and strong sense of ownership; comfortable carrying a quota tied to a specific customer outcome rather than activity metrics

- Strong discovery skills that uncover risk, redundancy, and renewal pressure — not just stated requirements

- Proven ability to earn “trusted advisor” status with large and strategic customers

- Strong technical presentation and communication skills, both verbal and written

- Track record of carrying a quota or operating in an outcome-based performance model

- Ability to work independently in a fast-paced, high-impact enterprise environment

**Nice to Have**

- Certifications: Microsoft Certified: Messaging Administrator, Google Workspace Administrator, CISSP, GIAC, or similar

- Familiarity with advanced email security technologies such as Machine Learning and Behavioral Analysis

- Experience working alongside Customer Success teams in a post-sales capacity

- Experience collaborating with Product Management and Engineering on product roadmap and strategy
