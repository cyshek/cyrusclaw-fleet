# **Associate Product Manager – Sell-Side**

We are seeking an Associate Product Manager to support the Sell-Side product suite. This role partners with the Head of Sell-Side Product to help execute the roadmap and coordinate delivery across Engineering, Client Solutions, and Support.

You will contribute to feature delivery, documentation, and client go-lives by ensuring requirements are clear, releases are organized, and delivered functionality works as expected. This is a junior product role suited for someone with strong organizational skills and growing technical fluency in a capital markets or SaaS environment.

# **Responsibilities**

Product Delivery & Execution

- Support execution of the Sell-Side product roadmap.

- Assist with requirement definition, backlog grooming, sprint coordination, and release tracking.

- Validate new features by testing API workflows and confirming expected system behavior.

- Coordinate development and production environment readiness, including configuration alignment, documentation updates, and structured handover for releases.

- Coordinate cross-functional communication to keep projects on track.

Client Coordination

- Support client onboarding and go-lives in partnership with Onboarding and Solutions teams.

- Participate in client demos, training sessions, and feedback collection.

- Help investigate client issues and coordinate follow-ups with Engineering.

Documentation & Reporting

- Maintain product documentation, release notes, and feature guides.

- Track delivery milestones and assist with product reporting and usage insights.

# **Qualifications**

- 2–3 years of experience in Product Management, Project Management, Business Analysis, or a related role (preferably in fintech, capital markets, or SaaS).

- Strong organizational and communication skills.

- Ability to manage multiple workstreams and follow structured processes.

- Comfortable testing APIs (e.g., Postman) and reviewing API responses.

- Familiarity with configuration files (YAML/JSON) and basic technical troubleshooting.

- Detail-oriented, proactive, and collaborative.

# **Preferred**

- Exposure to trading systems or financial technology.

- Basic familiarity with Git and reviewing configuration changes.

- Experience supporting B2B product releases or client implementations.
