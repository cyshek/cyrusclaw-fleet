## [Optional] What is one feature you would be excited to add or improve?

I would want to build a structured red-team feedback loop directly into the post-training pipeline. Right now at most organizations, adversarial testing and red-teaming happen as a gate near deployment, which means safety findings arrive too late to influence data collection or fine-tuning decisions without costly reruns. I would be excited to design a lightweight but rigorous workflow where red-team outputs, categorized by failure mode and severity, feed back into annotation queues and training data specifications on a rolling basis rather than a one-time pre-launch sprint.

The operational challenge is real: you need shared ontologies between safety engineers, annotators, and post-training researchers so that a red-team finding translates cleanly into a data generation task. That is exactly the kind of process development and cross-functional alignment work I find most interesting, and it would have a compounding effect on model safety and quality over time rather than just patching issues release by release.
