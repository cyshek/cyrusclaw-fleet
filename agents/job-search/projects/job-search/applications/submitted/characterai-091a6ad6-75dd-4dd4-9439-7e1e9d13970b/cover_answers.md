## [Optional] What is one feature you would be excited to add or improve?

I would want to build a structured model behavior specification and feedback loop that closes the gap between what safety and post-training teams intend a character to do and what users actually experience in production. Right now, the path from red-team finding to annotation guideline update to retrained model to validated deployment likely has significant latency and limited visibility at each handoff. I would design a lightweight ontology, grounded in the safety and UXR signals the team already collects, that lets any team member tag a production behavior observation against a named specification, route it to the right owner, and track its resolution through the training cycle. The goal is not more process but a shared vocabulary that makes it faster to catch when a model drifts from intent and faster to close that drift. At Microsoft I built something adjacent for infrastructure risk escalation, and the biggest lever was giving cross-functional teams a common language for what a failure mode looks like before it becomes a blocker, the same principle applies here at the model behavior layer.
