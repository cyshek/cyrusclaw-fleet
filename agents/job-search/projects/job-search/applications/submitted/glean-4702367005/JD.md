**About the Role:**

 

Glean is seeking a highly strategic and execution-oriented Product Manager to help define and build our Growth product area. This role sits at the intersection of product-led growth (PLG), platform-level activation, off-surface engagement, and broader product growth strategy.

 

You will own how Glean turns first-touch curiosity into durable, expanding usage — designing the loops, surfaces, and experiences that drive activation, retention, and organic expansion across users and teams. Glean's company strategy emphasizes becoming the trusted enterprise context and intelligence layer that every employee reaches for daily; getting there depends on a world-class growth motion that brings users in, gets them to value fast, and keeps them coming back across every surface they work in.

 

You will work closely with the Product lead and cross-functional partners across engineering, design, data, marketing, and go-to-market teams to identify the highest-leverage growth bets, bring structure to an ambiguous opportunity space, and translate growth concepts into concrete, measurable product experiences. We're currently a very lean product team, so you'll have an outsized role in shaping both the product roadmap and the product team itself.

This role is ideal for someone with 5+ years of experience, a deep understanding of PLG and growth mechanics, a passion for AI, and the ability to demonstrate granular product implementation skills such as designing specific user interfaces, onboarding flows, and experiments rather than staying at a high conceptual level.

 

**You will:**

- Help define and drive the product strategy and roadmap for Growth, translating broad company vision into specific bets across activation, retention, and expansion, informed by funnel data, experimentation, and customer behavior.

- Own the PLG motion end to end — from acquisition and onboarding through activation, habit formation, and self-serve expansion — and design the loops that turn individual adopters into team- and org-wide usage.

- Build platform-level growth capabilities such as referrals, invitations, notifications, in-product education, and lifecycle messaging that compound across surfaces rather than living in a single feature.

- Drive off-surface growth by meeting users where they already work — the browser extension, email, Slack and Teams, mobile, and other entry points — so Glean stays present and valuable outside of a single destination app.

- Partner deeply with engineering, design, and data teams to turn ambiguous concepts into well-scoped products and experiments, with clear user flows, interaction patterns, instrumentation, quality standards, and measurable outcomes.

- Build a rigorous experimentation and measurement culture, defining the activation and retention metrics that matter, instrumenting the funnel, and running A/B tests to learn fast and ship what moves the numbers.

- Drive customer-focused prioritization and efficient execution, balancing long-term platform opportunities with urgent user pain points, strategic customer asks, and company priorities.

- Collaborate closely with go-to-market, marketing, solutions, and support teams to learn from real customer problems and ensure our growth strategy reflects how users actually adopt and spread Glean.

- Bring a high bar for product craft by not only identifying the right opportunities, but also specifying how they should work in practice, including concrete onboarding flows, UI behavior, and adoption paths.

**About you:**

- You have 5+ years of experience in product management, with a track record of owning and shipping growth, activation, or PLG product areas in B2B SaaS, consumer, AI, or platform products.

- You are deeply fluent in growth mechanics — funnels, activation, retention, virality, and lifecycle — and you let data and experimentation guide your decisions, with the analytical fluency to define metrics, instrument funnels, and interpret results alongside engineering and data teams.

- You have strong product sense and granular product thinking, comfortable moving between strategy and detailed execution and specifying how a user should experience onboarding and adoption.

- You have a deep interest in AI and enterprise software, and you thrive in ambiguous spaces, creating clarity, momentum, and alignment even when the product area is still emerging.

- You are an excellent communicator, take ownership and initiative, collaborate effectively with cross-functional partners, and bring a learning and growth mindset.

- You are mission-first, measure your success by your product and team's success, and are an early adopter of AI for your own craft who's excited to help customers accelerate their AI adoption journey.
