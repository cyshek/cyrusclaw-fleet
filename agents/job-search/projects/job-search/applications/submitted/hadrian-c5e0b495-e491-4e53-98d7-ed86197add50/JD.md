**Hadrian - Manufacturing the Future**

Hadrian is building autonomous factories that help aerospace and defense companies manufacture rockets, satellites, jets, and ships up to 10x faster and up to 2x cheaper. By combining advanced software, robotics, and full-stack manufacturing, we are reinventing how America produces its most critical parts.

We’re accelerating our mission with the launch of Factory 3 in Mesa, Arizona, a 290,000-square-foot facility creating 350 new jobs. We are expanding rapidly to support thousands of future hires, launching Hadrian Maritime to expand into naval production, and introducing a Factory-as-a-Service model that delivers complete systems instead of individual parts.

Hadrian is backed by leading investors including T. Rowe Price, Lux Capital, Founders Fund, and Andreessen Horowitz, our fast-growing team is united around reindustrializing American manufacturing for the 21st century and beyond.

### **What you’ll do:**

- Define and own product strategy and requirements from discovery and requirements definition to launch, adoption, and iteration.

- Provide cross-functional leadership and communication to drive product success and accomplishment of business objectives, not only for current product lines but across the portfolio as we continue to expand.

- Work closely with and source requirements directly from the users you’re designing for, including: Technicians, CAD programmers, Manufacturing engineers, Operations leadership, etc.

- Translate customer workflows into clear software and hardware requirements with a seamless user experience.

- Synthesize and collaborate with core stakeholders to build the product roadmap ensuring business objectives are achieved, stakeholders are informed, and efforts are prioritized appropriately.

- Maintain a complete system approach to problem solving ensuring all interdependencies, future expansion, and change management are accounted for.

- Actively contribute to the product management culture and foundation of product management at Hadrian.

### **What we are looking for**

- 5+ years of product management experience, including at least 3+ years of experience in hardware, software and/or robotics.

- Strong track record of delivering end-to-end success across complex hardware and software systems.

- Ability to assess the essence of the problem, provide a structured approach to finding solutions, and organization to align the team.

- Proven ability to think critically about tactical challenges while also maintaining a clear long term strategic perspective.

- Ease in aligning and prioritizing cross-functional teams, including engineering, operations, and supply chain.

- Experience synthesizing a variety of inputs into a product roadmap that ultimately achieves business objectives.

- Enthusiasm towards the mission of rebuilding the American industrial base.

- Organization and ability to build processes and align diverse groups of stakeholders.

- Strong data driven approach with experience leveraging metrics, dashboards, and analytics to inform decisions.

- Familiarity with CNC Machining, additive manufacturing, MES/ERP systems, or factory automation preferred but not required.
