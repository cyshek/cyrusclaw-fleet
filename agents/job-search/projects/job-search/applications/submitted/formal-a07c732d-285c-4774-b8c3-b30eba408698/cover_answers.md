It ended up sustaining 45+ annual resilience drills and driving over $14M in business impact, while cutting operational toil by about 30%.

The part I'm most proud of is the rack-level drill program I pioneered inside that effort. I designed and shipped it in 4 months, hit a 94% recovery rate, and it surfaced critical hardware defects that nobody had caught before. That changed how the org thought about continuous validation, moving it from a reactive exercise to a proactive one. I also built an internal AI agent to restructure the planning workflows, which cut planning cycle time by 39% and increased drill capacity by 21%.

What made it satisfying was that it wasn't just a technical artifact.
