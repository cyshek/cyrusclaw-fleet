**THE ROLE**

Drive the future of global operations as the CTO Technical Project Manager leading our Supply and Demand Planning digital transformation. In this critical, high-visibility role, you will spearhead the integration of SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP) within Project Mercury (our enterprise ERP overhaul), directly shaping our future hardware Configure-to-Order (CTO) capabilities. You will collaborate closely with global supply chain leaders, technical engineers, and business stakeholders to translate complex requirements into scalable planning solutions. Your mission is to build a high-performing planning ecosystem that directly optimizes inventory and scales our global fulfillment strategy.

**WHAT YOU'LL DO**

- **Own Workstream Execution:** Lead the strategy, lifecycle, and deployment of the Supply & Demand Planning and Materials Management workstream, ensuring seamless integration of SAP IBP modules with our broader ERP ecosystem.

- **Drive Cross-Functional Alignment:** Partner with global supply chain operations, technical teams, and business leadership to run architecture workshops and design sessions, translating business goals into robust system capabilities.

- **Collaborate with Key Stakeholders:** Work closely with the Digital Technology Group (DTG), PMO (iOPS), and external systems integrators to ensure all operational planning requirements are accurately supported and delivered.

- **Optimize Business Impact:** Target KPIs centered on forecasting accuracy, process automation, and inventory optimization by designing high-performing Plan-to-Produce and Order-to-Fulfill workflows.

- **Architect Scalable Solutions:** Design planning infrastructure that will explicitly support complex, future-state Configure-to-Order (CTO) business flows, ensuring tight integration across materials management, order promising, and global fulfillment.

**WHAT YOU BRING**

- **Advanced SAP IBP Expertise:** Deep technical mastery in implementing, optimizing, and integrating SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP) modules, specifically across Demand Planning, Supply Planning, and S&OP/Control Tower environments.

- **Hardware Supply Chain Expertise:** Thorough operational understanding of hardware supply chain mechanics, including materials management, inventory optimization, master data alignment, and the intricacies of Configure-to-Order (CTO) business models.

- **Systems Integration Architecture:** Strong capability integrating SAP IBP with enterprise ERP systems (such as S/4HANA or ECC) to orchestrate complex order management and fulfillment processes.

- **Transformation & Project Leadership:** Exceptional stakeholder management and communication skills, with a proven ability to lead global, multidisciplinary teams through ambiguous, large-scale enterprise system transformations.
