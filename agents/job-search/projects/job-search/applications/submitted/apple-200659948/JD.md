## Description

We're looking for a Program Manager with strong operational, analytical, and communication skills. The ideal candidate is a self-starter with significant experience in discovery technology and program development. This role sits within Apple's Discovery team and is an opportunity to be part of something being built from the ground up. As a Program Manager supporting a team focused on Discovery analytics and review, you'll help shape the foundation of a new program by establishing best practices, driving process improvements, and exploring how to leverage AI to transform how the organization approaches analytics and review work. This is a role for someone who's energized by building, curious about emerging technology, and ready to make a lasting impact.
