**Why Rogo**

At Rogo, we are building Wall Street's first true AI banker. Our mission is to empower finance professionals at the world’s top investment banks, private equity funds, and investment firms with AI that delivers unparalleled speed, accuracy, and insight. We are not just improving financial workflows; we are redefining them from the ground up.

This is a unique opportunity to join a generational company at a key inflection point. With a rapidly growing, global client base, proven product-market fit, and backing from world-class investors, we are scaling quickly and defining a new category of enterprise AI.

Our team is sharp, motivated, and deeply committed to the mission. We operate with intensity, take ownership of complex problems, and stay relentlessly focused on our users. If you thrive in a fast-paced environment, demand excellence, and want to help build the future of finance, we invite you to join us.

We're looking for a product-minded operator with deep finance knowledge, technical instincts, and strong intuition around LLMs. You’ll work across product, engineering, design, and post-sales to build core product features, QA relentlessly, and help shape how the most sophisticated investors in the world interact with AI.

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Own feature development end-to-end—from scoping and spec to QA and go-to-market

- Work with engineers and designers to clearly define what needs to be built and unblock them constantly

- Design, evaluate, and iterate on prompts and system-level behavior for our LLMs

- Build finance workflows from scratch (think: investor memos, models, decks, comp tables, queries, dashboards)

- Continuously prioritize product improvements based on user demand

- Join sales and post-sales calls to help sell, demo, and explain product capabilities

**Qualifications:**

- 1-2 years of experience in finance (e.g. banking, investing, hedge funds)

- 1-2 years previous product or startup experience

- Technical degree (CS, Math, Engineering) or equivalent experience (Read: You can code and think algorithmically)

- Compulsive user of LLM tools and deep intuition for prompting and model behavior

- Exceptionally detail-oriented and organized; nothing slips through the cracks

- Thoughtful and opinionated, but deeply collaborative and open to others’ input

- Brings strong visual and product taste that resonates with the team’s values and instincts

Rogo is headquartered in NYC, and we work in person. This is a high-ownership role at the center of building one of the most ambitious AI companies in the world. If this sounds like you, we’d love to talk.
