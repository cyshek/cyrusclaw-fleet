## Description

As a System Integration EPM, you will work closely with system engineering, electrical, mechanical, algorithm and software teams in VPG and across the company to define and develop highly optimized hardware products. You will be responsible for: 

Driving the cross-functional development of requirements and validation plan at product, system and module level, based on experience targets and technology constraints. Identifying the most critical questions and organizing teams to find efficient ways to get answers. Understanding the link between technical decisions for the system and customer experience and communicating impacts clearly and concisely. Driving resolution to architectural/integration gaps to feed into subsequent engineering builds.
