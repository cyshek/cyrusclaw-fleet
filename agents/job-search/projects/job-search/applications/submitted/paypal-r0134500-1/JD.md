**Job Summary:**

Experience Design Operations exists to cultivate a thriving environment where people can focus on doing their best work by building efficient processes, providing the right tools and resources, and ensuring design has a strategic voice across the organization 

We are seeking a Design Program Manager to optimize our design processes, and workflows, enabling efficient and scalable product development on our Identity & Trust team. No two days are alike here and your responsibilities will range from operational execution to building for the future by implementing strategic improvements.

**Job Description:**

**Essential Responsibilities: **


- Scope: The project & program responsibilities are focused on the delivery of a product or computer-based system from design to release for customers.

- Develop and manage program plans, including timelines, milestones, deliverables, and procurement activities.

- Coordinate activities across multiple projects to ensure alignment with program objectives.

- Monitor program performance and implement corrective actions as needed.

- Communicate program status to stakeholders and executive leadership.

- Manage program budgets and financials.

- Facilitate cross-functional collaboration and resolve conflicts.

- Ensure adherence to engineering standards and best practices.


**Minimum Qualifications:**


- 5+ years relevant experience and a Bachelor’s degree OR Any equivalent combination of education and experience.

- Proven track record of managing complex programs within an engineering environment.


**Additional Responsibilities & Preferred Qualifications:**

- Proven ability to partner across multiple Identity, Trust, Risk, and platform product teams to drive alignment across complex, cross-functional initiatives.
- Experience guiding teams transitioning from waterfall to agile delivery models; able to introduce structure, cadence, and disciplined execution in mixed-method environments.
- Technical fluency in **Identity**, **authentication**, **authorization**, or platform capabilities—and an understanding of how platform priorities shift across consumer and merchant ecosystems (e.g., Oslo, WinXO, etc.).
- Demonstrated ability to synthesize multi-team inputs into **cohesive, multi-quarter design + delivery roadmaps**for large-scale platform programs.
- Strong analytical capability, with the ability to interpret and action complex data sets (e.g., RCP signals, quality metrics, operational KPIs).
- Experience establishing and driving **Design QA processes**, ensuring coverage, tracking, and communication of design defects through to resolution.
