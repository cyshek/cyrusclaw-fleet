At Apple, we focus deeply on our customers’ experience. We're looking for a curious, analytical and collaborative technical Product Manager to build advertising solutions with a deep understanding of Apple's values. Product scope can range from evolving the consumer experience for ads to improving campaign booking, creative management, and optimization features for brand advertisers.

## Description

As a product owner, you'll work across Product, Data Insights, Engineering, Design, Sales and Marketing teams to deliver product specifications that enable near & long-term strategic projects in our brand advertising business.
Responsible for end-to-end campaign management features, including advertiser onboarding, campaign setup, creative assets management, and targeting.
Define product features and communicate requirements to Engineering teams via clearly written requirements, diagrams and workflows.
Work closely with the Data Insights and Engineering teams to enable them to identify the critical path, prioritize and execute upon requirements.
Synthesize insights from quantitative research, customer meetings and sales feedback to inform product strategy and specifications.
Collaborate with partner product managers, Engineering, and Data Insights teams on the relevant feature delivery strategy and plans.

## Minimum Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technical field
3+ years of technical product management experience with at least one entire product development cycle from concept to launch or a Master's Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Economics or related area.
Strong technical and analytical abilities
Experience with or coursework in designing and deploying advertising or consumer products at scale.
Deep understanding on the brand advertising sales process, including video and display ads booking workflows
Outstanding written and verbal communication skills; ability to clearly articulate product vision, product strategy and requirements to engineers and internal business partners.
