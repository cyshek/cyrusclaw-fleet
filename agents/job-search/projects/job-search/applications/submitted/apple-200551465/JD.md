## Summary

Imagine what you could do at Apple! Everyday, new ideas have a way of becoming extraordinary products, services, and customer experiences very quickly. Do you bring passion and dedication to your job? If so, we are looking for individuals like you. The Apple Media Tech (AMT) group is at the center of audio, video, and graphics in Apple’s innovative products, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, AirPods, HomePod, Apple TV and VisionPro. 
AMT’s Audio team provides the audio foundation for various high profile features like Game Audio, Siri, FaceTime, media capture, playback and API’s for third party developers to enrich our platforms. The team is looking for talented engineers who are passionate about building audio features and products for millions of customers and care about overall user experience. You will revolutionize the audio experience for game and future audio technologies.

## Description

Our team is looking for an enthusiastic power and performance engineer to support our audio engineering and quality teams within the Audio & Media Technologies group. As a member of our team, your goal will be to help ship exciting features while ensuring power and performance quality across iOS, watchOS, tvOS, macOS and visionOS platforms. You will be using various internal tools that support measuring power and performance metrics. Additionally you will be interacting with prototype hardware and development boards regularly. This is a hands-on role for someone who enjoys taking initiative and is comfortable working with multi-functional teams. If you see yourself as a dedicated power and performance engineer with good communication skills and passion to succeed in an exciting and fast-paced environment, you will thrive here.

## Minimum Qualifications

Ability to problem solve and prove your own ideas.
Strong coding skills in C/C++, Objective C/Swift, Python
Excellent communication skills in English, both oral and written
Knowledge and experience with common performance benchmarks and workloads
Experience with system power, performance, and memory assessment tools. Familiarity with Instruments in Xcode.
