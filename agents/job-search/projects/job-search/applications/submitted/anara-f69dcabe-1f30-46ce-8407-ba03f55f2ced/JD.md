# Role

As our first and only product manager, you’ll work alongside our founder to drive the evolution of Anara and shape how researchers work with AI. You’ll build novel tools that will transform how scientific discoveries are made for decades to come.

As model intelligence accelerates, the ultimate form factor of agents for scientific research remains unwritten. We’re looking for a product manager who combines deep technical understanding, genuine love for science, and strong product intuition. You should be equally comfortable discussing customer feedback with world-class researchers, debugging user workflows with engineers, and presenting product strategy to the team.

### **Your work might include:**

- Performing discovery through user interviews, session replay, data analysis, etc. and present findings to the team.

- Translating cutting-edge AI advances into practical features for researchers.

- Surfacing trends and insights from customer feedback to the team at large to inform product choices.

- Optimizing existing flows to reduce friction, lower churn and increase conversion.

- Working closely with the engineering team to ensure Anara remains the best way to do scientific research with AI.

### **You’re likely a good fit if:**

- You are quick, scrappy and first and foremost a builder. Product at Anara moves lightning fast. We typically take features from idea to production in days, not weeks or months.

- Have a combined 2+ years in product management and engineering, including at least 1 year as a professional engineer.

- You have world-class design and product taste.

- Have independently built and scaled a product to at least a few thousand users (e.g. a side project or open-source project).

- Have worked in a high-growth, fast-paced environment (ideally another startup) and can drive things forward with relatively little oversight.
