#### **Network Planning Sales Engineer Associate (Summer 2026)**

Associate positions at Astranis typically last for twelve weeks, and are salaried roles designed for new grads who have graduated from a four-year university. 

As an Associate, you will have an amazing opportunity to work on hard problems — we pride ourselves on giving everyone at Astranis a chance to do meaningful work on challenging projects, no matter their seniority. Many past associates have designed and tested hardware/software that is heading to space on our first satellite, and many of them are now full-time employees at Astranis. 

If you are still a college student, please apply to join us as an Intern.

**Role:**

Pre-sales and post-sales network architecture and planning, which at its core involves optimizing high throughput satellite networks by understanding and trading dozens of parameters, including but not limited to:

- Digital payload configuration

- Orbital slot selection and frequency planning

- Antenna system optimization, user coverage area definition

- Gateway location(s) selection

- User terminal selection

- Networking / modem equipment selection and configuration

The role provides a balance of technical and business / customer facing responsibilities, and is high visibility and high impact. This role includes 1:1 mentorship from a senior, seasoned sales engineer, enabling rapid learning and growth!

**Requirements:**

- Strong ability to optimize systems by juggling many variables, considering all options, running a detailed trade study

- Process oriented mindset - constantly seeking more efficient ways of doing things, anticipating features needed from both internal and external tools

- Scripting language experience, preferably Python

- Enjoy a fast paced environment, with just the right amount of chaos ;) 

- Don't meet them all? Not a problem. Please apply even if you do not meet all these criteria.
