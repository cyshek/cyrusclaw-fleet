**Key Responsibilities**

- Develop comprehensive project plans and lead projects from conception and planning through implementation and deployment.
- Contribute to the development of project strategies, processes, and resource plans.
- Manage project schedules and task execution using project management tools, including status reports, tracking charts, checklists, and scheduling software.
- Drive cost, schedule, and performance objectives for equipment and deliverables supporting internal and external customers and global vendors.
- Participate in and support cross-functional design teams composed of internal, external, and matrixed resources to achieve project goals.
- Provide technical input and guidance to team members to support successful project execution.
- Interface with internal stakeholders across engineering, operations, supply chain, and business teams to drive supply chain projects supporting the AGS business unit.
- Identify project risks and issues, and develop mitigation and resolution plans.

**Job Requirements/Qualifications**

- Minimum of 5 years of experience in program management, project management, engineering, or supply chain management.
- Bachelor’s degree required in Engineering, Business, Supply Chain Management, or a related discipline.
- Some engineering/technical background with experience in the semiconductor industry and/or supply chain management is preferred
- Strong program management experience leading cross-functional initiatives in a technical or manufacturing environment.
- Preferred experience with semiconductor components including OEM components, process power systems, build-to-print parts, advanced materials, and mechanical or electrical assemblies.
- Experience working with global suppliers, vendors, and internal stakeholders.
- Strong communication, influence, and negotiation skills in a matrixed organization.
- Demonstrated ability to analyze and interpret large datasets, synthesizing insights and effectively presenting findings to senior executive leadership

**Core Competencies**

- Functional Knowledge: Demonstrates depth or breadth of expertise in a specialized technical or functional discipline.
- Business Expertise: Interprets business challenges and recommends best practices to improve products, processes, or services.
- Leadership: Leads teams or projects with moderate complexity, risk, and resource requirements.
