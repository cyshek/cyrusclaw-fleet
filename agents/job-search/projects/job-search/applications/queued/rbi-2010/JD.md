# Product Manager II, Seamless Ordering, Popeyes

Company: Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen (Restaurant Brands International)
Location: Miami, FL (Corporate)

The Product Manager, Seamless Ordering will drive the strategy and execution of customer experiences within the Popeyes App and Website, focusing on data-driven product development and collaboration with cross-functional teams.

## Responsibilities
- Define the roadmap for Popeyes' first-party digital experiences, balancing operating the product, making foundational improvements, and delivering new and improved features.
- Lead the end-to-end product development lifecycle: ideation, evaluation, requirements gathering, UI/UX collaboration, development, rollout and performance measurement.
- Analyze product metrics, business performance, and A/B test results to inform continuous improvements and report to leadership.
- Partner with engineering, design, marketing and operations to deliver an on-brand omni-channel guest experience.
- Support national promotions and menu initiatives for seamless digital ordering.
- Identify long-term channel opportunities based on research and analysis.

## Required
- 3+ years of product management experience on large scale consumer-facing products delivering best-in-class e-commerce experiences.
- Demonstrated success defining and launching products that enhance user experiences and deliver business impact.
- Experience working with product data and metrics; data-focused, fast decision maker, relentless driver of rapid iteration.
- Strong organizational and project management skills; strong knowledge of agile methodologies.
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences.
