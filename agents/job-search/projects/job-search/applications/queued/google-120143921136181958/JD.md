## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.
- 2 years of experience in program or project management.
- Experience with Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), Software Release Management (SRM), or feature planning and prioritization.

## Preferred qualifications
- 2 years of experience managing cross-functional or cross-team projects.
- Experience managing end-to-end feature planning or prioritization efforts on software releases.
- Experience with Ads domain (e.g., search, display, video, shopping, measurement) or UX.
- Experience with machine learning or data science.
- Excellent verbal and written communication and presentation skills, with the ability to translate complex technical concepts to non-technical audiences.

## About the job
As a Program Manager at Google, you'll lead complex, multi-disciplinary projects from start to finish — working with stakeholders to plan requirements, manage project schedules, identify risks, and communicate clearly with cross-functional partners across the company. Your projects will often span offices, time zones, and hemispheres.

Enterprise Platform (EP) consists of Search Ads 360 (SA360) and Display and Video 360 (DV360) enterprise advertising platforms. DV360 is the preferred demand-side platform (DSP) of major global media ad agencies to unify their display and video advertising data, media, and measurement, to achieve buying efficiencies, advertiser ROI and audience targeting precision across all digital channels. DV360 plays a crucial role in Google's strategy to accelerate the shift of traditional TV ad budgets to digital platforms, offering unified programmatic buying with unique inventory access (incl. YouTube), campaign optimization, and cross-channel measurement.

As a Program Manager, you will partner closely with the engineering and product management teams, as well as relevant cross-functional stakeholder teams, to enable the business to deliver its top priorities.

Google Ads is helping power the open internet — search, display, shopping, travel and video advertising, as well as analytics — creating trusted experiences between people and businesses with useful ads.

## Responsibilities
- Drive development and execution of feature planning and prioritization, and include relevant cross-team and cross-functional stakeholders for unified planning.
- Represent DV360 product and engineering teams across Google and Ads to uncover dependencies and drive shared Objectives and Key Results (OKRs). Stakeholders could include cross-ads partners, legal, privacy, UX, machine resources, sales, engineering productivity, and others.
- Establish the necessary programs and one-off projects for the team to plan and execute in order for the teams to be successful.
- Develop and deliver strategic plans for executive audiences.
- Develop and execute internal operational processes at scale.
