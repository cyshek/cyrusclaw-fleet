# Solutions Architect - Total Rewards
Location: USA - Remote

Role Overview:

We are seeking an experienced Solutions Architect with deep expertise in Compensation and Benefits systems to join our Talent (HR) Technology Engineering team. This role will cover end-to-end solution architecture and delivery for our Total Rewards domain, with a strong focus on integrating and optimizing core platforms, Workday, Beqom benefits and compensation and related HR technologies.

As an individual contributor, you will lead the design and execution of complex business solutions that drive measurable impact on our people operations and talent strategy. You will collaborate closely with business stakeholders, product managers, and technical partners to deliver scalable, efficient, and innovative solutions that support our growth and culture.

Key Responsibilities:

Configure and manage the Workday, Beqom and Total Rewards platforms

Responsible for designing, implementing, and supporting end-to-end processes between 3P platforms and Workday. This role will work closely with HR, Engineering, vendors, and third-party integrators to optimize talent workflows and ensure data integrity across platforms

Partner with stakeholders to define and enhance compensation, benefits, internal mobility, and leave processes

Troubleshoot issues and ensure data integrity, synchronization, and security across platforms

Support the rollout of new features across SaaS platforms 

Create and maintain documentation for system configurations, integration flows, and business processes

Stay current with updates to Workday and HR technologies, recommending improvements as needed

About You:

Extensive experience in HR technology architecture, with a focus on Total Rewards.

Deep technical expertise with Workday Beqom or similar Compensation tools, Workday, and related Benefits platforms and providers; strong understanding of system integration, data flows, and extensibility.

Proven track record of delivering end-to-end business solutions that drive significant operational or strategic impact.

Experience leading cross-functional initiatives and influencing at the organizational level.

Exceptional communication skills, with the ability to articulate complex concepts to technical and non-technical audiences.

Demonstrated commitment to fostering an inclusive, high-performing team culture.

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience in a high-growth, global technology or media company.

Familiarity with modern engineering practices, cloud platforms, and security best practices in HR tech.

Prior experience with agile product development
