Overview

We’re looking for an Associate Product Manager to join the Paramount+ and Pluto TV Content Discovery team within the Paramount Streaming product organization. Reporting to the Lead Product Manager, you will support the execution of high-visibility, high-impact features across the content discovery roadmap for both services. You’ll work closely with product stakeholders as well as partners in engineering, design, data, and content to bring new features to market and contribute to improving how users browse, search, and discover content.

This is an on-site role requiring (5) days in office based in New York City, NY.

Responsibilities

Support the development and execution of content discovery features across Paramount+ and Pluto TV
Conduct competitive analysis, gather user feedback, and analyze engagement trends to help inform product decisions
Assist in writing clear product requirements, user stories, and success metrics to support development
Monitor feature performance, identify anomalies, and develop a solid understanding of user behavior and journeys across both services
Help coordinate work across Design, Engineering, PMO, and content partner teams to ensure timely delivery of features
Support backlog management, including tracking tasks, bugs, and feature enhancements
Provide clear and timely status updates on feature development and support communication of progress to the broader team

Qualifications

0-1 year of product management experience supporting consumer-facing products, services, or features strongly preferred. Relevant internship experience may be considered in lieu of professional experience. 
Basic understanding of product development and software development lifecycles
Proficient interest in streaming, content discovery, and building engaging consumer experiences
Curiosity around measuring impact and using data to inform decisions
Detail-oriented, with the ability to write clear and structured documentation
Comfortable working in a dynamic, evolving environment
Collaborative team player with strong communication skills

Bonus

Familiar with video streaming experiences in the market
Bachelor's degree in technology, business, data, media or relevant fields

Paramount Streaming, a division within Paramount Global, is the home to the company's direct-to-consumer services spanning free and paid in the form of Pluto TV and Paramount+. Pluto TV is the global leader in free ad-supported TV, delivering more than 1,400 global channels and an extensive library of streaming content, including live and original channels. Paramount+, digital subscription video-on-demand and live streaming service, combines live sports, breaking news, and A Mountain of Entertainment™. Paramount+ features an expansive library of original series, hit shows and popular movies across every genre from world-renowned brands and production studios, including SHOWTIME®.
