# Product Manager — AI Integrity (Mandarin Speaking)

About the Team: We are part of Global Monetization Product & Technology – Business Integrity. Our mission is to protect user trust and platform integrity by governing risks and improving experience across the ads ecosystem—ultimately enabling sustainable conversion efficiency for commercial content on TikTok.

About the Role: We are looking for an experienced, data-driven Senior Product Manager to build and scale AI-powered governance products, with After Conversion Experience (ACE) as the primary business scenario. ACE focuses on risks and experience issues after a user completes an ads conversion (e.g., app install, form submission, purchase) and enters the advertiser's post-conversion journey. This role will lead product strategy and execution to identify, explain, and mitigate user harm and integrity risks beyond the initial click—leveraging ML/LLMs, large-scale signals, and automation.

What You Will Build:
- AI-native ACE governance system spanning: risk detection → decisioning → enforcement → feedback learning
- Risk taxonomy & policy framework that translates real-world ACE harms into scalable, machine-actionable definitions
- Multi-signal risk detection using creative/content, advertiser/website attributes, user feedback, conversion signals, and behavioral patterns
- LLM/AI productization for content understanding, risk summarization and policy violation diagnosis
- Automated enforcement (ads rejection, traffic deboost, account suspension etc.)
- Measurement & evaluation to continuously improve precision/recall

Key Responsibilities:
- Define the vision, strategy, and roadmap for AI-driven ACE governance
- Partner with Engineering, Data Science, and Operations to deliver scalable systems end-to-end
- Establish robust offline + online evaluation and experimentation to drive iteration
- Own key metrics (e.g. Risk leak rate, false positives, user negative feedback rate etc.)
- Drive cross-functional alignment across ads platforms, integrity, legal/compliance (as needed), and global stakeholders

Minimum Qualifications:
- Product management experience in AI applications, Integrity/Trust & Safety, Risk, Anti-fraud, or Ads platforms
- Strong track record delivering complex, cross-functional products from 0→1 and scaling them globally
- Solid understanding of ML/LLM product development (data loops, evaluation, deployment tradeoffs; not required to model/train)
- Fluency in both English and Mandarin required: the role involves frequent communication with global teams, clients and stakeholders in English-speaking regions as well as Chinese-speaking markets.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Demonstrated ability to operate independently and drive significant product initiatives from ideation to launch
- Experience of AI application products and large-scale advertising systems and understanding the lifecycle of an ad from submission to conversion
