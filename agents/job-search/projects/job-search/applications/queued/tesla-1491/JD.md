# Technical Program Manager, Electronic Systems, Displays

What To Expect
As a Technical Program Manager on the Electronic Systems Team, you'll drive hardware development for Tesla's Displays—the dynamic interfaces that power human-machine interaction, bringing real-life AI to vivid life and revolutionizing connections with intelligent systems. Your mission: Deliver program success through precise planning, execution, and communication, while enabling seamless cross-functional collaboration in our fast-paced, mission-driven environment.

If you excel in ambiguous, high-velocity settings and are passionate about global-impact projects, this role fits. You'll sit at the nexus of cutting-edge AI, advanced hardware, and Tesla's mission to build a world of amazing abundance—leading high-visibility programs from concept to mass production with clarity, speed, and accountability for elite engineering teams.

What You'll Do

Ensure engineering program goals, scope, and requirements are clearly defined, documented, and aligned with stakeholders
Build program roadmaps and detailed timelines outlining milestones, critical paths, and deliverables
Lead and synchronize cross-functional teams to ensure timely program delivery through effective coordination, spanning hardware engineering, supply chain, manufacturing, firmware, and test toward unified program objectives
Ensure team efforts align with project goals, optimizing productivity by steering teams away from non-critical activities
Track engineering sample demand and supply, coordinating with supply chain teams to meet program milestones
Proactively identify, track and resolve program risks with structured mitigation and contingency plans to minimize impact on program timelines and deliverables
Maintain open communication with stakeholders, ensuring their expectations are understood and managed
Monitor progress and provide regular updates on program status, including key metrics, wins, challenges, and escalations
Continuously refine project management methodologies and implement process improvements for cross-functional teams to enhance efficiency and program outcomes

What You'll Bring

Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or a related field or equivalent practical experience with demonstrated exceptional abilities
3+ years of experience in product development, ideally with experience shipping products in medium-to-high volume industries 
3+ years of experience in program management within an engineering or technical environment
Proven ability to rapidly grasp complex technical concepts
Strong attention to detail, coupled with excellent documentation, communication, and organizational skills
5+ years of experience in program management within an engineering or technical industry, leading cross-functional teams in the design, development, and delivery of complex technical products preferred
Proven experience in leading system-level product development, overseeing end-to-end product development of complex hardware systems preferred
Hands-on design engineering experience in display products or electromechanical product design is highly valued preferred
