# Product Manager, Associate - Crypto Trading

Job Description

The Self-Directed Investing product organization within JPMorganChase Wealth Management delivers digital tools that enable clients to invest through a seamless web and mobile experience. The Advanced Investing team builds and enhances trading capabilities across options, fixed income, and digital assets/crypto.

As a Product Manager on the Advanced Investing team, you will help build end-to-end wealth solutions and deliver robust features that enable clients to discover, evaluate, and execute trades. Ideal experience includes product management of trading platforms and have experience building custodian solutions, bringing strong technical fluency and a track record of shipping resilient trading experiences. You will partner closely with design, engineering, data, operations, legal/risk, and compliance teams to deliver high-quality outcomes.

Job Responsibilities

Own and drive the development of trading platform capabilities for digital assets, with emphasis on spot crypto trading 
Write product requirements and oversee build, test, and launch new features across web and mobile 
Lead discovery with cross-functional teams to identify solutions aligned to user needs and business goals 
Serve as an expert on existing features; triage stakeholder/client feedback and drive resolution 
Analyze competitor trends and internal insights to identify opportunities and improve client satisfaction and engagement 
Define, monitor, and analyze product performance metrics to inform prioritization and decision-making 

Required Qualifications, Capabilities, And Skills

2+ years of experience (or equivalent expertise) in product management, engineering, or a relevant domain 
Demonstrated knowledge of the product development lifecycle, including discovery and requirements definition 
Strong data literacy and comfort using metrics to drive decisions 
Technical fluency and ability to partner effectively with engineering teams 

Preferred Qualifications, Capabilities, And Skills

Experience building spot crypto trading platforms, exchanges, or brokerage integrated crypto trading 
Experience with integrating fully disclosed and omnibus account/custody models 
Engineering background or strong technical product experience preferred
