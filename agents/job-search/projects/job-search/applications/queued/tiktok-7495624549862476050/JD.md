# Ethics Office Program Manager

About the Team The Ethics Office was established with the goal of instituting and maintaining high ethical standards and a culture of integrity. The team achieves this goal through 4 main pillars - 1) developing an overarching ethical governance program, 2) implementing and maintaining ethics policies and processes, 3) providing education and awareness to employees via communications and training, and 4) ensuring a highly ethical culture by encouraging employees to speak up and to ensure potential misconduct is properly investigated. Position Summary We are looking for an individual with a Legal, Ethics, or Compliance background to support our employees in North and South America. The new hire will assist with Ethics Office initiatives, including general project management, assessing policy and procedure needs, developing and implementing policies and procedures, reviewing policy queries from employees, reviewing and remediating conflict of interest declarations, and collecting and analyzing ethics and compliance data. Responsibilities 1. Project Leadership: - Lead and oversee ethics project management initiatives, collaborating effectively with key stakeholders. - Manage and enhance the conflict of interest declaration system through technology upgrades. 2. Policy and Procedure Development: - Partner with Employment Legal, HR, Employee Relations, and business teams to draft and enhance policies and procedures. - Proactively identify and mitigate employment risks by utilizing quantitative and qualitative data. 3. Employee Support: - Address policy and procedure queries from employees, providing clear and concise explanations. - Develop relationships with key business teams to offer ethics and compliance assistance. 4. Data Management and Analysis: - Manage, aggregate, and analyze ethics and compliance data points to derive actionable insights. Minimum Qualifications 1. Experience: - Ethics, compliance, or legal roles, preferably within global companies in the Technology, Media, or Entertainment industries. - Proven ability to develop internal frameworks and contribute proactively to building out the Ethics Office functionality in the AMS region. 2. Communication Skills: - Excellent written and verbal communication skills, capable of explaining complex topics in clear, concise, and understandable terms. - Strong communicator and influencer with the ability to work collaboratively across functions and international teams. - Effectively communicates the purpose and value of ethics and compliance policies to employees at all levels, fostering understanding, engagement, and accountability across the organization. 3. Hands-on and Flexible: - Hands-on approach with a bias for action, adaptable to meet the evolving needs of a rapidly growing company. - Collaborates effectively across regions with a strong awareness of local market needs and cultural contexts, particularly in AMS and LATAM. Preferred Qualifications 1. Relationship Management: Experience in relationship management within a cross-functional environment, collaborating across global teams.
