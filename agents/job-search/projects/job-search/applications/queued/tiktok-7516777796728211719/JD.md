# TikTok Shop - Product Manager, 1P App Store Integration

About the team Our Partner Product team is responsible for our Open API Platform and TikTok Shop's App Ecosystem for 1P and 3P partners. This team focuses on products that will empower partners to collaborate with the platform together, providing rich and diverse service capabilities to help merchants and creators to operate more effectively and efficiently. We are looking for a product manager who will be responsible for collection of users’ requirements and partner product design to provide our target users/clients with effective product solutions. Responsibilities - Responsible for the function, architecture design, prototype design, and PRD writing of partner products, including but not limited to the planning and design of Open APIs, Open platforms, and seller integration tools. - By collaborating with DTC platforms (such as Shopify, Woocommerce, SFCC, etc.), implement efficient integration of TTS 1P App with DTC platforms, helping sellers establish connections between DTC and TTS, enabling the interoperability of merchandises, inventory, orders, and fulfillment services between DTC and TTS. - Through data analysis, identify product issues and explore seller requirements, continuously iterate products, enhance the seller experience, and be responsible for the business outcomes of seller use of the products (such as seller onboarding success rate, catalogue assortment coverage rate, fulfillment success rate, etc.). - Ensure the complete product lifecycle management from design to development, launch, and subsequent iterations through collaboration with multiple departments. Minimum Qualifications - A minimum of 2 years' experience as a product manager, involved in the entire process of product initiation, design, development, promotion, and iteration. - Familiar with the product functions and integration design of mainstream DTC platforms (such as Shopify, WooCommerce, SFCC, etc.) and third-party seller tools (such as Rithum, Zendesk, Yotpo, etc.), having engaged in integration product design or seller tool product design for related platforms. - Possesses excellent product experience design capabilities, logical analysis skills, and data analysis skills, with a business perspective. - Excellent communication, collaboration, and project management skills, able to flexibly adjust strategies in complex environments and advance the smooth progress of projects. - Bachelors degree or equivalent years of experience. Preferred Qualifications - Familiar with TikTok and mainstream e-commerce platforms, experience in content-based e-commerce is highly preferred.
