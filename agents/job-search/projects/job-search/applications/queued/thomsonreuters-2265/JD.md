# Product Manager - Thomson Reuters (Frisco, TX) — JREQ199813

Thomson Reuters leads at the intersection of content and technology with trusted data, workflow automation, and AI.

The Product Manager leads the delivery of end-to-end product experiences, harnessing AI and key technologies to create automated, connected experiences across Thomson Reuters' portfolio. Reports into Product Management within the Tax, Accounting & Advisory / Enterprise Technology org.

## About the Role
As a Product Manager, you will:
- Develop strong product intuition by interpreting and assessing customer problems, business needs, and opportunities, offering potential solutions.
- Build world-class product capabilities and lead cross-functional distributed product teams through discovery and delivery.
- Refine the product roadmap with leaders and stakeholders; play a hands-on role in execution.
- Collaborate closely with Product, Compliance, Engineering, TR Labs, and Design teams.
- Interpret and assess customer problems and translate them into product requirements and prioritized backlogs.
- Leverage data and metrics to measure outcomes and inform iteration.

## Qualifications
- Product management experience delivering customer-facing software products.
- Experience leading cross-functional teams through product discovery and delivery (agile).
- Strong analytical skills; data-driven decision making.
- Excellent communication with technical and non-technical stakeholders.
