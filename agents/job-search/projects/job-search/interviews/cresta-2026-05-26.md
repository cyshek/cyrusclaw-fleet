## Likely recruiter questions (drill these)

1. **"Walk me through your background."** — 90-second version. Lead with most recent + most relevant. End with "and that's why both these roles caught my eye."
2. **"Which of the two roles are you more interested in?"** ⭐ critical — have a clear answer. Suggested framing: "I'm genuinely interested in both because [common thread], but if I had to pick, I lean [X] because [specific reason tied to the JD]." Don't be wishy-washy — recruiters respect a take.
3. **"What do you know about Cresta?"** — Stanford AI lab origin, Thrun, Ping Wu/Vertex AI heritage, contact-center AI w/ named customers (Intuit/Cox/Hilton/Carmax).
4. **"Why are you looking?"** — Have a clean, forward-looking answer. NOT a complaint about current role.
5. **"Compensation expectations?"** — Know your number. Defer if possible: "I'd love to learn more about the role and the level first; happy to talk numbers once we both know it's a fit."
6. **"Work auth?"** — US citizen, no sponsorship needed.
7. **"Remote-only or open to hybrid?"** — Both roles are Remote US per JD. Confirm your preference.
8. **"Earliest start date?"** — 2 weeks notice from current role (or sooner).
9. **"Where else are you interviewing?"** — Mention 1–2 credible peers (Anthropic, Scale AI, etc.) without overselling. Signals demand without arrogance.
10. **"Questions for me?"** — see below

## Smart questions to ASK
