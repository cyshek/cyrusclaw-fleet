What Cresta does:
Cresta builds enterprise AI for the contact center. Their platform does three big things: real-time agent assist (whispering suggestions to reps mid-call), AI agents that handle full conversations end-to-end, and conversation intelligence / analytics that surface insights from millions of calls. They train their own models on contact-center-specific data, which is their main moat against generic LLM (Large Language Model) wrappers.

Born from the Stanford AI lab. Chairman Sebastian Thrun (Google X, Waymo, Udacity). CEO Ping Wu co-founded Google CCAI (Contact Center AI) and Vertex AI. Co-founder Tim Shi was early at OpenAI. Backed by a16z, Greylock, Sequoia. Customers include Intuit, Cox, Hilton, and CarMax.

What this role is:
Platform PM (Product Manager) owning Cresta's foundational platform capabilities, starting with IAM (Identity and Access Management) — authentication, authorization, user management, access control, and auditability. Sits at the center of how the enterprise AI platform scales, supports both human workflows and AI agents, and likely expands to other shared platform capabilities over time. They explicitly say day-one IAM expertise isn't required; they want product judgment, technical fluency, and bias to action.
