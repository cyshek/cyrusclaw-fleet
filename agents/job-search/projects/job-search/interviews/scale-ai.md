SCALE AI — Interview Prep
Role: PM (Product Manager), Public Sector GenAI (Generative AI) Test & Evaluation (T&E)
Locations: SF / St. Louis / NYC / DC

What Scale AI does:
Scale AI builds the data and evaluation infrastructure that frontier AI runs on. They started in 2016 doing data labeling for self-driving cars, but today the bulk of the business is providing high-quality training data, RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback), and evaluations to frontier model labs like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Meta — through a global network of vetted experts plus the tooling and ML (Machine Learning) to run that operation at scale.

Their fastest-growing arm is Public Sector — including Donovan, a GenAI platform for defense and intel analysts, and the T&E (Test & Evaluation) tooling that evaluates whether agentic AI is safe and performant enough for mission-critical government use. Customers include the US Army, Air Force, and DoD (Department of Defense) primes.

Founded by Alex Wang. In May 2026, Meta took a ~$14B / ~49% stake and brought Alex over to lead their superintelligence effort; Jason Droege is now interim CEO.

What this role is:
Technical PM owning the T&E stack for Public Sector agentic apps — the infrastructure that lets ML teams measure, improve, and prove agent performance for government use. Cross-org work across Infrastructure, ML, and Public Sector teams; "stalled-to-shipped" pattern explicitly called out.
