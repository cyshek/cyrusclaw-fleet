# Sales Engineer

If you’re looking for a special place to build or grow your career, you’ve found it. Location: Northeast United States (Connecticut preferred)
Travel: Up to 50%
Position Summary
The Sales Engineer serves as a technical expert and trusted advisor to customers, responsible for driving profitable growth through design‑in engineering, technical consultation, and strategic account development. This role combines application engineering expertise with value‑based selling to influence customer decisions and expand market presence.
Essential Duties And Responsibilities
Own and develop a defined sales territory across multiple industries, identifying and converting high‑value growth opportunities
Lead technical discussions with customer engineering and R&D teams to support design‑in of SFC KOENIG solutions
Translate complex technical requirements into clear customer value propositions
Build, manage, and close a robust sales pipeline aligned with business growth objectives
Act as the voice of the customer internally, collaborating closely with Product Development, Manufacturing, Quality, and Operations teams
Maintain strong customer relationships through regular on‑site visits, technical reviews, and solution‑focused engagement
Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in engineering (Mechanical, Industrial, or related discipline preferred)
1–4 years of experience in technical sales, applications engineering, or customer‑facing engineering roles
Strong technical aptitude with the ability to communicate complex concepts to both technical and non‑technical audiences
Experience with precision components, fluid power, or engineered products preferred
Willingness and ability to travel up to 50%
Preferred Characteristics
Customer‑focused, consultative mindset
Strong problem‑solving and analytical skills
Self‑directed with the ability to thrive in a decentralized, entrepreneurial environment
Excellent interpersonal and collaboration skills
Why Join IDEX
Entrepreneurial culture with real ownership and accountability
Opportunities for internal growth and career development across IDEX businesses
Exposure to global customers and advanced engineering applications
Competitive compensation, benefits, and performance‑based incentives
