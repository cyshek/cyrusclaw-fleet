# Founding Solutions Engineer

Founding Sales/Solutions Engineer
AI LegalTech | Enterprise SaaS
We’re partnering with a fast-growing, venture-backed AI LegalTech company hiring a Founding Solutions Engineer to help scale its enterprise commercial function.
This is a high-impact opportunity to join an ambitious team at an early stage, working directly with senior leadership to shape how the company wins and expands complex enterprise accounts.
The Role
You’ll act as the technical and strategic partner across the enterprise sales cycle — leading discovery, delivering tailored product demonstrations, supporting evaluations, and guiding customers through technical and operational requirements.
This is also a foundational hire, meaning you’ll help build the Solutions Engineering function from the ground up, creating scalable demo environments, technical assets, and repeatable processes that support future growth.

Cross-Functional Collaboration
