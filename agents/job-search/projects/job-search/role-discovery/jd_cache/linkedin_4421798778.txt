Job Summary
The Sales Engineer in the NJ plant is responsible for bridging the gap between engineering and sales. They will use their technical knowledge to understand customer needs, develop tailored solutions, and communicate the value of products and services to potential and existing clients. Their role involves both technical expertise and sales acumen, ensuring that solutions meet customer requirements while aligning with the company's business goals. The Sales Engineer will collaborate with their team and demonstrate ENNOVI’s values of accountability, teamwork, and integrity.
Main Responsibilities
•Partner with Sales Director and Site Inside Sales Engineer to manage and achieve product line revenue and margin.
•Partner with the Product Portfolio Management Team and Sales Director to set product line strategy and market growth roadmap direction.
•Identify market applications to grow the product and manage product life cycle.
•Create/establish strong customer relationships to promote product line and revenue growth. This will include at least 50+% domestic travel to customers within the NA region (US & Mexico) as required.
•Develop and propose technical solutions that address customer needs, often involving customized product designs or configurations.
Qualifications
•Engineering Degree or similar Technical Degree.
•Knowledge of electronic componentry products, stamping as a process, and/or electronics-stamped components.
•Have an established network in the electronics industries
•Marketing background with strong sales experience.
•Experience with the Soldering/PCB applications is a plus.
•Demonstrated business acumen.
•Must be comfortable with travel 50% or more of the time.
