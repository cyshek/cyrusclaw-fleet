# Product Management Manager, Luxury Footwear

Ralph Lauren Corporation (NYSE:RL) is a global leader in the design, marketing and distribution of premium lifestyle products in five categories: apparel, accessories, home, fragrances, and hospitality. For more than 50 years, Ralph Lauren's reputation and distinctive image have been consistently developed across an expanding number of products, brands and international markets. The Company's brand names, which include Ralph Lauren, Ralph Lauren Collection, Ralph Lauren Purple Label, Polo Ralph Lauren, Double RL, Lauren Ralph Lauren, Polo Ralph Lauren Children, Chaps, among others, constitute one of the world's most widely recognized families of consumer brands.

The Product Management Manager, Luxury Footwear, is responsible for leading the end-to-end product lifecycle from concept through commercialization and bulk production. This role partners closely with Design, Development, Sourcing, Planning, and regional teams to deliver brand-right, commercially viable footwear collections that meet luxury standards for quality, craftsmanship, and performance.
The ideal candidate combines strategic product leadership with operational excellence—driving calendar discipline, data integrity, and vendor collaboration—while supporting both seasonal execution and long-term business objectives.
,
Lead the end-to-end product lifecycle from concept through commercialization and bulk production, ensuring timely, brand-right, and commercially viable delivery
Act as a key cross-functional partner to Design, Development, Sourcing, Planning, and regional teams, aligning product vision, financial targets, and execution against seasonal and long-term goals
Own seasonal product calendars and milestones, proactively identifying risks and driving resolution to maintain on-time sample and production readiness
Oversee sample and production coordination across proto, SMS, and SRS phases, including sales sample PO creation, factory handoff, and status tracking
Drive operational excellence and data governance within PLM systems, maintaining accurate product frameworks, BOMs, costing, and attributes
Partner with Sourcing and Development to support costing alignment, margin targets, and feasibility without compromising luxury quality standards
Support and coordinate seasonal material development, ensuring material readiness, organized libraries, and cross-functional alignment
Lead commercialization readiness by tracking master approvals, coordinating fit sessions, wear testing, and risk reviews, and ensuring smooth handoff to bulk production
Ensure all product meets brand standards for quality, fit, craftsmanship, and performance prior to production approval
Own accuracy and completeness of product data for e-commerce, digital, and retail channels, including attributes and HTS classifications
Manage key operational processes, including invoice processing and vendor coordination, while fostering strong factory and regional partnerships
Identify opportunities to improve processes, timelines, and systems in support of scalability and long-term product management excellence
