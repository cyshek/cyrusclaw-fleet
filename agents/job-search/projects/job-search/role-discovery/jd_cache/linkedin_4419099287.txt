# Solutions Architect

Equifax is where you can power your possible. If you want to achieve your true potential, chart new paths, develop new skills, collaborate with bright minds, and make a meaningful impact, we want to hear from you.
Equifax has a hybrid work schedule that allows for 3 days onsite (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday) every week.
This role reports to our office in Alpharetta, Georgia.
This position does not offer immigration sponsorship (current or future) including F-1 STEM OPT extension support.
This is a direct-hire role and is not open to C2C or vendors .
What you’ll do
Architect, design and implement smart solutions in GCP/AWS using Cloud Native and AI capabilities.
Establish the high-level Solution Intent for given project or problem statement
Establish responsibilities of major logical components of the solution in collaboration with other team members
Develop architecture deliverables necessary to illustrate system behavior, including usage models, and cost of ownership models and failure analysis model
Ensure that the solution is aligned with the technology strategy, uses standard solution building blocks and can be built and operated in an efficient manner aligned with economic value of the project
Develop, analyze, split, and prioritize work required to implement the system’s non-functional requirements
Provide technical oversight of implementation, keeping the final build and overall implementation aligned with the solution intent
What Experience You Need
BS degree in Computer Science or related technical field involving coding (e.g., physics or mathematics), or equivalent job experience required
3+ years of experience architecting and developing software in the public cloud., including 5+ years overall relevant experience.
Experience in monitoring infrastructure and application uptime and availability to ensure functional and performance objectives.
Experience in one or more languages such as Python, Bash, Java, Go, JavaScript and/or node.js, Gen AI, GitHub Co-pilot code assist
Demonstrable cross-functional knowledge with systems, storage, networking, security and databases
Partner with Engineering, and SREs to analyze any outages or resiliency issues and provide remediation steps
Experience with microservice best practices and design patterns
Experience with event driven architecture and immutable data management patterns (CQRS)
Proficiency with continuous integration and continuous delivery tooling and practices
Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills
Cloud Certification Strongly Preferred
What could set you apart
Application Development/Programming - Experience working with software design, programming languages (preferably Python, Java, Javascript) and solution architecture. Knowledge of Event Driven Architecture, Functional/Object Oriented Programming
Cloud Services (AWS/GCP) - Leveraging cloud native GCP/AWS service while architecting particular product/platform
DevSecOps - Need to have a strong understanding of both security and DevOps principles. They need to be able to design and implement solutions that meet the security and compliance requirements of the organization. Ability to work with developers and operations teams to ensure that the solutions are implemented in a way that does not impact the development or deployment process.
Technical Communication/Presentation - Demonstrates strong written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to tailor to specific audiences. Work with others to achieve results and proactively address sources of conflict and emotion with focus on the best solution for Equifax
Technology Advising/Consulting - Understand business goals and how to use technology to achieve them. Understand the IT environment and the challenges the unit is facing. Develop a technology solution that will help achieve the BU goals. Provide ongoing support to ensure that it continues to meet the goals. Monitor the solution for any potential problems and take corrective action as needed.
