# Product Manager

Description
At Xe, we live currencies. We provide a comprehensive range of currency services and products, including our Currency Converter, Market Analysis, Currency Data API and quick, easy, secure Money Transfers for individuals and businesses. We leverage technology to deliver these services through our website, mobile app and by phone. Last year, we helped over 300 million people access information about the currencies that matter to them and over 225,000 people used us to send money overseas.
Job Title: Product Manager, AI
About The Role
We’re looking for an AI‑Native Product Manager who uses agent‑assisted coding and modern AI development tools to dramatically accelerate how products are built and shipped. In this role, you won’t just write specs — you’ll actively use tools like Claude Code, Codex, and other agent-based coding systems to prototype features, generate production-ready code, and help move ideas from concept to release much faster. You will be responsible for prioritizing and releasing features across our B2B money transfer product line that enabled thousands of businesses around the world to send billions of dollars a year.
What You’ll Do
Own product discovery and delivery for key fintech products and features
Help develop a strategy for our B2B money transfer product line and prioritize your roadmap
Use agent-assisted coding tools (Claude Code, Codex, and similar systems) to rapidly prototype features and product workflows
Build working prototypes and functional implementations to validate ideas and speed up development cycles
Help accelerate the development cycle by moving ideas from concept → prototype → shipped feature faster
Partner closely with engineering to refine AI‑generated implementations into production-ready systems
Identify opportunities where agentic development workflows can increase team velocity
Use AI tools to accelerate product work including research, documentation, analysis, and experimentation
Run rapid product experiments to validate customer value and iterate quickly
What We’re Looking For
2–5+ years of product management experience, ideally in fintech or technology
Strong product sense and ability to turn ambiguous problems into clear solutions
Hands-on experience using AI coding agents such as Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, or similar tools
Comfort using AI agents to write or generate code, prototypes, scripts, or internal tools
Strong technical fluency (APIs, databases, system architecture basics)
Experience collaborating closely with engineers and designers
Strong analytical mindset and data-driven decision making
Experience using data analytics tools like Amplitude or Posthog
Strong design sense with experience in Figma
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
Nice to Have
Experience with fintech products such as payments, banking, or financial data integrations
Prior real-world developer experience (e.g., software engineer, technical founder, or heavy coding background)
Experience building personal projects, side projects, or entrepreneurial ventures
Demonstrated habit of building and shipping things independently using modern development tools
Experience working in fast-moving startup environments
