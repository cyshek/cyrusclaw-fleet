# Product Manager in civic tech | Remote

At Last Call, we partner with government agencies to build digital services that are clear, accessible, and human. Our product managers sit between client vision and engineering delivery, keeping teams unblocked, backlogs healthy, and work moving.
You'll own the delivery side of the product: the client relationship, the work pipeline, and the day-to-day momentum of the team. You'll work closely with engineers, designers, and client stakeholders to make sure what gets built is well-defined, appropriately scoped, and lands as intended.
Requirements
This role is a good fit if you:
Have strong product instincts and can make and defend good decisions with incomplete information
Know how to work with clients to understand what they need and translate that into work teams can execute
Keep delivery moving without turning every problem into a process
Have worked on distributed teams and know how to maintain momentum across time zones and competing priorities

Full benefits: health, dental, vision, life and disability insurance, flexible PTO
Professional development and work from home funds
Work from anywhere in the U.S
