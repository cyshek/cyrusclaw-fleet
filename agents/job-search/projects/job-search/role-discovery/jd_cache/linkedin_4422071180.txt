Position Overview:
We are seeking an experienced GCP Solution Architect to bridge the gap between client business needs and technical delivery for cloud transformation initiatives. This role is responsible for translating business and technical requirements into pragmatic, secure, and scalable Google Cloud solutions that can be confidently handed off to delivery teams.
Solution Architects operate at the intersection of cloud architecture, platform engineering, and delivery planning. They ensure solutions are technically feasible, aligned with enterprise standards, and positioned for rapid execution—without requiring excessive upfront design. This role supports a portfolio of initiatives focused on Google Cloud Platform (GCP), including infrastructure modernization, data platforms, application migration, and DevOps enablement.
Key Responsibilities:
Intake & Requirements Translation
Review PRDs and intake artifacts to assess:
Functional requirements
Data sources and dependencies
Success criteria and value drivers
Identify gaps, ambiguities, and assumptions requiring clarification
Ensure PRDs remain focused on what is needed, not how it is built
Solution Design & Cloud Architecture
Define end-to-end GCP solutions, including:
Target cloud architecture
Workload placement and migration patterns
Core GCP services (e.g., Compute Engine, GKE, BigQuery, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions)
Networking, identity, and security design
Design and implement GCP landing zones aligned with best practices for:
Scalability and resilience
Security and compliance
Governance and cost management
Determine appropriate design depth:
“Just enough” architecture to begin delivery
Iterative refinement as implementation progresses
Decide when initiatives require:
Proofs of Concept (POCs)
Phased migration approaches
Direct progression to build and production
Platform & Feasibility Assessment
Evaluate proposed solutions against:
Platform readiness and maturity
Security, compliance, and regulatory requirements
Operational and support considerations
Recommend viable on‑premises to GCP migration strategies, including rehost, refactor, and re-architect approaches.
Provide input into:
Delivery sequencing and workstream definition
Technical complexity and risk assessment
GCP service selection and architectural tradeoffs
Delivery Enablement & Technical Leadership
Serve as the technical handoff point to delivery teams.
Collaborate with engineering and DevOps leads to:
Clarify architectural intent
Adjust designs based on implementation realities
Resolve technical blockers
Support implementation of:
Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform
CI/CD pipelines for automated build, test, and deployment
Monitoring, logging, and operational best practices
Optimize GCP environments for performance, reliability, and cost efficiency.
Cross-Team Collaboration & Practice Development
Work closely with:
Client leadership and technical teams
Internal delivery, platform, and security teams
Business development and pre-sales teams
Support proposals, technical discovery sessions, and client presentations related to GCP solutions.
Mentor and coach junior consultants on:
GCP technical best practices
Cloud architecture fundamentals
Effective client engagement
Contribute to practice and Center of Excellence initiatives by sharing:
Patterns, accelerators, and reference architectures
