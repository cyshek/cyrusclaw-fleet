Role Summary
We are seeking a Technical Project Manager (TPM) with deep, hands-on ERP expertise to lead our most strategic customer implementations. In this role, you are not just a project scheduler; you are the project leader ensuring Tessera’s multi-agent AI solutions are integrated predictably, safely, and successfully into complex enterprise environments.
You will serve as the critical bridge between Tessera’s cutting-edge AI agents and the intricate reality of enterprise systems across ERP, CRM, Finance, and operational platforms. This role requires strong technical fluency across enterprise architectures, business processes, integrations, and modernization programs.
You must be able to communicate effectively with customer Solution Architects, technical teams, system owners, and business stakeholders, translating operational and technical requirements into actionable plans for Tessera’s AI and engineering teams. Your understanding of enterprise systems modernization will allow you to provide guidance during issue resolution, support architecture discussions, and ensure teams stay aligned on what is required and technically feasible.
The ideal candidate has spent years delivering ERP transformation programs within System Integrator environments or large enterprises and is excited to apply AI to accelerate modernization initiatives at scale.
