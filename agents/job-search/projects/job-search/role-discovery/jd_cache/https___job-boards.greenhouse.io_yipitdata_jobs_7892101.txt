<p><strong>About This Role</strong></p>
<p>We are hiring an Associate Product Manager (APM) to support our Metrics &amp; Feeds initiatives on the Public Investor team. This role sits at the intersection of data, platform infrastructure, and client delivery. You will be responsible for ensuring that YipitData’s data products, specifically metrics tables and client-facing feeds, are accurate, standardized, scalable, and reliably published. Your work will directly enable external dashboards and data delivery that power critical investor workflows, while enabling our data to be more reliably consumed through programmatic and AI-powered surfaces.</p>
<p>This is an ideal role for someone early in their product career who is excited about data systems, platform thinking, and operational rigor, and who wants to drive meaningful impact across a highly cross-functional organization.</p>
<p><strong>In this role, you’ll gain experience</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Owning metrics publishing quality and standards</strong> across data teams, ensuring datasets reliably power dashboards and client feeds</li>
<li><strong>Partnering cross-functionally</strong> with Data, Engineering, and Product to define requirements, prioritize improvements, and drive adoption of best practices</li>
<li><strong>Improving and scaling data platform workflows</strong>, from ingestion through transformation to delivery, enabling internal productivity and client value</li>
<li><strong>Leveraging AI tools</strong> to enhance workflows, automate processes, and improve data quality and efficiency</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You are likely to succeed if you…</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Have 2–4 years of experience in product management, analytics, data, or a related field.</li>
<li>Have strong data fluency, including <strong>SQL</strong> and familiarity with modern data platforms (eg <strong>Databricks</strong>), pipelines, and <strong>PySpark</strong>.</li>
<li>Have experience working cross-functionally with engineering or data teams and/or an understanding of the product development lifecycle (e.g., agile practices)</li>
<li>Enjoy solving ambiguous problems and building structure where none exists</li>
<li>Care deeply about data quality, consistency, and scalability</li>
<li>Are comfortable and have familiarity with AI tools (e.g., LLMs, prompt engineering, workflow automation) as productivity enhancers.</li>
<li>Have strong analytical, communication, and collaboration skills.</li>
<li>Are curious, adaptable, and eager to learn.</li>
</ul>
