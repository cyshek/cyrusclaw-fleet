# Pre-Sales Solutions Engineer

<p>Our platform handles everything that breaks at scale, so teams can ship integrations, not infrastructure. It provides flexible, AI-enabled build experiences through code-native, low-code, and an embedded workflow builder, plus self-service configuration and an embedded marketplace.</p>

<p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Partner with Account Executives through the full sales cycle: discovery, demo, POC, technical close</li>
<li>Become a domain expert on Prismatic's platform, including our Code Native Integration (CNI) TypeScript SDK, embedded marketplace, and integration workflow builder, so you can run a credible conversation with engineering leaders and ICs alike</li>
<li>Lead technical demos and proof-of-concept work that translates Prismatic's capabilities into a prospect's specific integration pain</li>
<li>Scope and run POCs end-to-end: set success criteria, drive customer engagement, prove out the build</li>
<li>Translate complex technical concepts for non-technical executive audiences, and translate business priorities back to engineering teams (yours and theirs)</li>
<li>Own technical content during deals: RFPs, security questionnaires, architecture deep-dives</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, Customer Success, and Support to remove blockers and accelerate deals</li>
<li>Feed customer signal back to Product and Marketing to influence roadmap and positioning</li>
<li>Build and contribute reusable assets (demos, playbooks, technical content) to scale the SE function</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years in a solutions, sales, or pre-sales engineering role at a B2B software company</li>
<li>Hands-on experience with APIs (REST, GraphQL, webhooks), authentication patterns (OAuth, API keys), and at least one programming language. TypeScript or JavaScript a plus; they're our SDK's native tongue.</li>
<li>Proven ability to lead technical conversations with both engineering ICs and executive buyers</li>
<li>Comfortable running discovery, scoping POCs, and managing the technical side of a deal from first conversation through close</li>
<li>Fluent with modern AI tooling (Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT, or equivalent) and curious about how it's reshaping technical sales work</li>
<li>Excellent written and verbal communication. Able to make complex things sound simple.</li>
<li>Self-directed and biased toward action. Comfortable with ambiguity, ownership-oriented, ready to ship.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Bonus Points:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience at a B2B SaaS or developer-tools company</li>
<li>Prior embedded iPaaS or integration platform experience</li>
<li>Prior software engineering experience. Our customers are developers, and they regularly ask SEs for guidance on CI/CD, deployment patterns, observability, and engineering best practices.</li>
<li>Built integrations professionally (not just demoed them). Bonus if you've shipped TypeScript or Node-based integration code.</li>
<li>Built or customized your own AI workflows (custom prompts, agents, plugins, automations)</li>
</ul>
