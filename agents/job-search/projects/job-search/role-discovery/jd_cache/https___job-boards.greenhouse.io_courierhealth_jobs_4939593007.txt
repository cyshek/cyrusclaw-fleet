# Product Manager

<div class="content-intro"><p>Courier Health is on a mission to solve one of the biggest and most meaningful opportunities in healthcare: reinvent how people living with chronic and rare diseases are supported.</p>
<p>We are building the future of patient engagement for life sciences companies. Our software is leveraged by biopharma companies to support patients in their complex journey from diagnosis to initiating and remaining on therapy to achieve optimal health outcomes.</p></div><p>We are looking for talented individuals who are motivated by the opportunity to have an outsized impact on not just their company, but the healthcare industry at large.</p>
<p><strong>What You'll Do</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Own the design, development, and rollout of key features for our core Application team.</li>
<li>Prioritize work based on business value, customer needs and overall company strategy.</li>
<li>Effectively manage and communicate with stakeholders both internal and external regarding roadmap, timelines, requirements and escalations.</li>
<li>Coordinate cross functionally, partnering closely with Engineering, Client Services, and Sales.</li>
<li>Build deep business context, user empathy and product knowledge to effectively research and define impactful product development efforts.</li>
<li>Drive release and sprint planning to ensure customer delivery dates are met.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3-5 years of experience directly relevant to the product management profession.</li>
<li>Experience defining and executing B2B enterprise SaaS products and features, especially in the healthcare domain and/or with CRM products.</li>
<li>Experience with agile methodologies and creative problem solving.</li>
<li>Ability to define measurable success for product development efforts.</li>
</ul>
