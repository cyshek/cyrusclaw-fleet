About Flint
Flint is the agentic platform that ships pages from your brand and ad campaigns. AI doesn't replace marketers. It removes the work that should never have been theirs: building a one-off landing page for every keyword, every campaign change, every A/B test, then waiting on design and engineering to do it. Today we deliver on-brand pages on your own domain in minutes, with no rebuilds and no engineers. We're backed by Accel, Sheryl Sandberg (former Meta COO)'s venture fund SBVP, and Neo.
The visionSandberg instantly understood Flint's vision. "I was sharing how it took (me) five teams three months to build one A/B test just to increase conversion by 10% on our Google ad," Lim said. "And then she stopped me, [and] said, 'Michelle, it was 140 people at Meta who had to do this'." — TechCrunch
Imagine a website that builds new pages, tests creative, and optimizes performance in response to real-world signals: competitive shifts, user behavior, trending search queries, the way an autonomous vehicle responds to road conditions and traffic. The marketer still owns the work that compounds and the work that requires taste: strategy, audience, creative direction, brand judgment. Flint takes the manual production off their plate so they get those hours back.

The role
We're looking for a Solutions Engineer to work directly with customers to help them succeed with Flint. You will work with customers throughout the life cycle, through building sales demos, running point on implementation, assisting them with expanding their usage, and unblocking customers through support. This is a contract-to-hire position.
You will:
Represent Flint in customer Slack channels (joining brainstorms, offering creative guidance, and helping shape their web presence).
Leverage prompt engineering in Flint's platform to craft pixel-perfect, production ready pages on time and on target, supercharging the efficacy of our AI agent.
Assist with design system extraction of customer sites, finetuning the initial work Flint agents have done to derive design systems from customer sites
Triage and redirect complex questions + feature requests to the core engineering team, driving resolution back to the customer.
Drive AI native customer life cycle through running the post-sales CRM tool, Pylon
Review competitor websites, hunt for assets, partner with customers on landing page layouts that will most effectively deliver their messaging with cohesive, high quality content.
Use GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and ChatGPT/Claude to accelerate development and maintain craftsmanship.
Basic Qualifications
You have a background in web development or in a customer-facing engineering role.
You have experience building in React and Tailwind CSS, and are comfortable inspecting web pages and debugging layout and style issues.
You are proficient in TypeScript and familiar with component-based web architectures.
You have held client-facing roles and are experienced collaborating directly with creative teams to build and launch sites.
Ideally, you’ve built websites for at least 3 customers before - and not only built them for a one-time go-live, but maintained and extended them over a significant time period.
Extreme ownership - able to call out risks early, close the loop with customers without exception, provide proactive ETAs for assignments
Success in this role is prompt responsiveness - we care deeply about giving our customers a world class experience.
You have excellent async communication skills (and are confident navigating Slack, especially external Slack channels and automations).
You have an extremely strong eye for design and detail; nothing passes your eagle eye.
You are able to prioritize effectively in dynamic, fast changing environments.
You are comfortable using an AI IDE (e.g. Cursor) as your primary coding tool.
You genuinely enjoy fast-paced, early-stage environments where flexibility, focus, and creativity are key.
Preferred Qualifications
Experience working with Pylon or Zendesk
Experience working with more complex animations on websites
Familiarity with Linear and Slack for project management
Familiarity with AI agent development workflows and tools (e.g. Copilot, Cursor, LangChain, or similar).
Motivated by impact — you take pride in delivering something tangible every day.
