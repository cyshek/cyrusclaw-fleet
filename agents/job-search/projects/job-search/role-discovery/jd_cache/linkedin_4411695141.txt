Job Description
Overview
We are seeking an experienced Solutions Architect – AI to design, lead, and deliver innovative artificial intelligence solutions for our clients. This role blends deep technical expertise with strong consulting skills, enabling you to translate business challenges into scalable, AI-driven architectures. You will work closely with client stakeholders, delivery teams, and leadership to drive successful outcomes across a variety of industries.
Key Responsibilities
Client Engagement & Advisory
Partner with client stakeholders to understand business objectives and identify opportunities to leverage AI/ML solutions
Serve as a trusted advisor, guiding clients on AI strategy, roadmap development, and best practices
Lead workshops, discovery sessions, and solution envisioning engagements
Solution Architecture & Design
Design end-to-end AI/ML architectures, including data pipelines, model development, deployment, and monitoring
Define scalable, secure, and high-performing solutions across cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
Evaluate and recommend tools, frameworks, and platforms for AI development (e.g., LLMs, NLP, computer vision)
AI/ML Delivery Oversight
Provide technical leadership across AI initiatives from concept through production
Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and developers to ensure successful implementation
Establish AgenticOps practices, including CI/CD for models, model versioning, and lifecycle management
Pre-Sales & Business Development
Support sales teams with solutioning, proposals, and client presentations
Develop reusable assets, accelerators, and AI solution offerings
Contribute to thought leadership, whitepapers, and go-to-market strategies
Governance & Best Practices
Ensure responsible AI practices, including ethics, bias mitigation, and compliance
Define architecture standards, design patterns, and governance frameworks
Stay current on emerging AI trends and technologies
Qualifications
Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or related field
10 + years of experience in technology consulting, software architecture, or solution architecture
Hands-on experience designing and delivering AI/ML solutions in production environments
Experience with API design, microservices architecture, and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
Experience working with Frontier AI models, local and open source models
Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and their AI/ML services
Proficiency in multiple programming languages
Experience designing data architectures and working with large-scale data platforms
Preferred Qualifications
Familiarity with tools such as LangChain, vector databases, and prompt engineering techniques
Knowledge of data engineering tools (Spark, Kafka, Databricks, Snowflake)
AI/ML certifications or cloud certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Machine Learning, Azure AI Engineer)
Experience with Legacy Systems and Architectures including SaaS
Key Skills & Competencies
Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
Ability to translate complex technical concepts into business value
Leadership and mentoring capabilities
Adaptability in fast-paced, client-facing environments
What Success Looks Like
Delivering scalable, high-impact AI solutions that drive measurable business outcomes
Building strong client relationships and becoming a trusted advisor
Contributing to the growth of AI capabilities and offerings within the firm
Enabling teams through best practices, innovation, and technical leadership
