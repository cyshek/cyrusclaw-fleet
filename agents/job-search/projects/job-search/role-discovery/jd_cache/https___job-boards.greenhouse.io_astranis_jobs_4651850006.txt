<h4><strong>Network Planning Sales Engineer Associate (Summer 2026)</strong></h4>
Associate positions at Astranis typically last for twelve weeks, and are salaried roles designed for new grads who have graduated from a four-year university.&nbsp;</div>
<p>As an Associate, you will have an amazing opportunity to work on hard problems — we pride ourselves on giving everyone at Astranis a chance to do meaningful work on challenging projects, no matter their seniority. Many past associates have designed and tested hardware/software that is heading to space on our first satellite, and many of them are now full-time employees at Astranis.&nbsp;</p>
<p>If you are still a college student, please apply to join us as an Intern.</p>
<p><strong>Role:</strong></p>
<p>Pre-sales and post-sales network architecture and planning, which at its core involves optimizing high throughput satellite networks by understanding and trading dozens of parameters, including but not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Digital payload configuration</li>
<li>Orbital slot selection and frequency planning</li>
<li>Antenna system optimization, user coverage area definition</li>
<li>Gateway location(s) selection</li>
<li>User terminal selection</li>
<li>Networking / modem equipment selection and configuration</li>
</ul>
<p>The role provides a balance of technical and business / customer facing responsibilities, and is high visibility and high impact. This role includes 1:1 mentorship from a senior, seasoned sales engineer, enabling rapid learning and growth!</p>
<p><strong>Requirements:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Strong ability to optimize systems by juggling many variables, considering all options, running a detailed trade study</li>
<li>Process oriented mindset - constantly seeking more efficient ways of doing things, anticipating features needed from both internal and external tools</li>
<li>Scripting language experience, preferably Python</li>
<li>Enjoy a fast paced environment, with just the right amount of chaos ;)&nbsp;</li>
<li>Don't meet them all? Not a problem. Please apply even if you do not meet all these criteria.</li>
</ul>
