About The Role
We are hiring a Product Manager to lead one of the most critical verticals within our product organization: the end-to-end ownership of all things related to self serve laundry across the different verticals Cents supports. This role owns a broad and highly integrated product surface area spanning device and machine management, firmware-supported hardware integrations, operator business tools, employee workflow applications, customer-facing mobile payments and machine tracking, and mobile tools for route operators servicing multi-location environments such as apartment communities. You will operate at the intersection of software, hardware, and real-world operations — acting as the connective tissue between the core Cents business management suite and the hardware and networked device worlds. This role works closely with engineering (mobile, firmware, backend), hardware teams, leadership stakeholders, distributors, and external operators. This is not a single-product PM role. It is a systems-level role responsible for defining the roadmap and long-term strategy for the self-service experience across multiple user personas: route operators, business owners, employees, and end customers. The work you drive will directly impact device adoption, transaction volume, operator efficiency, and overall scale of the self-service business. If you thrive in technically complex environments, can navigate multiple stakeholder groups, and enjoy building products that connect hardware and software in real-world environments, this role is for you.
We are open to remote work, but employees must work EST hours.
