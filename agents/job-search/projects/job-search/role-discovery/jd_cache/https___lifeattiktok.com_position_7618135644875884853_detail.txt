Responsibilities
About the team:
The Scenario-Led Growth team focuses on unlocking step-change growth opportunities through high-impact commerce scenarios such as Live and Auction. We combine deep user segmentation, content understanding, and cross-functional execution to drive breakthrough improvements in new user acquisition (DAC) and mid/long-term value growth (MAC).
Responsibilities:
- Define and execute growth strategies for key commerce scenarios (e.g., Live, Auction) to drive step-change improvements in DAC and MAC.
- Leverage user segmentation and behavioral insights to identify high-potential growth opportunities within specific scenarios.
- Partner closely with cross-functional teams including Content, Creator, Ops, Marketing, and Algorithm to deliver integrated growth solutions.
- Design and scale scenario-specific growth mechanisms, including traffic allocation, incentives, and content-product integration.
- Drive experimentation strategy to measure incremental impact on acquisition, engagement, and conversion metrics.
- Identify structural gaps in current user journeys and build product solutions to unlock scenario-based growth.
- Translate insights into repeatable frameworks that can scale across categories and markets.
Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s degree or above, experience in product management, growth, or strategy roles in consumer tech or e-commerce.
- Strong problem-solving skills with ability to operate in ambiguous, fast-moving environments.
- Experience working cross-functionally to deliver complex initiatives.
- Data-driven mindset with ability to evaluate performance and iterate quickly.
- Experience in content-commerce, live streaming, or auction-based platforms.
- Familiarity with user segmentation and growth frameworks (DAC, MAC, lifecycle).
- Exposure to experimentation and performance-driven product optimization.
Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience in e-commerce is a plus.

About TikTok
TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. At TikTok, our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy. TikTok's global headquarters are in Los Angeles and Singapore, and we also have offices in New York City, London, Dublin, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Jakarta, Seoul, and Tokyo.​
Why Join Us
Inspiring creativity is at the core of TikTok's mission. Our innovative product is built to help people authentically express themselves, discover and connect – and our global, diverse teams make that possible. Together, we create value for our communities, inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we work towards every day.​
We strive to do great things with great people. We lead with curiosity, humility, and a desire to make impact in a rapidly growing tech company.
