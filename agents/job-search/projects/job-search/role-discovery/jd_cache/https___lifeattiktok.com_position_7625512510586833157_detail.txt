About TikTok
TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. At TikTok, our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy.
TikTok's global headquarters are in Los Angeles and Singapore, and we also have offices in New York City, London, Dublin, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Jakarta, Seoul, and Tokyo.
Why Join Us
Inspiring creativity is at the core of TikTok's mission. Our innovative product is built to help people authentically express themselves, discover and connect – and our global, diverse teams make that possible.
Together, we create value for our communities, inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we work towards every day.
We strive to do great things with great people. We lead with curiosity, humility, and a desire to make impact in a rapidly growing tech company.
Every challenge is an opportunity to learn and innovate as one team. We're resilient and embrace challenges as they come.
By constantly iterating and fostering an "Always Day 1" mindset, we achieve meaningful breakthroughs for ourselves, our company, and our users.
When we create and grow together, the possibilities are limitless.
Join us.
About the Team
Our team plays a crucial role in ensuring the company’s success. We seek people who are willing to learn and put in the effort to solve problems. Our challenges are not your regular day-to-day problems - you’ll be part of a team that’s developing new solutions to new challenges. It’s working fast, at scale, and we’re making a difference. We are looking for talents to join us on this exciting journey!
Responsibilities
Analyze, and coordinate marketing performance-related activities for Creators and Partners on the company’s e-commerce platform.
Research with and visit Creators and Partners to gather feedback and identify requirements and objectives.
Summarize best cases study, and present findings to senior management.
Assist in developing actionable methodologies and strategies for the e-commerce ecosystem of Creators & Partners.
Monitor and track status on the implementation of marketing initiatives for Creators & Partners.
Lead marketing training programs for Creators & Partners to enhance their integrated marketing capabilities.
Effectively manage project and stakeholder meetings, drive discussions to conclusions, and keep programs on track.
Qualifications
Qualifications
Must have a Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent degree in Computer Science, Engineering (any), Information Systems, Management, Business Administration, Marketing or a related field, and 2 years of related work experience.
Of the required experience, must have 1 year of experience in each of the following:
Project management, including collaborating with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and implement data-driven business strategies;
Managing key stakeholder relationships and gathering feedback to build programs aimed at driving customer revenue, growth, and retention;
Managing project or program risk and issues related to meeting deadlines, project schedule, resourcing, or budget by identifying, communicating, and triaging resolution;
Translating data using SQL, Python, Tableau, and/or Excel; and
Planning, developing, and executing projects in Waterfall or Agile methodologies.
Travel Requirement: Domestic travel required up to 10%.
Type: Full time, 40 hours/week
Location: New York, NY
Salary Range: $93600 - $152000 per year
