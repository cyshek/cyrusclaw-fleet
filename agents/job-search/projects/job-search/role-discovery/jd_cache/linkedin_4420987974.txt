# Product Manager - 397481

Client Overview
Our client is a global leader in animal health solutions, dedicated to advancing livestock wellness and productivity. The company is recognized for its innovative approach and commitment to sustainable practices. Employees thrive in a collaborative, growth-focused environment. They are now looking for a Product Manager.
Salary/Hourly Rate
$40/hr
Position Overview
The Product Manager will drive product strategy and lifecycle management for animal health solutions. This role collaborates with cross-functional teams to launch new products, optimize existing offerings, and ensure compliance with regulatory and market requirements.
Responsibilities Of The Product Manager
Develop and execute product strategies for animal health solutions.
Conduct market research and competitive analysis.
Manage product lifecycle from ideation to launch and post-launch optimization.
Collaborate with R&D, sales, and marketing teams.
Monitor product performance and customer feedback.
Ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards.
Prepare and present product updates to stakeholders.
Identify opportunities for innovation and improvement.
Required Experience/Skills for the Product Manager:
Strong project management and organizational skills.
Excellent communication and stakeholder engagement abilities.
Analytical mindset for market and product data.
Ability to work collaboratively across departments.
Knowledge of animal health or a related industry is required.
Preferred Experience/Skills for the Product Manager:
Experience in the animal health or life sciences industry.
Exposure to product management in a regulated environment.
Familiarity with product launch and commercialization processes.
Education Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in Animal Science, Life Sciences, Business, or a related field.
Benefits:
Atrium Care Package available upon eligibility, including healthcare plans, discount programs, and paid time off.
