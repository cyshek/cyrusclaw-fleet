Responsibilities
About the Team
We are seeking an experienced candidate to play a key role on TikTok's PMO team, managing and strategizing critical business projects across the company. We are looking for a proactive self-starter with a strong project management background and the ability to coordinate diverse stakeholders across functions and time zones. The ideal candidate will bring humility, curiosity, excellent communication skills, and strong relationship-building abilities while contributing to strategic decision-making.
Responsibilities
- Champion multiple complex projects simultaneously, driving alignment among PMOs and business leaders to ensure cohesive goals, plans, and deliverables across global teams.
- Collaborate on project scope definition and performance criteria to align deliverables with business strategy.
- Own end-to-end project timelines, maintain a master project calendar, manage work-back schedules, document meetings, and track key project artifacts.
- Anticipate and proactively address potential blockers, risks, and dependencies before they impact delivery.
- Prioritize workloads strategically and escalate issues to leadership in a timely manner.
- Build and nurture relationships across the organization, serving as the primary point of contact for internal teams and select external stakeholders.
Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.
- Extensive experience in project management or program management roles within fast-paced organizations.
- Proven ability to lead multiple complex, cross-functional projects simultaneously from initiation through delivery.
- Strong organizational and time management skills with a high attention to detail.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Demonstrated ability to manage and align multiple stakeholders toward shared outcomes.
Preferred Qualifications
- MBA or similar advanced degree.
- Experience in large, complex global organizations or technology companies.
- Proficiency with project management tools and methodologies (e.g., Agile, PMP, or similar frameworks).
- International or multi-regional project management experience.
- Experience supporting executive-level decision-making with data-driven insights and strategic recommendations.

The Company reserves the right to modify or change these benefits programs at any time, with or without notice.​

About TikTok
TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. At TikTok, our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy. TikTok's global headquarters are in Los Angeles and Singapore, and we also have offices in New York City, London, Dublin, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Jakarta, Seoul, and Tokyo.​
Why Join Us
Inspiring creativity is at the core of TikTok's mission. Our innovative product is built to help people authentically express themselves, discover and connect – and our global, diverse teams make that possible. Together, we create value for our communities, inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we work towards every day.​
We strive to do great things with great people. We lead with curiosity, humility, and a desire to make impact in a rapidly growing tech company. Every challenge is an opportunity to learn and innovate as one team. We're resilient and embrace challenges as they come. By constantly iterating and fostering an "Always Day 1" mindset, we achieve meaningful breakthroughs for ourselves, our company, and our users. When we create and grow together, the possibilities are limitless. Join us.​
