# Technical Program Manager (TPM) – Manufacturing & NPI

About the Business
Tata Electronics Private Limited (TEPL) is a greenfield venture of the Tata Group, a global enterprise operating in over 100 countries across six continents. Our mission is simple yet powerful: To improve the quality of life of the communities we serve globally, through long-term stakeholder value creation based on leadership with trust.
At Tata Electronics, we are redefining the EMS (Electronics Manufacturing Services) landscape by delivering innovative consumer electronics products, services, and solutions. If you’re passionate about customer success, operational excellence, and driving strategic growth, join us to help shape the future of electronics manufacturing.
The Senior / Staff / Principal Technical Program Manager (TPM) is a technical and operational leader responsible for delivering complex, high‑volume consumer electronics programs from concept through mass production. This role is the primary bridge between customers and India‑based manufacturing and engineering teams, accountable for execution excellence across SMT, System/FATP, NPI, and global production ramps.
This position operates with high autonomy, drives cross‑organizational alignment, mentors TPMs and engineers, and influences factory and product strategy. The role requires travel to India 3–4 times per year and approximately 15% overall domestic and international travel.
Key Responsibilities
Program & Customer Leadership
Serve as the single‑threaded owner for end‑to‑end program execution
Lead executive‑level customer communications, escalations, and trade‑off decisions
Own program plans, critical paths, dependency management, and risk/MIL governance
Drive program alignment across engineering, operations, quality, supply chain, and test
Manufacturing & NPI Execution (India‑Based)
Lead SMT and System/FATP NPI builds and production ramps in India
Ensure factory readiness across BOMs, material flow, capacity, labor, tooling, fixtures, and testing
Drive resolution of complex build, yield, capacity, and schedule challenges
Partner with factory leadership on capacity expansion and long‑term scaling plans
Process, Quality & Reliability Ownership
Drive DFM/DFA strategy, manufacturability trade‑offs, and design readiness reviews
Own yield, OEE, scrap, and defect reduction strategies across builds
Lead FAI validation, RCA execution, and systemic corrective action closure
Own REL testing scope, execution alignment, and results communication
Execution Cadence & Operational Excellence
Establish and lead BOD/EOD, build reviews, and executive operating rhythms
Ensure readiness for packaging, OQC, customer shipment, and ramp transitions
Champion operational rigor, documentation, and continuous improvement across programs
Essential Attributes
Own multiple concurrent programs or a flagship, high‑complexity product line
Operate as a trusted customer counterpart and internal executive escalation owner
Set execution standards, operating cadence, and best practices across factories
Mentor and guide junior TPMs and cross‑functional leaders
Influence manufacturing strategy, factory readiness models, and ramp frameworks
Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or equivalent technical field
10+ years of experience launching complex, high‑volume consumer electronics products
Proven ownership of SMT, FATP, NPI, and global production ramps
Demonstrated success working with manufacturing and engineering teams
Strong background in manufacturing systems, quality, and factory execution
Exceptional leadership, communication, and decision‑making skills under ambiguity
Preferred Qualifications
Hindi language capability
Experience influencing factory or product strategy at scale
PMP, Six Sigma, or equivalent program/process certification
Prior experience mentoring TPMs or leading TPM communities of practice
