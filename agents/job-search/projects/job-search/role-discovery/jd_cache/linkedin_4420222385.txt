# Technical Program Manager, AI Hardware & Autopilot

What To Expect
Program Managers within the Autopilot/AI Hardware team work side by side with cross-functional teams to support the development and scaling of both our AI silicon and vehicle hardware. Teams include design (electrical, mechanical & firmware), supply chain and manufacturing, to name a few. They will lead complex programs with limited up front guidance. The successful candidate for this position must have a broad background in engineering design and experience with program management. Must be a self-starter and able to figure out how to do things.
What You'll Do
Spearhead the next generation of AI hardware at both SOC and ECU levels, ensuring seamless integration into future vehicle products to drive innovation and performance
Engage monthly with AI and HW executives to foster alignment and strategic guidance for AI hardware programs across vehicles, robots, and AI data centers
Create program roadmaps and detailed timeline outlining milestones, critical paths, and deliverables
Lead engineering, hardware, AI software, operations, and procurement teams to deliver AI programs on time and within budget
Manage scope and schedule changes to minimize impact on overall objectives
Manage and track engineering sample demand and supply, coordinating with the supply chain and others to meet engineering needs
Maintain open communication with all stakeholders, ensuring their expectations are understood and managed
Monitor program progress and provide regular updates on status, including key performance metrics, challenges, and escalations
Track and manage potential risks with mitigation and contingency plans to minimize program impact
What You'll Bring
Degree or equivalent experience in Engineering
5+ years experience in product development, ideally with product shipping experience in medium-to-high volume industry
Flexibility to work long hours and weekends when required
Able to work well under pressure while managing competing demands and tight deadlines
Excellent written & verbal communication skills
Strong organization skills with meticulous attention to detail
