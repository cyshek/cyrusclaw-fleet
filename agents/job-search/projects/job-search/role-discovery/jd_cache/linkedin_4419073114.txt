What is the role?
As a Product Manager at Howden, you will partner closely with business stakeholders to identify problems worth solving, define desired outcomes, and translate needs into a clear product vision, roadmap, and prioritized backlog. You will work together with Engineering Project Managers and development teams to plan and execute delivery, providing delivery oversight to ensure work stays aligned to scope, timelines, quality standards, and business value.
You will evaluate existing capabilities and help design and deliver technical solutions that improve collaboration and efficiency across the client and colleague engagement value chain (including AI-driven products and enterprise technologies). You will lead requirements definition (epics, features, user stories, and acceptance criteria), facilitate trade-off decisions with stakeholders, and coordinate cross-functional dependencies. You will also manage vendor relationships, including partnerships with AI innovators—and ensure appropriate governance, risk, and compliance standards are met. The ideal candidate combines insurance domain knowledge with strong product management fundamentals and comfort working with data-driven solutions in a fast-paced environment.
What will you be doing?
Key Responsibilities
Product strategy & discovery: Partner with business leaders to define problems, outcomes, and success metrics; maintain the product vision and roadmap aligned to transformation objectives.
Backlog ownership: Create and maintain epics, features, and user stories with clear acceptance criteria; prioritize continuously based on value, risk, effort, and dependencies.
Delivery oversight (with Engineering PMs): Work with Engineering and Engineering Project Managers to plan releases and delivery timelines, manage scope and trade-offs, and ensure delivery meets quality and business expectations.
Business case support: Collaborate with business stakeholders to build business cases (problem statement, options, benefits, costs, risks, and expected ROI) and support funding and prioritization decisions; business stakeholders retain budget ownership and final approval.
Cross-functional stakeholder management: Facilitate alignment between business, IT, engineering, analytics, and operations; drive decision-making and remove blockers through clear communication and structured governance.
Solution evaluation & vendor management: Evaluate and select platforms and tools (including AI and data products) and manage third-party vendors/partners, including performance, delivery commitments, and commercial considerations.
Governance, risk & compliance: Ensure appropriate data protection, security, and compliance controls are incorporated into product design and delivery in partnership with legal, risk, privacy, and IT teams.
Measurement, adoption & continuous improvement: Use analytics and user feedback to measure product performance and drive improvements; partner with stakeholders on rollout and adoption plans.
Executive communication: Prepare concise updates and materials for senior leadership, including progress, risks, decisions needed, and outcomes.
