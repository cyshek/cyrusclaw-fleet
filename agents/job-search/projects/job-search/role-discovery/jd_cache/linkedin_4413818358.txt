# AI Solutions Architect

About the job
We're looking for a Solution Architect to join a forward-thinking AI Solutions programme within a major global automotive OEM.
The role sits at the heart of a large-scale AI transformation effort — responsible for proposing and designing technical, AI-based solutions across a broad Product Development and Technology (PDT) organisation. The mission is clear: improve efficiency, speed, inventiveness, and quality by applying production-grade AI at scale, adapted to the people who use it every day.

Responsibilities
Analyse existing problem descriptions and product requirements, gathering additional context where needed in collaboration with the Product Manager
Design high-level technical architectures that bring together AI components, front end, back end, infrastructure, data pipelines, and neural network models into cohesive solutions
Define service boundaries, APIs, and scalable system architectures suited to enterprise-scale environments
Translate product needs into clear data requirements and architecture decisions
Produce technical documentation including architecture diagrams, tradeoff analyses, and decision records
Work in close collaboration with Product Managers, Project Managers, and technical implementation teams
Ensure solutions are production-grade, observable, secure, and trustworthy at scale

Experience with cloud infrastructure, deployment patterns, and secure system design
Excellent technical documentation skills

Degree in an engineering or science field preferred
Ability to work across global, cross-functional teams and build alignment
