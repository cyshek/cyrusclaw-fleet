<p>Fix Alpaca’s operational bottlenecks for scale.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Your mission is to own and break the cycle of operational debt by transforming high-risk bottlenecks into automated, scalable solutions. You will operate as the critical bridge, ensuring Alpaca’s ambitions are balanced against operational reality to secure the foundation for parabolic growth.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Things You Get To Do:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Own Structural Bottlenecks: </strong>Own the product roadmap for Alpaca’s internal workflows and tooling.<strong> </strong>Drive root cause analysis and execute on the most complex and high-risk operational problems (e.g. AML/KYC workflows, reconciliations, corporate actions, cost basis processing, tax reporting, and security master/reference data management)</li>
<li><strong>Automation Strategy:</strong> Design systems and leverage internal tooling, AI, and workflow orchestration, so existing operations teams can support 100x growth. Eliminate critical failure modes by automating core workflows.&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>Delivery &amp; Measurement:</strong> Define clear success metrics and SLAs. Write crisp specifications, map processes, and work cross-functionally to ship high-leverage tooling, iterating based on operational feedback.</li>
<li><strong>Data-Driven Prioritization:</strong> Design and manage the data framework for selecting strategic projects, blending inputs from the Risk, Sales and Support.&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>Enforce Operational Readiness:</strong> Collaborate with Sales and Product to establish, manage, and enforce the operational capacity standards, acting as the critical check between ambitions and operational reality.</li>
<li><strong>Platform Strategy &amp; Sourcing:</strong> Drive critical Build vs. Buy evaluations for core platform components, ensuring compliance and optimal platform integrity.</li>
</ul>
