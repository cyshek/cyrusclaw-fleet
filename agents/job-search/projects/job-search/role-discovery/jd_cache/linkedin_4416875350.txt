# Associate Product Manager / Entry-Level PM

About The Role
The role owns specific product areas end-to-end - writing specs, running discovery, analyzing metrics, coordinating delivery, and measuring outcomes.
Candidates making the transition from software engineering, data analytics, or an MBA are actively encouraged. Prior domain knowledge is an asset here, not a liability.
Key Responsibilities
Write clear, well-researched product requirements documents (PRDs) and user stories that give engineering teams the context they need to build the right thing
Conduct structured user interviews, customer support analysis, and usage data reviews to generate validated product insights
Work with engineering to prioritize backlog items using a rigorous, data-informed framework - not gut feel or loudest voice
Define success metrics for features before they are built; measure outcomes after launch using Amplitude, Mixpanel, or internal analytics tools
Coordinate cross-functional delivery: align engineering, design, QA, and operations stakeholders on scope, timeline, and launch criteria
Plan and run A/B tests with appropriate statistical power; interpret results and make clear recommendations
Communicate clearly upward with founders and outward with customers - written and verbal
What We Are Looking For
0–3 years of product management experience, OR equivalent experience in software engineering, data analysis, business analysis, or consulting - with genuine PM ambition
Data fluency: SQL preferred; product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel, or equivalent) strongly preferred
Project management fundamentals: Jira or Linear, agile/scrum participation, ability to manage scope and timeline
Strong written communication - your PRDs are clear, your Slack messages are precise, and your customer emails are warm
Genuine product curiosity: you think about user problems and product strategy outside of work hours
Bonus: prior experience in logistics, operations, supply chain, or manufacturing technology
