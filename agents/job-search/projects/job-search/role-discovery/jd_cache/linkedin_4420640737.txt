# Product Manager

We are seeking a strategic and user-focused Product Manager to join our growing healthcare SaaS team. This role is ideal for someone with a strong foundation in product management and a passion for improving healthcare through technology. You will be responsible for driving the development and enhancement of digital products that serve healthcare providers, payers, and patients, ensuring they are compliant, scalable, and impactful. As a Product Manager, you will collaborate cross-functionally with engineering, design, clinical, and compliance teams to deliver solutions that meet market needs and regulatory standards. You’ll own the product lifecycle from discovery to delivery, leveraging data, customer insights, and industry trends to inform decisions and prioritize features.
Key Responsibilities
Healthcare Industry Knowledge & Regulatory Acumen
Familiarity with clinical workflows and terminology
Ability to navigate clinical frameworks and translate to product requirements
Awareness of patient safety and data privacy concerns (PHI)
Maintains general understanding of HIPAA and other compliance standards
Strategic Product Thinking
Align product vision with business goals and market needs.
Develop product strategies that drive value for both users and the business
Prioritize features based on impact, feasibility, and compliance to create a roadmap
Identify market opportunities and de-risking product ideas through experimentation, voice of customer, and market/competitive research
Technical Proficiency & Integration Awareness
Understanding of software development processes and agile methodologies
Knowledge of healthcare SaaS platforms (e.g., EHRs, telemedicine tools)
Ensures interoperability with legacy systems and existing healthcare infrastructure
User-Centric Design & Customer Insight
Conducts user research and usability testing to gather feedback, update requirements, and provide commercial guidance on value proposition
Helps designs intuitive, accessible, and compliant user experiences
Champion intuitive, accessible, and compliant user experiences tailored to anesthesia workflows
Cross-Functional Leadership & Communication
Serves as the bridge between engineering, design, clinical experts, and business teams.
Leads cross-functional teams through the product lifecycle including refinement and launch
Communicates clearly with technical and non-technical stakeholders
Align diverse teams around shared goals and manage expectations effectively
Required Qualifications
Education & Experience Guidelines
Bachelor’s degree in: Computer Science, Business Administration, Health Informatics, Engineering, Life Sciences or related field
4-6 years of relevant work experience
Proficiency in AI/ML technologies and data-driven product management
Proven track record of launching and iteratively improving digital products.
Demonstrated success leading cross-functional teams (engineering, design, clinical, compliance).
Experience working in agile environments and managing product backlogs including systems like JIRA or Azure DevOps.
Hands-on experience with product analytics tools (e.g., Google Analtyics, Gainsight, etc).
Ability to interpret healthcare data and use it to inform product strategy.
Experience working with legal or compliance teams during product development.
Travel up to 30% may be required
Other Preferred Knowledge, Skills, Abilities Or Certifications
Familiarity with EHR systems, clinical workflows, or health data standards (e.g., HL7, FHIR).
Exposure to HIPAA, GDPR, or other healthcare-related compliance frameworks.
Master’s degree or certifications in:
Product Management (e.g., Pragmatic Institute, General Assembly)
Healthcare Administration or Public Health
MBA with a focus on technology or healthcare
Certifications like Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) or PMI-ACP

About Provation
Provation is a leading provider of healthcare software and SaaS solutions for clinical productivity, care coordination, quality reporting, and billing. Our purpose is to empower providers to deliver quality healthcare for all. Provation’s comprehensive portfolio spans the entire patient encounter, from pre-procedure through post-procedure, with solutions for physician and nursing documentation (Provation® MD, Provation® Apex, MD-Reports, Provation® endoPRO®, and Provation® MultiCaregiver), anesthesia documentation (#1 Best in KLAS Provation® iPro), patient engagement, surgical care coordination, quality reporting, and billing capture (Provation® SurgicalValet™), order set and care plan management (Provation® Order Set Advisor™ and Provation® Care Plans), and EHR embedded clinical documentation (Provation® Clinic Note). Provation has a loyal customer base, serving more than 5,000 hospitals, surgery centers, and medical offices, and 700 physician groups globally, including 19 of the top 20 U.S. hospitals. In 2021, Provation was acquired by Fortive Corporation, a Fortune 1000 company that builds essential technology and accelerates transformation in high-impact fields like workplace safety, engineering, and healthcare. For more information about our solutions, visit provationmedical.com and follow us on Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Our purpose at Provation is to empower providers to deliver quality healthcare for all. To deliver on this commitment, we’re guided by our core values – Provation has a culture of CARES:Community - We have a shared sense of improving healthcare, enriching the broader world we live in and serve. Accountability - We own it and get it done with integrity. Respect - We build diverse teams that collaborate and communicate with positive intent and trust. Excellence - We welcome new ideas as we innovate quality solutions. Service - We are passionate about putting customers first.
