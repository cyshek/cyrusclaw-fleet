# Technical Product Manager

Responsibilities
Interface with clients to understand their requirements and help develop product roadmaps.
Help create ideal digital user experiences with our in-house UX Designers to meet client needs
Write user stories and acceptance criteria for Software Engineers based on product business value and help build out the defined user experience
Lead the technical decision-making process for projects, identifying suitable solutions based on client needs and constraints.
Drive project execution and oversee product development work streams on a day-to-day basis. Plan development sprints and identify and resolve blockers for the team to keep the projects on track.
Conduct product acceptance and quality assurance on delivered software
Work with our offsite engineers to plan their work, provide them what they need and conduct acceptance of their work
Communicate effectively to articulate the product’s current state, risks if any to internal and external teams.
Be a mentor and guide for the team and contribute towards technical leadership.
