Overview and Responsibilities:
The Paramount+ Live & Video team is looking for a versatile and highly motivated Product Manager with strong data and analytics skills to join our team. In this role, you will shape our Live Sports experience and work closely with all necessary teams to ensure a smooth and reliable rollout of experiences across our properties. This role reports to the Product Lead on Live & Video for Sports and collaborates closely with stakeholders, designers, and engineers to build best‑in‑class streaming apps, prioritize requirements based on business goals and KPIs, and deliver exceptional consumer experiences across platforms and sports properties.
Responsibilities Include:
Support the product roadmap and strategy and be a SME for Live Sports
Evolve and maintain homes for sports leagues and ensure consistency across all platforms
Draft requirements to enable feature innovation across Live TV, search, and discovery
Clearly define feature requirements and business logic for agile scrum teams, CMS and API teams, and design partners
Set KPI-driven priorities and use data to understand customer habits and needs
Align stakeholders (marketing, operations, data tracking, etc.) on delivery scope for sports events and leagues
Support strategic discussions on the future direction of the Paramount+ product and user experience
Manage cross-functional relationships and navigate competing priorities
Drive testing and UAT for feature and league rollouts across all sports leagues
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Capability to develop product strategies and communicate recommendations to management
Help foresee and resolve issues that affect development timelines
Help teams get unblocked on issues (QA, live testing, etc.)
Basic Qualifications:
3+ years of experience in product management or related field
Experience leading projects/initiatives and managing cross-functional stakeholders
Experience drafting detailed product requirements (user stories, PRDs)
Experience working with agile scrum methodologies and engineering teams
Ability to assess market trends and bring innovative thinking
General interest in sports
Bonus Skills
Experience working in Live Sports or Live Streaming
Experience with video metadata and live schedule data
Bachelor’s degree in business, technology, computer science, media, or related field preferred
Experience using agile methodologies
Familiarity with JIRA and Confluence
Ability to analyze data and communicate insights to stakeholders and development teams
Ability to develop product strategies and communicate recommendations to management
Creative problem-solving skills, including ability to articulate technical solutions
Experience with design tools such as Figma
