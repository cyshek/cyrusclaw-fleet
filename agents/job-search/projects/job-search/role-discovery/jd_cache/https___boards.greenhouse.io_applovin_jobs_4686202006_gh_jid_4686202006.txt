# Associate Product Manager

<div class="content-intro"><h3><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>About AppLovin</strong></span></h3>
<p>AppLovin makes technologies that help businesses of every size connect to their ideal customers. The company provides end-to-end software and AI solutions for businesses to reach, monetize and grow their global audiences. For more information about AppLovin, visit: <a href="http://www.applovin.com/">www.applovin.com</a>.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To deliver on this mission, our global team is composed of team members with life experiences, backgrounds, and perspectives that mirror our developers and customers around the world. At AppLovin, we are intentional about the team and culture we are building, seeking candidates who are outstanding in their own right and also demonstrate their support of others.</span></p>
<p>Fortune recognizes AppLovin as one of the Best Workplaces in the Bay Area, and the company has been a Certified Great Place to Work for the last four years (2021-2024). Check out the rest of our awards <a href="https://www.applovin.com/jobs/">HERE</a>.</p></div><h2><span style="color: #27251e;"><strong>About the Role&nbsp;</strong></span></h2>
<p><span style="color: #27251e;">We're seeking an Associate Product Manager to drive high-impact initiatives across our Ads organization. This role sits at the intersection of product strategy, analytics, and engineering with ownership across our entire ads portfolio from day one. You won't be siloed into a single feature; you'll drive initiatives that touch Ads Manager, creative workflows, mobile app install, ecommerce, CTV, measurement, and strategic partnerships.</span></p>
<p><span style="color: #27251e;">You'll operate with significant autonomy in a fast-paced environment, working directly with leadership to shape product direction across AppLovin's advertising ecosystem.</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #27251e;"><strong>A Day in the Life</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Design and launch new advertiser experiences across our ads products, including creative-first workflows, campaign management tools, and new ad formats.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Own advertiser pilot programs by identifying early adopters, coordinating feedback across Product, Engineering, Design, and Client Strategy teams, and measuring uplift in key KPIs like usage and adoption.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Experiment with ad experience formats and analyze performance data to determine what drives better outcomes for advertisers and users.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Define success metrics and evaluate product performance through rigorous experimentation and A/B testing.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Build analyses using SQL to understand advertiser behavior, product usage, campaign performance, and revenue impact.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Identify product gaps and growth opportunities, develop product recommendations, and influence roadmap decisions through quantitative data, customer feedback, and market insights.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Help scope and launch integrations with third-party measurement, attribution, and ecommerce platforms.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Partner with Engineering, Product Design, Business Development, and Growth teams to drive initiatives from concept through launch including ideation, scoping, prototyping, QA testing, gathering client feedback, and post-launch measurement.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Monitor key business metrics, investigate performance trends, and develop recommendations that influence product strategy.</span></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><span style="color: #27251e;"><strong>The Impact You'll Make</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Drive measurable improvements in advertiser adoption, engagement, and product usage.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Shape the evolution of AppLovin's advertising platform through experimentation and data-driven decision making.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Influence product strategy across mobile, ecommerce, and CTV advertising products.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Help advertisers achieve stronger outcomes through improved workflows, tools, and measurement capabilities.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Build scalable frameworks for experimentation, product measurement, and feature rollout.</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><span style="color: #27251e;"><strong>Who You Are</strong></span></h2>
<ul>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Bachelor's degree or higher in Engineering, Computer Science, Economics, Finance, Statistics, Mathematics, Business Analytics, or a related quantitative field.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">2–4 years of post-graduation experience in product, business operations and analytics, management consulting, investment banking, or investing.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Strong SQL skills and proficiency in at least one scripting language (Python strongly preferred).</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Demonstrated ability to independently scope ambiguous problems, design analyses, synthesize findings, and present recommendations.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Strong track record of driving measurable business impact through data-driven decision making.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Highly analytical with excellent problem-solving skills and business judgment.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Comfortable operating in a fast-paced environment with significant ownership and autonomy.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Strong communicator who can influence both technical and non-technical stakeholders.</span></li>
<li style="color: #27251e !important;"><span style="color: #27251e;">Naturally curious, proactive, and motivated by solving complex business problems.</span></li>
