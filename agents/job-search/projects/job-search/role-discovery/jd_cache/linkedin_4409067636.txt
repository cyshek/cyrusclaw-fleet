# Product Manager

Job Title: Founding Product Manager
Location: NYC, onsite 5 days/week.
This is a full-time, in-person opportunity located in the NoHo neighborhood of Manhattan with a high-growth technology business revolutionizing the sports sector. Our client operates an online prediction platform that has seen a massive surge in user engagement and market expansion. They are seeking a strategic lead to help scale their core product as they disrupt the intersection of sports media and data science.
As the founding Product Manager, you have the unique opportunity to build the product function from the ground up at an early-stage powerhouse. This is the #1 feature of the role: you aren't just managing a backlog; you are defining the vision and roadmap for a platform poised to shake up the global sports industry. If you are looking for a high-impact, high-visibility role where you can bridge the gap between complex data analytics and intuitive user experiences, this is the ultimate growth environment.
Required Skills & Experience
4+ years of professional Product Management experience (preferably within tech, data, or platform-based products).
Proven track record of managing the full product development lifecycle in an Agile environment.
Strong experience working with data-driven products, analytics platforms, or predictive technologies.
Exceptional stakeholder management skills with the ability to communicate technical requirements to engineering and data science teams.
Must be authorized to work in the US without sponsorship.
Desired Skills & Experience
A genuine passion for sports and a deep interest in predictive analytics.
Hands-on experience with tools such as Jira, Productboard, Mixpanel, or Google Analytics.
Previous experience in a "founding" or early-stage startup capacity.
What You Will Be Doing
Tech Breakdown
70% Platform & Feature Roadmap
30% Data Analytics & User Insights
Daily Responsibilities
60% Hands-On (Defining requirements, managing backlog, and execution)
20% Strategic Vision (Roadmapping and business goal alignment)
20% Team Collaboration (Engineering, Design, and Data Science syncs)
The Offer
Salary Range: $ 120,000-$150,000
Performance-based incentives and bonuses.
You Will Receive The Following Benefits
Comprehensive Health, Dental, and Vision Insurance.
Generous Vacation and Paid Time Off.
Opportunity for growth within a rapidly evolving industry.
Dynamic, collaborative team culture in a prime NYC location.
Applicants must be currently authorized to work in the US on a full-time basis now and in the future.
