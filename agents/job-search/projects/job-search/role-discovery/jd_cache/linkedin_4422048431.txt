# Technical Program Manager (PMP)

Job Title: Technical Program Manager
Location: Philadelphia, PA 19103 (CTH)
Duration: Contract-to-Hire
Job Description
We are seeking an experienced Technical Program Manager with a strong technical background
and proven expertise managing cross-functional programs in fast-paced environments. The
ideal candidate will have experience in Agile/DevOps environments, strong stakeholder
management skills, and prior exposure to Embedded, Broadband, or Video domains.
Required Qualifications
BS/MS degree or equivalent practical experience
6 8 years of experience in Technical Program Management or combined TPM and
engineering experience
PMP or Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) certification preferred
Strong technical background with the ability to manage and guide complex technical
initiatives
Experience working in Agile/Scrum methodologies within DevOps environments
Expertise with Jira, Confluence, and Smartsheet
Experience managing geographically distributed and cross-functional teams
Excellent communication, collaboration, and stakeholder management skills
Ability to balance competing priorities and drive projects to completion
Experience in Embedded systems domain, preferably Broadband or Video technologies
Previous experience in Quality Engineering or Release Management is a plus
Key Responsibilities
Define and manage technical program scope, milestones, timelines, and deliverables
Coordinate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful program execution
Build and maintain detailed program plans, schedules, and tracking documentation
Identify and manage critical paths, risks, dependencies, and mitigation strategies
Facilitate development of program goals, objectives, and success criteria
Support budgeting and resource forecasting activities
Drive tradeoff discussions balancing scope, cost, and timelines
Track program performance and adjust plans to align with business objectives
Communicate project status, risks, and milestone updates to stakeholders and
leadership
Manage changes to program scope and ensure proper communication and
documentation
Ensure quality and timely delivery of program deliverables
Support deployment planning, customer trials, and implementation activities
Work with stakeholders to manage epics, user stories, and defects through Agile sprint
cycles
Collaborate with distributed teams across the USA, Europe, and India
Deliver detailed reporting on operational and strategic activities while identifying
process improvements
Thrive in ambiguous environments and proactively resolve issues
Mandatory Skills
Technical Program Management
Agile/Scrum Methodologies
PMP or CSM Certification
Jira, Confluence, Smartsheet
Embedded Systems Domain Experience
Broadband/Video Industry Experience
Cross-functional Team Leadership
Risk & Dependency Management
Stakeholder Communication and Reporting
