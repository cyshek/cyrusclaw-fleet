# Sales Engineer

Powering Customer Partnerships Through Technical & Commercial Excellence!
MEPPI’s High Voltage Switchgear Division (HVSD) is seeking an experienced and customer-focused Sales Engineer II to manage both the technical and commercial relationships with assigned customers. In this key role, you will lead proposal development, oversee customer account activities, coordinate order execution, and strengthen long-term partnership relationships that support growth and customer satisfaction.
What You’ll Do
Build & Strengthen Customer Partnerships: Develop productive relationships with new and existing partnership customers by collaborating with Regional Managers, manufacturers’ representatives, and internal teams.
Prepare Technical & Commercial Proposals: Review customer specifications, technical drawings, and commercial terms to develop accurate proposals for products, parts, and services. Clarify, negotiate, and close proposals, and obtain customer feedback for continuous improvement.
Manage Manufacturing Slot Reservations: Reserve production capacity for partnership customers and monitor schedules to ensure delivery commitments align with customer expectations.
Process & Turn Over Orders: Confirm incoming customer orders match approved proposals. Enter orders and coordinate turnover to Engineering, Production, and Contract Administration. Communicate all contract requirements and commitments.
Provide Customer Reporting: Prepare and distribute monthly customer reports summarizing deliveries, production slots, and upcoming shipment expectations.
Serve as Primary Customer Contact: Ensure timely communication, issue resolution, and escalation for customer inquiries by effectively utilizing internal resources.
Lead Customer Meetings: Conduct face-to-face meetings and partnership reviews to address technical and commercial topics, including new products, design changes, field and quality incidents, and modifications or repairs.
Develop Sales Materials: Create sales aids, customer presentations, and supporting documentation.
Support Pricing & Margin Goals: Calculate product, parts, and service costs; recommend pricing aligned with company margin requirements and current market conditions.
What You Bring
Education & Experience:
Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline
3–5 years of experience in engineering, marketing, or related technical roles, or an equivalent combination of education and experience
Technical & Commercial Skills
Ability to read and understand technical drawings, specifications, and materials
Knowledge of negotiation techniques for customer contract discussions
Skills & Attributes
Advanced interpersonal, communication, and presentation skills for interaction with customers and internal stakeholders
Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
Ability to manage multiple customer accounts and projects simultaneously
Technology Proficiency
Advanced computer skills with MS Office products
Experience with MRP/ERP systems
