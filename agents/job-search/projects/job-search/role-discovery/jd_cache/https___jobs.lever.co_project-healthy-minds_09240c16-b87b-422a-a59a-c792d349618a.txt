# Product Manager
About Project Healthy Minds

Project Healthy Minds is a mental health nonprofit that runs like a tech startup. We are pioneering a first-of-its-kind model: applying technology to address one of the biggest social issues of our time: the growing mental health crisis. We believe in a simple but profound idea: that it should be as easy to find mental health services as it is to book a flight or a restaurant reservation. That's why we're building the world’s first digital marketplace for finding mental health services that are right for you. 

To do that, we work with some of the most inspiring and influential leaders in America - business titans, tech entrepreneurs, clinicians and scientists, musicians, artists, athletes, media stars, policymakers, and more - because we believe that the world's most complex challenges require interdisciplinary teams and solutions.

We are backed by a powerhouse of industry-leading Board of Directors and Advisory Council Members who are passionate, hands-on, and dive into the important work with us.

Learn more at projecthealthyminds.com and @projecthealthyminds on social.

About The Role

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

The Product Manager owns the day-to-day execution of PHM's product roadmap—running sprints, driving analytics-informed decisions, and shipping features that move users toward better mental health outcomes. This role exists to free up strategic bandwidth while ensuring a steady drumbeat of small, high-impact wins. This is a full-time, in-office position.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

• Own sprint planning and delivery cadence using Linear, keeping engineering focused and accountable
• Identify low-effort, high-reward opportunities through analytics and user feedback
• Write and own PRDs for new features, running them through product leadership
• Partner with engineering and design to ship quickly with minimal overhead
• Use modern AI tools to accelerate analysis, ticket creation, and documentation
• Build and maintain dashboards that surface actionable insights for the team and leadership
• Contribute to capacity planning and delivery forecasting for management visibility

WHAT YOU'LL BRING

• Product management experience in a fast-moving startup or early-stage environment
• Demonstrated ability to ship without a large support structure around you
• Comfort with data: college-level statistics or professional analytics experience; you don't need SQL—you need to think clearly about what the data means
• Familiarity with healthcare, mental health, insurance, or marketplace domains (preferred)
• Experience with or strong interest in using AI tools to accelerate product work
• Resourceful, low-ego, high-agency mindset
• A belief in our mission: connecting millions of Americans to mental health services that actually work for them

WHY THIS IS A UNIQUE OPPORTUNITY

• A world-class Board, Clinical Advisory Committee, supporters, and collaborators. We've assembled a truly best-in-class network across business, academia, and media.
• A big, bold vision with backing. Multi-year track record of strong YoY growth, funded by philanthropy and earned revenue.
• Startup mindset, social mission ethos. You'll work with a team from venture-backed startups, market-leading enterprises, and everything in between—united by the chance to drive massive positive impact.
• Direct impact on product direction. You'll work closely with the VP of Product on a small team where your decisions directly shape what millions of users experience.
