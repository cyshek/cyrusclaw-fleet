# Inside Sales Engineer

We are seeking a highly motivated Inside Sales Engineer to play a critical role in a two-person team managing a multi-state territory. This position sits at the intersection of engineering, customer support, and commercial execution.
You will work directly with customers to understand application requirements, develop technical solutions, and deliver timely, accurate quotations for custom-engineered heating, temperature sensing, and control products. Success in this role requires strong technical aptitude, responsiveness, and the ability to translate customer needs into practical, value-driven solutions.
Customer Engagement & Technical Sales
Alongside your Outside Sales Engineer partner, serve as the primary day-to-day contact for customers, responding to phone and email inquiries with urgency and professionalism
Interpret customer requirements (drawings, specifications, application conditions) and develop appropriate product recommendations
Prepare accurate and timely quotations aligned with customer needs and company capabilities
Provide technical guidance that differentiates our solutions and strengthens customer confidence
Order Management & Execution
Process customer purchase orders and enter sales orders accurately into the system
Coordinate with Engineering, Quality, and Operations to ensure orders are executed correctly and delivered on time
Proactively communicate order status, lead times, and any issues to customers
Territory & Pipeline Development
Partner with the Outside Sales Engineer to execute territory strategy and achieve revenue targets
Actively manage and develop a pipeline of opportunities using a defined sales process
Identify new business opportunities within existing accounts and through inbound inquiries
Application Engineering & Product Expertise
Develop strong product and application knowledge across industrial heating technologies
Apply engineering fundamentals to support diverse applications across multiple industries
Help customers solve performance, reliability, and cost challenges through tailored solutions
