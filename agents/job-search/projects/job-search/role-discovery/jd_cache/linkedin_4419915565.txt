# Solutions Architect - 2026 (US)

Description
By applying to this position, your application will be considered for all locations we hire for in the United States.
Do you want to help us build new things and evangelize use of AWS with customers? As an Associate Solutions Architect you will partner across AWS to help customers craft highly scalable, flexible and resilient cloud architectures that address customer business problems. As a trusted customer advocate, you will help organizations understand best practices around cloud-based solutions, and how to migrate existing workloads to the cloud. You will have the opportunity to help customers use AWS within their organizations ranging from new start-ups to large enterprise customers.
This position is part of an accelerated career development program for those who want to advance both their technical and business skills. This is a unique opportunity for driven, self-starters to play a key role in a fast-growing business, and to deliver significant value to AWS customers of all sizes from nimble startups to global brands. The program will provide you with training and work experience that aligns in the field of technical training. This unique program consists of mentoring by subject matter experts, project-based learning curriculum and experiential learning assignments.
If this sounds exciting to you - come build the future with us!
Key job responsibilities
Create solutions that address customer business problems and accelerate the adoption of AWS services
Interface with customer stakeholders, including developers and team leads
Drive effective feedback gathering from customers, distill and translate feedback into clear business and technical requirements for product and engineering teams
Basic Qualifications
Bachelor's degree or above in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields
Experience with one of the following programming languages: Python, Ruby, Node.js, C#, or C++
Experience with AI/ML technologies
Experience with one or more of the following domains: analytics, security, storage, DevOps, application development, or machine learning
Graduation conferral date between August 2024 and August 2026
Excellent communication skills with the ability to effectively articulate technical challenges and solutions to both small and large audiences
Preferred Qualifications
AWS or other cloud certification
Experience applying established frameworks and design patterns to deliver working software (can include coursework, personal projects, hackathons, or previous internships)
