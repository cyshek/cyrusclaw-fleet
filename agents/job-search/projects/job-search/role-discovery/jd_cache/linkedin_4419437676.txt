# Product Manager

Product Manager — AI-Native Travel Experience (Agentic Systems)
We are building a deeply personal, AI-native travel companion designed to become the default way people plan, book, and experience travel over a lifetime. Travel is one of the most meaningful parts of life — yet today’s tools remain fragmented, transactional, and impersonal. We are rethinking this from first principles.
Our product is built around an intelligent agent that learns the user over time, applies judgment, and reduces complexity — making travel more personal, seamless, and meaningful.
We are seeking a Product Manager to own a core area of the product end-to-end, shaping how users interact with an AI travel agent that plans, recommends, and executes travel decisions on their behalf.
What You’ll Do
Own a Core Product Surface End-to-End
Depending on company priorities, you may own one of the following areas:
Planning & Discovery — how the system understands users, builds preferences, and co-creates trips
Booking & Optimization — how the system commits on behalf of users and ensures optimal outcomes
In either case, you will own strategy, execution, and measurement from end to end.
Design Agentic Product Behavior
Define what the AI agent should do, recommend, and communicate
Design guardrails, policies, and behavioral frameworks for agent reliability and trust
Balance usefulness with safety and user trust in non-deterministic systems
Build and Iterate Rapidly
Translate ambiguous user needs into clear product requirements across UX, systems, and agent behavior
Run fast, structured experimentation loops during private beta
Ship MVPs quickly, measure outcomes honestly, and iterate based on real user behavior
Partner Across Design and Engineering
Work closely with design to create high-quality conversational and visual experiences
Collaborate with engineering and AI teams on:
Retrieval and personalization systems
Tool use and orchestration workflows
Transactional booking flows
Monitoring and evaluation systems
Define Measurement and Truth
Build rigorous measurement frameworks combining qualitative and quantitative signals
Define success metrics tied to real user outcomes, not vanity metrics
Use product analytics to identify issues and guide roadmap decisions
Lead With Clarity and Judgment
Act as a truth-teller in product decisions — challenge weak assumptions and unclear thinking
Write clear, decisive product specs and make trade-offs explicit
Maintain high standards for product quality and user experience
How You Work
Use AI as a core productivity multiplier across research, writing, prototyping, and analysis
Operate with high autonomy in ambiguous environments
Drive momentum without waiting for direction or permission
Default to action and experimentation over speculation
What We’re Looking For
3–10+ years of product management experience (level calibrated based on impact and trajectory)
Strong product intuition and consumer-grade taste
Proven ability to ship in ambiguous, fast-moving environments
Excellent written communication and structured thinking
Demonstrated ability to define metrics and evaluate product success rigorously
Deep, practical use of AI tools in day-to-day product work (not just familiarity, but fluency)
Founder mindset: ownership, urgency, and bias toward action
Strong Plus If You Have
Experience building AI-native products (LLMs, agents, personalization, or retrieval systems)
Experience with complex transactional systems (payments, orchestration, multi-step workflows)
Background in travel, marketplaces, fintech, or other high-trust consumer domains
Experience designing human + AI hybrid workflows with guardrails and escalation paths
Early-stage or 0→1 product experience
Ability to prototype using tools like Figma, SQL, or lightweight scripting
Why This Role Matters
Define what it means for an AI agent to act on behalf of users in real-world decisions
Help build a new category of consumer product centered on trust, judgment, and personalization
Work at the frontier of AI-native product design and agentic systems
Shape how millions of people will experience travel in the future
About the Company
We are a small, senior team building an AI-native travel platform from the ground up. We operate with high trust, high standards, and a bias toward action. We value clarity, speed, and truth over comfort.
We are committed to building an inclusive workplace and encourage applicants from all backgrounds to apply.
