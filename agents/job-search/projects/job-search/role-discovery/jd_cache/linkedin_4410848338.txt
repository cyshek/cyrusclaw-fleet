# Solutions Architect - CI/CD PaaS Leader

Solutions Architect — Mobile DevOps & CI/CD (Remote, U.S.)
Solve high-impact engineering challenges at scale. This role is built for technical experts who thrive in complex environments—where identifying the root cause quickly and delivering meaningful solutions can transform how entire engineering organizations operate.
This opportunity is with a client of Andiamo, a technology-driven organization operating in the mobile development and DevOps ecosystem. You’ll work directly with large-scale engineering teams building applications used by millions, helping them optimize how software is built, tested, and delivered.
Role Overview
As a Solutions Architect, you will act as a senior technical partner to enterprise customers, helping them navigate challenges within their mobile development pipelines. From diagnosing performance bottlenecks to designing scalable CI/CD workflows, your expertise will directly influence engineering productivity and product delivery.
This is not a passive advisory role—you’ll be hands-on in debugging, architecting solutions, and guiding teams toward best practices while also influencing future product direction through real-world insights.
Key Responsibilities
Technical Problem Solving
Dive into complex build and deployment challenges, quickly identifying root causes and delivering practical, scalable solutions that improve performance and reliability.
Customer Advisory & Enablement
Serve as a trusted technical partner, guiding engineering teams on best practices across mobile development, CI/CD pipelines, and DevOps processes.
CI/CD Optimization
Analyze and improve build pipelines, reducing inefficiencies and enabling faster, more reliable release cycles.
Cross-Functional Collaboration
Work closely with product and engineering teams to relay customer insights, helping shape future platform capabilities and enhancements.
Escalation & Support Leadership
Act as a senior escalation point for technical issues, supporting internal teams and ensuring critical challenges are resolved efficiently.
Implementation & Onboarding Support
Partner with customer-facing teams to support onboarding, solution design, and adoption of platform capabilities.
Thought Leadership
Contribute to the broader technical community through knowledge sharing, including speaking engagements, written content, and industry participation.
Relationship Building
Develop strong relationships with technical stakeholders, helping them maximize value while becoming advocates for the platform.
