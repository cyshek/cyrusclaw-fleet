# Import and Trade Compliance Program Manager

## Description
In this role, you will manage the daily import clearance of shipments and oversee key compliance programs, including Broker Management and product classification. You will collaborate with internal business units, global suppliers, and customs brokers to translate evolving regulatory requirements into scalable policies and operational workflows.

## Minimum Qualifications
5 or more years of experience in US import operations, trade compliance, or customs brokerage.\nExperience managing daily import clearance, broker management, and product classification.\nKnowledge of US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations, including AEO and CTPAT programs.\nExperience with post-entry compliance, duty drawback, and Importer Self-Assessment.\nExperience designing and implementing compliance control frameworks and operational workflows.

## Preferred Qualifications
Ability to collaborate cross-functionally with legal, supply chain, and external stakeholders.\nAbility to communicate regulatory requirements to diverse audiences through training and documentation.\nAbility to analyze data, identify risk indicators, and propose solutions.\nAbility to manage multiple projects and adapt to evolving regulatory environments.
