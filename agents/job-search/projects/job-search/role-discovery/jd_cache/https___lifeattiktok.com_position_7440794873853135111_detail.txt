Responsibilities
About the Team
The infrastructure team supports the company's fast growth by building and operating hyper-scale data centers as well as vastly distributed global edge networks. To support the fast growth and manage the massive content on the platform, our infrastructure team is building hyper-scale data centers globally which host hundreds of thousands of servers.
Responsibilities:
- Lead the creation of the Build, Operations and Maintenance Plans for all global entity PoPs
- Work on operational issues including RMA and repairs for all network and server equipment.
- Drive and manage multiple projects around optimizing PoP build outs, improving documentation and building new tools
- Lead Day 2 Operations, Repair Troubleshooting, and Maintenance improvement strategies
- Design and deliver large-scale network projects composed of dark fiber, leased waves, IP transit and peering circuits, colo space, power, cross connects, point of presence and content delivery networks build
- Work on optimizing operational processes and designing more efficient workflows
Qualifications
Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree in Computer science, Electrical engineering, Mechanical engineering or any equivalent technical field
- Knowledge of co-location infrastructure including facilities, site selection, operations
- Knowledge of Linux and hardware systems
Preferred qualifications
- 3 years of experience in network infrastructure space, related to solutions and/or project management experience in CDN and/or telecom-related field
- 3 years of experience managing cross-functional and/or cross-team projects
- Technical experience in telecommunication networks including Fixed Network, Transmission and IP Technologies(IPT, IX, Peering)
- 3 years of experience in edge infrastructure related fields
- Excellent communication. Ability to express ideas clearly and provide effective remote technical guidance and troubleshooting
- Knowledge of co-location infrastructure including facilities, site selection, operations
- Enjoys solving technical problems and challenges.

About TikTok
TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. At TikTok, our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy. TikTok's global headquarters are in Los Angeles and Singapore, and we also have offices in New York City, London, Dublin, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Jakarta, Seoul, and Tokyo.​
Why Join Us
Inspiring creativity is at the core of TikTok's mission. Our innovative product is built to help people authentically express themselves, discover and connect – and our global, diverse teams make that possible. Together, we create value for our communities, inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we work towards every day.​
