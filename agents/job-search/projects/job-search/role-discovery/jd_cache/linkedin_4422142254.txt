# Network Program Manager

Key Responsibilities:
Manage and coordinate assigned program workstreams, ensuring timely delivery of milestones and deliverables.
Maintain project plans, schedules, RAID logs, and status reporting using Jira, Excel, and SharePoint.
Support planning and execution of global WAN migration and network modernization activities.
Prepare weekly/monthly status reports and executive-level updates.
Facilitate governance meetings, deployment readiness reviews, and stakeholder communications.
Track risks, issues, dependencies, and mitigation plans across multiple teams and vendors.
Coordinate with Network Engineering, Security, Data Center Operations, Procurement, and regional delivery teams.
Support change management activities and migration readiness efforts.
Required Qualifications:
6+ years of Program Management or Senior Project Management experience.
Strong experience managing large-scale infrastructure, network, or technology programs.
Expertise in project scheduling, dependency management, RAID management, and executive reporting.
Hands-on experience with Jira, SharePoint, Excel, and PowerPoint.
Excellent stakeholder management, communication, and coordination skills.
Experience working in complex, matrixed enterprise environments.
Preferred Qualifications:
Experience supporting WAN, network, data center, or infrastructure modernization projects.
Background in financial services or highly regulated environments.
Experience with global deployments, migration programs, or hardware refresh initiatives.
