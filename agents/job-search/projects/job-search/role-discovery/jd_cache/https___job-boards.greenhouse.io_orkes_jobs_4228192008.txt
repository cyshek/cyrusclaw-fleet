# Product Manager

<div><strong>About Us</strong></div>
<div>Orkes is a platform for developers to build durable, distributed event driven applications. Based on the popular open source orchestration engine&nbsp;<a class="postings-link" href="https://github.com/conductor-oss/conductor">Conductor</a>, Orkes lets developers focus on faster go to market with applications, scaling them to handle&nbsp;<a class="postings-link" href="https://medium.com/orkes/running-a-billion-workflows-a-month-with-netflix-conductor-5fd358d9b6c1">billions of workflows</a>&nbsp;without having to worry about failures, scalability or visibility into the executions.</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div>We are looking for a Product Manager with a focus on identifying, defining and launching products and services that will drive the adoption of Orkes with engineering teams. In this role, you will be pivotal in shaping our user journey, driving customer onboarding, activation, and conversion. If you are passionate about data-driven decision-making, optimizing user experiences, and driving customer activation and conversion, this role is perfect for you.</div>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<div><strong>Your day-to-day at Orkes:</strong></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Take ownership of the first days of a user journey and ensure a seamless and engaging experience.</li>
<li>Implement strategies to improve user onboarding, reduce friction, and enhance user satisfaction during initial interactions with our product.</li>
<li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams (Engineering, DevRel, Sales, Marketing) to define and track key performance metrics related to user acquisition, activation, conversion and expansion.</li>
<li>Analyze the user journey, from initial onboarding to conversion and beyond, and identify areas for improvement.</li>
<li>Continuously monitor and improve conversion rates throughout the user journey. Collaborate with Product Engineering and Design teams to implement changes that enhance conversion at key touch points.</li>
<li>Regularly analyze user behavior and engagement data to uncover insights and trends. Use these insights to make informed decisions and iterate on growth strategies as well as continuous product evolution across core Conductor and cloud offerings.</li>
<li>Embrace a culture of experimentation.</li>
<li>Test hypotheses, run A/B tests, and iterate on features based on real-time data and user feedback.</li>
<li>Create and deliver regular reports on growth metrics and initiatives to company leadership. -Clearly communicate progress, challenges, and opportunities to stakeholders.</li>
<li>Utilize data analytics to measure and analyze key performance indicators.</li>
<li>Make informed decisions based on data insights and continuously optimize strategies.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><strong>Qualities that will help you succeed:&nbsp;</strong></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Proven experience as a Product Manager&nbsp;<strong>for a technology product (preferably a product for application developers)&nbsp;</strong>with a track record of driving growth and optimization.</li>
<li>Previous exposure to and understanding of product-led growth strategies, including alignment with Go-to-Market (GTM) teams.</li>
<li>Proven experience bringing structured thinking and prioritization to what needs to be built and launched while thriving in an environment where there is ambiguity</li>
<li>Results-oriented mindset with a focus on driving measurable outcomes.</li>
<li>A deep understanding of user-centered design principles and user experience best practices.</li>
<li>Excellent project management skills with the ability to prioritize and manage multiple initiatives simultaneously.</li>
<li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills and a bias for action.</li>
<li>Reverse engineering from customer needs.</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate effectively with distributed cross-functional teams.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><strong>You will have a fast start if:&nbsp;</strong></div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>Engineering background (e.g., you wrote some code before)</li>
<li>You have defined and launched SaaS offerings in the cloud infrastructure space</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div><em>The requirements listed in the job descriptions are guidelines. You don’t have to satisfy every requirement or meet every qualification listed. If your skills are transferable we would still love to hear from you.&nbsp;</em></div>
