# Solutions Engineer

Who we are:
Spot AI turns cameras into coworkers.
We are building the category of AI coworkers for the physical world. Our platform enables businesses to understand, automate, and act across their environments in real time.
Spot AI is relied on at over 10,000 locations by more than 1,000 customers across manufacturing, construction, logistics, and retail.
Backed by over $110 million from investors including Scale, Redpoint, Qualcomm Ventures, StepStone, and Bessemer, we are building the system that brings AI out of the digital world and into the real one.
We move fast, take ownership, and focus on outcomes. We are building a company for people who want to solve real problems and have a direct impact on how businesses operate.

We’re looking for a Solutions Engineer who wants to win by helping organizations solve their biggest problems with AI:
You will work alongside Account Executives and partners to win complex, multi-stakeholder deals across enterprise and upper mid-market customers.
These are not standard demos; you are not walking through slides or features. You will shape how customers think about their problems, design AI solutions from first principles, and prove value in environments that are often ambiguous and operationally complex.
Every deal involves working through partners to build trust, expand reach, and accelerate outcomes. The SEs who win here are not reactive. They create clarity, drive urgency, and use technical depth to turn curiosity into committed deals.
Your assignment:
Drive technical strategy in deals
Partner with Account Executives and channel partners to lead the technical side of the sales cycle
Run deep discovery to understand environments, workflows, and constraints
Shape the solution and drive the customer toward a clear path forward
Prove value in real environments
Design and execute POCs, pilots, and trials that demonstrate measurable business impact
Build lightweight integrations, workflows, or prototypes using APIs and AI tools
Own RFPs, security reviews, and technical validation end to end
Win through partners
Work closely with channel partners as a core part of every deal
Enable partners technically and position Spot AI as a premium solution
Use partners to accelerate trust, access, and deal velocity
Build for scale
Create repeatable demos, assets, and technical playbooks
Partner with Product and Engineering to influence roadmap and improve the product
Use AI to automate work, accelerate output, and increase the quality of your solutions
Who you are:
You have experience as a Solutions Engineer, Sales Engineer, or similar role in a SaaS or AI company
You have 5+ years supporting enterprise or upmarket sales with 6+ month cycles and six- to seven-figure deals
You have a strong technical foundation in networking, APIs, and integrations
You go beyond demos and build scrappy solutions or integrations that prove value
You have proven success running technical sales cycles, including discovery, demos, POCs, and evaluations
You are comfortable operating in ambiguity and figuring things out without a playbook
You embrace working through partners and influencing technical stakeholders across ecosystems
You communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical audiences
You enjoy working face to face with customers, and are willing and able to travel up to 50% of the time
You're an especially strong candidate if you have experience with video systems, edge environments, or physical-world operations, or if you actively use AI tools to augment your work.
This role could click if you:
Like solving messy, real-world problems with no obvious answer
Enjoy being in the field, working directly with customers and partners
Take pride in building solutions that actually get used
Move fast and don’t wait for perfect information
This role likely won’t if you:
Need a defined playbook or you wait for direction instead of creating it
Avoid ambiguity or ownership of open-ended problems
Want to stay purely theoretical instead of building and proving solutions
