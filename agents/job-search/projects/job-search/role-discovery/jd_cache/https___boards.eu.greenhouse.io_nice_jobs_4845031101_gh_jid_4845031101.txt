<p><strong>So, what’s the role all about?</strong></p>
<p>A NiCE <strong>Program Manager</strong> of GTM Operations &amp; Business Insights leads cross-functional programs that build scalable systems, reporting infrastructure, and governance frameworks empowering leadership to make informed, data-driven decisions. Sitting at the intersection of revenue performance, analytics, and operational execution, this role translates complex data into clear, actionable insights that strengthen organizational alignment and improve performance outcomes.</p>
<p>This position serves as a strategic program partner to Sales, CX, and executive leadership — ensuring visibility into KPIs, driving program execution, and implementing improvements that streamline processes, reduce friction, and accelerate growth across the business.</p>
<p><strong>How will you make an impact?&nbsp;</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><strong>Program Delivery &amp; Reporting Systems</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Lead the design, maintenance, and continuous optimization of executive-ready dashboards, reports, and performance summaries across Excel, Power BI, and CRM data sources.</li>
<li>Build and scale automated reporting systems that consolidate data for sales and CX leadership, reducing manual processes and improving reporting accuracy.</li>
<li>Establish and manage structured reporting cadences supporting weekly, monthly, and quarterly business reviews.</li>
<li>Develop centralized documentation, templates, workflows, and governance that improve reporting consistency across teams.</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><strong>KPI Governance &amp; Performance Management</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Define and maintain KPI frameworks that ensure alignment across Sales, CX, and Executive teams.</li>
<li>Drive metric accuracy, consistency, and clarity across Salesforce dashboards, financial data, and operational reporting.</li>
<li>Partner with Revenue and CX leadership to support forecasting, MRR/ARR analysis, and pipeline visibility.</li>
<li>Manage program-level performance tracking systems that highlight risks, opportunities, and execution gaps.</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><strong>Data-Driven Program Analysis</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct deep-dive analysis into revenue, customer, and operational trends to surface insights used in leadership planning and decision-making.</li>
<li>Deliver concise, executive-level summaries that translate complex datasets into clear, actionable recommendations that drive program direction.</li>
<li>Support strategic planning discussions with data modeling, scenario analysis, and performance insights.</li>
<li>Identify patterns or gaps in CRM usage and recommend improvements to enhance data quality and reporting integrity.</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><strong>Cross-Functional Program Coordination</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as the central coordination point for programs spanning Sales, CX, Operations, Enablement, and Executive stakeholders.</li>
<li>Create and manage structured workflows, intake processes, and operational templates that improve cross-team program execution.</li>
<li>Support program-focused meetings, including agenda planning, facilitation support, action tracking, and follow-up execution.</li>
<li>Identify bottlenecks across teams and drive system or process solutions to improve execution, communication, and accountability.</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><strong>Process Improvement &amp; Program Scalability</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Evaluate existing workflows and design program improvements that reduce friction and increase transparency.</li>
<li>Implement automation, templates, AI-driven tools, and standardized processes to create operational scale.</li>
<li>Lead initiatives that improve performance visibility, streamline data sources, and enhance organizational readiness for leadership reviews.</li>
<li>Partner with system owners and cross-functional teams to improve Salesforce usability, reporting structures, and data integrity.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Have you got what it takes?</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Program &amp; Project Management (planning, governance, execution, risk management)</li>
<li>Revenue &amp; Forecasting Analysis (MRR/ARR frameworks, pipeline visibility)</li>
<li>KPI Design, Governance &amp; Leadership Reporting</li>
<li>Salesforce Reporting, Data Quality &amp; Dashboard Insights</li>
<li>Advanced Excel &amp; Power BI</li>
<li>Process Documentation, Workflow Design &amp; Automation</li>
<li>Executive Communication &amp; Presentation Development</li>
<li>Cross-Functional Stakeholder Management &amp; Alignment</li>
<li>AI-Driven Productivity &amp; Reporting Enhancements</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Role Impact</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Programs are delivered on time and in scope, with clear structure and strong cross-team alignment.</li>
<li>Leadership has consistent, accurate visibility into operational, sales, and revenue performance.</li>
<li>Decision-making quality improves through centralized data, elevated insights, and streamlined executive reporting.</li>
<li>Organizational scale increases through process standardization, automation, and workflow optimization.</li>
<li>Cross-functional execution becomes more predictable, efficient, and accountable.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Experience in Program or Project Management within SaaS, GTM, or operational environments.</li>
<li>Strong analytical background with the ability to translate complex data into leadership-ready insights.</li>
<li>Experience partnering closely with senior leadership in a fast-moving environment.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to streamline processes, deliver programs, and build scalable frameworks.</li>
</ul>
