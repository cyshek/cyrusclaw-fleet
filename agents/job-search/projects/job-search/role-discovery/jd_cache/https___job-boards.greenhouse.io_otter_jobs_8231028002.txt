# Product Manager Growth - San Francisco, CA

<p><strong>Who we are</strong></p>
<p>In the past, to be a successful restaurateur, you simply had to have a passion for food and a passion for people - but to succeed as a digital restaurateur you also need to have a passion for technology. We believe in the joy of serving others, and that's why we created Otter – to help restaurateurs succeed in online food delivery. Restaurants around the world, both large and small, including Chick-fil-A, Ben &amp; Jerry’s, KFC, and Eataly trust our software to power their delivery business. We increase sales, reduce order issues, and decrease delivery headaches.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<p>As Product Manager for Product-Led Growth (PLG), you’ll own the roadmap for how Otter grows through the product itself. This role is focused on building a self-serve growth engine that improves acquisition, activation, monetization, and retention. You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Design and run experiments to improve funnel efficiency, reduce CAC, and increase conversion from lead → customer → expansion.</li>
<li>Build features and flows that generate organic growth.</li>
<li>Drive monetization through pricing experiments, upsell opportunities, and CRM-driven campaigns that expand LTV.</li>
<li>Partner with Marketing, Sales, and Data Science to enrich leads, improve attribution, and optimize channels.</li>
<li>Define and track success metrics – CAC, LTV, conversion rates, activation velocity, and self-serve revenue.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with engineering, design, and GTM to ship fast, learn quickly, and scale what works.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we’re looking for</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of product management or relevant experience.</li>
<li>Proven track record of building and scaling growth-focused product experiences.</li>
<li>Strong quantitative skills: comfortable with SQL, experimentation, and growth modeling.</li>
<li>Creative problem solver with a bias toward fast learning and iteration.</li>
<li>Excellent communicator who can align GTM, data, and product teams around a shared growth roadmap.</li>
<li>Bonus: experience in SaaS, marketplaces, or restaurant/commerce technology.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Why join us</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Growing market: You’ll be focused on an $80 billion market that’s projected to reach at least $500 billion by 2030 in the US alone.</li>
<li>Industry transformation: You’ll be part of a team redefining how restaurants run both online and offline.</li>
<li>Impact at scale: What you build will directly power thousands of restaurants and millions of diners.</li>
<li>Collaborative environment: You’ll work closely with talented colleagues across teams, with the support and guidance to grow your career.</li>
</ul>
