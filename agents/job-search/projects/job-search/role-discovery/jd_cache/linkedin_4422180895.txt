About The Role
We are looking for a highly-motivated Partner Solutions Engineer - Ads in Palo Alto. This is not a traditional support role. You will operate as a forward-deployed engineer at the intersection of product, engineering, and our partner ecosystem, owning the technical relationship with our most strategic 3P integration partners.
We are actively building next-generation integration flows with partners. This role exists to make those integrations succeed, accelerate adoption of our CAPI and tracking infrastructure, and ensure that integration friction gets fed back into the product rather than buried in a ticket queue. 90% of this role is partner-facing: guiding advertisers and integration partners through correct implementation, troubleshooting issues quickly, providing strategic guidance, and proactively identifying gaps in our APIs and flows. The remaining 10% is hands-on: when you see a recurring friction point, you build the fix.
We want someone who treats every recurring partner issue as a signal that something in our product, API, or documentation can be improved, and who has the technical depth to act on it. You will work directly with AppLovin's engineering teams and leadership, with real influence over how our integration ecosystem evolves. The partners you support will be some of the most strategically important to Axon's growth.
A Day in the Life
Partner Integration & Enablement
Own the end-to-end technical onboarding and integration experience for strategic 3P partners across CAPI, web SDK, and APIs
Guide advertisers and partners through correct implementation of Axon's tracking and data infrastructure, proactively identifying and resolving misconfigurations before they impact performance
Serve as the primary technical point of contact for partner-specific issues across new integration pilots, including API flows and sandbox/testing environments
Drive adoption by translating complex technical requirements into clear, actionable guidance for both technical and non-technical audiences
Analytical Problem Solving
Investigate, reproduce, and resolve technical issues with web SDK, CAPI, and API integrations, pinpointing root causes through data analysis
Query and visualize large data sets to identify anomalies, performance drops, attribution discrepancies, and integration edge cases
Build internal tooling and scripts to streamline troubleshooting, automate validation steps, and accelerate partner debugging
Spot patterns across partner issues and translate recurring friction points into concrete product improvement recommendations
Cross-Functional Impact
Work closely with AppLovin's engineering teams to architect and prioritize solutions to integration problems at scale
Act as the connective tissue between partners and our internal engineering teams, ensuring partner feedback directly influences product and API roadmap decisions
Support new integration pilots across partners, owning sandbox validation and proactive issue resolution so core engineering stays focused on building
Influence decision-making through data-centric presentations to internal stakeholders and leadership
Who You Are
2+ years of professional software engineering experience, with production-quality code in dynamic environments
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related technical field, or equivalent practical experience
Strong foundation in web-based integrations: experience with CAPI, server-side tracking, APIs, pixels, or equivalent ad tech infrastructure
Proficiency in at least one scripting or general-purpose language (Python, JavaScript, or similar) with the ability to build lightweight tooling independently
Experience querying large data sets with SQL; comfort working directly in data to debug, validate, and surface insights
Clear, precise written communicator, equally at ease in internal technical discussions and external partner communications
Preferred Qualifications
Experience in ad tech, performance marketing, or e-commerce advertising, with familiarity with attribution, data signals, ROAS, and measurement concepts
Hands-on experience with e-commerce platforms such as Shopify, Google Tag Manager, or equivalent
Experience working with enterprise or strategic B2B partners in a technical capacity
Track record of identifying product gaps from operational experience and driving improvements back into engineering roadmaps
Comfort operating in a fast-moving, ambiguous environment where the answer isn't always written down
Prior experience in a solutions engineering, partner engineering, or forward-deployed engineering role
