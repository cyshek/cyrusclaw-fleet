# Solutions Architect – Amazon Connect

Job Title: Solutions Architect – Amazon Connect
Focus: Cloud Contact Centers | Amazon Connect Implementations
Location: Remote (US-based)
Employment Type: Full-time / Contract / Consulting (flexible)
About The Role
We are looking for an experienced Solutions Architect with hands-on expertise in Amazon Connect to design, implement, and scale cloud-native contact center solutions for innovative customers.
This role is ideal for someone who has implemented Amazon Connect in real-world environments, enjoys working closely with business and technical stakeholders, and is comfortable delivering POCs, pilots, and production-ready solutions in fast-moving settings.
About Us
AES is a 22-year-old niche boutique consulting firm with a strong track record supporting large enterprise customers. Building on that foundation, AES has launched a dedicated innovation and startup-focused business unit delivering rapid solutions, POCs, and modern cloud architectures for high-growth organizations.
This role offers the opportunity to work on modern customer experience platforms while leveraging the stability and credibility of an established consulting firm.
