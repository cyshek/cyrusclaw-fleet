# digital product manager, Delivery

Now Brewing – digital product manager, Delivery! #tobeapartner
From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. We are known for developing extraordinary leaders who share this passion and are guided by their service to others.
As a Starbucks digital product manager, Delivery, you will play a key role in influencing how customers experience the Starbucks brand beyond the walls of our coffeehouses. You will manage key aspects of Starbucks Delivery to enable growth and innovation in one of Starbucks’ fastest growing businesses.
As a digital product manager, Delivery, you will…
Analyze performance data and user feedback to identify and prioritize areas for optimization
Manage and streamline processes to ensure smooth business operations end-to-end
Support discovery process including ideation, opportunity assessment and business casing
Define, document and validate requirements for features and enhancements
Collaborate with Operations to optimize Delivery order fulfillment in our coffeehouses
We’d Love To Hear From People With
3+ years prior work experience in brand management, program management, digital product management, and/or operations
Experience working with external partners, including strong prioritization skills to balance partner asks with internal constraints and priorities
Ability to manage day-to-day operations, escalations, and partner requests while maintaining a long-term product vision
Ability to manage multiple initiatives, tests, and partner requests in parallel
Strong communication skills, both written and verbal
Proficient in data-informed decision-making and comfortable using metrics to evaluate impact and tradeoffs
Curious, highly motivated self-starter with strong ownership and accountability
