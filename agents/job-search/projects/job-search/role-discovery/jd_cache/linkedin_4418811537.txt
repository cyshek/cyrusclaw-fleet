# Sales Engineer

Company Description
Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories (SEL) is a global leader in designing and manufacturing products and solutions that enhance the safety, reliability, and efficiency of electric power systems. With over 40 years of innovation, SEL's devices, software, and systems serve customers in 176 countries. Headquartered in Pullman, Washington, SEL partners with public utilities and private industries worldwide, offering an industry-leading 10-year warranty and lifelong no-cost technical support. SEL's groundbreaking innovations continue to modernize and safeguard the global power grid, making reliable energy access possible for diverse communities and global partners.
Role Description
SEL has multiple full-time roles for a Sales Engineer based in Lynnwood, WA, Beaverton, OR. or Anchorage, AK. As a Sales Engineer, you will support customers by providing technical expertise, ensuring products meet their needs, and addressing any technical challenges. Responsibilities include conducting product demonstrations, assisting in designing solutions, delivering technical consultations, collaborating with the sales team to identify and meet customer requirements, and maintaining strong customer relationships through regular communication and support.
Qualifications
Proficiency in Sales Engineering techniques and principles
Experience in providing Technical Support for products and solutions
Strong Communication skills, including the ability to convey technical information effectively
Background in Sales and Customer Service, with a focus on building and maintaining client relationships
Technical aptitude with a Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field preferred
Problem-solving skills with attention to detail and the ability to handle multiple projects
Interest in the energy or utilities industry and familiarity with power systems or related technology is a plus
