OVERVIEW
As Solutions Architect you will be responsible for developing leading edge digital
solutions that span multiple functions, like Product, Engineering, QE, UX, MarTech,
and Data. You will help shape technology strategy, evolve and future proof our
platform. The ideal candidate will be motivated and proactive with a strong technical
background, leadership, and communication skills; and a track record of delivering
high-quality software.

for digital retail operations

for adoption of good engineering practices, managing waste and technical debt
