# Associate Product Manager

Job Summary
The Associate Product Manager is responsible for the development and management of small and/or basic product initiatives that serve to drive key KPIs across a given product area. This role collaborates closely with engineering, design, and business teams to define product requirements, prioritize the backlog, and ensure seamless execution of product roadmaps.
Job Responsibilities
Responsible for supporting product management activities for one or more digital products. May have full product management accountability for smaller products or product enhancements. Conducts technical and market research to support product managers.
Identifies and resolves issues for assigned product area that may impair the team’s ability to meet goals.
Works closely with the engineering technology team and user interface designers to define detailed key product requirements.
Identifies and quantifies the customer segments with strong growth potential, creating the business case for targeting. Coordinates the work of cross-functional teams. Manages the ROI to ensure benefits outweigh costs to move forward into execution.
Measures and analyzes impact of existing functionality and growth initiatives.
Identifies opportunities to innovate and improve, providing additional benefits to the customer.
Builds strong internal cross-functional relationships to drive implementation and goals.
Conducts business and financial analysis, including forecasting, monitoring and reporting.
Identifies industry best practices and builds vendor relationships. Understands how technology advancements drive sales and increase customer satisfaction.

Founded in 1901, Walgreens (www.walgreens.com) has a storied heritage of caring for communities for generations and proudly serves nearly 9 million customers and patients each day across its approximately 8,500 stores throughout the U.S. and Puerto Rico, and leading omni channel platforms. Basic Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree and at least 2 years of product management experience in on-line technology OR High School/GED and at least 4 years of product management experience in digital technology.
At least 1 year experience in consumer-facing online commerce
At least 1 year experience in product marketing, product/program management, business development, and/or management consulting
Experience working with product development lifecycle.
Experience developing and delivering presentations to various audience levels within an organization.
Experience analyzing and reporting data in order to identify issues, trends, or exceptions to drive improvement of results and find solutions.
Willing to travel up to 10% of the time for business purposes (within state and out of state)
Preferred Qualifications
Experience in retail industry, preferred. Pharmacy or Healthcare industry is a plus.
Experience collaborating with cross functional team to develop strategies that meet department goals within budget and established timelines.
Experience identifying operational issues and recommending and implementing strategies to resolve problems.
