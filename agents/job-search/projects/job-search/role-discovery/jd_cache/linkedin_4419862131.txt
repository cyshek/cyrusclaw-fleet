Work You'll Do
As a GenAI Solutions Architect, you'll work cross-functionally with data scientists, machine learning engineers, business leaders, and industry experts to develop robust AI infrastructure and deployment services for our novel applications. Key to this role is the ability to demonstrate both traditional data engineering expertise, leveraging cloud/enterprise/open-source solutions, and constructing modern IT improvements for organizations across a wide variety of industries. Key responsibilities:
Work with clients to design, architect, and implement AI-enabled solutions from inception to production deployment
Define client GenAI/AI strategy and technical architecture
Stay ahead of industry trends, evaluate new tools/features, pilot innovative solutions, and shape roadmaps for client-facing GenAI tools
Lead technical strategy and blueprinting of AI/GenAI systems, aligning solution architecture with business objectives, security, and scalability requirements
Collaborate with data scientists, engineers and domain experts to integrate AI outputs into software and product workflows
Oversee deployment of AI applications into production environments, ensuring performance optimization, model monitoring, version control and continuous improvement
Participate in architectural and deployment discussions to ensure solutions are designed for successful scale, security, and high availability in the cloud or on prem
Mentor, motivate, and coach junior members on technical best practices and inspire professional development
