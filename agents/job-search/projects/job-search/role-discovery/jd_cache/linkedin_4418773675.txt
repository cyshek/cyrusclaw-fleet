# Outside Sales Engineer

About the Company: We are seeking an Outside Sales Engineer to manage and grow our Eastern Pennsylvania and Southern New Jersey territory. In this role, you will act as a trusted advisor to municipalities, industrial facilities, and engineering firms, helping them solve complex water and wastewater challenges with innovative, reliable equipment.
About the Role: Our most successful Outside Sales Engineers:
Education: Bachelor's degree in Civil, Environmental, or Chemical Engineering, or a related technical field required. Advanced degree or EIT/PE licensure strongly preferred.
Industry Expertise: Demonstrated success in technical sales or process engineering within the water and wastewater industry, with a proven ability to drive revenue growth and build long-term client relationships.
Process Knowledge: Deep understanding of pumping systems, hydraulics, and water and wastewater treatment processes, along with associated equipment technologies.
Communication Skills: Highly effective communicator with the ability to translate complex technical concepts into clear, compelling messaging for a wide range of stakeholders, including operators, consulting engineers, and municipal decision-makers.
Drive and Initiative: Self-directed and results-oriented, with the ability to manage a broad territory, prioritize opportunities, and travel as needed for client engagement and industry events such as WEF and AWWA.
Compliance Requirements: Must be able to successfully pass background and reference checks, as well as a pre-employment drug test.
Licensing and Credentials: Must maintain an active REAL ID-compliant driver's license with a clean driving record, or a valid U.S. passport, as required for access to federal facilities.
Technical Advisory: Act as a trusted technical resource by providing expertise in water and wastewater treatment processes and delivering tailored equipment and service recommendations.
Project Leadership: Manage the full sales lifecycle, including technical presentations, product demonstrations, proposal development, and successful hand-off to project execution teams.
Strategic Collaboration: Partner with engineering and marketing teams to develop and execute territory-specific strategies and create effective technical sales materials.
Industry Intelligence: Stay current on emerging technologies, market trends, and regulatory requirements to serve as a knowledgeable advisor to clients.
