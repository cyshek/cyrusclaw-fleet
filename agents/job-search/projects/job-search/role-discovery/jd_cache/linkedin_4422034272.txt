About the Opportunity
A leading innovator in autonomous mobility technology is seeking a highly motivated Technical Program Manager to support Advanced Hardware Engineering (AHE) Operations and AI Enablement initiatives.

You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams spanning hardware, firmware, software, vehicle engineering, procurement, and manufacturing.
The ideal candidate thrives in fast-paced environments, excels at coordinating complex technical programs, and enjoys building scalable operational processes and AI-powered workflows.

Support TPM operational frameworks that improve engineering efficiency, visibility, and system reliability

Develop concise reports, dashboards, and executive-ready status updates highlighting program health, risks, dependencies, and milestones
Facilitate effective collaboration across diverse teams and stakeholders to support successful program execution
