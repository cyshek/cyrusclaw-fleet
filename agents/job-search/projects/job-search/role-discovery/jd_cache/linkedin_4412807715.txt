# Forward Deployed Engineer

(No H1B or OPT Visa Sponsorship will be provided).
About Shelf:
There is no AI strategy without a data strategy. While generative AI is now mission-critical for most enterprises, nearly 90 percent of AI initiatives fail to reach production. The primary reason is poor data quality. Bad data leads directly to unreliable outputs, undermining trust and ROI.
Shelf exists to solve this problem. We unlock AI readiness by providing the core infrastructure enterprises need to deploy GenAI at scale with confidence. Our platform improves the accuracy and reliability of GenAI systems by identifying and eliminating poor-quality data in documents and files before that data ever reaches a large language model. The result is better answers, faster deployment, and measurable Day 1 ROI.
We work with leading global organizations including Amazon, Mayo Clinic, American Family Insurance, and Nespresso to operationalize their AI strategies. Shelf partners with major enterprise technology providers including Microsoft, Salesforce, Snowflake, Databricks, and OpenAI to bring trusted GenAI solutions into production environments.
Our mission is simple and ambitious: to empower humanity with better answers everywhere.
Role Overview:
The Forward Deployed Engineer is a highly autonomous, customer-facing technical leader responsible for delivering proof-of-value engagements with enterprise prospects. This role sits at the intersection of data modeling, ontology design, and hands-on engineering execution, with direct impact on revenue and customer adoption.
You will independently lead rapid 3–5 day pilot engagements that demonstrate Shelf’s value, solve real customer data challenges, and convert into closed deals. These pilots require deep understanding of enterprise data architectures, the ability to design high-quality semantic and ontology-driven models, and the technical execution skills to configure and implement working solutions quickly.
Reporting to the Field CTO, you will partner closely with Account Executives across the full sales lifecycle, owning the technical evaluation and POV phases. You will also support expansion opportunities within existing customers as part of Shelf’s land-and-expand growth strategy. During engagements, you will collaborate directly with the Engineering team, while also contributing back reusable templates, ontology patterns, and best practices that scale across the organization. This is a rare opportunity to work at the cutting edge of AI and data quality, embedding directly with enterprise customers to prove value in days, not months.
