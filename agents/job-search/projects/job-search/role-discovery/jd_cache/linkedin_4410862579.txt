# Product Manager, Strategic ISV Products

Company Overview
Docusign brings agreements to life. Over 1.5 million customers and more than a billion people in over 180 countries use Docusign solutions to accelerate the process of doing business and simplify people’s lives. With intelligent agreement management, Docusign unleashes business-critical data that is trapped inside of documents. Until now, these were disconnected from business systems of record, costing businesses time, money, and opportunity. Using Docusign’s Intelligent Agreement Management platform, companies can create, commit, and manage agreements with solutions created by the #1 company in e-signature and contract lifecycle management (CLM).
What you'll do
As Product Manager - Strategic ISV Products, you will define and execute the strategy for Docusign's integrations with key strategic partners and Independent Software Vendors (ISVs). Your initial focus will be on delivering seamless Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) integrations with the goal of building a scalable framework for expansion to other enterprise ecosystems. You will own the integration lifecycle, from discovery to adopted solution, ensuring Docusign IAM is a native extension of partner platforms for mutual customers. This is a highly cross-functional role, sitting at the intersection of product, engineering, marketing, business development, and the partner ecosystem. You will work closely with Senior Product Management and Business Development leaders, Prospects, Customers, Compliance, Security, Support while collaborating with User Experience, Engineering, IT, Marketing, Product and Program Management to help develop the roadmap, vision, definition, design and deliver the underlying platform systems and applications necessary to support continued system scale, a broadening product portfolio, the basis for new innovative product capabilities, and improve operational efficiencies related to these industries. Your objectives will be directly linked to business metrics such as revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
This position is an individual contributor role reporting to the Senior Director of Product Management.
Responsibility
Support the definition and own the execution of technical product strategy for Docusign’s ISV integration platform
Own the end-to-end product roadmap for specific Docusign-integrated products and experiences with market-leading Enterprise Chat and Workflow ISV partners, from initial concept and partner alignment to development, launch, and post-launch iteration
Champion a data-driven culture, utilizing metrics such as adoption rates, feature usage, data synchronization success, and the impact on customer retention and expansion to measure success and identify new opportunities
Provide drivership across multiple product and engineering teams, using your influence and deep market knowledge to ensure core Docusign platform roadmaps (e.g., APIs, Maestro, Navigator) account for the needs of our ISV partners and integration use cases
Collaborate with Business Development, Partner Marketing, and Sales teams to build joint GTM plans, create field enablement content, and support our mutual customers with our partners
Own timely, transparent communication to leadership on the progress of their charter, attainment of key metrics, and strategic roadmap

What you bring
Basic
5+ years of product management experience in enterprise SaaS, platform, or integration-focused roles
Experience identifying customer and partner problems, defining joint product strategy, and delivering complex, cross-functional solutions that achieve high adoption
Experience working in an Agile development environment in a global/multi-geolocated setup
Experience engaging in design discussions with engineering on topics like API design, data modeling, embedded components, and integration architecture
Preferred
An experienced influencer with the ability to collaborate well and drive alignment in a highly matrixed environment, especially with external partners and internal GTM teams
A data-driven mindset with experience using analytics to inform product strategy, prioritization, and adoption
