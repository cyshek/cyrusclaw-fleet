# Solutions Architect

ARIS is a Process Intelligence company that helps organizations discover, create, and transform their processes to deliver measurable business outcomes and scale AI with confidence.
It combines process design, real execution insight, and AI-driven optimization in a single, integrated platform-enabling organizations to build a governed digital twin of their operations, understand how work actually happens, and continuously deliver value across the enterprise.
By providing the process foundation, visibility, and governance required for AI adoption, ARIS enables businesses to deploy and scale Agentic AI safely and effectively, ensuring agents operate on trusted processes, data, and controls.
With over 30 years of expertise and recognition from leading analysts such as Gartner and Forrester, ARIS is trusted by global enterprises to turn process intelligence into real business impact.
For more information, visit www.ARIS.com and follow ARIS on LinkedIn.
Be you, join us.
We are currently seeking a Solutions / Value Architect to join our Pre-Sales team in the USA (remote).
In this role, you will support complex enterprise sales engagements by connecting process intelligence capabilities to measurable business outcomes. You will partner closely with the go-to-market team at account level to shape opportunities, lead value-based discovery, and articulate differentiated business impact in competitive sales cycles.
Positioned at the intersection of business strategy, process excellence, enterprise technology, and AI-driven transformation, you will help customers understand why change matters, not just how the platform works. Acting as a trusted advisor to both business and technical stakeholders, you will help ARIS drive pipeline growth, improve win rates, and expand long-term customer value.
Essential Functions
Partner with Account Executives to shape deal strategy, account plans, and executive engagement models.
Lead value-focused discovery sessions to uncover business challenges, operational friction, risk exposure, and transformation objectives.
Translate customer priorities into clear value hypotheses aligned to process performance, cost optimization, risk reduction, and operational excellence.
Develop quantified value narratives, including ROI drivers, efficiency gains, compliance impact, and transformation outcomes.
Build and articulate business cases and outcome models that resonate with executive stakeholders, including CIOs, COOs, CFOs, and Transformation Leaders.
Apply business case discovery and value articulation methods, including ROI, IRR, NPV, ROCE, cost analysis, and related financial impact models.
Position BPM, process intelligence, GenAI, automation, and agentic AI as strategic enablers of enterprise transformation, not just technical tools.
Design and deliver value-oriented demonstrations, Proof of Value engagements, executive presentations, and strategic workshops.
Clearly articulate business and competitive differentiation, especially against process-intelligence-centric competitors.
Contribute to RFP/RFI responses, executive summaries, and value-based proposals.
Provide structured competitive intelligence and field feedback to Sales and Product teams.
Share best practices, assets, and methodologies across the Pre-Sales organization.
Maintain a strong point of view on how agents, AI, agentic AI, and automation are changing the process landscape and the implications for enterprise operating models.
