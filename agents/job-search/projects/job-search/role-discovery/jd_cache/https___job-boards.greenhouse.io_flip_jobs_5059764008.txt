# Product Manager

<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>TITLE:</strong> Product Manager</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>REPORTING TO</strong>: Product Lead</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><strong>LOCATION</strong>: Brooklyn, NY (Onsite Monday - Friday)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Who The Flip Is Flip?</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Ever call your favorite eCommerce brand or healthcare provider, and have a crazy good Voice AI answer? That’s Flip. Hundreds of brands from Under Armour and Tory Burch in retail to Experity and Fast Pace Health in healthcare trust Flip to have millions of phone calls with their customers every month.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">We’ve built our company on the idea that the best way to grow is to have a reference list as long as our customer list, and a constant belief that the team is the best thing we’ve built. Both are true today and increasingly so every day.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Many on our team would tell you this is their favorite place they’ve ever worked, and the hardest they’ve ever worked. It’s ideal for people who want to go all-in, do the work, ride the roller coaster, and have a great Flippin time doing it. We’re a small and mighty team of 50 today with offices in NYC, LA, and the UK.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>What You’ll Do</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Own defined areas of Flip’s product end to end, including defining product direction, shaping solutions, and driving execution across core voice experiences, automation capabilities, and real-world product behavior. Reporting to the Product Lead, you set direction and ensure execution within your scope, defining and deciding what gets built, what doesn’t, and why.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You’ll work on problems like:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Automating complex customer calls (subscriptions, billing, healthcare workflows)</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Designing AI behavior in ambiguous, multi-turn conversations</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Improving retention, resolution rates, and customer experience through automation</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Building systems that combine voice, AI, APIs, and real-time decisioning</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">This role sits at the intersection of:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">product</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">AI systems</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">customer experience</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">business outcomes</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Day-to-Day</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You care about the customers’ customers and understand their needs along their consumer journey</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Own the success of one or more core product features, focusing on measurable outcomes and real-world impact - not just outputs, timelines, or feature delivery&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Collaborate with Customer Success, Engineering, Sales, and Marketing to ensure strong product adoption, clarity of value, and real customer impact.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Comfortable meeting with our customers (Retail or Healthcare companies), and are confident in showcasing to them how we can increase their phone automation over time&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">A copilot to Engineering when it comes to setting priorities, making detailed specs, helping with QA, as each additional call automated is an additional dollar in revenue&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Know why each new feature is impactful and important and can align with Marketing on getting it to the right customers&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Set a high bar for execution, usability, and coherence across the vertical portal</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Excited to continue delivering our WEEKLY product releases (https://flipcx.com/product-releases/)</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><strong>Who You Are, As A PM</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You’ve built products where success depended on getting the system behavior right — not just shipping features</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You think in terms of outcomes (revenue, retention, efficiency) - not just features</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You’re comfortable working on products that combine software, data, and AI behavior</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You have strong intuition for systems, APIs, and product constraints (latency, reliability, scale)</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You’ve worked on or closely with AI, automation, or complex workflow products</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You can design experiences for high-stakes user actions (payments, cancellations, order by phone, scheduling)</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You are highly data-driven and define success through clear, measurable metrics</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You are known for thinking through edge cases, failure modes, and real-world usage</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">You thrive in fast-paced environments where you define the problem before solving it</span></li>
<li style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Eager and willing to support your teammates - just like they will with you!</span></li>
</ul>
