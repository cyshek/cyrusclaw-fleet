What we’re looking for:
5+ years managing complex data center infrastructure or deployment programs
Strong understanding of AI/GPU infrastructure, power density, cooling, networking, and deployment sequencing
Experience bridging construction handover and hardware deployment execution
Strong stakeholder management and cross-functional leadership skills
Comfortable operating in fast-paced, high-growth environments with evolving priorities
