About Document Crunch
Document Crunch is a premier AI-powered contract intelligence platform for the construction industry. By simplifying complex construction documents, Document Crunch empowers project teams to understand their risks and manage contracts with confidence. As part of the Trimble ecosystem, this technology serves as "intelligent DNA," accelerating AI adoption to create a future of zero project disputes and increased transparency across the AECO industry.
What Makes This Role Great
In this role, you'll be at the forefront of translating construction industry realities into winning solutions, directly influencing product strategy, market positioning, and customer success outcomes. You will connect customer business needs to our AI-driven platform and act as a thought leader internally and externally, guiding customers on how to manage risk more effectively as we move the construction industry toward a world of zero disputes and stronger relationships.
Key Exciting Responsibilities
Own deep industry understanding of customer needs and workflows, converting them into market opportunity briefs and pitches that drive customer success.
Collaborate with Product, Sales, Marketing, and CS as the primary conduit for voice-of-customer signal, gathering unbiased customer business needs, vetting pain points, and feeding product discovery, prioritization, and roadmap planning.
Spearhead packaged solution development in partnership with Sales and Product Marketing, positioning their value and supporting internal and external education on how to employ them to improve results.
Drive excellence in delivery by specifying key use cases, defining what "great" looks like for each, and ensuring quality standards are met as we deliver AI solutions to address them.
Act as a thought leader, internally and externally, defining solutions and guiding customers on how to use them to manage risk more effectively across the project lifecycle.
Essential Skills & Experience
5+ years in construction operations, construction tech, implementation, or product-adjacent roles in vertical SaaS.
Proven track record of translating complex industry workflows into crisp requirements, messaging, and repeatable solutions that deliver measurable outcomes.
Strong problem-solving skills with a passion for delivering "visionary pragmatist" results, working with high agency in fast-paced, ambiguous environments that actually work.
Strong cross-functional influence, with the ability to align Product, Sales, Marketing, and CS without formal authority.
Excellent communication and storytelling skills in both written and verbal forms.
Bonus Points For
Experience in construction risk management, contract compliance, or legal tech.
Familiarity with AI-driven product experiences and evaluation frameworks.
Previous involvement in go-to-market launches for construction or vertical SaaS products.
