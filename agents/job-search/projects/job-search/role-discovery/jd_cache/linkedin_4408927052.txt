# Solutions Architect

About Us
Simon AI was founded in 2015 by a team of successful serial entrepreneurs. We are an enterprise customer data platform that empowers marketers to create personalized data-driven experiences for the customers. We’re scrappy problem solvers who believe in tackling big challenges with disruptive thinking and giving our customers the support they need to deliver great next-generation experiences at scale.
At Simon, we firmly believe that business success starts and ends with people. We all do our best work when we are surrounded by other friendly top performers who want to succeed together. This attitude is core to our values. When you trust your team, invest in their development, and give them ownership, great things happen.
The Role
Solutions Architects are primarily responsible for integrating and onboarding new customers, onboarding existing customers to new product offerings. You'll be executing short and long-term customer projects through a queue. You will coordinate closely with the CS organization which owns each account to deliver technical discovery, stakeholder management, and architectural recommendations. We’re looking for process-oriented people, who enjoy working in a fast-paced environment, and who love learning. You are critical to the long-term success of our customers as you guide them through complex integrations and campaign optimization.
What You’ll Do
Lead customer onboarding and professional services engagements.
Contribute to the design and improvement of processes and materials that enhance efficiency and customer experience during onboarding
Drive rapid time-to-value through quality project execution and deliverables that meet Simon's standards for technical excellence
Work with CS, product, and engineering teams to advise them on how Simon will fit into their technology stack
Maintain and manage internal stakeholders in support of fostering a cohesive and compelling customer experience
Take ownership of technical workstreams for various internal and external projects.
Advocate customer business use-cases to product development
Understand and document client scope and integration approaches with exceptional technical clarity, ensuring successful project execution and seamless transition of technical stakeholders as clients move from onboarding to value creation
Qualifications
5+ years of professional work experience; at least 2 of which are in solutions architecture, sales engineering, or SaaS implementation
Experience with complex data models and mapping business needs to technical needs and vice versa
Exceptional technical writing skills with ability to produce clear documentation for technical implementations, integration guides, and customer-facing materials
SQL expertise or similar relevant programming languages
Deep understanding of APIs, web/mobile SDKs, and related technologies
Results-oriented strategic thinker who is able to thrive in an unstructured and fast-paced environment
Intellectually curious and a problem solver
Superior emotional intelligence and communication skills
Ability to quickly absorb and comprehend the ever-changing landscape of data and marketing technology (prior knowledge a plus)
Ability to work independently to deliver outcomes, and collaborate with and across teams
Bachelor's Degree (Computer Science, Data Analytics or technical a plus)
