# IAM Solutions Engineer

About The Role
What if your deep knowledge of identity systems and access-control logic could directly strengthen the security posture of organizations at scale? We're looking for an experienced IAM Solutions Engineer to evaluate identity workflows, authentication mechanisms, and permission structures — identifying gaps, inconsistencies, and opportunities for improvement across complex environments.
This is a fully remote, flexible contract role built for seasoned IAM professionals who can work independently, think critically, and deliver clear, actionable findings.
Type: Hourly Contract
Location: Remote
Schedule: Flexible — work on your own terms
What You'll Do
Review end-to-end identity workflows covering authentication and authorization logic
Analyze access-control policies for least-privilege alignment and misconfigurations
Validate SSO, MFA, federated identity, and directory integrations across platforms
Identify security gaps in privilege provisioning and identity lifecycle management
Document permission structures in clear, structured, and actionable formats
Support recurring IAM reviews across multiple identity platforms and environments
Surface improvement recommendations grounded in industry best practices
Must-Have
Who You Are
Hands-on experience with IAM platforms, identity protocols, and access-control models
Strong understanding of authentication flows — SAML, OIDC, and OAuth
Ability to evaluate complex permission structures and translate findings into clear documentation
Methodical, detail-oriented approach with strong independent judgment
Comfortable working asynchronously and delivering quality work without close supervision
Nice To Have
Experience designing or implementing enterprise IAM solutions
Familiarity with zero-trust architecture principles and deployment patterns
Background in security auditing, compliance frameworks, or identity governance
Why Join Us
Work on meaningful, high-impact security projects that directly influence real-world access controls
Fully remote and flexible — structure your work around your schedule
Freelance autonomy with the consistency of ongoing, structured engagements
Sharpen your expertise across diverse identity platforms and enterprise environments
Potential for extended and recurring contract work as new projects launch
