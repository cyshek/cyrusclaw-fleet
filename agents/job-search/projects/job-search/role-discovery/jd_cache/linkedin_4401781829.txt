# Sales Engineer

About Corintis
Corintis offers innovative microfluidic cooling technologies for AI chips/GPUs and CPUs used in data centers. Working with many of the world’s largest tech companies, our solutions improve compute sustainability and tackle the excessive electricity consumption associated with data center cooling, which consumes more electricity than New York and London combined.
Ranked as the No.1 Engineering startup in Switzerland for 2025, Corintis offers a friendly and team-oriented workplace, bringing together over 70 people from over 25 nationalities to solve the most significant computing challenges of tomorrow. Based on the EPFL campus near Lausanne, we are closely connected to the local ecosystem and are located a few minutes walk from Lake Geneva.
The Role
We looking for an expert in Business development, Customer relationships, Marketing and Sales to join our Thermal Testing Team, where we developing Thermal Test Solutions for our Data Center customers and their supplier, who need a high precision test environment for their product calibration and characterization. We have a great engineering team developing and designing Test Emulation platforms (we call it Therminator) and Thermal Test Vehicles (TTVs), but need to extend the relationships towards our growing existing and new customers.
Your role is essential for the team success, as you are the face to our customers, being the first point of contact, collecting their requirements of feature enhancement needs, change wishes, complains or new orders with time line requests and align them closely with the engineering team. One of your main tasks is to work out & provide quotes for our products based on the customer requests, take care during the assessment process, over the PO placement till the delivery of the products. Further on you attract new customers on exhibitions where we present our products on our own booth. Also take care on our new customer interface at our web side, by providing first product information and collect the application needs of the customer.
In addition you traveling to existing or new customer with product demonstrators to show and convince them, who are mainly located in North America, Europe and Asia.
With your success of presenting & selling Thermal Testing products, you contribute directly to the goals and revenue of Corintis.
As a Business Development & Sales expert, you will be responsible for filling the potential sales funnel and selling our Thermal Testing products by achieving a high customer satisfaction. You will report directly to the head of the Thermal Testing BU and collaborate closely with the engineering team and the Program Managers & Directors of the cold plate business group. This role plays an important part in Corintis goal of becoming the leader in cooling solutions for High Performance Compute Data Centers.
