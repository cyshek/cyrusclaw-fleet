# Solutions Architect, Business AI

Business Engineering is looking for impact-driven, entrepreneurial solutions architects ready to drive projects from ideation to implementation and beyond. We are AI and technology experts that apply our technical skills to bring a unique blend of business and technology expertise to ensure our partners succeed. Our team is at the intersection of business and AI innovation where you will operate similarly to a Forward-Deployed Solutions Architect by solving real-world business challenges with game-changing AI technology that impacts businesses and consumers across the world.You will deliver specialized, customer-embedded technical expertise, bridging the gap between sales and engineering. You will work alongside leading industry executives to turn complex customer needs into scalable, AI-powered products that accelerate business growth. This role lets you apply your passion for customers, engineering, and problem-solving to delivering real-world results at Meta scale. Our Business Engineering team is at the forefront of the AI frontier - and we're just getting started.
Solutions Architect, Business AI Responsibilities:
Contribute to building next-gen AI products and prototypes in collaboration with Meta product engineering teams and cross functional partners to meet business needs
Understand business challenges to define and drive strategies to help businesses grow at scale
Use broad range of technical and soft skills to build productive relationships with our partners, and independently resolve complex technical and business needs
Work closely with sales teams to unlock and develop new opportunities by developing an understanding on business goals and industry trends
Influence the AI industry through technology by participating in industry conferences and presenting to executive-level audience
Understand and apply knowledge of products, technologies and business to build solutions to solve for problems at scale
Break down coding projects into tasks and partner with all applicable teams and partners to meet pre-established goals
Build relationships with team members and cross functional partners
Influence decision-making through presentation of data-centric business topics
Participate in interviewing and on-boarding of new team members
Minimum Qualifications:
Experience with scalable software platforms applications and taking products from zero-to-one to a level of maturity
Experience with web, mobile, or enterprise technology stack
Experience communicating with technical and business audience at prospects or clients
Experience working with LLMs, agentic AI, and/or related AI/ML technologies
Experience working in highly collaborative, dynamic, and ambiguous environments
Experience demonstrating knowledge of industry technology areas and trends
4+ years of experience working with software systems including querying, SQL scripting, and working with API development
Willingness to travel up to 25% of the time
Fluency in English, both verbal and written, is required
Preferred Qualifications:
Experience working in a startup or similar early stage company
Knowledge of the AI, advertising, and social media landscape globally
Experience working with enterprise customers and B2B GTM motion
Experience with agile software development methodologies
Experience working with global teams
Demonstrated ability to integrate AI tools to optimize/redesign workflows and drive measurable impact (e.g., efficiency gains, quality improvements)
Experience adhering to and implementing responsible, ethical AI practices (e.g., risk assessment, bias mitigation, quality and accuracy reviews)
Demonstrated ongoing AI skill development (e.g., prompt/context engineering, agent orchestration) and staying current with emerging AI technologies
