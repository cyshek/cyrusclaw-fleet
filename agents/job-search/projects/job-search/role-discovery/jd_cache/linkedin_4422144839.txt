# Product Manager - USDS

Responsibilities
Team Intro:
The USDS Tech & Product organization is the engineering engine responsible for the reliability, scalability, and performance of TikTok’s ecosystem in the U.S.
We are looking for an experienced Product Manager to help expand our team's work delivering this mission. You will need to wear a variety of hats as you work with these different teams in order to ensure project success.
Responsibilities
- Drive product strategy for the US in multiple business areas.
- Architect and implement product systems, including user-facing products, operational tooling and machine learning.
- Have a deep understanding of machine learning model development and measurement to partner with our Machine Learning Engineering (ie. Algo) teams.
- Lead the ideation, prioritization, and execution of initiatives.
- Lead cross-functional initiatives around user research, product ideation, KPI design, A/B testing and go-to-market.
- Define the long-term product roadmap and OKRs; collaborate with partner teams on product, technical, and privacy decisions.
- Ensure our products and strategies meet local and regional compliance, legal and security requirements.
- Manage competing priorities in a fast-paced, ambiguous, and constantly changing environment.
- Work with global teams across multiple regions, time zones, languages; open to domestic and international travel.
Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications:
- BA/BS degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Economics, Engineering, Business Management or equivalent.
- 5+ years in Product Management for consumer-facing social media, user-generated content, mobile or SaaS companies.
- Strong analytical skills to drive product decisions with data.
- Excellent communication and project management skills, with the ability to work effectively across diverse teams and influence stakeholders at all levels.
Preferred Qualifications
- Experience in understanding, detecting and moderating AI-generated content.
- Experience in product management with cross-functional teams, including both technical and non-technical audiences, across different time zones, cultures, and languages.
- Adept at collaborating with technical teams such as Data Science, Machine Learning and Software Development teams.
About USDS
TikTok USDS Joint Venture LLC is dedicated to the safety and security of millions of Americans who create, discover, and connect with what they love on the apps we operate. The Joint Venture has been established in compliance with the Executive Order signed by President Trump on September 25, 2025. Our foundation is a comprehensive data privacy and cybersecurity program we operate under defined safeguards to protect national security and secure U.S. user data, apps and the algorithm. We safeguard the U.S. content ecosystem, holding decision-making authority for trust and safety policies and moderation. USDS Joint Venture helps ensure Americans can continue to express their creativity, discover new hobbies and interests, and build thriving communities and businesses on a global scale.​
