# Founding Technical Product Manager

Founding Technical Product Manager
Knowtex  •  In-Person/Hybrid (San Francisco)
About Knowtex
Knowtex is building the clinical intelligence platform for clinicians, transforming the way medicine is practiced, and how patient care is completed. Founded by Stanford AI scientists with deep federal, clinical, and technical experience, we’re experiencing explosive growth across both commercial health systems and government healthcare. Our ambient documentation platform scales to thousands of clinicians across hundreds of specialties, and we’re serving clinicians across the country. We’re at an inflection point where cutting-edge AI meets real clinical impact—giving clinicians hours back each day to focus on what matters most: their patients.
Position Overview
Knowtex is hiring a founding Technical Product Manager to own critical product areas at the intersection of AI, clinical workflows, and healthcare infrastructure. These roles require strong technical depth, the ability to work directly with clinicians and healthcare administrators to understand complex workflows, and the skill to translate those insights into clear engineering requirements and product strategy.
Key Responsibilities
Define product roadmap, prioritize features, and drive execution for your product area
Conduct discovery with clinicians, healthcare administrators, and IT leaders to deeply understand clinical workflows and pain points
Translate complex clinical and operational requirements into clear engineering specs, user stories, and acceptance criteria
Partner closely with engineering, AI/ML, and design teams to ship high-quality products on tight timelines
Own product metrics, conduct data analysis, and use quantitative and qualitative signals to drive decisions
Manage cross-functional stakeholders including clinical operations, compliance, sales, and customer success
Evaluate build vs. buy vs. partner decisions for integrations and capabilities
Communicate product vision and progress to leadership, customers, and external partners
