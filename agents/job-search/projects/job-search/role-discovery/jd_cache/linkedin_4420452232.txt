# Solutions Architect

Title: Solutions Architect
Location: Phoenix, AZ 85034 or Remote
Employment Type: 12+ Month W2 Contract. No C2C or sponsorship available
Job Description
Talent Groups is seeking a Solutions Architect to design and deliver SQL Server based solutions for complex legal services projects. This role partners with client services, SQL developers, and business stakeholders to translate requirements into scalable, high performance data solutions.
The ideal candidate brings strong technical depth, clear communication skills, and the ability to confidently present complex concepts to non technical audiences. This is a client facing role requiring ownership, initiative, and collaboration.
Key Responsibilities
Translate settlement agreements, legal documents, and client discussions into technical data solutions
Design scalable SQL Server solutions and data processing strategies for large datasets
Collaborate with project managers and developers to ensure accurate and efficient delivery
Develop, test, and optimize T SQL code
Analyze large datasets to validate data quality and completeness
Communicate technical concepts clearly to business stakeholders
Manage multiple priorities in a fast paced environment
Required Qualifications
Experience
5+ years leading development of technical solutions
10+ years working with SQL Server or other relational databases
Experience analyzing and processing large, complex datasets
Advanced T SQL development and performance tuning experience
Strong ability to translate business processes into technical solutions
Core Skills
Technical fluency with business translation ability
Strong client facing communication skills
Initiative and ownership mindset
Practical problem solving across technical and business constraints
Strong organization and collaboration skills
Preferred Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
Experience in legal services, claims administration, or regulated industries
Experience with enterprise scale data migration or analytics platform
