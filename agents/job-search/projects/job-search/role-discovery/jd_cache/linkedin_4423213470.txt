# Sales Engineer

One of the largest HVAC manufacturers representatives in the New York Metro area.
ADE is seeking entry-level and experienced individual with a technical / engineering background to join our growing team as sales engineer.
Candidates with an interest in the engineering side will provide inside application and technical support to the engineering and architectural community for the HVAC products our company represents, as well as provide support for our outside engineering consultant team. Experienced individuals will have the opportunity to join the outside consultant team concentrating in their area of expertise.
Candidates with outside sales interests or experience will have the opportunity to join our sales team either assisting or as a part of outside sales calling on mechanical contractors, building owners, and consulting engineers selling the HVAC products our company represents.
Many new products have been added recently, especially on the equipment side; if you are experienced in equipment sales it is worth your time to discuss the opportunities.
The Following Experience is a Plus
Five to 10 years experience in HVAC sales or engineering
Equipment and system experience to specify products on the engineering side
Experience selling system solutions to owners
Salary & Benefits Overview
Salary range: $55,000-$125,000 per year
Salary based on experience
Product training and support provided in house and factory training
Excellent benefits program including medical, dental, 401k, vacation, paid holidays
Opportunity for advancement
