# Product Manager (NetSuite)

🍬 Nassau CandyProduct Manager, Business Applications
📍 Location: Hicksville, NY (Hybrid or Onsite Preferred)
🏢 Department: Business Systems
📊 Reports To: Director, Business Applications
🏢 About Nassau Candy
Nassau Candy is a premier manufacturer, importer, and distributor of specialty confections, fine foods, and promotional products. As we continue to scale, we are investing in modern business systems to drive efficiency, visibility, and growth across our manufacturing, distribution, and promotional business lines.
🚀 Position Summary
We are seeking an experienced Product Manager, Business Applications to lead a strategic ERP transformation to NetSuite in partnership with a third-party implementation firm.
This role serves as the critical bridge between business and technology—focusing not just on delivery, but on defining what to build and why. You will drive prioritization, user experience, and long-term platform evolution to support scalable operations across the organization.
🔧 Key Responsibilities🎯 Product Strategy & Vision
Define and own the NetSuite product vision aligned to company growth strategy
Develop and maintain a multi-phase product roadmap (implementation + post-go-live)
Identify opportunities to standardize, simplify, and digitize processes
Balance short-term delivery with long-term scalability
📋 Backlog Management & Prioritization
Own and manage the NetSuite product backlog across key functions:
Manufacturing (production, quality, lot/traceability)
Distribution (inventory, warehousing, logistics)
Promotional/Souvenir business (pricing, rebates, trade programs)
Translate business needs into user stories, acceptance criteria, and success metrics
Prioritize features based on business value, ROI, and operational impact
Make trade-off decisions between customization and standard functionality
🤝 Business & User Engagement
Partner with stakeholders across Finance, Supply Chain, Operations, and Sales
Lead discovery sessions, workshops, and user interviews
Act as the voice of the user to ensure intuitive and effective solutions
Drive alignment on process changes and system capabilities
🛠️ Collaboration with Delivery Teams
Work closely with:
Internal IT and engineering teams
External NetSuite implementation partners
Participate in solution design sessions
Validate proposed solutions and ensure requirements are clearly understood
Accept or reject deliverables based on defined criteria
📊 Data, Reporting & Insights
Define KPIs and success metrics for NetSuite capabilities
Ensure dashboards and reporting support decision-making
Drive data consistency and governance across business units
Leverage analytics to identify continuous improvement opportunities
🔄 Change Management & Adoption
Champion adoption of new processes and system capabilities
Partner on training strategy and materials
Ensure business readiness for releases
Gather post-launch feedback and drive continuous improvement
🔁 Post-Go-Live Product Ownership
Transition from implementation to ongoing product ownership
Manage release cycles, enhancements, and optimization initiatives
Build internal expertise to reduce reliance on external partners
Drive innovation leveraging NetSuite and its ecosystem
