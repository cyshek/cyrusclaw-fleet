Responsibilities
Team Introduction
The HR operations team endeavours to continuously build and deliver a seamless and positive employee experience across the whole employee lifecycle. We're dedicated to leading with care and have empathy in mind. We strive to develop efficient and simple people processes, systems, policies, and programs. We aspire to ensure employees feel heard and that they are given the appropriate resources and support needed to be effective and efficient during their careers.
Responsibilities
1. Lead automation and process improvement initiatives within HR Operations, identifying high impact use cases and driving implementation;
2. Translate dynamic business processes into structured, system and AI executable workflows; develop clear BRDs and deliver end-to-end solutions;
3. Drive integration of automation capabilities with core HR systems (e.g., People, Workday) and foundational tools (e.g., Bitable/Base) to build scalable solutions;
4. Support adoption of new tools and ways of working through training, enablement, and best practice sharing.
Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications:
1. Bachelor's degree or above, open to all majors and experience levels;
2. Strong sense of purpose and highly self-driven; not bound by the status quo;
3. Agile and innovative mindset, with a strong interest in process optimization and new technologies;
4. Capable of driving cross functional alignment to solve complex problems and synthesizing various methods to find optimal solutions.
Preferred Qualification:
1. Candidates with prior experience in HR strategy, HR products, or AI related fields are highly preferred.

About TikTok
TikTok is the leading destination for short-form mobile video. At TikTok, our mission is to inspire creativity and bring joy. TikTok's global headquarters are in Los Angeles and Singapore, and we also have offices in New York City, London, Dublin, Paris, Berlin, Dubai, Jakarta, Seoul, and Tokyo.​
Why Join Us
Inspiring creativity is at the core of TikTok's mission. Our innovative product is built to help people authentically express themselves, discover and connect – and our global, diverse teams make that possible. Together, we create value for our communities, inspire creativity and bring joy - a mission we work towards every day.​
We strive to do great things with great people. We lead with curiosity, humility, and a desire to make impact in a rapidly growing tech company. Every challenge is an opportunity to learn and innovate as one team. We're resilient and embrace challenges as they come. By constantly iterating and fostering an "Always Day 1" mindset, we achieve meaningful breakthroughs for ourselves, our company, and our users. When we create and grow together, the possibilities are limitless. Join us.​
