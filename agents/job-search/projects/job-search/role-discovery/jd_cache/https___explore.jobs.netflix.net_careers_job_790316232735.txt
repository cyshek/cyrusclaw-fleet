# Product Manager, Plans Innovation
Los Gatos,California,United States of America

At Netflix, our mission is to entertain the world. Together, we are writing the next episode - pushing the boundaries of storytelling, global fandom and making the unimaginable a reality. We are a dream team obsessed with the uncomfortable excitement of discovering what happens when you merge creativity, intuition and cutting-edge technology. Come be a part of what’s next.
As we grow globally, we have increasing opportunities to communicate with and delight our members and prospective members in new ways. One of the primary levers for growth is developing new plans, with differentiated features and price points, so consumers can choose what is right for them.
As the Product Manager, Plans Innovation, you will be responsible for driving price-tiering innovation and new-feature development.
As a Product Manager, you will be expected to design, strategize, execute, and prioritize projects. You should be comfortable at the hub of a fast-paced environment where you interface daily with cross-functional experts. You can build consensus and alignment but are not afraid to push ahead when there remain some critics and lead in the face of ambiguity.
Location: This role can be based in our Los Gatos office (preferred) in a hybrid working model. This role can also be remote-based from the West Coast (PST timezone). Some travel to Los Gatos will be required if remote-based.
As a Product Manager at Netflix, you will:
Build a strategic roadmap and take product concepts from idea through validation, shipping, measurement, and iteration.
Lead a team of stunning colleagues across design, engineering, consumer insights, and data science to deliver outstanding products to our customers.
Build business cases and measure the impact of your product innovation, with your data science partners.
Author strategy memos and presentations, and represent your team’s work to senior executives.
Deeply understand and socialize the evolving needs of our customers, with your consumer insights and design partners.
As the Product Manager, Plans Innovation, you will be responsible for:
Evolution of the plans offering, with a balance between global scale and local relevance
Exploration, evaluation, development, launch, testing, and iteration of potential new features for plan tiers
Collaborating closely with the pricing team, integrating revenue objectives into plans development
Ideal Skills:
Passion for Growth, Product, Pricing, and/or Behavioral Economics!
7+ years of product management experience with a proven record of prioritizing and successfully launching impactful products or platforms.
Direct experience with growth, plans, monetization in a direct-to-consumer business, preferably with global scope where the consumer is paying for the service.
Ability to inspire, motivate and lead a cross-functional team - your ability to build strong relationships is critical to success.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills - especially when writing memos and presenting product ideas to partners across the organization
Comfortable using data to inform priorities and evaluate impact of initiatives
Strong instincts for outstanding customer experience.
Embodies the Netflix real values while also bringing a new perspective to improve our culture. We strongly believe that diversity of experiences, perspectives, and backgrounds will lead to a better environment for our employees and better products for our customers.
