# Technical Sales Engineer

At ARaymond, we create cutting-edge fastening and assembly systems for the machines and products that keep the world moving.
Founded in Grenoble in 1865, and still headquartered in the city to this day, we’re a family-run business with a proud history of innovation – including the invention of the press stud.
Human values have driven our success for over 158 years, embodied by our servant-leadership model of management that empowers employees and puts their wellbeing first.
Today we employ more than 8,500 people in 25 countries worldwide. And we’re committed to leading in sustainability for the global fastening and assembly market.
Connect with a team that’s always behind you
Enjoy the freedom to innovate, grow and make a difference as part of our global sales team. You’ll work in partnership with our clients to provide custom proposals for their fastening and assembly needs.
Position Summary: The Technical Sales Engineer serves as the primary strategic and technical liaison between the company and our key clients. Thriving at the intersection of engineering and commerce, the incumbent acts as a trusted advisor who diagnoses technical challenges and architect solutions, from our portfolio or customized. This role leads the technical discovery process, simplifies complex concepts for stakeholders at all levels, and leverages expert relationship-building skills to negotiate and close high-impact deals. Roles and Responsibilities:
Maps US market landscapes, identifying key decision-makers, supply chain owners, and competitive threats.
Identifies and qualifies prospects aligned with the Value Proposition to meet Business Plan revenue targets.
Converts high-level business plans into actionable account strategies for specific target verticals.
Feeds local market information to PLM to support new product lines and global market offerings.
Secures stakeholder access through tailored elevator pitches and high-impact technical presentations.
Executes the full sales cycle, utilizing negotiation and value-based selling to close complex deals.
Owns and accountable for Pricing, contractual negotiation (development, supply agreements, etc.).
Cultivates long-term client partnerships, anticipating and addressing evolving technical needs.
With support of PLM, helps translates complex requirements into Engineering Requirements for New Developments.
Performs root cause analysis and provides technical troubleshooting during product integration.
Orchestrates communication between customers and internal teams (R&D, Engineering, Logistics) to meet project milestones.
With support of PLM. delivers "Voice of the Customer" insights to R&D and Marketing to influence the product innovation pipeline.
Supports custom solution development to ensure design standards and safety compliance for product launches.
Authors technical documentation and presentations for internal and external stakeholders.
Maintains meticulous records of sales activities, customer engagements, and project status.
Other related duties and/or projects as assigned.
