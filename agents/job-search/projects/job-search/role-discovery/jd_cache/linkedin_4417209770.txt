# Solutions Architect

Job Title: Solution Architect
Location: Pleasanton, CA 94588 (REMOTE)
Duration: 12+ Months
As a Solution Architect, you will be responsible for translating client requirements into differentiated, deliverable solutions by leveraging your in-depth knowledge of technology, function, or platform. You will collaborate closely with the Sales Pursuit and Delivery Teams to develop a winnable and deliverable solution that supports the client value proposition and business case. You are expected to be a subject matter expert, lead and manage the team to perform effectively, make key team decisions, engage with multiple teams, and contribute to important decisions. Providing solutions to problems that impact multiple teams is a critical part of this role. Advanced proficiency in Solution Architecture is required. Advanced proficiency in Databricks Unified Data Analytics Platform is recommended. Advanced proficiency in Requirements Analysis, Microsoft Power Business Intelligence (BI), Microsoft Azure Modern Data Platform, and Healthcare Management is suggested.
Need someone with Databricks, data modeling (star schema) and Power BI metadata design skills.
Need the resource to be an end-to-end solution consultant in the above skills.
Develop comprehensive solution designs that align with client business objectives and technical requirements.
Drive collaboration across cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration and delivery of solutions.
Analyze complex challenges and propose innovative approaches to enhance solution effectiveness and efficiency.
Support continuous improvement by sharing expertise and insights to elevate team capabilities and project outcomes.
Monitor emerging technologies and industry trends to inform solution strategies and maintain competitive advantage.
