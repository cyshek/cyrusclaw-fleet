About The Role
At Daloopa, we’re building AI-powered products that transform how financial analysts work, eliminating manual processes and unlocking efficiency at scale. As a Senior Product Manager, you’ll own and accelerate Scout. Scout is our next-generation AI native product, evolving the way we always intended; from a tool that responds to prompts to one that truly understands, executes, and works alongside an analyst’s workflows. This is a high ownership role focused on transforming Scout into a workflow-driven product used by analysts, not just an Excel prompt-based tool.
You’ll be responsible for defining product direction, driving execution, and turning AI capability into real habitual user behavior. You’ll collaborate with engineering, design, operations, and go-to-market teams to deliver a roadmap that keeps our product differentiated, scalable, and deeply aligned with customer needs. This is an opportunity to build products at the frontier of AI-driven workflows in an industry just beginning its transformation.
