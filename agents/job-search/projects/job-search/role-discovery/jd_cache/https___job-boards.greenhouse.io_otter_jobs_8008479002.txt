# Product Manager - Data Products - Seattle, WA

<p><strong>Who we are</strong></p>
<p>In the past, to be a successful restaurateur, you simply had to have a passion for food and a passion for people - but to succeed as a digital restaurateur you also need to have a passion for technology. We believe in the joy of serving others, and that's why we created Otter – to help restaurateurs succeed in online food delivery. Restaurants around the world, both large and small, including Chick-fil-A, Ben &amp; Jerry’s, KFC, and Eataly trust our software to power their delivery business. We increase sales, reduce order issues, and decrease delivery headaches.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What you’ll do</strong></p>
<p>Join a high-leverage team building Otter’s data products – the foundation that helps restaurants understand, predict, and improve performance. You’ll own end-to-end product outcomes in a portfolio that also includes analytics, live monitoring &amp; alerts, operational insights, and data reliability. You will:</p>
<ul>
<li>Translate operator problems into products that turn messy, multi-channel data into clear, actionable insights.</li>
<li>Raise the trust bar by partnering with platform, data, and finance teams to improve freshness, accuracy, lineage, and metric definitions – so our products become the system of record operators rely on.</li>
<li>Design for action: prioritize use cases that prevent revenue loss, identify defects, and drive measurable operational improvements.</li>
<li>Build for scale and speed: create modular capabilities that accelerate new use cases without custom work.</li>
<li>Work cross-functionally with Engineering, Design, Data Science, GTM, and Customer teams to ship iteratively and learn fast.</li>
<li>Own the outcomes, not just the roadmap: define success metrics and move them – adoption, engagement, customer satisfaction, data SLAs, and revenue impact.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>What we’re looking for</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>3+ years of product management (or equivalent) with data/analytics, platform, or operational tooling.</li>
<li>Demonstrated ability to ship insight-to-action products (dashboards, alerts, workflows, or similar) that delivered measurable business results.</li>
<li>Data fluency: you’re comfortable in SQL, metrics design, and experiment/impact analysis; you can partner deeply with data science and engineering.</li>
<li>Strong product judgment &amp; UX instincts – you know the difference between informative and actionable, and design for busy operators.</li>
<li>Cross-functional leadership across engineering, design, GTM, and customer-facing teams; excellent written and verbal communication.</li>
<li>Bonus: experience with data quality/governance, metric standardization, BI/visualization tools, or multi-location enterprise customers.</li>
</ul>
