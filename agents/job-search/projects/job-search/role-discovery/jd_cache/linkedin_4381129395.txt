# Partner Solutions Architect, Global Partner Development

Description
Amazon Ads is looking for an experienced solution architect to join its global solutions architecture team advising ISVs (AdTech, MartTech and analytics), diverse range of digital advertising agencies as well as large customers who have invested in API-based advertising solutions.
Amazon Ads helps brands create experiences that delight customers and deliver meaningful results. With 300+ million worldwide active customers, and first-party insights based on shopping, streaming and browsing signals, Amazon enables brands to craft innovative campaigns that help customers find and discover anything they want. Our solutions on Amazon.com, services like Twitch, Freevee, Alexa, Amazon Music, and partnerships with third-party publishers and exchanges make Amazon Ads the ultimate amplifier for brands to reach the right audiences in the right places, both on and off Amazon. Amazon Advertising is a key strategic focus for Amazon and is in a rapid and exciting growth phase.
We start with the customer and work backwards in everything we do, including advertising. If you’re interested in joining an innovative team working to build a unique, world-class advertising business with a relentless focus on the customer, you’ve come to the right place.
Amazon Ads Partners help brands achieve and exceed their desired outcomes through tech innovation and expert managed services. Our team builds partnerships across the advertising landscape to drive innovation and growth for all brands, including those that do not sell on Amazon.com. In order to accelerate the next phase of Amazon Ads development, we are committed to transforming the depth, scale and ambition of our partnerships with a diverse landscape of partners across a full range of independent agencies, as well as AdTech and MarTech SaaS providers. With a full suite of ad tech products available, development of partner technical solutions is foundational for customer success.
As Ads partners work across all ad products, supply sources and geographies, this is an exciting opportunity to support the business. As trusted partners and customer advocates, our solutions architects advise organizations on how to develop advanced cloud-based solutions and prioritize their ad tech roadmap. You will have the opportunity to build mindshare and broad use of Amazon ad tech including Amazon Marketing Cloud within the digital advertising community. You should also have a demonstrated ability to provide critical support to address business, products, and technical challenges. You will be partnering with other teams within Ads and AWS such as product, business development, service teams, marketing, and sales, prioritizing your initiatives and aligning goals to maximize long term value.
Key job responsibilities
Build and maintain relationships with tech leaders across ad agencies, SaaS providers and large customers to promote awareness of Amazon Ads ad tech.
Build relationships with partners and customers to become a trusted advisor for their most critical workloads/solutions that support digital advertising and marketing transformations. Hands-on solution architect with strong REST API and SDK development skills to build and scale solutions with partners
Support Global leaders who own achievement of revenue objectives through the adoption of ad products, applications, software, and services, in partnership with business development.
Serve as key business partner to educate customers on the value proposition of Amazon ad tech and participate in architectural discussions to ensure solutions are designed for successful deployment.
Build strong relationships with internal product teams to ensure alignment of the product roadmap, vision, and strategy to fully take advantage of future changes and new markets.
Support the education and training of partners on the positioning of Amazon Ads and contribute to industry thought leadership.
Key Requirements
Microservices & SOA — designing and building distributed systems
Application Integration — API management, event-driven architectures
Cloud Technologies — AWS, Azure, or similar platforms
Emerging Tech — Gen AI, MCP, and agentic solutions
A day in the life
On a day to day basis you will work with Amazon Ads partners to create awareness for, implement, and activate advertising technology use cases.
Collaborate with key stakeholders internally to ensure we bring back customer feedback and increase the efficiency by improving self service capabilities of advertising products

Experience in written and verbal communication skills to communicate with technical and non-technical audiences, including senior leadership
3+ years of customer-facing work, engaging with customer executives, technologists or partners to solve business problems with advanced technologies experience
Experience in online or digital advertising

Preferred Qualifications
Experience in technology/software sales, pre-sales, or consulting
Experience working with end user or developer communities
Experience working directly in customer implementations
2+ years of communicating with and presenting to executive and senior audiences experience
2+ years of leadership experience with business reviews and adapting to work to business review outcomes
