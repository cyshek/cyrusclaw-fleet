# Forward Deployed Engineer

Profound is the marketing platform for the AI era. As people increasingly turn to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini to decide what to buy, we give brands the intelligence to see how AI represents them and the Agents to act on it. Today, ~13% of the Fortune 500, plus companies like Ramp, Figma, Chime, Calendly, and DocuSign, use Profound to turn AI Search from a black box into a measurable growth channel.
Backed by Lightspeed, Sequoia, Kleiner Perkins, and Khosla Ventures at a $1B valuation, we're a lean, fast-moving team across NYC, SF, Buenos Aires, and London, shipping at a relentless pace and defining a new category at the biggest shift in marketing in 25 years. If you want to do the best work of your career at the frontier of AI, come build it with us.
As a Forward Deployed Engineer at Profound, you will be the technical bridge between our engineering team and our Fortune 500 clients. You will combine deep engineering expertise with exceptional communication skills to ensure our most strategic customers successfully integrate and maximize value from our platform.
You will write production code, architect custom integrations, and solve complex technical challenges while sitting in client meetings, presenting to CTOs, and translating between engineering and executive stakeholders. This is not a support role. You are a full-stack engineer who loves working directly with customers.
What you’ll do
Design and build custom integrations connecting Profound’s platform to enterprise systems
Write production-quality code in TypeScript, Python, and Next.js for client-specific features
Architect solutions that handle enterprise-scale data volumes and security requirements
Debug complex issues spanning client infrastructure and Profound systems
Optimize performance for clients processing millions of daily queries
Lead technical discussions with client engineering teams and C-suite executives
Join client calls to understand requirements and translate them into technical solutions
Present technical architectures and implementation plans to diverse audiences
Build trusted relationships with technical champions at Fortune 500 companies
Conduct technical workshops and training sessions for client teams
Identify patterns in client needs and partner with Product to build scalable solutions
Contribute to core product development based on field insights
Build reusable components and libraries for common integration patterns
Document technical solutions and create implementation guides
Influence the product roadmap with direct customer feedback
Lead enterprise proofs of concept that unlock multi-million dollar contracts
Design data pipelines integrating with client business intelligence tools and data warehouses
Build custom dashboards and reporting solutions for executive stakeholders
Develop automation workflows connecting AI insights to business processes

2 or more years of software engineering experience working with production systems

Database experience including PostgreSQL and time-series databases

Rust experience is a plus but not required
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
Ability to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
Comfort presenting to and building relationships with executive audiences
Experience working directly with enterprise clients
Strong problem-solving skills under pressure
Ability to context-switch across multiple client engagements
Strong customer obsession and genuine care for client success
Engineering rigor with a focus on clean, maintainable, production-ready code
Strategic thinking that connects technical work to business impact
Ownership mentality with end-to-end responsibility for client relationships
