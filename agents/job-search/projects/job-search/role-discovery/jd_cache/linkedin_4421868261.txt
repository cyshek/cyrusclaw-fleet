# Azure Solution Architect

Title : Azure Solution Architect
Location : Indianapolis , IA , Location: Should be willing to work from Customer’s Indianapolis location on a hybrid basis
1.     Role Summary
Tata Elxsi seeking an experienced Azure Solution Architect to lead the architecture, scalability, security, and cloud engineering strategy for an enterprise-grade, AI-driven Optimization Platforms deployed on Microsoft Azure.
·       The ideal candidate will design and govern a scalable, secure, high-performance, and future-ready cloud architecture, enabling advanced scheduling, optimization, and data-driven decision-making capabilities across the enterprise.
·       Experience Level: 12+ years of overall experience in software architecture and with 5+ years in Azure Solution Architecture.
