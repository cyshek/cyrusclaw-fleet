About Market Activation
Market Activation (MA) ensures our commercial teams are fully prepared to bring Ads products to market with clarity, speed, and impact. We set the standard for go-to-market readiness and launch execution by:
Driving structured, repeatable launch execution
Translating product capabilities into clear seller workflows, enablement, and GTM strategy
Acting as the connective tissue across Product, Sales, and Operations to ensure aligned, high-quality commercialization
The Role
We are hiring a Program Manager to lead Commercial Readiness for the Media Planning & Activation product domain, one of the most critical operational pillars of our Ads business.
This role sits at the center of product, and sales,and is responsible for ensuring that new capabilities are operationalized, adopted, and scaled effectively across global markets.
Responsibilities
Own end-to-end commercial readiness and launch execution for Media Planning & Order Management products
Effectively execute the change management of new capabilities by collaborating with multiple teams across the organization including Sales, Account Management, Market Planning & Operations, Product, and other cross-functional teams.
Drive change management, including documentation, communication, and enablement inputs
Drive cross-functional alignment on commercialization strategy and end-user positioning.
Lead structured forums to surface risks, unblock decisions, and maintain execution momentum
Translate product capabilities into clear, scalable operational workflows
Coordinate UAT and validate end-to-end workflows to ensure systems and processes are production-ready
Establish feedback loops for business users and Product to surface friction, inefficiencies, and product gaps
Partner with Enablement to align on right-sized training and rollout strategies
About you:
8+ years of experience in the advertising industry, including 5+ years leading complex, cross-functional programs; deep expertise in the CTV and streaming ecosystem
Proven track record of delivering complex initiatives in dynamic, highly cross-functional environments
Experience leading launches of advertising business tools (e.g., media planning, order management, billing systems), including operational readiness, communications, positioning, workflow design.
Highly organized and detail-oriented, with the ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
Demonstrated ability to navigate ambiguity and drive execution across diverse stakeholder groups
Strong relationship builder with the ability to influence and operate as a trusted partner in business transformation
Adaptable and resilient, with a proven ability to thrive in evolving, high-growth environments
