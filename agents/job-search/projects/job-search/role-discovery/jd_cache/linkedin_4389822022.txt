# Product Manager - Recura

Company Overview
Designed for beauty, built for growth, PatientNow brings clinical-grade EMR with intuitive charting, smart AI automation, advanced photography, marketing, patient engagement, and seamless practice management into one elegant system. Everything works together naturally, helping clients create a smooth, intentional patient experience from consultation to checkout. Supported by a U.S.-based team that understands the pace and artistry of aesthetics, PatientNow provides the technology and partnership to scale with confidence.
This role will focus on the Recura product line, a newly integrated part of PatientNow.
Vision: Empowering practices and businesses to improve health and wellness worldwide by helping their clients look and feel their best.
Mission: PatientNow is elevating businesses focused on beauty, wellness and medical aesthetics with innovative, all-in-one software and consulting service solutions. People who partner with us stand out in the industry with strong market differentiation, while streamlining operations for long term success and sustainable growth.

Position Summary
Recura, a PatientNow company, is building an AI-powered growth engine for medical aesthetics and elective care practices. Our platform powers an AI receptionist that manages voice, SMS, scheduling, and payments. These workflows directly impact revenue and patient experience.
As the Founding Product Manager, you will own product strategy and execution across the Recura platform. You will partner closely with the CTO and engineering team to bring structure, clarity, and long-term direction to a rapidly expanding product. This role balances strategy with execution. You will ensure features are grounded in customer workflows and aligned to business outcomes. Your north star is Net Revenue Retention, driven by adoption, value, and a seamless user journey.
Essential Duties And Responsibilities
Own and maintain the product roadmap across voice, messaging, scheduling, payments, and automation workflows
Partner with leadership to define product strategy, priorities, and long-term direction
Translate ideas into clear product requirements including PRDs, user stories, and acceptance criteria
Work closely with Engineering and QA to ensure high-quality and timely delivery
Map and improve end-to-end customer journeys and identify gaps or inconsistencies
Conduct customer interviews and turn insights into product direction
Track adoption and product performance to ensure features drive measurable value
Analyze competitors and market trends to inform strategy
Improve internal communication on what is being built, shipped, and why
Ensure product releases are cohesive and avoid fragmented feature development
