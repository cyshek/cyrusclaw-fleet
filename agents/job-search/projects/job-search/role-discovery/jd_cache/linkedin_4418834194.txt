Responsibilities
The Junior Solution Engineer for Amazon Connect is responsible for advanced operational support, troubleshooting, and configuration management of customer Amazon Connect contact center environments within a managed services model. This role serves as the primary escalation point from Tier 1 and focuses on platform stability, complex issue resolution, and customer-facing operational support that does not require full development or architectural design.
Schedule: Tuesday - Friday, 10AM - 9PM Central Time
Execute and validate approved low-risk production changes across the platform.
Update and maintain configurations including contact flows, IVR tuning, prompts, and routing logic
Ensure accuracy and compliance with established change management standards
Monitor platform health using system and cloud-based monitoring tools
Identify trends, recurring issues, and potential service degradation
Perform proactive remediation to maintain stability and performance
Support service reporting and availability monitoring efforts
Collaborate with customers, Tier 3 engineers, and internal teams to resolve issues and deploy solutions
Mentor Tier 1 analysts and contribute to operational reviews and governance activities
Document and provide initial troubleshooting findings for escalation to higher-tier engineering teams
Qualifications
2–4 years of experience supporting Amazon Connect in a managed services or operational environment
3+ years of IT experience, including at least 1 year supporting cloud-based or contact center solutions
Strong knowledge of contact center configurations including contact flows, queues, routing profiles, and security profiles
Understanding of telephony fundamentals and cloud-based contact center solutions
Familiarity with ITIL-aligned incident and change management processes
Ability to support multi-channel communication environments
Working knowledge of AWS services and Amazon Connect platform capabilities
Experience operating in structured support environments with escalation and incident management responsibilities
Exposure to after-hours or on-call support rotations
AWS Cloud Practitioner certification within 30 Days Required
