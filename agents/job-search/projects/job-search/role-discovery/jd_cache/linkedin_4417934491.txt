# Product Manager - Growth [33102]

As an Product Manager, Growth, you’ll help scale a healthcare store that makes it delightfully simple for people to take care of their health. We provide upfront cash and insurance prices for virtual and in-person visits, prescriptions, labs, imaging, and more. You will build the systems that connect our platform to payers, employer partners, and healthcare networks. This isn’t a maintenance role — it’s foundational. You’ll define how complex external relationships translate into scalable, reliable infrastructure inside our product.
We’re looking for a scrappy, analytical PM who’s comfortable in ambiguity and strong in execution. You’ll dive into messy inputs, structure hard problems, and work side-by-side with engineering to ship integrations, APIs, and workflows that power real-world operations. You’ll set clear roadmaps, define success metrics, and ensure what we build scales cleanly as volume and complexity grow.
This role sits at the intersection of product, data, and backend systems. You’ll turn cross-functional complexity into durable architecture — building the enterprise layer that enables everything else to move faster.
What You’ll Do
Own enterprise-facing product initiatives end-to-end, from problem definition through launch and iteration
Define and maintain a clear enterprise product roadmap aligned to company goals and partner needs
Design and prioritize complex integrations with payers and other external partners, balancing technical constraints with business impact
Translate regulatory, contractual, and operational requirements into clear, testable product specifications
Partner closely with engineering to scope APIs, data models, workflows, and system architecture decisions
Define success metrics and monitor system performance, reliability, and partner experience post-launch
Identify opportunities to simplify, standardize, and scale partner integrations over time
Drive structured decision-making across cross-functional stakeholders
Contribute to the long-term strategy and evolution of our B2B platform
What We’re Looking For
4–8 years of product management experience, ideally in B2B SaaS or integration-heavy environments
Experience owning technically complex product surfaces or infrastructure
Strong analytical skills and comfort working with multi-source data and operational systems
Ability to define metrics, evaluate tradeoffs, and prioritize under constraint
Technical fluency — comfortable discussing APIs, data flows, and system design with engineers
Track record of operating effectively in ambiguous, fast-moving environments
Clear, structured communicator who can align engineering, leadership, and external partners
Nice to Have
Experience working with healthcare, fintech, or other regulated systems
Experience scaling enterprise integrations across multiple partners
Quantitative or technical academic background
This is a hybrid role in SF.
Why Join
We’re an experienced team that’s built in this space before and have a distinctive vision for what healthcare can look like when designed around transparency and simplicity. We believe that modern growth is a product function — not just marketing — and we’re building for the long term.
You’ll be the first Growth PM, reporting directly to the CEO and shaping how we define and drive growth across the company. Your work will have immediate, measurable impact and deep visibility with leadership.
This role is based in San Francisco (near Market and Spear St). We expect our team to work from the office at least three days per week.
