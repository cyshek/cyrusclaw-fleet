## ADDENDUM (2026-06-26): Parent-wiring resolution вҖ” a structural constraint

**The literal task ("add the 5-asset 12-1 book to GATE_PASSING_PARENTS") is not achievable as-is**, for two independent hard reasons discovered while wiring it:

Verified across all legs: **SMA50 is the longest lookback that profiles in these windows; SMA100 already fails.** A 12-1 monthly signal is structurally too slow for the parent profiler.
2. **decide_xsec vs single-name.** The validated book is a `decide_xsec` basket; the profiler/`walk_forward` are single-name only (key on `params['symbol']`). `tests/test_parent_pool_diversity.py` explicitly rejects `decide_xsec` parents (names `allocator_blend` as the disallowed case).

**Resolution (shipped):**
- The validated 5-asset 12-1 book stays on disk at `strategies/xa_tsmom_12_1/` (`decide_xsec`) as the **evidence artifact** and a candidate for a **standalone PAPER tracker** (the correct venue for a slow monthly cross-asset allocator, like `allocator_blend`) вҖ” NOT a mutation parent.
