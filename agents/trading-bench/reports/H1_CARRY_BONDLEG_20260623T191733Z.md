**The signal (monthly duration tilt scaled by curve steepness, per В§5 sketch):**
- At each **month-end T**, steepness = FRED `T10Y2Y` (10yrвҲ’2yr) PIT value (latest obs with date вүӨ T). Curve-slope constant-maturity spreads are daily market quotes (un-revised), so `vintage="latest"` is correct and PIT-safe вҖ” but the **signal is still lagged 1 trading day into the trade** (non-negotiable).
- **Duration weight** `w_dur = clip(steepness / scale, 0, 1)` with `scale = 1.5` pct-pts вҶ’ a clean, monotonic mapping (steeper вҮ’ bigger long-duration tilt; **flat/inverted вҮ’ 0 duration, sit in SHY**). Long-duration sleeve = `TLT` (primary) or 50/50 `TLT`+`IEF` (variant). Remainder `(1вҲ’w_dur)` вҶ’ `SHY`.
- **Vol-target the LEG** to a modest **9% annual** budget using **20-day realized vol** (data вүӨ T), **capped at 1.0Г— (never levered)** вҖ” a diversifier sleeve, not a SPX-beater.

**Anti-lookahead mechanics (the ballgame):**
- Signal computed on data **strictly вүӨ dates[T]**; resulting weights become **effective at T+1** (1-day lag). Weights held constant daily, marked on adjclose returns. On a rebalance day the **freshly-traded weights do NOT capture that day's already-realized return** (they earn starting the next bar) вҖ” conservative, no same-bar leak.
- **Cost** = `(bps/1e4) В· ОЈ|w_new вҲ’ w_old|` charged on the trade day. Round-trip grid 0/1/2/5 bps (monotonic).
- **Sharpe** = `(mean/std)В·вҲҡ252`, sample std **ddof=1**, on the **continuous concatenated daily return series** вҖ” mirrors `runner/fp_sharpe.py:sharpe_from_returns` exactly. **Never median-of-windows.**

### Disposition
**Do NOT promote the bond leg as a standalone sleeve** (fails the standalone Sharpe + uniform-robustness bars). **DO proceed to build the commodity-carry leg** вҖ” this is the green light the task gated on the bond-leg result. The bond leg cleared the *hard* gate (beats its controls, orthogonal, real) and missed only the *magnitude* gate. The H1 thesis is that a **combined** bond+commodity carry sleeve, equal-risk-weighted, is the diversifier вҖ” and the bond leg's profile (positive, orthogonal, control-beating, but thin) is exactly a leg that **wants a second uncorrelated carry leg bolted on** to lift the combined Sharpe over 0.5.

### What the COMMODITY leg must add to make the combined sleeve PASS (the explicit bar)
Treat the bond leg as a **risk-comparable building block**: OOS Sharpe вүҲ0.43, ~5% OOS vol, corrвүҲвҲ’0.15/вҲ’0.20 to the book. For an equal-risk 50/50 bond+commodity carry sleeve to clear **c1 (combined OOS Sharpe > 0.5)**, the commodity leg needs, *at minimum*:
1. **OOS Sharpe вүі 0.4 standalone** (comparable to the bond leg) вҖ” anything materially below ~0.35 drags the combined under 0.5.
2. **Low correlation to the bond leg** (the whole AQR point вҖ” carry legs are "unconditionally low-correlated across asset classes"). Two вүҲ0.43-Sharpe legs combine to roughly `0.43В·вҲҡ(2/(1+ПҒ))` вүҲ **0.54 at ПҒ=0.3, ~0.61 at ПҒ=0** вҖ” i.e. **the diversification alone tips it over 0.5** *if* the commodity leg is real and uncorrelated to the bond leg. That is the entire bet.
3. It must **beat its own EW-of-same-commodity-ETFs control** (mandatory вҖ” the `DBC`/`USO`/`GSG` front-vs-optimized-roll spread must not be pure ETF-mechanics noise) and survive the **2008/2020/2022** stress slices, net of cost.
4. **Caveat to respect:** the commodity-carry ETF proxy (front-roll vs optimized-roll spread) is *dirtier* than the bond leg (expense-ratio drag, tracking error, shallower history ~2006+). If the commodity leg's "signal" is dominated by fund mechanics rather than backwardation/contango curve shape, it fails its EW control and the whole H1 sleeve dies with it.

### Revisit-triggers for the BOND leg specifically
- **Reinstated risk-adj bar / allocator wants an eff-N raiser:** the bond leg is already shelf-worthy as an orthogonal **eff-N** contributor (corr вҲ’0.15/вҲ’0.20) even at 0.43 Sharpe вҖ” if the mandate shifts from "beat SPX raw" to "raise eff-N / Sharpe of the blend," revisit it *as a small risk-weighted sleeve combined with the commodity leg*, not standalone.

- **Combined-sleeve result:** if the commodity leg comes back вүҘ0.4 Sharpe AND вүІ0.3-correlated to the bond leg, build the combined H1 carry sleeve and re-run the full criteria on the *combined* series вҖ” that is the path to an actual PASS.
