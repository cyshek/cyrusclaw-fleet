The Problem

After a job wrapped, the owner manually sent invoices and chased payments weeks later — cash flow constantly stuck in limbo.

What We Built

Job marked complete → invoice auto-generated + sent → reminder at 7 days → escalation call/SMS at 14 days → everything logged to QuickBooks.

Average payment time dropped from 23 days to 6 days — with zero manual chasing from the owner.
