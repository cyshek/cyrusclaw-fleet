# Demo call script for MockSTT.
# Format: "them: ..." (prospect/client) or "me: ..." (the rep using the copilot).
# Lines starting with # are ignored. This simulates a B2B SaaS discovery/closing call.

me: Thanks for hopping on. I know you've been evaluating tools to cut down manual data entry between your support desk and your CRM.
them: Yeah, that's the main pain. My reps are copy-pasting ticket info into Salesforce all day. So tell me how you're different from the others.
me: Great context. At a high level we sit between your help desk and CRM and sync both ways automatically.
them: Okay but honestly we already use Zapier for some of that. Why would we switch?
them: And what about pricing? We're a 40-person team and budget is tight this quarter.
me: Totally hear you on budget.
them: My other concern is security — we handle a lot of customer PII, so that's a real worry for us.
them: This sounds interesting but I think I need to think about it and talk to my team.
me: Of course.
them: One last thing, does this actually integrate natively with Salesforce or is it through some middleware?
me: Let me show you exactly how that works.
