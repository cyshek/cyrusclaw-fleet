**Title:** I built a free Chrome extension that shows you exactly how your page looks when shared on Slack/iMessage + instant SEO grade

**Body:**
Hey r/webdev — I just published **PagePeek** on the Chrome Web Store and wanted to share it here.

**What it does:**
One click on any page gives you:
- SEO health score with the specific issues flagging (missing H1, no meta description, no viewport tag, slow load, etc.)
- Live preview of exactly how the page's link card looks when shared on Slack, iMessage, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn

**Why I built it:**
I kept running into the same problem: clients would share a link to their website in a chat and it would show up as a blank white square. No image, no title, just a raw URL. Turns out most small business sites have broken or missing Open Graph tags and they have no idea. This extension makes it instantly visible — one click, no signup, no account, 100% free.

It's useful for:
- Quickly auditing any page you're browsing
- Catching OG tag issues before a client calls you about their ugly share card
- A fast sanity check on SEO basics without opening a full audit tool

**Body:**
I published a small Chrome extension called **PagePeek** that does two things in one click:

1. **SEO grade** — scores the page and flags the specific issue (missing meta description, no H1, not mobile-friendly, slow load, missing alt text, broken links, etc.)
