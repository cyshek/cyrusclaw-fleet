## Sales Call Structure (Carson's framework)

**Discovery → Demo → Close** (one-call close default)

### Call framing (open)
> "Hey, look man... the whole point of this call is just to see if I can even help you or not. And then if I can, I'm going to jump into a little bit of what I do... I want to spend the next 10, 20, 30 minutes max just asking you some questions, figuring out what's going well for you, what you think you should improve on, and see if there's any way I could help. Sound good?"

### Demo transition
> "Okay man, John, I think we can really help you here. Um, if you're good, I'll just pull up my screen share and I'll kind of walk you through everything we do."

### Unique mechanism
- Name your offer so it's tangible/sounds cool. Examples: "AI patient acquisition system," "three-pillar AI patient sales process," "lead-to-listing AI generator," "real estate AI three-pillar system."
- Carson's old real estate version = 3 pillars: lead generation → appointment setting → sales.

### Tailoring the deck
- Tailor to THEIR problems, not what you want. "Put yourself in their shoes." Check in slide-by-slide: *"Hey, does this make sense? Does everything good here?"* Act as the "band-aid" patching each problem they raised.

### Close
- **Double tie-down**: get them 100% bought in, make THEM explain what they liked and what will help their business.
- Then state pricing + what each option gets → **GO SILENT.** "Let them digest it. Don't overspeak."
- **DON'T** discount unprompted: *"Oh yeah okay, it's five grand but we can do two grand. No, do not do that. It's five grand. Yep. Let them think about it."*
- Then handle objections/rebuttals.

### Two-call close (optional)
- Initial **15–20 min discovery phone call** to qualify → then book Zoom appt. Requires adding a calendar + extra GHL setup. Carson recommends **stick with one-call close** for course followers.

## Objection Handling (Ben Williams masterclass — VERBATIM)

Guest: **Ben Williams**, longtime sales coach/trainer, monthly guest speaker in Carson's mastermind.

**1. "Just checking out other agencies" (tire-kicker):**
> "I call them out immediately... 'So you just get on calls with different agencies every single day just to hear about their product, or are you actually looking to make more money?' ... Nobody books a call just to get more information. That's like somebody walking into a gym to be like, I just want to see what your membership prices were. No, you came in here because you're fat and you want to be in better shape... Either you're selling a product or they're selling you an excuse."

**2. "It's too expensive / competitor is cheaper":**
> "Okay, so you saw a service similar to ours that cost less money... Why do you think theirs is cheaper?... So they want to service everybody... Why do you think everybody doesn't drive a Lamborghini?... And the agency that you want to work with, do you want them to be the cheapest or the best?... Then that's why people work with us."

**3. "Prove you're the best":**
> Pull up a testimonial sheet. If no testimonials yet: *"How many testimonials would you need to see for it to make sense before you bought?"* → *"Is it that you want to see a couple people, or that you just want to feel confident in your decision?"* → *"There's two options. You can either make the decision to change and move forward with a guarantee that you're going to make money, or you can make the decision not to move forward, and I can guarantee you nothing will change."*

**4. "I'll come back in November / money issue":**
> "Is it a cost issue or a money issue?... If you're not making money, I definitely think something needs to change. Now, hypothetically, if you had our service or anybody else's, do you think you'd be in a position where you had more money?... So it makes more sense to take a risk now and stop living the same life over and over so that way we could live a different life tomorrow, right?... Cool. Ready to get started?... Check your email. There's a payment link going out."

## Fulfillment — How the service is built/delivered

### Onboarding (must happen within 48hr of close — buyer's-remorse window)
1. **Tag client "closed client"** in GHL Contacts → triggers **"Client Closed" workflow** (instant SMS + email).
   - SMS: *"Hey [first name], welcome to the family. We are super excited to work with you and get closer to your goals using our AI systems. Please click the link below to get your onboarding process started."* + onboarding funnel link.
   - Email: same "welcome to the family" message.
2. **Client Onboarding Funnel** (pre-built in snapshot) — 5 steps:
   - **Step 1: Expectations video** (3–10 min; reiterate everything from sales call; align expectations — "most important")
   - **Step 2: Connect Facebook Business Manager** — client invites your email via Business Portfolio → People → **Full Control / full admin** (NOT Partners route — partners = limited visibility/headaches). Help them create Page + Ad Account if missing.
   - **Step 3: CRM overview** (3–5 min video on GHL; client gets login to their own sub-account)
   - **Step 4: Intake form** — first/last name, phone, email, business name, state, city, **home address**, what they need help with, brand logos/files.
     - 🔑 **Home address "sauce":** send a **gift/card/chocolates/flowers (~$50)** = major anti-churn / anti-refund move. "The big agencies do it; smaller ones don't."
   - **Step 5: Next steps video** (support email; excited for kickoff/game-plan call)
3. **Kickoff/Onboarding call ("success call")** scheduled 48–72 hrs after close (sooner = better; biggest agencies do it same day).
   - Walk through a **110-page success slide deck** covering all expectations (his agency's CSM runs it now).
   - Get business info for **A2P** verification; buy phone number for client sub-account.
   - **Sign agreement** (see below).

### Client Onboarding SOP (6 steps, in snapshot doc)
1. Schedule onboarding call → 2. Send onboarding funnel → 3. Client completes funnel → 4. Review submissions → 5. Host onboarding call → 6. Begin fulfillment.

### Sub-accounts & contracts
- Make each client a **GHL sub-account** (Agency view → create sub-account → blank). $497 plan auto-creates on form completion.
- Invite client: Settings → Team → Add User → Account/User role → assign their sub-account only (can restrict visibility of sites/automations/contacts to avoid confusion).
- **Agreements:** GHL → Payments → Documents & Contracts → upload PDF (draft via ChatGPT; "not a lawyer / not legal advice"). Add text fields, e-signature, date for client. **Replaces DocuSign/PandaDoc.** States: Waiting for others / Completed / Draft.

### Expectations / timeline script (the fulfillment promise)
- Ads + AI caller setup: **7–10 days** to fully set up/go live.
- Then **7–14 days testing creatives** — NO guarantee of qualified leads in this window.
- **Month 1:** don't expect crazy volume (still optimizing).
- **Month 2:** leads should be qualified and ready to buy.
- Principle: **underpromise, overdeliver.** "If you tell them 5 appts and get 15, they keep paying. If you promise 15 and get 10, they're pissed."
- Ethics emphasized; first **5–10 clients = "test subjects"** to build a great product.

### Retention / communication cadence
- **Weekly Zoom calls for first month** (4 weeks) → **bi-weekly for next 2 months** → **monthly** thereafter.
- Designated comms channel (Slack for higher-ticket; GHL "starred contacts" via SMS number for lower; WhatsApp). Avoid email as primary.
- Reporting: "Client CEO dashboard" (Google Sheet) — lead count, show rate, CPL, spend, CTR (auto-fed via GHL/Zapier); advanced = client-facing custom GPT fed live data.

### Upsells / "stickiness" (V6)
- Use Manus on full client chat thread to craft upsell offers w/ pricing.
- **Make yourself sticky:** take over client's main organic website, replace their (unchecked) form with YOUR GHL form → captures organic leads + makes you hard to leave. Add **Closebot** chatbot.
- Examples: ads-only client → upsell AI caller later (leads need contacting); organic traffic → add form/chatbot.
- Timing: don't pitch on call 1; wait month 2/3/4; justify with traffic/conversion numbers.

### AI caller logic (V7)
- Calls opted-in leads **within ~5 min**; handles qualifying questions, books appts live onto GHL calendar, 24/7.
- Build multiple agents per task: (a) call new opt-ins to book, (b) call 24hr before appt to confirm/reschedule live onto open calendar slots.
- **Compliance:** research per-state US laws + HIPAA; only call consented/opted-in leads; don't spam scraped lists. "Be educated, not paranoid."
