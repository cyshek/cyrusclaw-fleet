Protect your store data with automated backups and instant one-click restore.

Avada Backups & Restore keeps your Shopify store safe by automatically backing up your products, orders, and store data on a scheduled basis. Back up theme files and restore everything with one click whenever something goes wrong. Set your backup schedule once and let the app run in the background. No technical knowledge needed. Works for all Shopify merchants who want peace of mind without manual effort.
