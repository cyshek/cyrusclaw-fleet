## 1. Product Research

### Tools
- **Kalodata** (calodata.com) — primary weapon. Scrapes TikTok Shop best-sellers + the actual UGC video ads attached to them. [smRBadM78wQ], [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~13m], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~1m]
- **Meta Ad Library** — second-pass validation. Search the product name and competitor keywords. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~8m]
- **Google Trends** — 5-year view, US filter. Catch products on the rising slope, not at the peak. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~13m], [vcQnKWhOC9Y]
- **Burner method** — fresh IG/TikTok account, like dropship/ad-style content until the feed serves you products. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~3m]
- **PPI Ads / WinningHunter / Atria** — supplementary. Bundled in **Luxury Tools** ($20–25/mo via whop.com) which also bundles Kalodata + ChatGPT + Canva + Flair. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~47m], [smRBadM78wQ]

### Criteria (all on a spectrum, rank-ordered)
1. **Deep desire / problem solving** — tied to insecurity, time-saving, day-to-day pain, not a first-world annoyance. Easier makeup routine taps insecurity; supplements tap health-fear. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~2m], [smRBadM78wQ]
2. **Visual / self-explaining** — beginners can't write direct-response scripts, so the product must SHOW the effect (lashes applied in seconds; vibration plate shaking the body). [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~3m], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~2m]
3. **Trust** — buyer must SEE it working in real time. Vibration plate = body movement = "exercise" = trust. Pimple-patch-for-weight-loss = no trust = needs prelander + DR scripts. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~3m]
4. **Unique + perceived value** — must not look like a $15 item from Walmart. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~3m]
5. **In demand on an IMPULSE platform** (TikTok Shop or already on Meta), NOT Amazon. Amazon = intent buyer; Meta = brain-rot scroll. AliExpress/Amazon products usually fail on Meta. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~7m]
6. **COGS / shippable** — 3–4× markup achievable, under ~60 cm and ~5 kg (volumetric weight cutoff from China). [smRBadM78wQ], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~6m]
7. **Virality / scroll-stopper** — "what the f*ck is that." Not seen at Target. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~6m]
8. **TAM right-sized** — weight-loss huge; book-readers small but passionate works; ultra-niche gardener-of-one-plant fails. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~6m]
9. **New to market on Meta** — kalodata go DEEP (page 30+, not page 1–3) for $200–300k/mo TikTok Shop products that aren't yet ported to Meta. Be FIRST on the Meta auction. [smRBadM78wQ], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~5m], [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~14m]

### Disqualifiers (especially for beginners)
- **Needs 5–20 min VSL** → old-people products like hearing aids ("Hearing Co" example, 20k likes, scaled team). Can't compete. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~18m]
- **Insecure middle-aged niches with mature competition** (hair-loss DHT — quiz landers, 10 ads/day pipeline). [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~19m]
- **Abstract / invisible mechanism** — filtered shower head, water sink anti-acne filter, supplements where you can't see the effect for weeks. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~16m], [smRBadM78wQ]
- **Already saturated on Meta** — competitor running 6+ months, 10 ads/day, quiz lander. You can't out-spend them; FB auction will crush you. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~9m]
- **Heavy / oversize** — kills shipping. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **Year-old TikTok Shop trend** — peak passed. Want month-old. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **No buying intent on impulse platforms** — if it only sells on Amazon, it won't sell on Meta. [hL8jqLjB57Q @ ~7m]

## 3. Pricing & Margins

### Markup math
- **3× markup minimum**, 4–5× ideal, off (product + shipping + ~$1–2 agent service fee). [smRBadM78wQ]
- $10 COGS → $30 sell = $20 acquisition buffer. $50 sell = $40 buffer (much better). [smRBadM78wQ]
- Break-even ROAS math: $100 sell, $20 COGS → BE ROAS 1.25, target 1.8–2.0 minimum, 2.2–2.4 to scale. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~9m]

### AOV preference
- **Higher-AOV preferred.** A $20 product at 2 ROAS = ~$1 profit/order. A $100 product at 2 ROAS = ~$25 profit/order (25×). [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~10m]
- Target product price floor: **$30, preferably $40+**, even $100. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~10m]
- After scaling, price-test in increments: $5 for sub-$30 products, $10 for higher. Duplicate the product page at the new price, run top ads to it via a new campaign for 3–4 days. NEVER edit original price live. One brand stretched AOV by $30. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~30m]

### 1688 sourcing
- **1688.com is THE source.** AliExpress and Alibaba both source from 1688. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~17m], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~10m], [smRBadM78wQ]
- Hierarchy: AliExpress (noob) → Alibaba (intermediate) → 1688 (OG). 1688 is Chinese-only; needs an agent.
- Workflow: use Google Translate to get the Chinese product name, search 1688, get factory pricing. Ex: $30 on Alibaba = $15–20 on 1688. [smRBadM78wQ], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~10m]
- **Avoid CJ Dropshipping, AutoDS, Zendrop** — affiliate-shilled trash. [smRBadM78wQ], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~10m]
- **Get a real agent.** Find on Twitter via keyword search. Agent integrates with Shopify with auto-tracking. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~17m], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~10m]

### Real break-even (must subtract from "profit")
- Processing fees: ~2–3%
- Chargebacks: ~1% (without CS) → up to 5–10% (with bad batch and no CS)
- Returns: ~3–6%
- On $100k revenue, ~$9k vanishes → a "20–30% margin" becomes 10–20%. [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~24m]
- Subtract a rolling 6-month chargeback% + refund% (e.g., 6%) from sales daily to get **true free cash flow.** Don't reinvest Shopify-held reserves. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~20m]

## 4. Ad Creatives

### UGC stealing flow (the standard play)
1. Open Kalodata, navigate to "Videos & Ads" for the product. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~14m]
2. Download the top 6–10 performing TikTok Shop videos.
3. Use **snaptik → CapCut** to strip TikTok watermarks. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~6m]
4. Crop out the "TikTok Shop" badge. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~15m]
5. Cross-check Meta Ad Library — if competitors are already running the same clips, you'll lose the auction. [smRBadM78wQ]
6. Upload 1:1 to Meta.
7. **"Anyone doing real numbers doesn't film themselves."** [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~6m]

### AI actor generation (VEO3)
- Used for a brand doing **>$10k/day**, largely VEO-driven ads. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~0m]
- **Why VEO over UGC creators:**
  - Mass-test 3+ avatars cheap: $600 on UGC vs. minutes on VEO. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~1m]
  - Bold concepts UGC can't do (surreal skits — bald guy magically grows hair + women appear).
  - 2 min per clip → 4–5 ads/day solo.
  - Trend recognition + shock factor → hook rates >50%.
- **Tool: Prompt Cowboy.** Standard prompt → select "VEO3 prompt" mode → step-by-step description → paste optimized JSON into VEO/Gemini. ~2 min generation. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~13m]
- **VEO ad structure:**
  - **HOOK** = VEO shock visual (skit, fake street interview, product-action shot).
  - **BODY** = copy via Claude/ChatGPT + manual human-touch edits. **Delayed payoff**: state the promise early, explain the mechanism (e.g., "DHT causes hair loss, DHT blocker prevents conversion"), reveal product as solution at the end. Educate, don't sell. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~8m]
  - **VEO B-roll** between TikTok stock clips every ~10s to keep hold rate up.
  - **CTA** — low importance.
- Real example shown: "V, your car smells like success" — surreal VEO hook for a car-scent product. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~6m]
- AI UGC platforms also referenced: **TopView**. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~6m]
- Real UGC creators (when you graduate from VEO/ripping): **Incense** + Twitter. Negotiate hard — pitch "first of long-term cooperation" and get them from $300 down to $100. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~7m]

### Ad account warmup
- **Engagement campaign with a picture of his dog, $3–4 total spend.** Proves you're not a bot before your ad account starts running money. [smRBadM78wQ]

### Copy rules
- **Hook / Body / Offer** framework. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~7m]
  - Hook: appeal to the specific demo's pain ("transformed my stomach in seconds" for shapewear)
  - Body: USPs raw / genuine — don't scream "ad"
  - Offer: BOGO best where it fits; BOGO50; free bonus item. Avoid "50% off today" (saturated, also FB rejects). [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~9m], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~13m]
- **80% raw long-form UGC testimonial** (1–5 min). Subtle relatable touches ("foot was healed," "fluid retention water weight") make it feel human. 20% mix of shorter ads. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~11m]
- Steal Ad Library competitor primary text → run through ChatGPT to rewrite. Headline = the offer. [smRBadM78wQ]
- If rejections cascade: strip primary text, use simple headline like "Enjoy comfort now" instead of "Never wake up again" or "50% off." Avoid scammy/promise words. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~23m]
- **Rejected ad recovery trick:** swap the rejected ad's creative for a compliant cat photo and pause it (don't delete). Internal "Hiva" trust score recovers — deletion does not. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~24m]

### Creative diversity (post-Andromeda update)
- Stop iterating the same angle. Build separate creatives per avatar (pillow example: neck pain, hot sleeper, comfort, posture). Tailored copy makes each avatar feel "this is made for me." [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~15m, ~25m]

## 5. Meta Ads Campaign Structure

### Exact setup (the canonical Subi test)
- **Objective:** Sales (or Manual Sales depending on which video). [smRBadM78wQ], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~18m]
- **Campaign type:** CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization), NOT ABO. [smRBadM78wQ], [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~13m]
- **Budget:** $100–150/day total. He uses $150 as default. [smRBadM78wQ], [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~14m], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~19m], [vcQnKWhOC9Y]
- **Geo:** USA ONLY for tests (or USA + Canada in one early video). UK/AU/NZ launched as separate CBOs later when scaling. [smRBadM78wQ], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~14m]
- **Audience:** Advantage+ Audience ON (don't pick interests). [smRBadM78wQ], [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~18m]
- **Placements:** Advantage+ Placements ON. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **Ad sets:** 6–12 ad sets, ONE ad per ad set (duplicate ad set, swap creative). Same identical settings across all. [smRBadM78wQ], [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~13m], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~13m]
- **Start time:** 12am–5am for a fresh day of pixel learning. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **Days 1–3: do not touch.** No add/remove ads, no budget changes. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **One testing campaign per store.** New ads = new ad sets inside the same campaign. NEVER 20 campaigns. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **Why Advantage+:** Meta hides the button to switch back on purpose. If their AI lost you money you'd stop spending → they'd lose. Trust the pixel. Interest targeting is being deliberately deprecated. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~18m]

### Daily target metrics
[PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~2m–15m], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~20m], [smRBadM78wQ]

| Metric | Day 1 ideal | Acceptable | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPM | <$30 | $30–70 (up to $150 in beauty/supps) | Mostly noise — drops with spend |
| CPC | <$1.50 USD | $1–3 (sometimes $4–6 if niche) | Read with CTR |
| CTR | 3–5% | 2–3% baseline; 7–10% = banger | Read with CPC |
| **ATC %** | **5–10%** | 5–10% **always required** | Read from Shopify, not Meta |
| ROAS | varies | 0.8–2 D2; break-even D3 | Compute from COGS+AOV |

- Higher CPM in a niche (beauty/supps) is FINE if CPC stays under $1.50 and CTR is high — proves there's competition AND demand. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~7m]

### Scaling-only metrics (after a winner)
- **Hook rate** (first 2–3 s view-through): 40–60% target. <30% = bad hook losing 20–30% of customers. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~40m]
- **Hold rate** (made it to body): 15–25%.
- **Frequency** <1.5 for top of funnel; >2 = retargeting territory or fatigue.
- **ATC → purchase ratio:** target 20–30%. Low = price/offer/trust issue.
- **RPV** (revenue per visitor): ~$2 baseline, scales with CPC. **Primary metric for A/B tests.**
- **NC-ROAS** vs ROAS: at scale, return customers inflate overall ROAS. Watch **New-Customer ROAS** — that's the ad account's actual job. Scaling on inflated ROAS = death spiral. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~44m]

## 6. Day-by-Day Decision Protocol

### Days 1–3 test cycle (canonical)
[PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~27m], [smRBadM78wQ], [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~20m]

- **Day 1:** look for METRICS + a few sales — NOT profitability. Pixel is finding the avatar. ATC 5–10% = the green light.
- **Day 2:** CPM should drop. ROAS 0.8–2 OK. ATC trending up.
- **Day 3 (verdict day):** CPM $30–40, CPC $1–2, ATC 5–10%, break-even or better. Don't add emotion.

### Branching logic
- **Horrible metrics + broke D1** → kill same day. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~29m]
- **Sick metrics D1** → run full 3 days, don't touch.
- **Mid + sales** → run D2–3.
- **Mid + no sales + bad ATC** → kill D2 (D1 if budget-constrained).
- **Great metrics + no sales** → cause = **offer, landing page, or product photos.** Fix offer FIRST. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~36m]
- **High spend ad set, no sales** → concept OK, execution bad — rebuild script/copy/footage under the same concept. [smRBadM78wQ]
- **High spend ad set + sales** → reiterate variations of that concept.
- **Low spend + sales** → leave it alone (low-hanging-fruit ad, force-spend kills it).

### Q4 cadence (much tighter)
- 1–2 days only per test. Too many winners to dwell. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~7m]
- D1 CPC $3+, CTR 2–3%, ATC <5%, ROAS <0.5 → cut D1 or D2.
- If break-even D1–3, train pixel, swap creators D2–3, fix offer; by D5 if not profit → cut.

### Real day-by-day from the $101k brand
[8CZyDFU9xng @ ~21m]
- D1: spent $150 → $120 rev, 10% ATC, 3 sales. **Keep budget.**
- D2: $246 rev, ATC up, profitable.
- D3: dropped. **Normal. Don't panic.**
- D4: woke to $200 rev pre-noon at 4–5 ROAS → **surf-scaled** ($150 → $300 → $600 every couple hours) while ROAS held. Stabilize when ROAS hits 2–3.
- D5: reset to $300–400 (avoid forced reoptimization).
- After: +20% bumps every 2–3 profitable days. CVR climbed 1.5% → 3.8%.
- Bad day with CVR drop (2.3 → 1.5) → read as "bad Facebook day" (competitors, CPM spike) → **scale DOWN 20%**, let it reoptimize.
- First 5-figure day on day 13. Peak ~$15k/day.

## 7. Scaling: $0 to $100k+

### Budget scaling rules
- **+20% campaign budget every 2–3 profitable days.** [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~23m]
- **Surf-scale** on hot days: double budget every couple hours while ROAS holds. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~22m]
- **Reset down 20%** after a forced reoptimization or bad CVR day. [8CZyDFU9xng]

### Iterating on winners
- Don't copy-paste the winning ad. Extract the **concept** and produce variations (new angle, new actor, new opening line). [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~15m]
- Then go test NEW avatars (pillow: neck pain → hot sleeper → posture → comfort). [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~4m]
- Once voiceovers show proof of concept on a new avatar → commission UGC creators.
- ONE testing campaign per store; new ads enter as new ad sets in the same campaign. [smRBadM78wQ]

### Funnel architecture at scale (the $2M/mo brand)
[qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~15m]
- **Top of funnel (~80% of spend, $700–800k of $1M/mo):** long UGC testimonials. Runs at **1.5–1.6 ROAS** — thin profit OK because it feeds the rest.
- **Middle funnel (~10–15%):** voice-over, product-aware education ads. 2–3 ROAS. Cheap to produce (scrape clips + voiceover).
- **Bottom funnel (~5–10%):** static ads, sale/offer, brand-aware retargeting. 3–5 ROAS. Warmest buyers. Math: 10 statics at $50/day @ 4–5 ROAS = $2.5k/day. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~19m]
- Facebook AI auto-routes audiences across the funnel stages.

### Team hires (in order)
[qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~22m], [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~25m], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~22m]
1. **CS VA** ($200/mo, Upwork or OnlineJobs.ph, Philippines/Pakistan). Hire MOMENT you get a winner.
2. **Video editor** (cheapest creative hire).
3. **Creative strategist** (LinkedIn / Twitter). Writes scripts, sources UGC creators, manages editor. Goal: 20–30 ads/week delivered hands-off.
4. **CS manager** (oversees multiple VAs, KPI tracking, SOPs, weekly reports). Add when ticket volume is unmanageable.
5. Subi's daily reality at scale: "wake up, check employees via WhatsApp and Discord, check ads, look for products. Done. 8 hours of nothing to do." [bHoLaKt0N_w @ ~36m]

### Omnichannel (after consistent $10–20k days)
- **Google** first (best after Meta — outsource to agency, don't run yourself). [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~33m]
- Then **Snapchat, TikTok, AppLovin**.
- Don't move off Meta until Meta is solid. Adds 6-figures/mo and hedges Meta off-days.

## 8. Fulfillment + Ops

### Sourcing chain
- AliExpress only initially (one-off orders while testing). [smRBadM78wQ]
- After winner: switch to a real agent buying from 1688. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~10m], [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~17m]
- At scale: forecast inventory and pre-buy from factory (agent has its own delay). [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~30m]

### Tariff workaround (post May 7 de minimis death)
- Bulk ship China → US 3PL, then 3PL → customer. [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~1m]
- HS-code tariff (~25% on bulk) is cheaper than $25/30%/unit retail surcharge per-order.

### Customer service
- Promise 10–12 day shipping, overdeliver 7–10 days. **Never tell the customer it's from China. Humans not AI.** [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~31m]
- Saved a $200k/mo loss during a product quality crisis using partial refunds + "go green" scripts — kept chargebacks <1% instead of 5–10%. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~26m]

### Chargebacks & processors
- Without CS: chargebacks balloon to 5–10%. With trained VAs: <1%. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~27m]
- **Shopify Payments will shut you down** when you scale too fast. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~21m]
- 10–20% reserves are common. Subi was capped at **$50k/month** for 1–2 months mid-scale because of reserves and processor freeze. Eventually unlocked an "unlimited" processor via a personal connection.
- Don't treat held money as free cash flow. Don't reinvest reserves. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~21m]

## 9. Seasonal Play (Q4)

[vcQnKWhOC9Y], [8CZyDFU9xng]

- **Definition:** products with spike-in-demand windows where demand pre-exists (fleece tights, heated jacket, cooling blanket, hand warmers). You just supply it — no convincing needed.
- **Find:** scan Kalodata daily for seasonal keywords (heated, fleece, comfort, warm). Rip ads as-is.
- **Validate:** Google Trends 5-year US view. Launch on the **rising slope, NOT at the peak.**
- **Campaign:** 1 CBO, USA only, 8–12 ad sets × 1 ad each.
- **Headlines:** "Black Friday 50% off" / "Black Friday BOGO" even in OCTOBER — consumers respond now.
- **Budget:** $150/day per test. Scale aggressively once metrics work — speed = market share because the trend grows exponentially.
- **Cadence:** D1–3 pixel training, break-even acceptable. Profit usually kicks in around D12 if the trend was caught right.
- **Tradeoff:** seasonal = fast cash, higher margins, one-man op, **no exit value.** Evergreen = lower margins, harder, needs creative team, **exitable.** Strategy: stack Q4 seasonal cash → deploy into an evergreen brand built to exit.
- **Failure mode unique to seasonal:** scaling fast attracts sabotage (see §12).
- Real $101k/14-days run used fleece-lined tights as the example/winner. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~21m]

## 10. AI / Non-Human Creator Ads (eIv4UoxJNv4 — full)

- Brand currently doing **>$10k/day**, "well over." Composed mostly of VEO ads. [eIv4UoxJNv4 @ ~0m]
- **Why this exists:** UGC creators cost $200/avatar. To test 3 avatars = $600 and weeks of negotiation. VEO = ~$0 marginal cost and 2 minutes per clip.
- **Bold concepts unlock:** UGC can't produce surreal/skit/shock content. VEO can — and shock = recognition = hook rates pushing 50%+.
- **Trend factor:** VEO ads are recognized as VEO ads. The "what the f*ck" reaction IS the hook.
- **Output multiplier:** 4–5 quality video ads per day, solo, no creator scheduling.
- **Prompt Cowboy** ($) — convert your loose description into a VEO3-optimized prompt automatically. Standard prompt → "VEO3 prompt" mode → step-by-step → paste into VEO/Gemini.
- **VEO ad anatomy:**
  - **HOOK** = VEO (the high-leverage piece). Types: skit, fake street interview, product-action shot (e.g., pill thrown on head → hair spawns).
  - **BODY** = copy via Claude or ChatGPT (Claude slightly better) + manual human edits.
  - **Delayed payoff** structure: promise → mechanism explanation → reveal product as the solution. Don't sell — educate first.
  - **B-roll** = VEO clips inserted between TikTok stock clips every ~10s, keeps hold rate up during body.
  - **CTA** = unimportant.
- Real ad example: "V, your car smells like success" — surreal VEO opener for a car-scent product.
- **AI UGC alternatives mentioned elsewhere:** TopView for AI UGC actors. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~6m]
- **Discipline:** "If you can't even use VEO you'll lose to others who do." AI literacy now mandatory in dropshipping.

## 12. Failure Modes

1. **Payment processor freeze.** Shopify Payments will shut you down when you scale too fast. 10–20% reserves common. Subi was capped at $50k/month for 1–2 months. [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~21m]
2. **Chargeback bleed.** Without CS VAs, chargebacks hit 5–10% of revenue. On $2M = $200k/month evaporated. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~27m]
3. **Creative fatigue.** Every winning ad peaks then crashes. Top of funnel dies first; whole funnel collapses if you don't pipeline new concepts. [smRBadM78wQ], [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~21m]
4. **Ad-account sabotage.** Documented case: paid insider inside his Meta ad account deleted his winning ads while a competitor reuploaded them to their account. Brand crashed from $15k/day to $800/day in a week. Sabotage-for-hire services exist on Discord ($1000s). Targets fast-scaling seasonal launches. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~30m]
5. **Meta ad bans, especially in Q4.** Avoid scammy words ("never," "50% off") in primary text. Rejection cascades drop your hidden "Hiva" trust score. Replace rejected ads with compliant cat photos and pause (don't delete) to recover Hiva. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~23m]
6. **Cutting a winner too early.** Wrong-headed reading of a single metric (CPM, CPC) when the combination would have said "scale." Lost millions in potential brands. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~0m]
7. **Inflated ROAS at scale (NC-ROAS trap).** Return customers inflate overall ROAS. Scaling on overall instead of New-Customer ROAS = death spiral. [PEi_nGNqOZI @ ~44m]
8. **Product quality crisis** at scale (bad batch) → mass refunds and chargebacks if CS team isn't trained. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~26m]
9. **Tariff shock** (post May 7 de minimis death). If you don't bulk-ship to a US 3PL, per-order surcharges kill margin. [6e1IFGs9Clk @ ~1m]
10. **Analysis paralysis.** Spending weeks researching before launching. "$300k profit gone every month you wait in Q4." [bHoLaKt0N_w @ ~5m], [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~11m]
11. **Stealing-only forever.** TikTok-rip ads work for ~weeks then die because (a) competitors catch up, (b) FB auction prefers older accounts, (c) creative fatigue. Must transition to custom UGC or VEO content to sustain. [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~21m]
12. **Pump-and-dump exposure.** A pure-dropship store has zero defenses against sabotage, supplier loss, or processor freeze. Custom-everything + USA production = the only real moat. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~33m]

## 13. What's Repeatable for $1–3k Capital + AI Agent Operator + No Meta-Ads Instinct (brutally honest)

### What IS plausibly replicable
- **Product research via Kalodata + Ad Library cross-check.** Mechanical. - **Copy via Claude/ChatGPT** with delayed-payoff structure. Mechanical given the framework. [eIv4UoxJNv4]

### What is NOT realistic on $1–3k + no Meta instinct
- **Reaching the $100k+/day headline numbers.** Those require:
  - Custom UGC pipeline ($1k+/mo for creators + strategist hire) [qtI6Sb64P4o @ ~23m]
  - Patience through Shopify Payments freezes and ~$50k/mo cash flow caps [2_Ms19677Uw]
  - A creative strategist + video editor + CS manager + 1688 agent [qtI6Sb64P4o], [AQGM2TlZqtU]
  - Custom product + branding + USA 3PL to defend against sabotage [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~33m]
- **Capital reality.** Subi himself says expect to lose **~$1,500 across ~5 dead products** before a hit. [bHoLaKt0N_w @ ~5m] On $1k–3k you get **one or two real attempts.** If both miss, you're out. The $115k/10-day and $101k/14-day stories are survivor-bias outliers, not the median outcome.
- **Reading mid-test metric combinations** is THE skill subi spends a 50-minute video teaching, with explicit warnings that misreading kills brands. [PEi_nGNqOZI] An LLM agent can apply the rules but the edge cases ("good metrics no sales = OFFER, but only if landing is fine, but only if photos are visual...") are judgment-heavy. An agent operator will likely cut winners early or scale losers late on the first few attempts. **Budget for this learning tax.**
- **Sabotage defense.** Custom branding + DMCA requires a name, registered trademark, and an attorney. Not $1–3k territory. [8CZyDFU9xng @ ~33m]
- **Payment processing at scale.** Subi unlocked his unlimited processor "via a personal connection." [2_Ms19677Uw @ ~24m] No agent-operator equivalent exists.
- **Exiting.** Brokers want 3+ year operating history for a 2–3× multiple. Anything under = 1× or less. [AQGM2TlZqtU @ ~28m] An agent-operator dropship is not exitable.

### The honest agent-operator playbook (compressed)
1. Capital: **$2k minimum** (one product = ~$450 in ad test budget + ~$30 Shopify + ~$50 sample + ~$25 Luxury Tools + ~$50 app stack + ~$1,300 reserve for round 2). Better with $3k.
2. **Pick a Subi S-tier visual product** with trust built in (vibration-plate-class, magnetic-lashes-class). Skip supplements, hearing aids, hair-loss DHT, filtered shower heads.
3. **Rip Kalodata UGC + generate 2 VEO hook variants** = 6–8 ads total.
4. **Run the canonical Subi test:** CBO, USA, $150/day, 6 ad sets × 1 ad, Advantage+ on, don't touch for 3 days. Start 12–5am.

6. **If a winner emerges:** spawn a CS VA, set 20% bump cadence, surf-scale only on confirmed >4 ROAS hours. Add the funnel (middle = voiceover, bottom = static retargeting) once at $5k/day.
7. **Expect to need 3–5 attempts** to land a winner. Plan for $1,500–3,000 in losses.
8. **Realistic target:** $2k–10k/month net within 2–3 months IF you hit. Not $100k. Anyone promising the headline figures on $1–3k is selling a course.
9. **Floor:** lose everything by month 2. Likely outcome unless the operator (human or AI) develops Meta-ads pattern recognition through real spend, which is exactly what subi spent years and tens of thousands learning.
