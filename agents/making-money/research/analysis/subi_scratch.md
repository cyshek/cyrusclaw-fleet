  - CVR: NO universal ideal. If CPC=$1 cheap traffic → 1-3% normal; if CPC=$3-5 expensive → need 4-6%+. [~11:50-13:30]
  - ATC rate: ALWAYS look for 5-10% (fixed — window shoppers exist at any price). [~14:30-15:30]
- Reading combinations:
  - Low CPC ($1) → CTR/CPM matter less; ATC 5-10% still required.
  - High CPC ($4-6) → NEED high CTR (5-10%) AND high CVR (5-6%) to justify.
  - Mid metrics ($2 CPC, 3-4% CTR) → ATC becomes the tiebreaker (need 7-10%).
- Cut signal: high CPC + low CTR + low ATC (<3-5%) = kill.
- Test cadence: 3 days, $100-150/day, 1 CBO, USA only. Look for METRICS + a FEW sales — NOT profitability. Pixel needs time to find avatar. [~27:00-28:30]
- Day-by-day branching:
  - Day 1 horrible metrics + broke → kill day 1.
  - Sick metrics → run all 3 days.
  - Mid + sales → run to day 2-3.
  - Mid + no sales + bad ATC → kill day 2 (day 1 if broke).
  - Great metrics + no sales → issue is OFFER, landing page, or product photos. Test offer first. [~29:00-31:30]

  - Hook rate (first 2-3s view-through): want 40-60%. <30% = bad hook losing 20-30% of customers.

  - Frequency: <1.5 (top of funnel). >2 = retargeting territory or fatigue.
