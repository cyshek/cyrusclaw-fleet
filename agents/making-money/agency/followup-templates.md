# FOLLOW-UP TEMPLATES — Agency cold email, "Touch 2" (v1, 2026-06-29)

_Cold outreach roughly DOUBLES reply rate with a second touch. This file is the per-vertical "touch 2" — sent ~3–4 days after the first email if no reply. Same Gmail thread is ideal (reply on the original), shorter than touch 1, soft, no guilt-trip._

## Why a 2nd touch
- Most replies to cold email come on touch 2–3, not touch 1. The first email often just gets buried.
- The bump must be SHORT, reference the first email lightly ("floating this back up"), restate the ONE core benefit, and give an easy out. No new pitch, no new links beyond the booking page.

## Universal touch-2 rules
- **Under ~70 words.** Shorter than touch 1.
- **One link only:** the booking page `https://cal.com/cyshek`.
- **Soft re-open:** "floating this back up" / "didn't hear back — totally fine if it's not a fit."
- **Restate ONE benefit:** a new lead gets a real response in ~60 seconds, 24/7 (so they stop losing leads to whoever answers first).
- **Easy out:** "just reply 'not now' and I'll leave it." Removes pressure, lifts reply rate.
- **Same signature:** — Cyrus. No logo wall.
- **After touch 2, optionally ONE breakup email (touch 3) at ~7–10 days, then STOP.** Three emails is the hard ceiling (the SiteLens bounce mess came from over-sending).
