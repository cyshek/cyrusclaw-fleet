**Role Description **

This is a full-time, on-site position based in San Francisco, CA, for a Product Manager at Lance. The Product Manager will oversee the roadmap and lifecycle of assigned products, collaborating with cross-functional teams to define requirements, prioritize features, and deliver innovative solutions. Responsibilities include conducting market research, gathering customer feedback, managing development cycles, and evaluating product performance. The role demands proactive leadership, effective communication, and a commitment to delivering exceptional tools that drive client success.
